1. Reflection about making an impactful resume and professional brand online​.


Objective statements are becoming a thing of resume past, so utilize that space (and no more) to describe your brand. Your professional summary should just be a short line or two about you as a professional that highlights your skills and experience.

Effective personal branding will differentiate you from the competition and allow you to build trust with prospective clients and employers. You never get a second chance to make a first impression—make it one that will set you apart, build trust and reflect who you are.

By developing a personal brand, you're giving yourself a competitive advantage. People will remember you and your brand when they look for someone with your particular skills and expertise. In addition, a strong personal brand can lead to new opportunities.

13. why should you highlight important details in making a resume​


Answer:

The highlight section of a resume, also known as a resume summary, lists notable achievements, expertise and experience that are relevant to the job position someone is applying to. Applicants with extensive experience can take advantage of this section to catch the attention of hiring managers. Having a well-written highlights section on your resume provides many benefits, it allows employers to determine your suitability for the job quickly. It allows you to display your experience and skills, when you are well-qualified for a position, the highlights section of your resume will immediately showcase your compatibility with the company. You can include searchable keywords, applicant tracking systems catch keywords in a resume that are relevant to the job listing. Including relevant searchable keywords in the highlights section of your resume may give you a better chance at appearing in an employers’ applicant tracking system. Even when employers are not using the applicant tracking system, you should decipher some relevant keywords and include them in the highlights section. Including a well-written resume highlights section allows hiring managers to quickly see any skills and experience relevant to the position, and determine if you are the best candidate for the job.

Explanation:

Here is a 2-3 minute speech about the topic of sustainability:

"Hello everyone, today I want to talk to you about sustainability and why it is important.

As we all know, the earth's resources are limited, and it is our responsibility to use them wisely and preserve them for future generations. Sustainability is the practice of meeting our current needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.

There are many ways we can contribute to sustainability in our daily lives. One way is by reducing our consumption and waste. This can be as simple as bringing our own reusable bags to the grocery store, or choosing to buy products that are made from recycled materials. We can also reduce our energy consumption by turning off lights and appliances when they are not in use, and by using energy-efficient products.

Another way to contribute to sustainability is by supporting local businesses and buying locally-grown produce. This helps to reduce the carbon emissions associated with transportation and supports the local economy.

In conclusion, sustainability is important for the well-being of our planet and its inhabitants. By making small changes in our daily lives, we can all contribute to a more sustainable future. Thank you."

A Dialogue Between Two Friends on Reading an Interesting Novel

Mikasa: Hello Eren, how are you?

Eren: I am fine and you?

Mikasa: I am fine too. What's the book you are reading. It seems you are deeply absorbed in it.

Eren: Yes, I have already read the book once. Now it is my second reading. The book is indeed very interesting.

Mikasa: I see. What is the name of the book and by whom it is written?

Eren: The title of the book is "Attack on Titan" and it is written by famous mangaka Hajime Isayama

Mikasa: Yes, I know. I have read his "Shounen Jump: One Piece". That is also a very interesting comic. It tells the story of a man who lives a hard life who wants to be a pirate. What's the book of "Attack on Titan" about?

Eren: The book is about a boy who wants to revenge his family's death because of titans. The hero is a thirteen years' old boy named Eren Yeager.

Mikasa: I feel very interested to know the story of the book.

Eren: The story is really very interesting. Eren accidentally happened to be a good titan.

Mikasa: It is okay. Please tell me the story in brief.

Eren: Yes. The secret was kept hidden in a far off island called Paradis by some Eldians. Eren and his allies were looking for it.

Mikasa: Oh, I see. But why is this book so interesting to you?

Eren: The book is really very interesting as the author with his powerful pen keeps the readers spellbound with adventures, brave deeds, and the horrors and terrors of the struggle from the beginning to the end.

Mikasa: Excellent! Please give me the book after you will have finished reading it. Good bye now.

Eren: Ok Goodbye!

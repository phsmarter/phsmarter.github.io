6. what will happen if the loam with monggo clay with monggo and sand with monggo is inside our house​


Loam, sand, and clay all have different properties that may affect the way they behave inside a house. Loam and clay can both absorb a lot of water, which may cause them to swell and crack in certain conditions. Sand is typically inert and will not expand or contract, so it is less likely to cause problems. However, sand can get dusty and be difficult to clean up, so its use inside a house may be limited. Overall, the properties of various soil types and how they behave inside a house can make them a useful or problematic choice for construction or landscaping materials.

The head chef oversees the kitchen operations, creates menus, and manages inventory. The assistant chefs assist in the preparation of ingredients, cooking, and overseeing cleanliness and safety in the kitchen area. The cook is in charge of grilling, frying, or baking food, while the trainee student helps with cleaning, preparing ingredients, and learning the ropes of kitchen operations.

The dining staff includes a supervisor who oversees the dining area's operations and four (4) servers who attend to the customers' needs. The servers are responsible for welcoming guests, taking orders, delivering food, ensuring customer satisfaction, and keeping the dining area clean and tidy.

In terms of the restaurant's market, the restaurant caters to people of all ages and backgrounds, including families, groups, and individuals. The restaurant offers affordable prices, making it accessible to customers who are looking for good food at a reasonable rate.

Overall, the company is a bustling establishment that serves quality food and provides an excellent learning opportunity for the TVL student. The OJT student will learn various essential skills, such as food preparation, kitchen safety, customer service, communication, teamwork, and time management, that are essential to becoming a competent and successful professional in the restaurant industry.

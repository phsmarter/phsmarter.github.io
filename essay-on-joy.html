Note:

This is an open-ended(?) question so fill in the blanks.

1st blank, write the things you love to do(ex. your hobbies, playing games, and such)

2nd blank, write who you love spending your time with(ex. family, friends, neighbours, classmates, pets)


3. make a 5 sentenced essay about "There is Joy in Waiting"​


Answer:

Waiting can be difficult, especially in a world that often values immediate gratification. But there is joy to be found in the waiting itself. It can be a time to reflect, to slow down, and to appreciate the present moment. By embracing the waiting, we can find contentment and gratitude in the here and now, rather than constantly striving for what is next. In the end, the joy that comes from waiting can be just as sweet, if not sweeter, than the joy of the thing we are waiting for.

Athletes must be aware of the psychological. The factors in sport, the important role they play and how they contribute towards maintaining health and well-being.

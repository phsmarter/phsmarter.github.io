Explanation:

The ideology of totalitarianism is characterized by a single party rule, state control of the economy, and the use of force and propaganda to maintain power. Kim Jon-un's order to kill the first people who got infected with COVID-19 in North Korea exemplifies this ideology because it shows a willingness to use extreme and violent measures to maintain control over the population, even at the cost of human lives.

The community spirit that has emerged during the pandemic reflects a strong sense of nationalism, where people are willing to help each other and work together to overcome challenges. For example, the Bayanihan E-Konsulta program allows people to consult with doctors online for free, helping to provide medical assistance to those who cannot afford it. This program shows how Filipinos are using technology and innovation to respond to the challenges of the pandemic while demonstrating their patriotism and commitment to the common good.

13. 2. Which of the following is NOT an element of biodiversity?a. Genetic diversity c. species diversityc. ecosystem diversity d index of diversity​


BIOLOGICAL DIVERSITY or BIODIVERSITY

Simply refers to the Different Kinds of Life on Earth.

Are the main components that sustain and make up the biological life of nature and all living organisms.

It is directly connected and related to the functions and balance of communities of living things and their surroundings or the ecosystems.

It is important for the processes that connect all the life on Earth, including humans. It includes a wide range of animals, plants and microorganisms. A healthy ecosystem is able to provide clean air to breathe and food to eat.

It is composed of Three Main Types:

1. GENETIC DIVERSITY – it refers to the inherent compositions of the species in the diversity, whether it is in a form of plants, animals, and even small organisms called microorganisms. Genetical diversity is a representation of wide differences, variations, or diversity within the kinds of species.

2. SPECIES DIVERSITY- the term itself, refers to the different kinds or variety of breeds/families of living organisms that can be found in the diversity of different habitats in a community.

3. ECOSYSTEM DIVERSITY- It focuses on the wide variations of places that a living organism can stay and sustain its need to survive. Habitat in the form of communities and ecological processes in the biosphere for any kind of living thing. It is considered a complex level of diversity.

12. basic economic problems confronting the development of the philippines in the 21st century


The main economic problem confronting the development of our country in the 21st century is poverty and unequal distribution of income. Poverty is when a family has insufficient income to provide the basic needs, including nutritional requirements for everyday living.


Low investment in Human Resource Development also affects the economic problem in our country. Some income are less than the minimum requirement; they are underemployed. A large portion of Filipinos are still unemployed, even those who graduated with a degree.

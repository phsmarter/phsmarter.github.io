4. Propose a solution to a problem with technology. Then, persuade your audience that your solution to the problem is viable. Write a speech/essay with 2-3 paragraphs


Answer:Key Points

Persuasive speeches can come in many forms, such as sales pitches, debates, and legal proceedings.

Persuasive speeches may utilize the three modes of persuasion: ethos, pathos and logos.

Ethos is the most important appeal in a persuasive speech.

Factors such as body language, the willingness of the audience, and the environment in which the speech is given, all affect the success of a persuasive speech.

Audience Analysis is important in a persuasive speech, as the audience will be convinced for their own reasons, not for the speaker’s reasons.

Key Terms

persuasion: the process aimed at changing a person’s (or a group’s) attitude or behavior

Logos: logical appeal to the audience; does the speaker’s argument make sense?

pathos: emotional appeal to the audience

Audience Analysis: the speaker’s understanding of the audience’s knowledge, personal experience, and proximity to a topic

ethos: credibility of the speaker, assigned to them by the audience

Expository text is used to present information in a way that is both instructional and useful. The content is fact-based, with the goal of exposing the truth via a trustworthy source. 25. Waste management speech 1st paragraph - introduction 2nd paragraph -Problem 3rd paragraph - solution 4th paragraph - conclusion


Waste management is a critical issue facing our planet. Every day, we generate huge amounts of waste, and it is our responsibility to ensure that this waste is managed in a way that is safe, sustainable, and environmentally friendly.

One of the biggest problems with waste management is that we are generating more waste than we can effectively manage. Landfills are reaching capacity, and the burning of waste generates harmful emissions that contribute to climate change. In addition, much of the waste that we generate is not being properly recycled, which means that valuable resources are being lost.

There are several solutions to these problems. One solution is to reduce the amount of waste that we generate by adopting sustainable consumption practices. This means choosing products that are made from recycled materials, using reusable containers, and avoiding single-use plastics. Another solution is to improve our recycling efforts by investing in better technology and infrastructure, and by educating the public about the importance of recycling.

In conclusion, waste management is a complex issue that requires a multi-faceted approach. By adopting sustainable consumption practices and improving our recycling efforts, we can work towards a more sustainable future and reduce the negative impact that waste has on our planet.

27. write an informative speech about covid-19 using problem and solution ​


Answer:

Good day everyone,

Today, I want to talk to you about a pressing issue that has been affecting our world for over two years now - the COVID-19 pandemic. COVID-19 is a highly contagious respiratory illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It first emerged in Wuhan, China in December 2019 and has since spread rapidly around the globe, resulting in millions of deaths and overwhelming health care systems.

The problem we are facing is that the COVID-19 pandemic has caused a significant impact on our daily lives, as well as on the global economy, health systems, and social structures. The virus has claimed countless lives, and it has become a significant threat to public health.

However, there are solutions that can help combat the spread of COVID-19. The most effective approach to fighting the virus is through the implementation of public health measures, such as wearing masks, practicing social distancing, and frequent hand washing. These measures are essential in preventing the spread of the virus from person to person.

Another critical solution to address the COVID-19 pandemic is through vaccination. Vaccines have been developed to provide immunity to the virus and reduce the risk of severe illness and hospitalization. It is vital to get vaccinated to protect oneself and others from COVID-19. Moreover, the vaccination campaign must be accessible to everyone, regardless of their socioeconomic status or geographic location.

In addition, governments and health care systems must work together to ensure that individuals who test positive for COVID-19 receive proper medical care and support. This includes the provision of adequate medical facilities, staff, and equipment to address the needs of patients.

Furthermore, it is essential to address misinformation and disinformation surrounding COVID-19. Governments and health care systems should provide accurate and reliable information to the public to help combat the spread of rumors and false information.

In conclusion, the COVID-19 pandemic is a global problem that requires a collective effort from everyone to address. By implementing public health measures, getting vaccinated, providing proper medical care and support, and combating misinformation, we can overcome the COVID-19 pandemic and return to a sense of normalcy in our daily lives. Thank you for listening

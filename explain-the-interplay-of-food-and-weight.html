1. Explain the interplay of food and weight​


Explanation:

It is no secret and definitely not new to everyone that the amount of food calories we take in has a direct effect or impact to our weight. This means that there is an interplay of food and weight. Take a read of the following of how the interplay of food and weight works.

If you consume the same number of calories that the body burns over time, the weight stays stable or normal.  If you consume more than the body burns, the weight will definitely go up (overweight).If you consume less than the body burns, the weight will surely go down (underweight).

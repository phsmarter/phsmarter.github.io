20. Compare Latin dances to Modern Standard dances in terms of movements and custumes.​


COMPARE LATIN DANCES TO MODERN STANDARD DANCES

In terms of movements, Latin American dances are consists of  Rumba, Cha Cha Cha, Samba, Jive, and Paso doble while Modern Standard dances include Waltz, Foxtrot, Tango, Quickstep, and Viennese Waltz. Furthermore, in the standard dances, the couple maintains contact hold, whereas, in Latin American dances, non-contact positions are generally taken.

In terms of costumes, Latin outfits became more limited and changed generally in style. This pattern proceeds right up 'til today, and standard outfits are long and streaming, while Latin ensembles are short and various in style. Thus, both costumes are different yet overflow with grace in between the dance performance.

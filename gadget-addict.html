10. 5 reasons why young people continue to be addicted to gadgets​


Answer:

1. Instant gratification: Young people are used to getting what they want immediately, and gadgets provide that instant gratification. Social media, games, and apps offer instant rewards that keep them hooked.

2. FOMO (Fear of Missing Out): Young people are constantly bombarded with images and updates on social media, which creates a fear of missing out on something. They feel the need to constantly check their phones to stay updated and connected.

3. Social validation: Gadgets provide a platform for young people to showcase their lives and receive validation from others. The likes, comments, and followers provide a sense of self-worth and affirmation, which can be addictive.

4. Escape from reality: Gadgets offer an escape from reality and the stresses of daily life. They provide a virtual world where young people can numb their minds and disconnect from the real world.

5. Lack of self-discipline: Young people have not fully developed their self-discipline skills, which makes it difficult for them to limit their gadget use. They find themselves spending more time than intended on their devices and struggle to put them down.

28. In a survey conducted among grade 6 students about the effects of the use of electronic gadgets in studying, for every 10 students, 7 responded positively that use of electronic gadgets is helpful in their studies . What percentage of the students responded positively in the survey?don't answer incorrectly or you will be reported​


Answer:

70%

Step-by-step explanation:

Percentage Formula is

% = ( number of something (in this case, Responded Positively) / total number of Respondent ) times 100

% = (RP / R) x 100

% = ( 7 / 10 ) x 100

% = ( 0.70 ) x 100

% = 70%

2. Define physical education in your own words?​


Physical Education

Physical Education is simply defined as "education through physical activity." Its goal is to improve students' physical competence, knowledge of movement, and ability to apply these skills to a variety of activities related to the development of an active and healthy lifestyle.

Physical education is a school-based programme that focuses on the development of physical fitness. Effective physical education classes assist children in developing competence and confidence, maintaining physical fitness, learning about personal health and wellness, and displaying positive social skills.

Physical education entails preparing the body for improved physical and mental growth. Individuals can improve their motor and cognitive skills by engaging in regular and strenuous exercises, activities, athletics, and other activities.

More about Physical Education:

Physical Education has become a required curriculum in many schools around the country, if not the world. Physical education has been given top priority in various schools and industries in recent years. In addition, many universities offer physical education as a degree program.

Physical Education is critical for students' confidence, as well as their ability and competency to participate in a variety of physical activities. Physical education becomes an important aspect of a student's life. A high-quality P.E. program guarantees that kids can safely participate in a variety of physical activities while having fun.

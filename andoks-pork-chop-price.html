3. 4. Jay bought pork for his pork chop menu. The pork in the supermarket costs P250.50. A 2% sales tax was added to it. How much was the sales tax? How much is the buying price of the pork? Show the sulution​


Answer:

Sales Tax : The sales tax of the pork was Php 5.01.

Pork Price : The price of the pork was Php 255.51.

Solution:

Sales Tax Solution:

Convert 2% to decimal like the procedure below.

2% ÷ 100 = 0.02

Then multiply 250.50 by 0.02 like the procedure below.

250.50 x 0.02 = 5.01

Sales Tax : Php 5.01

Pork Price Solution:

Add 250.50 and 5.01 like the procedure below.

250.50 + 5.01 = 255.51

Pork Price : Php 255.51

Answer:

1. If I found a bag full of money in the streets, I would not keep it. I believe in the importance of honesty and integrity, and keeping found money would be a violation of those principles. Additionally, the money likely belongs to someone else and keeping it would be wrong. I would report the find to the police or an authority figure so that the money can be returned to its rightful owner.

2. If I were taking my second periodic examination in English and saw my best friend cheating, I would report the incident to the teacher or the appropriate authority figure. Cheating is unacceptable and undermines the integrity of the education system. Furthermore, I believe that friend would not be benefitted by cheating and in the long run, it would only harm him. Additionally, if I were to stay quiet and not report the incident, I would be complicit in my friend's dishonesty, which would be a violation of my own principles.

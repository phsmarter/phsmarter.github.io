30. 1. Myself 2. My family 3. My childhood 3.1. achievements 3.2. failures/frustrations 4. My school life 4.1. achievements 4.2. failures 4.3. significant decisions 4.4. significant persons 5. My plan for the future LONG ESSAY


Answer:

1. Myself

I am a person who values hard work and determination. I have always been driven to succeed and to make the most of my abilities. I am a positive and optimistic individual who believes that anything is possible with the right mindset. I have always been a goal-oriented person and I am always looking for ways to improve myself and my circumstances.

2. My family

I come from a close-knit family that has always been there for me. My parents have always been my biggest supporters and have always encouraged me to pursue my dreams. They have instilled in me a strong work ethic and a sense of responsibility. My siblings are also a big part of my life, and we have always been there for each other through thick and thin.

3. My childhood

3.1. achievements

During my childhood, I had many achievements that I am proud of. One of my biggest achievements was winning a math competition in the third grade. I was also a part of the school debate team, and we placed first in the district competition. I also received a gold medal in the science fair during my fifth-grade year.

3.2. failures/frustrations

Despite my successes, I also had my fair share of failures and frustrations. One of my biggest failures was not being able to make it to the finals of the spelling bee competition in fourth grade. I was also not able to pass the entrance exam for the gifted program in my school. I struggled with feeling like an outsider in my school and often felt like I didn't belong.

4. My school life

4.1. achievements

In high school, I was able to achieve many things that I am proud of. I was a member of the school's varsity debate team and we placed first in the regional competition. I also received a scholarship for academic excellence. I also became a member of the school's honor society.

4.2. failures

Despite my many achievements, I also had my fair share of failures in high school. I struggled in my first semester of my senior year and received a failing grade in one of my classes. I also had to retake a class the following summer, which was a difficult experience.

4.3. significant decisions

One of the most significant decisions I made during my school life was to attend a university far from home. This decision allowed me to gain independence and experience a new environment. It also allowed me to meet new people and make new friends.

4.4. significant persons

One of the most significant persons in my school life was my debate coach. He was a constant source of support and encouragement, and he helped me to develop my critical thinking and speaking skills. He also taught me the importance of teamwork and the value of perseverance.

5. My plan for the future

In the future, I plan to pursue a career in a field that I am passionate about. I also plan to continue to grow as a person and to make a positive impact on the world. I want to become a leader in my field and to be an inspiration to others. I also plan to continue to be involved in community service and to give back to those in need.

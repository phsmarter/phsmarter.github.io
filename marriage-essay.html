Firstly, due to the heavy workload arising from new responsibilities and duties, such as earning the living, taking care of children, organizing time properly, running the household, the newly married youngsters mature much earlier than their peers. The idea is that giving birth at an early age is not likely to be followed by any health problems.

Argumentative Essay:Cross-cultural marriages add up to racial tolerance.

Cross-cultural marriages have become more prevalent in recent years, and they have been the subject of much debate. Some people argue that cross-cultural marriages lead to cultural erosion, while others believe that they promote racial tolerance. In my opinion, cross-cultural marriages contribute to racial tolerance and understanding in society. In this essay, I will discuss how cross-cultural marriages can promote racial tolerance and help to break down barriers between different cultures.

Firstly, cross-cultural marriages help to promote racial tolerance by exposing people to different cultures and traditions. When two people from different cultures come together, they are forced to learn about each other's customs, beliefs, and values. This can lead to a better understanding of different cultures and help to break down stereotypes and misconceptions. For example, a person from a Western culture who marries someone from an Asian culture will be exposed to a different way of life, different foods, and different customs. This exposure can help to break down barriers and promote understanding between the two cultures.

Secondly, cross-cultural marriages can help to create a more diverse and tolerant society. When people from different cultures come together, they bring with them different experiences, perspectives, and ideas. This diversity can lead to a more tolerant and accepting society, as people learn to appreciate and respect the differences between them. For example, a child born to parents from different cultures may grow up with a more open-minded and accepting view of the world, as they are exposed to different cultures and traditions from a young age.

Finally, cross-cultural marriages can help to reduce racial tensions and promote peace between different cultures. When people from different cultures come together and form relationships, they are less likely to view each other as "other" or "foreign". Instead, they see each other as individuals with their own unique qualities and characteristics. This can help to break down the barriers that often exist between different cultures and promote peace and understanding.

In conclusion, cross-cultural marriages can play an important role in promoting racial tolerance and understanding in society. By exposing people to different cultures, creating a more diverse society, and reducing racial tensions, cross-cultural marriages can help to break down barriers and promote a more peaceful and accepting world.

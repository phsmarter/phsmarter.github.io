It would be a partnership built on trust, love, and mutual understanding, where we are able to grow and evolve together.

When I compare my family now to my ideal marriage, I see areas where we can improve. While my family is supportive and loving, there are times when communication could be better, and we could work on being more understanding of each other's perspectives. In my ideal marriage, I would like to have a deeper level of communication and understanding with my partner, where we are able to express ourselves freely and without judgment.

To have my ideal marriage, I need to work on myself first. This means being self-aware, understanding my own needs and desires, and being open to growth and change. I also need to be willing to put in the effort to build a strong and healthy relationship with my partner, which includes being patient, understanding, and supportive of each other's growth.

The people around me can help me achieve my ideal marriage by being supportive and understanding. This includes my family, friends, and loved ones who can offer advice, guidance, and a listening ear when I need it. I also value having a community of like-minded individuals who share my values and beliefs, and who can help me grow and evolve as a person.

For me, people being part of my success is very important. I believe that we are all interconnected, and that we all have something to offer each other. Whether it's through emotional support, guidance, or simply being a sounding board, having people around me who support and believe in me is essential to my success. I also believe that success is not just about achieving goals, but about having a positive impact on the world around us, and I value having people around me who share this perspective.

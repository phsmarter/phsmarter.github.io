As a user of ICT think of a situation or an event, where you observed both the advantages and disadvantages of using any or all the components of ICT. You may be guided by The What, So What, Now, What Framework (Rolte, et al, 2001) by answering the guide questions below WHAT? 1. What problem, issue, or situation did you experience? 2. What happened? 3. What did you notice? 4 What was everyone's reaction to the event or situation? 5. What positive and negative aspects did you observe? SO, WHAT? 1. How did this event affect you? How does it affect our future? 2. Why is it important? What critical questions does this information caused you to ask? 3. What emotions did the event evoke? How did it make you feel? 4. What conclusions can you draw from this experience? 1 What do we need to do to move forward? How can we turn this event into something positive? 2. What have you learned? How can we use them for our benefit?​


Answer:

In my experience as a student and consumer, I have observed both the advantages and disadvantages of using ICT technologies, online systems functions, and platforms.

WHAT?

The situation that I experienced was during an online examination for one of my courses. I was using my laptop to take the exam, and midway through, my internet connection suddenly got disconnected.

WHAT HAPPENED?

As a result, my answers were not saved, and I had to retake the exam. It caused me to panic, and I felt frustrated because I had to start over.

WHAT DID YOU NOTICE?

My experience was not unique, and I noticed that some of my classmates had also encountered similar issues with their internet connection during the exam.

WHAT WAS EVERYONE'S REACTION TO THE EVENT OR SITUATION?

Some of my classmates remained calm and composed while others became agitated and complained to our professor.

WHAT POSITIVE AND NEGATIVE ASPECTS DID YOU OBSERVE?

The positive aspect of the situation was that the online exam was convenient, and it allowed me to take it at the comfort of my own home. However, the negative aspect was that the reliability of the internet connection could not be guaranteed, which caused me to stress about the possibility of encountering such issues again in the future.

SO, WHAT?

HOW DID THIS EVENT AFFECT YOU? HOW DOES IT AFFECT OUR FUTURE?

This event made me realize that while ICT technologies, online systems functions, and platforms have made our lives more convenient, they are still not foolproof. It is essential to have backup plans and contingencies in case of technical issues. In the future, the reliability of the internet connection needs to be improved so that students and consumers alike can use these systems without the fear of experiencing technical issues.

WHY IS IT IMPORTANT? WHAT CRITICAL QUESTIONS DOES THIS INFORMATION CAUSED YOU TO ASK?

This information caused me to question the reliability and security of online systems. As a consumer, it is essential to be vigilant about cybersecurity and data privacy, especially when using online platforms to store personal information or conduct financial transactions.

WHAT EMOTIONS DID THE EVENT EVOKE? HOW DID IT MAKE YOU FEEL?

The event evoked feelings of frustration and anxiety. It made me feel nervous about using online systems that rely heavily on internet connectivity.

WHAT CONCLUSIONS CAN YOU DRAW FROM THIS EXPERIENCE?

One conclusion that I drew from this experience is that as a user of ICT technologies, I need to have backup plans in case of technical issues. Another conclusion is that the reliability and security of online systems need to be improved to ensure that users can use these platforms without fear of technical issues or cybersecurity threats. It also highlights the importance of being vigilant about cybersecurity and data privacy as a consumer.

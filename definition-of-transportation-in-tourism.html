3. 1 . definition of tourism ? 2. effect of tourism ?


Answer:

1. Tourism is defined as the act of traveling for leisure, recreation, or business purposes. It involves the movement of people to destinations outside of their usual place of work or residence, and can include activities such as sightseeing, visiting friends and relatives, and participating in cultural, sporting, or other events.

2. Tourism can have both positive and negative effects on a destination. On the positive side, tourism can bring economic benefits to a region, such as increased employment and revenue from the sale of goods and services to travelers. It can also contribute to the preservation and conservation of natural and cultural heritage sites.

Explanation:

On the negative side, tourism can also have negative impacts on a destination. Overcrowding and overdevelopment can lead to environmental degradation and loss of natural habitats. In addition, the influx of tourists can lead to increases in the cost of living for local residents, and can also contribute to the erosion of traditional cultures and values.

Overall, the effects of tourism on a destination will depend on a variety of factors, including the level of tourism, the management of tourism development, and the sensitivity of the destination to the impacts of tourism.

6. What are the meaning of the following: Physical Density Refractive Index Index of Refraction Angle of Refraction Electro Magnetic Spectrum


Physical Density- A measure of a Quantity of some physical property (usually mass) per unit length, area or volume (usually volume).


Refractive Index- The ratio of the velocity of light in a vacuum to its velocity in a specified medium.



Index of Refraction- Is a dimensionless number that describes how light propagates through that medium.



Angle of Refraction- The angle made by a refracted ray with a perpendicular to the refracting surface.



Electro Magnetic Spectrum- The range of wavelengths or frequencies over which electromagnetic radiation extends.

14. how does a refractive index of a material affect the refraction of light?​


Answer:

When light enters a material with higher refractive index, the angle of refraction will be smaller than the angle of incidence and the light will be refracted towards the normal of the surface. The higher the refractive index, the closer to the normal direction the light will travel.

19. formation of small business relation establish of good relationship with the employees to protect the interest of everyone.An organization with good industrial relations may have effective communication lines which is very important in any organization.​


Answer:

﻿Yes,, the formation of good relationships with employees is crucial for the success of small businesses, especially in terms of creating a positive and productive work environment. Good industrial relations can help to foster trust, cooperation, and mutual respect between employers and employees, which can ultimately lead to improved productivity, job satisfaction, and profitability.

Effective communication is a key component of good industrial relations. When communication lines are open and transparent, employees are more likely to feel valued and respected, which can lead to increased motivation and engagement. Employers who actively listen to their employees and take their feedback into consideration are more likely to create a culture of collaboration and trust.

In addition to promoting a positive work environment, good industrial relations can also help to protect the interests of everyone involved. Employers who prioritize the well-being of their employees are more likely to create fair and equitable policies and practices, which can help to prevent conflicts and disputes. This can also help to minimize the risk of legal action and other negative consequences that can arise from poor industrial relations.

Overall, establishing good industrial relations is essential for the success of small businesses. By prioritizing communication, collaboration, and mutual respect, employers can create a positive work environment that fosters productivity, job satisfaction, and profitability while also protecting the interests of everyone involved

24. Take a look at the paragraph below. Try to determine if it follows the principle of unity in writing a paragraph. If not, identify which part of the paragraph can be divided. High emotional intelligence can help a manager improve workplace communication skills, employee motivation and organizational effectiveness. If a manager has high empathy, a key component of emotional intelligence, he or she will be able to listen to the concerns of employees and will be more understanding of their needs, wants and concerns. This will most likely translate into increased motivation and satisfaction of employees and ultimately, will have a positive effect on the effectiveness of the business. It is important to note, however, that emotional intelligence is a concept that is not universally understood to have the same meaning and is not universally believed to be a key determiner of business success. Highly authoritarian, top down approaches are employed in many successful businesses today.


This paragraph does not follow the principle of unity, as it contains multiple distinct thoughts or ideas that are not clearly related to one another.

In particular, the second and fourth sentences introduce new concepts that are not directly connected to the main idea of the paragraph, which is that high emotional intelligence can improve workplace communication, employee motivation and organizational effectiveness.

The second sentence "If a manager has high empathy, a key component of emotional intelligence, he or she will be able to listen to the concerns of employees and will be more understanding of their needs, wants and concerns. " provide an example of how emotional intelligence can be beneficial.

The fourth sentence "It is important to note, however, that emotional intelligence is a concept that is not universally understood to have the same meaning and is not universally believed to be a key determiner of business success. " is discussing a point regarding the definition and importance of emotional intelligence which is not related to the main topic.

The fifth sentence "Highly authoritarian, top down approaches are employed in many successful businesses today." brings another idea in the paragraph which is not related to the main topic of the paragraph discussing emotional intelligence and its impact on business.

Overall, the paragraph does not maintain a clear focus on a single main idea, but rather jumps around between several different ideas. In order to maintain unity, it would be best to separate these different thoughts into different paragraphs and to focus on one main idea at a time.

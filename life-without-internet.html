21. Imagine life without technologies, electricity, mode of transportation, gadgets and internet. How do you feel about it? ​


Answer:

Without these advancements, life would likely be much different and potentially more difficult. Many daily tasks and activities that we currently take for granted would become more time-consuming and challenging. For example, communication with others might be limited to face-to-face interaction, transportation would likely be slower, and access to information would be greatly reduced.

On the other hand, life might also be more simple and less stressful, with people having more time to focus on relationships, nature, and other aspects of life that technology has taken away.

Overall, it is difficult to say how exactly one might feel about such a life, but it would certainly be a big change from the world we know today.

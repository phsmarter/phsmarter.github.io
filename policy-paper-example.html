EXPLANATION:

CLAIM OF FACT:

Claims of fact are something that really happened and statements that are undeniably true. Sentences that contain factual claims are sentences that are written based on facts, events, situations that really happened and are objective. In the fact sentence the data contained is clear about an event. Not only accurate but also precise. In the sentence the data can be in the form of statistical numbers, date and time of occurrence.

CLAIM OF POLICY:

In general, a claim of policy is something that is used to show a person's behavior. In this case, the person in question could be an official, an institution, or even a certain group. The goal is to solve a problem that is being faced by them. Policy is a guideline and restrictions that are carried out in general. Limits that become the direction of an action that must be done. As well as a rule that must be followed by the perpetrators.

CLAIM OF VALUE:

Claim of value can be interpreted as a price, award, or estimate. This means that the price or award attached to an object. The object referred to in this case can be in the form of objects, goods, circumstances, actions, behaviors, or other events. In other words, a claim of value can be interpreted as a form of appreciation and a situation that is beneficial to humans as a determinant and reference in assessing and carrying out an action. By referring to a value, a person can determine how he should act and behave properly so that it does not deviate from the definition of prevailing social norms.

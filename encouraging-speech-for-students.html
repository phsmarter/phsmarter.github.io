9. public speaking encourage the senior high school students to become a criminology student ​


Sure, here's a speech encouraging senior high school students to consider becoming a criminology student:

Good morning/afternoon everyone,

Today, I want to talk to you about a field of study that is essential to maintaining a safe and just society - criminology. Criminology is the scientific study of crime, criminals, and the criminal justice system, and it plays a crucial role in ensuring that our communities are safe and secure.

As senior high school students, you are at a crucial point in your lives where you are starting to think about your future careers. I want to encourage you to consider criminology as a field of study, and a potential career path.

Firstly, studying criminology can be intellectually stimulating and challenging. It involves examining and analyzing complex issues related to crime, law, and social justice. You will learn about the psychology of criminal behavior, the workings of the justice system, and the impact of crime on society.

Secondly, criminology is a field that provides a wide range of career opportunities. You can work in law enforcement, the courts, corrections, or in various government agencies. You can also pursue careers in research, policy analysis, or advocacy work.

Moreover, criminology is a field that is constantly evolving, and new developments and challenges arise all the time. This means that there will always be opportunities for growth and learning throughout your career.

Finally, as a criminology student and potential future professional, you will have the opportunity to make a real difference in people's lives. You will be contributing to the important work of creating a safer and more just society for everyone.

In conclusion, criminology is an exciting and rewarding field of study that offers a wide range of opportunities for personal and professional growth. I encourage you to consider becoming a criminology student and to explore the many opportunities that this field has to offer. Thank you for listening.

22. encourage senior high school students to become criminology student​


If you are looking to encourage senior high school students to pursue a career in criminology, here are a few points you could use:

1. Diverse Career Opportunities: A degree in criminology can lead to diverse career opportunities, including roles in law enforcement, corrections, investigations, forensic science, and private security. This means that students who pursue criminology have a range of career paths to choose from.

2. Importance of the Field: Criminology plays a critical role in maintaining public safety, preventing crime, and ensuring justice for victims. Students who pursue a degree in criminology can contribute to society by working to make their communities safer and more just.

3. Real-world Experience: Many criminology programs offer students opportunities to gain real-world experience through internships, research projects, and other hands-on learning opportunities. This can provide students with valuable skills and insights that they can apply in their future careers.

4. Intellectual Challenge: Criminology is a multidisciplinary field that draws on insights from psychology, sociology, law, and other disciplines. This means that students who pursue criminology will be challenged to think critically, analyze complex data, and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems.

5. Job Security: Criminology is a growing field, with increasing demand for professionals who can help maintain public safety and prevent crime. This means that students who pursue criminology can expect to have good job prospects and long-term job security.

Overall, if you want to encourage senior high school students to pursue a degree in criminology, highlighting the diverse career opportunities, importance of the field, real-world experience, intellectual challenge, and job security can be effective ways to do so.

24. Give me a speech of encouraging the youth to be a "makers not breakers of communication"​


A SPEECH OF ENCOURAGING THE YOUTH TO BE "MAKERS OF COMMUNICATION":

Good afternoon, Ladies and Gentlemen.

With all my respect, to the committees that give me a chance to deliver my speech. And all my beloved friends. First, let's us give thanks to God who has given grace and health for all of us so that we can meet in this lovely room. My speech is discussed about “YOUTH AS THE MAKERS OF COMMUNICATION”.

Whether it's at home, on campus, or in the world of work, most of the youth may often not dare to express an opinion or the maker of communication. Classic reasons such as fear of saying the wrong thing may be something that crosses the minds of youths. In fact, there is nothing wrong or right in expressing an opinion, as a result you just follow other people's opinions or just follow the flow of the conversation.

There are several factors why someone is difficult to speak up. One of them may be because there are too many ideas and feel nervous that it is difficult to express them. But, there are times when you have to be brave to show your aspirations, because a small input or suggestion can be a big change, you know.

Some of the reasons you should be makers of communication are the first so that you are not easily underestimated. You are a good worker, diligent, and have good achievements, but, often you feel belittled not because of your skills, but because you are shy or don't dare to have an opinion. Usually, you will tend to be taken advantage of by them, and assumptions such as 'he just does the work, he always wants to, really'. If you wait for people to be concerned about you, or feel compassion, you may be mentally disturbed. So, collect the belief that you have in yourself and communicate with those around you what you feel is weighing on you at that time.

Second, reduce misunderstandings. The main cause of misunderstandings, usually occurs when there is a failure in communication and at any time. Lack of communication, can make other people think badly of us. Therefore, it is important to have the courage to speak up, because this effort can help your friends or people around you to understand your intentions and vice versa. Not a bad thing, right?

Third, you should know that silence doesn't change anything. Although there is a saying that says silence is gold, but you continue to be a quiet person, maybe people around you will be shy with you. For example, you are discussing with a friend, and you do not agree but prefer to remain silent. Of course nothing will change. It's better to be open with your friends, or relatives who dare to express their opinions. Rest assured, there is no right or wrong because everyone has a different mindset. So, don't be afraid of it, okay?

That's my speech today. So that youth can understand their role as agents of change, especially as makers or communication.

That's all, thank you very much.

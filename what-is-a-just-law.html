22. 6. Suppose you just had a boat ride in Bumham Lake and you suddenly jumped from the boat onto a dock. Describe what happens from the perspective of the Newton's 3rd Law 2 pts​


Answer:

As you jump from the boat, you exert a force on the dock. In response, the dock exerts an equal and opposite force on you. This causes you to stop moving forward and start moving backward.

Explanation:

According to Newton's 3rd Law, for every action there is an equal and opposite reaction. So, when you jumped from the boat onto the dock, the dock would have been pushed away from you with an equal force. If the dock was not securely anchored, it would have moved away from you.

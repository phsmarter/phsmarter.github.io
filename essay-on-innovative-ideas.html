21. Inclusive innovative interconnected English essay.


Answer:

We live in a society that's rapidly changing and morphing far from our parents and grandparents can ever imagine. Yet in some way, the innate goal for a progressive, modern and harmonious remains the same. We all want to live in a society that accepts who we are as long as we pursue noble aspirations, a society that celebrates science and technology as pinnacle of human achievement and a society that embraces cooperation and a bit of healthy competition among its constituents to move forward.

Inclusivity, Innovation and Interconnectedness. All three words start with the letter 'I' and may very well begin with the idea of 'I' as in 'I, a human being'. But its end-result definitely leads to 'we, as a people'.

But why should these three words form the foundation of a society we want to live in? Let's break each down:

Inclusive  

When you say a society, a corporation, a school or a barangay is inclusive, it means it accepts all people regardless of physical attributes, gender, sexual identity, age and race. It treats everyone the same, and in some ocassions, it offers opportunities for those who were not physically endowed such as an amputee or a blind person, to perform like a normal person. It doesn't discriminate nor judged based on superficiality.

Innovative

Human potential is virtually limitless. As the famous saying goes, the human capacity to develop and create new technologies entirely depends on his will to do so. In an innovative world, curiosity, thirst for knowledge and creativity are strongly encouraged. Technologies for a better life are continuously developed, not to control nature but to create a way of life that co-exists with nature. This also means sustainability which can benefit the planet and the future generations.

Interconnected

It's difficult to be 'disconnected' these days. Even our smartphones demand us to be connected at all times. But interconnected is more than that. It simply means acknowledging the notion that we are part of a whole and we must do our part to make the whole thing work. We are all striving for the same goal and we must work together to achieve it.

With these traits combined and channeled for a positive development, we are really building a world where our future children can be proud of.

Perhaps this is why I am not discouraged by life. I am grateful that He gifted me with parents who reach their hands incessantly when I am in need and with others, particularly friends, who made me feel blessed with all of life's grandeur.Additionally, I am blessed with lovely siblings who inspire me on my life-long adventure. What more could I possibly ask? Individuals can put their trust in me. Though I occasionally unintentionally reveal a secret, I can still confidently state that I am trustworthy.

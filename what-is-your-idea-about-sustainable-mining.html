27. can you list down you idea on how mining can be sustainable in the Philippines​


Answer:

How can mining be sustainable?

Mining activities must reduce the impact on the environment around mine sites to become more environmentally friendly. For a sustainable future, reducing inputs such as water , electricity and land disruption, and reducing outputs such as pollution, acid rock drainage and reclamation are crucial.

Is mining legal in the Philippines?

Republic Act No. 7942, better known as the Philippine Mining Act of 1995 (the Mining Act) and its implementing rules and regulations, DENR Administrative Order No. 2010-21 (Mining Act IRR), are the major laws that govern the mining industry, both of which have not been revised in the past year.

Can mining be environmentally friendly?

Mining in the Green. In recent years , new mining technologies and legislation have increased mining productivity dramatically and have reduced the environmental effects. Generally, as productivity is improved so less waste is generated, mining techniques become much more environmentally responsive.

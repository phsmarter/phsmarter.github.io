1. what are the advantages and disadvantages of distance learning??​


Answer:

Advantages of distance learning

1. You can pursue a job along with studies: Distance education comes as a blessing for such students. You can study on the weekends, when you're back from work or even in the middle of the night. You get to learn while you earn!

2. You can save money: For any given program, the fee of a distance education degree (online or otherwise) may be much more affordable than the fee of a regular on-campus degree.

3. You can save your time: There's no time wasted in going to and from college, no time wasted waiting for a bus or train.

4. You can learn at your own pace: if you are self-disciplined and self-motivated, the best benefit of distance education is that you can learn at your own pace.

5. You can study whenever, wherever: You need not be stuck in a classroom, but can go study in your garden, on your living room couch or in the comfort of your own bed. Irrespective of whether you're a morning lark or a night owl, you can choose an optimum time to study whenever you're at your productive best.

Disadvantages of distance learning

1. Chances of distraction high: You need to keep yourself motivated and focused if you want to successfully complete your distance learning course.

2. Hidden costs: Although the cost of a distance education program is usually cheaper than a regular program, there can be hidden costs involved. For example, if your distance learning course is offered online, you might have to incur some initial expenses like installing a computer and getting a reliable Internet connection.

3. Complicated technology: Overdependence on technology can be a major drawback in distance learning mode of education, especially when the learning takes place in an online environment.

4. Quality of faculty compromised: Sometimes the technology might not do full justice to the delivery and design of the course.

5. Questionable credibility of de6grees: With the increase in the number of distance/online programs, the number of scam operators are also rising. This affects the credibility of recognised distance learning degrees among prospective employers.

6. Lose out on networking: A distance learning program loses out on this very important aspect.

Advantages of Distance Learning

1.      More resources in online world

The main advantage of distance learning is mainly “the resources.” They can instantly search and browse the internet to find the result or answer they were researching. Tutorials on video-sharing sites make it easier to learn. Online math solvers are there to give trustworthy information about a particular topic.

2.     Flexibility

Aside from that, one of its main advantages is flexibility. One can independently answer and review their learning lectures at any time and place while still having time for family activities and chores.

3.     Transportation

There is no transportation fare since one can study at home, unless for the every 2-week retrieval and deployment of learning materials.

Disadvantages of Distance Learning

1.       Effectivity

Even though there were many advances in distance learning, there are also disadvantages present in the modality itself. The first one is effectivity. Other than teachers would have more time in elaborating it in face-to-face modality, comprehension would be lesser to students than what should is expected.

2.      Time Management

Distractions from outside noise, sudden chores, and the like are one of the reasons why one fails to manage allotted time.

3.       Reliability

On the side of the teachers, they lack evidence to prove that a learner’s answers are plagiarized or copied from online sources. Online sources are not always accurate, and some misled the students from learning better. Internet sources may also cause confusion on the side of readers or learners by giving complex and unexplained solutions to their homework.

4.     Communication

When it comes to communication, the main issue connection problems, which makes it harder to communicate with teachers to students and vice versa.

5. Financially burden

Buying gadgets is costly. Distance learning is a “financial burden” because of expensive data charges. In these trying times, one needs to manage money well to have enough money, not for school fares but the load for online classes.

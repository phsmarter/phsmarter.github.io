The mother felt very sad to think of killing it, and so she hid it in the big box (kaban) where they kept their clothes. As soon as the Sun returned, he asked the Moon, "How about our baby?" At once the Moon replied, "It was a girl: I killed it yesterday." The Sun had only a week to stay at home with the Moon. One night he dreamed that a boy with white hair came to him from heaven. The boy stood close to him, and spoke these words:- "Your wife got a baby, but it was a girl; and she hid it away from you in the box."

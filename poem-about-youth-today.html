Of your grace be seen,

Fair hope of my fatherland!

With thy mighty hand,

Swifter than the wind's violation,

Raise the eager mind to a higher station.

Come down with pleasing light

Of art and science to the fight,

O youth, and there untie

The chains that heavy lie,

Your spirit is free to blight.

See how inflaming zone

Amid the shadows thrown,

The Spaniard's holy hand

A crown's resplendent band

Proffers to this Indian land.

On wings of rich emprise,

Seeking from Olympian skies

Songs of sweetest strain,

Softer than ambrosial rain;

Thou, whose voice divine

Rivals Philomel's refrain

And with varied line

Through the night benign

Frees mortality from pain;

Thou, who by sharp strife

Wakest thy mind to life ;

And the memory bright

Of thy genius' light

Of Phoebus, to Apelles dear ;

To spread around the fame,

And in victory acclaim,

Through wider spheres the human name.

Day, O happy day,

Fair Filipinas, for thy land!

So bless the Power to-day

That places in thy way

This favor and this fortune grand!

19. essay about the voices of youth today​


Answer:

The voice of youth is a powerful force that can drive change in society. Young people have a unique perspective on the world and often bring fresh ideas and innovative solutions to the challenges we face. Their energy, passion and creativity can inspire and motivate others to take action, and their voices can be a powerful tool for advocacy and social change.

One of the most significant ways in which young people use their voices is through activism. From the civil rights movement of the 1960s to the climate strikes of today, young people have played a pivotal role in driving social and political change. They have organized protests, created social media campaigns, and used their social networks to raise awareness and mobilize others.

In recent years, the voice of youth has been particularly prominent in the fight against climate change. Young people around the world have taken to the streets to demand action from their governments and corporations, and have used social media to amplify their message and build a global movement. Their efforts have helped to raise public awareness about the urgency of the climate crisis and have put pressure on leaders to take bold action to address it.

The voice of youth is also important in shaping public policy. While young people may not have the right to vote, they still have the power to influence decision-makers through advocacy and public engagement. By sharing their perspectives and experiences, they can help to shape policies that reflect their needs and priorities, and that are more responsive to the challenges facing young people today.

In addition to activism and policy advocacy, the voice of youth is also important in promoting diversity, equity and inclusion. Young people are often at the forefront of efforts to challenge stereotypes, combat discrimination and promote social justice. They bring new perspectives to conversations about race, gender, sexuality and other forms of identity, and can help to create more inclusive and welcoming communities.

In conclusion, the voice of youth is a powerful force that can drive change in society. Young people have unique perspectives on the world and can bring fresh ideas and innovative solutions to the challenges we face. Through activism, policy advocacy and promoting diversity, equity and inclusion, young people can use their voices to shape a better world for themselves and future generations.

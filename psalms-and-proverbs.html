21. Which wisdom book teaches living in the real world without compromising faith in God? A. EcclesiasticusB. PsalmsC. ProverbsD. Wisdom​


ANSWER

If by the given choices, the answer is C., Proverbs. The literature of Wisdom in the Bible is not only in Proverbs but in three books - The book of Job, Proverbs, and Ecclesiastes. King Solomon wrote both the Ecclesiastes and Proverbs but the Author of the Book of Job remains unknown. Jewish Rabbis believe that it was written by Moses.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

A. Ecclesiasticus - or Sirach is a Jewish book about ethics that is considered cannon by the Catholics. However, the Jews excluded it from their Tanakh and considered in as Apocryphal (books with doubtful origins).

B. Psalms - is a collection of poems, hymns, and prayers written by King David, father of King Solomon.

D. Wisdom​ - is having experience and knowledge to make good judgments/choices.

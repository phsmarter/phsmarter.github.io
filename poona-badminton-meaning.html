1. badminton originated from a sport poona what country did poona originated in?​


Poona is believed to have originated in India. It was a colonial British military game that was played in Pune, India (formerly known as Poona). The exact origin of the game is unknown, but some believe it originated as a children's game. The sport is thought to have been adapted from the ancient game ti-jian-zi, which is still popular in China today.

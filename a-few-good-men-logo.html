Answer:

1. Brand Recognition

2. Professionalism

3. Show Your Personality

4.Build Loyalty

Explanation:

As your business expands, your logo is going to become more widely recognizable. More people are going to know who you are and what you’re about. This is going to create the perceptions that you’re accessible and trustworthy as a business.

Think of brands you know. Take Nike for example, when you’re looking for sportswear and you see that tick, you’re more likely to choose that item. That is because you know the logo and associate it with a brand you can trust.

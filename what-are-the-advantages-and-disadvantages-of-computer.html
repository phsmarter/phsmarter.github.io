4. what are the advantages and disadvantages of using computer


What are the advantages and disadvantages of using computer?  

Computer is an electronic device that creates documents and performs logical operations and thousands of mathematical operations at a time. It is because, computer is a machine that understood commands and codes given by programmers and operators. Using this device, it gives benefits to businesses and people who are using this.

Such benefits include :   People can produce digital documents which they could send to another person or location as long as they have internet connection and computer. In this case, people can keep their documents without losing them. People can work and produce output faster. Through the use of computer systems and technologies, some of the manual processes perform by human can be done by computers. These processes can help humans to become more productive and achieve their goals. People can do their work with minimal errors. Systems are design to perform processes automatically with the minimal errors possible. Businesses can operate widely and it cause them to earn large profit. You can do online shopping with the use of computers and it helps businesses who operates the website to earn more since the scope of the internet is unlimited.

However, computers can have disadvantages too, like: Computers can cause cyberbullying to some people. Cyberbullying is a form of bullying which could happen to young people in internet and social media, wherein bullies would post something mean and bad about them and encourage more people to hate the victims. This could cause emotional damage to the victims. Computers can also cause addiction to some people, especially when they are playing too much games or talking to somebody too often via Internet and social media chats. Apparently, people who do this has the sense of belonging and can withdraw themselves from their community because they have their own world in their computer by means of playing games and talking to some people with the same interest. Computers and systems can make people go lazy. Since most of the processes are automated and faster, people will rely on the technology often than do things on their own.

Computer ergonomics addresses ways to optimise your computer workstation to reduce the specific risks of computer vision syndrome, neck and back pain, and carpal tunnel syndrome. It also reduces the risk of other disorders affecting the muscles, spine, and joints.

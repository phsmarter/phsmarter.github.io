1. Problem and solution text type short examples


Short example of problem and solution theme:

The greatest blight on the social life of our society is illiteracy. Literacy rates in India are estimated to be around sixty percent of the population. They live their entire lives in the shadows of their ignorance. Without an increase in the literacy rate, no initiative to improve social and economic conditions can be successful.

About Problem And Solution theme

Problem and Solution is an organizational pattern in which information in a passage is expressed as a difficulty or troubling issue (a problem) and something that could be or should be done to resolve this issue (solution or attempted solution). The problem and solution structure may appear easy to recognize. Still, it can be reasonably difficult to recognize because it is commonly confused with the cause-and-effect pattern of an organization, as they both have interpersonal structures. However, read the passage and look specifically for a challenge and a solution to the issue. You should be able to distinguish it from cause and effect, as cause and effect passages do not propose a solution.

2. Problem and solution text type short examples


The following is an example of a discussion text, which is a text containing problems and solutions along with their structure.

Tittle

Garbage is Issue No. 1

 

 Introduction

Garbage is one of the most troubling problems because it has many negative impacts on the environment. Garbage that is allowed to accumulate can be a source of disease and even cause flooding. This is also inseparable from the problem of improper waste management. Actually, solving the problem of waste has been a debate for a long time.

 

Contents (Arrangement of Arguments)

Some people think that garbage is something that is no longer needed.

Therefore, waste should be destroyed by burning. But for those who love nature, this method is not a solution but instead brings new disasters. Burning garbage will produce air pollution and can damage the ozone layer.

People who love the environment, of course, prefer solutions that are more environmentally friendly, such as decomposition and recycling of waste. An example is by making dry leaves, tree trunks, and similar waste into compost, while inorganic waste is recycled.

 Conclusion

Basically, waste can be processed and reused. Environmentally friendly waste processing and handling is the most appropriate and effective way to solve existing waste problems.

3. example of short paragraph problem & solution text type​


Answer:

Problem and Solution is a pattern of organization where information in a passage is expressed as a dilemma or concerning issue (a problem) and something that was, can be, or should be done to remedy this issue (solution or attempted solution). The problem and solution text structure may seem like it would be easy to recognize, but it can be moderately difficult to identify because it is frequently confused with the cause and effect pattern of organization, as they both have relational structures; however, if you read the passage and look specifically for both a problem and a solution to the problem, you should find it pretty easy to distinguish from cause and effect, as cause and effect passages do not propose solutions to any negative occurrences within the passage but rather just explain why or how they happen.

Example: It seems like there has been a surge in teen pregnancies these days. Teen pregnancies make it very difficult for young mothers to pursue their dreams and meet the demands of an infant. Fortunately, most teen pregnancies can be easily prevented by using birth control; however, even birth control is not 100% effective. The most effective way to prevent teen pregnancies is abstinence, which is 100% effective.

Examples of problem and solution text, descriptive text, and procedural text.

There are many types of texts. Generally, they are distinguished by their contents. For example, a procedural text will explain how to do something or steps to achieve something. Meanwhile, a descriptive text, as how it is named, deals with describing a particular theme or topic.

Likewise, a problem and solution text will contain both a problem and its solution that readers can use to solve certain problems. Here are few examples of such texts:

Problem and solution text

In case you have difficulty in sleeping or what people generally call "insomnia", read on as I have a few tips that might help you. First, you might want to exercise during the daytime to make sure that your body is already tired in the evening. Second, don't overthink anything. Third, try to read some books as you approach your sleeping time. That concludes a few methods that you can use.

Descriptive text

Everyone seems to be confused about the definition of dehydration. While it is true that it is a condition when people are deprived of water, actually dehydration means that one person's body is losing the amount of fluids compared to the amount of fluids which are consumed. Try to count the amount of fluids and you will be fine.

Procedural text

In this text, I will demonstrate how to cook a boiled egg. Here are the steps required:

First, you must provide the raw eggs.Take a pan and fill it with water, roughly half full of the entire capacity.Turn on your stove as per the stove's instructions.Place the pan on the stove and place the eggs into the pan.Wait until the water boils, but let the eggs stay there.Set a timer and wait for 15 minutes.Turn off the stove and let the pan and eggs cool down.

In the present day, we still struggle with the issues of power imbalance, inequality, and abuse of authority—all of which were catalysts for the Mutiny. But while the events may have happened in a different time, the effects are still the same.

Ultimately, we strive to fight the power disparity that was once seen during the mutiny by standing up for one another and embracing our collective rights and voice.

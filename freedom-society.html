18. in the development of society, which should come firts, freedom or constrain?​


Answer:

In the development of society, we should focus on freedom.

First of all, freedom is what makes us human. We have the freedom to think, to speak our minds and make decisions for ourselves. When we are free to act and make decisions for ourselves, then we can grow and develop as people.

Second, when there is no freedom in our lives, then there is not much chance to develop as a person. In order for someone to be able to develop into a better person, they need to be able to make their own choices without being held back by other people or situations.

Explanation:

The development of society is a very complicated process. It's not just about freedom or constraint.

Freedom is a great thing, but it can also be bad. If everyone is free to do whatever they want, then you have no limits on what people can do. And if everyone has the same amount of freedom, then there's not much room for creativity or innovation because everything has been done before.

Constraint is great too—but only if it's used properly. When someone is constrained by that person or situation, then they have less freedom than they would otherwise have had. But when people are constrained by other people who are still free to do whatever they want, then we get the kind of democracy we need for peace and prosperity in our society!

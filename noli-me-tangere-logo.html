30. Give Me 10 Examples Of Ethos,Pathos,Logos.​


Answer:

Ethos: A doctor giving a presentation on the benefits of a new medicine. The doctor's professional expertise and reputation lend credibility to their argument.

Pathos: A charity advertisement showing images of starving children to appeal to the audience's emotions and persuade them to donate.

Logos: An economist presenting statistical data to support their argument that a certain economic policy will be beneficial for the country.

Ethos: A celebrity endorsement for a product, using the celebrity's fame and reputation to appeal to the audience's trust.

Pathos: A political speech that emphasizes the personal stories and struggles of ordinary people, in order to appeal to the audience's emotions and sense of empathy.

Logos: A scientist presenting a well-researched and detailed argument for the benefits of a new technology.

Ethos: A lawyer citing legal precedent and expertise in their field to support their case in court.

Pathos: An animal rights group using images of animals being mistreated to appeal to the audience's sense of compassion and urge them to take action.

Logos: An engineer presenting a logical, step-by-step argument for the feasibility of a new bridge design.

Ethos: An expert witness in a trial, using their knowledge and credibility in a specific field to support their testimony.

7. 7.what is not a purpose of an organizational structureA. To coordinate people and resourcesB. To organize lines of communicationC. To formalize authorityD. To limit workers rightsOrgMan pa help po​


Based on the choices given, to limit workers' rights is not the purpose of the organizational structure.

Details of explanation

Organizational structure is a system or structure designed to divide and arrange positions in an organization or company. This structure is usually made in the form of a chart that displays a hierarchy.

Let's talk about the purpose of the organizational structure.

1. To coordinate people and recourses is the purpose of the organizational structure. With the existing organizational structure, people and recourse can be arranged more easily and clearly so that activities in the organization or company can run effectively.

2. To organize lines of communication is the purpose of the organizational structure. A hierarchical organizational structure can clarify the flow of communication from all positions by providing clarity to whom everyone should speak to if there is something to be communicated.

3. To formalize authority is the the purpose of the organization. Each position in the organizational structure is made to clarify the authority of each position so that there is no abuse of authority.

4. To limit workers' rights is not one of the purpose of the organizational structure. The rights of all workers should always be the same regardless of their position. Examples are the right to receive a salary, the right to receive equal treatment, and others.

Other than the three purposes above, there are more purposes for organizational structure

To divide tasks evenly and clearly

The existence of an organizational structure allows each position to get tasks according to their abilities and capacities

To minimize conflict.

With an organizational structure that divides the tasks of each position, conflicts in the organization can be reduced because everyone will focus on their tasks.

To provide clarity of responsibilities

With the organizational structure, each position will get its own responsibilities. It can also make it easier for the chairman or person in charge to evaluate the performance of the organization or company.

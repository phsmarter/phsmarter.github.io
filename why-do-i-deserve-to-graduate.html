21. do these people deserve to experience the intentional injuries?why or why not?


No one deserves to experience intentional injuries. Intentional injuries are injuries caused by another person to another human. It is termed intentional because a human has made a conscious decision to hurt someone whether himself or another person. So no one deserves to be hurt, despite the many bad things we do on a daily basis. Below are some examples of intentional injuries that we or someone else can cause.

Examples of intentional injuries

mugging  - physical violence against another person, usually to get their valuablestorture  - intentional physical pain inflicted on another person to get information (usually)murder  - the killing of another personsexual assault  - can be in various forms, such as touching private parts or rapephysical abuse  - hurting someone stalking  - following someone resulting in major psychological trauma to the person being followed

How do we prevent this?

Of course the best way is for criminals not to commit these crimes--but there are also steps we can take to avoid being a victim. This is not victim-blaming but rather, taking preventive actions.  

Be alert all the time. Know where you are going and know the nearest safest place is (like a police station, 7-11). Do not engage with anyone suspicious on social media.  Also be careful of what you post online.Have something that can protect you, like an umbrella--but when someone points a gun at you, it is best to surrender what they are asking for (like you wallet).  Do not be a hero in this case,If you think you being followed, go to a crowded place and lose the person.  Report any incident of abuse to the relevant authorities. Abuse in any form should not be tolerated.

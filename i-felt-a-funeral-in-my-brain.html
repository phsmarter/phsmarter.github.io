30. How do I want people to speak of me at my funeral?​


Speaking at funerals can be one of the sincerest actions people can make for the person that they are honoring.  Most of the time, it is a moment of remembering the good deeds, kindness, generosity, and compassion of that person.  It is an honorable commemoration of how the person lived his or her life well.

I believe that everyone desires to have an honorable eulogy at their funerals.  We want to be remembered as someone who was great and worthy of friendship or any other relationships to share with.  We want people to share the best memories we had when we were with them.  We want them to speak of our achievements, successes, and most valued contributions in life.  We certainly want to be honored as someone worth living.

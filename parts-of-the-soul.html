22. How do you explain Aristotle claim the soul is the part of man that animates the body the soul is composed of both rational and irrational elements?


Since a human's function as a human means the proper functioning of the soul, Aristotle sought to describe the nature of the soul. The soul is the form of the body. As such the soul refers to the total person. Accordingly, Aristotle said that the soul has two parts, the irrational and the rational.

18. 4. How do husbands and wives benefit from equal partnership in terms of domestic work?...​​


Answer:

Equal partnership in terms of domestic work can benefit both husbands and wives in several ways:

Reduced workload and stress: When both partners share domestic responsibilities, it can reduce the workload and stress on each individual. This can lead to a more harmonious and less stressful home environment.

Improved communication and teamwork: Working together on domestic tasks requires communication and teamwork, which can strengthen the relationship between spouses.

Increased satisfaction and happiness: When both partners are equally responsible for domestic work, it can lead to a greater sense of satisfaction and happiness in the relationship.

Greater gender equality: An equal partnership in domestic work can help to challenge traditional gender roles and promote greater gender equality in the home.

Improved work-life balance: When domestic work is shared equally, both partners can have more time and energy to devote to work, hobbies, and other interests.

Overall, an equal partnership in domestic work can lead to a more balanced and fulfilling relationship between spouses.

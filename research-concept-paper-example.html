1. example of concept paper for research


1. What are you curious about (within the general subject matter of this course)?

2. Choose one of the areas of curiosity and develop some specific questions (this is called "question framing"). Many research questions classified as:

exploratory (trying to find out about something)descriptive (trying to obtain descriptive data)explanatory (trying to explain the relationship between variables

3. Do any of your questions lend themselves to a research hypothesis? If so, write out any hypotheses

4. Identify the ideal evidence (data) and how you will probably try to gather that evidence (your methodology)

5. Write a concept paper

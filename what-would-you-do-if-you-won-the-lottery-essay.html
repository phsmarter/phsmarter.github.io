30. what kind of changes do you think could happen if ther won the lottery​


Answer:

Winning the lottery can change your life in many ways. The most heard-of stories though, are the ones where lottery winners spent their whole fortune in a matter of months or years. From being millionaires to losing it all, even their property. How your life will change after winning the lottery has a lot to do with how you choose to either invest or spend your wealth.

There are not only tragic stories worth mentioning about winning the lottery. In many cases the winners are smart enough to invest their money and it will continue to grow, or by giving a good amount of their fortune to charities or organizations that work for subjects they care about.

Some winners, feel that they have a whole new aspect of life and what’s important after winning a big sum. Being able to help others in need can do a whole lot of things to person’s psychology.

Others continue to live their lives in the exact same way as prior to their lottery winnings. There are stories where winners still live in the same house and continue to work at the same company. Many people who chose to maintain their normal life also chose to stay anonymous after winning the jackpot.

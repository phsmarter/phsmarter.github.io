A descriptive text usually focuses on describing a single location, object, event, person, or place. It endeavors to engage all five of the reader's senses to evoke the sights, sounds, smells, tastes, and feel of the text's subject.

An instruction text is a piece of non-fiction text that gives instructions on how to complete a task. Also called an instructional text, or an instructive text. The text may include organisational devices such as bullet points or numbers, diagrams, and pictures.

29. 119Properties of Equality18. Reflexive Property of Equality19. Symmetric Property of Equality20. Transitive property of equality21. Addition Property of Equality22. Subtraction Property of Equality23. Multiplication Property of Egquality24. Substitution Property of Equality25. Distributive Property of Equality​


Properties of Equality

18. Reflexive Property of Equality

For all real numbers x , x=x .

A number equals itself.

19. Symmetric Property of Equality

For all real numbers x  and y ,

if x=y , then y=x .

Order of equality does not matter.

20. Transitive property of equality

For all real numbers x,y,  and z ,

if x=y and y=z , then x=z .

Two numbers equal to the same number are equal to each other.

21. Addition Property of Equality

For all real numbers x,y,  and z ,

if x=y , then x+z=y+z .

22. Subtraction Property of Equality

For all real numbers x,y,  and z ,

if x=y , then x−z=y−z .

23. Multiplication Property of Egquality

For all real numbers x,y,  and z ,

if x=y , then xz=yz .

24. Substitution Property of Equality

For all real numbers x  and y ,

if x=y , then y can be substituted for x in any expression.

25. Distributive Property of Equality

For all real numbers x,y,  and z ,

x(y+z)=xy+xz

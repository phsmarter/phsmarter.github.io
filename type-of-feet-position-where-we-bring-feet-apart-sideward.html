Type of feet position where we bring feet apart sideward is second position.

Explanation :

There are five basic positions of Ballet :

First Position: heels together, toes pointing out to the sides.Second Position: feet are moved apart, hip-width apart, toes pointing out to the sides.Third Position: feet pointing outward, one foot is placed in front of the other with the front heel touching the back of the foot.Fourth Position :Fitfth Position : feet pointing outward, legs crossed and feet touching, with the heel of one foot placed next to the toes of the other.

Second Position

A good way to get into the second position is to start from the first position, then, maintaining the same rotation, slide the legs away. The balls of the two feet do not turn out to be quite comfortable, with the heels separated by a foot length.

Answer:

Favorite Scene:

The favorite scene in Five Feet Apart is when Stella and Will share their first kiss. Will has been struggling with his illness and Stella has been trying to stay away from him to protect her own health, but when they finally come together, it's a powerful moment of connection and hope. The moment is even more special because of the challenges they have faced to get to that moment.

Dislike Scene:

The dislike scene in Five Feet Apart is when Stella's best friend Poe collapses and is rushed to the hospital. The scene is difficult to watch as Poe has been a source of strength and joy for Stella throughout the film. Seeing her friend in such a vulnerable state brings up a lot of fear and sadness for Stella, and it's a difficult moment to watch.

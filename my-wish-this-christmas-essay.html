15. my favorite symbol Christmas essay​


Answer:

The Christmas season is a time for joy, celebration, and spending time with loved ones. One of the most iconic symbols of Christmas is the Christmas tree, a evergreen tree decorated with lights, ornaments, and other festive decorations. The tradition of bringing a tree into the home dates back to the 16th century, and it is a beloved symbol of the holiday season for many people around the world.

Another important symbol of Christmas is the star, which is often associated with the story of the three wise men who followed a star to Bethlehem to visit the baby Jesus. The star is a symbol of hope, faith, and guidance, and it is often depicted on Christmas trees and other decorations.

Gift-giving is also an important part of the Christmas tradition, and the image of Santa Claus delivering presents to children on Christmas Eve is a well-known symbol of the holiday. Santa, with his red suit, white beard, and jolly demeanor, is a beloved figure for many children and adults alike.

In addition to these symbols, Christmas is also associated with certain colors, such as red, green, and white. These colors are often used in Christmas decorations, and they are thought to represent the warmth and joy of the season.

Overall, Christmas is a time of celebration and joy, and the symbols associated with the holiday help to capture the spirit of the season. Whether it is a Christmas tree, a star, or a gift from Santa, these symbols remind us of the love, hope, and happiness that the holiday season brings.

30. essay about my christmas vacation


               My Christmas Vacation

Christmas is the most awaited season of almost all people in the world especially the children. We expect receiving and giving gifts during this time. Christmas vacation also means time out from classes and temporarily goodbye to early waking up in the morning.

I feel very satisfied and happy about my Christmas vacation. We had family reunion and I was able to see my cousins from far and near again. We ate delicious food and we played games. In Christmas eve, Mother and Ate cooked food for media noche. There were ham, spaghetti, chicken salad, chicken joy and ice cream on our table. I think all my favorites were served during that night. Mother and father also gave us gifts and we opened the gifts together after eating. Ate, kuya and I also gave our gifts to Mama and Papa. We also offered them songs and dance for entertainment. In some other days, we went to different beaches everyday and felt the fresh air while enjoying the scenery. We visited grandma and grandpa in the province and we had our new year there. I could say that we’ve done and enjoyed many activities during the Christmas vacation.  

Starting today,I will save money in my piggy bank so I can buy more gifts when Christmas will come again this year. Mama said, it is a season for giving though everyday is also okay. If I have money in December, I can also give something to my cousins if we meet in the reunion again.

The second paragraph tells about what happened or the narrative of your chosen topic. The last paragraph is composed of the wrap up/conclusion about your topic or your future plans regarding what you have written.

5. Activity 1.2 ICT across the Fields Information Communication Technology supports many industries. Using the internet as your source, search the internet for specific application(s) of ICT in each industry indicated on the boxes below. EDUCATION TELECOMMUNICATIONS ENTERAINMENT COMMERCE​


Answer:

What is ICT?

Information and communications technology (ICT) is a concept that emphasizes the role of communication networks and the integration of telecommunications (telephone lines and wireless signals) and computer systems, as well as the necessary enterprise applications, development tools, storage, and audiovisual, to enable users to access, store, transmit, comprehend, and process data.

The convergence of audiovisual and telephone networks with computer systems via single cabling or link system is also referred to as ICT. There are significant financial benefits to combine the telephone and computer networks into single unified wiring, signal distribution, and system management. ICT is a broad phrase that encompasses all forms of communication, including radio, television, cell phones, computer and network hardware, satellite systems, and so on, as well as the myriad services and appliances that go along with them, such as teleconferencing and distant learning.

Application of ICT in Education

It has been established that using ICT in the classroom enhances student motivation, causing them to become more interested and involved in the subjects they are studying. ICT enables the use of novel educational materials and the renewal of learning methods, allowing students to collaborate more actively and simultaneously acquire technological expertise.

Moreover, ICTs are quite beneficial in the development of discernment. Some of the most significant abilities that students learn as a result of their usage of ICT include the ability to search for and compare multiple sources, as well as the ability to arrange data.

Application of ICT in Telecommunications

The field of information and communication technology (ICT) is continuously evolving, with new breakthroughs affecting every part of daily life, from health care to computing to transportation. Each invention opens the door to the creation of whole new products. It's a thriving and interesting industry to work in, with new specialties popping up all the time.

This industry is well-established in the Irish economy and is responsible for a large range of high-demand items around the world, including software, hardware, and telecommunications.

Engineers that work in the computer software fields create cutting-edge computer hardware, software, and data management.

Application of ICT in Entertainment

Home and leisure - you have a variety of machines to assist you with household tasks such as cooking, laundry, and air conditioning. Microwaves are used to heat meals, and washing machines are used to clean clothes. And ICT is used to create these devices. We would have to cook our dinner over an open fire if it weren't for ICT.

Application of ICT in Commerce

ICT and commerce are inextricably linked words, as the e-commerce business relies on ICT for its operations and expansion. E-commerce is also known as the use of information and communication technology in business and commerce. ICT is a broad phrase that encompasses the use of computers, including hardware, software, and networks, to communicate, store, and manage data. E–commerce is a notion that has been expanding for a number of years and is driving economic growth in both developed and emerging economies.

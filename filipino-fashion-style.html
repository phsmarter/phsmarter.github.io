6. give 5 or more types of fashion styles​


Answer:TYPES OF FASHION STYLE(S)

1.) STREET STYLE

True to its name, street style dressing is not born in highend fashion studios, but on the streets of cities like Paris, Tokyo and even Mumbai.

2.) FORMAL STYLE

Formal means "approved"! So, you can expect some stringent dressing rules coming your way, which are laid down by the concerned authorities of the ceremony or a place.

3.) VINTAGE

If you believe in the "old is gold" concept, Vintage clothing style is just for you. This clothing style heavily draws its ideas from the bygone years, atleast 30-50 years old.

4.) BOHEMIAN

Bohemian clothing style is all about using natural elements like sea shells, threads, feathers and fabrics. It is a part of the hippe fashion which intends to break the conventional dressing norms and explore your free, personal style.

5.) ETHNIC

Ethnic fashion refers to the clothing style of a particular culture or a geographical location

6.) SPORTY

The sporty clothing style is born in America, where activewear like pyjamas, jerseys, sports shoe, track suits etc. ended up becoming mainstream fashion

7.) ARTSY

Artsy style is all about being "You"! It represents your unique taste and personality. Most people who are drawn towards the Artsy clothing style prefer handmade, artistic pieces that are truly one-of-a-kind!

8.) GOTHIC

Dark, mysterious and smokey – that's gothic clothing for you! If you want to try this style, say goodbye to bright, peppy colors and instead stick to black and crimson red.

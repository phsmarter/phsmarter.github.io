Their main function is to prevent sweat, water, and other debris from falling down into the eye socket, but they are also important to human communication and facial expression.

Each guard has been entrusted with five rooms & each room contains five pugs. Each pug has four baby pugs. Now you have to find that how many legs are there in the palace ?​​


SOLUTION⤵️

Let x be the legs of the guards

y be the legs of the pugs

Note that guards have a 2 legs and pug have 4 legs

Since they stated how many guards are there,I will just find how many pugs are there:

Now. In each room of 5 rooms, they have a 5 pugs with 4 babies each.

So, 5 x 5 x 4 = 100 pugs

Equation:

5x + 100y = total legs5(2) + 100(4) = total legs10 + 400 = total legs410 = total legs

ANSWER⤵️

There are a total of 410 legs in the palace.

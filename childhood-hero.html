1. Technology: Children today have grown up with technology, such as smartphones, tablets, and social media, as a central part of their lives. 2. Outdoor play: Children in the 1980s often spent more time playing outside and engaging in unstructured play, such as riding bikes, playing ball games, and exploring their neighborhoods. 3. Parental involvement: In recent years, there has been a trend towards more involved parenting, with parents taking an active role in their children's lives and education.

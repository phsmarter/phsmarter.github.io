1. the nature of mathematics essay


Mathematics is an abstract science that involves the study of patterns, numbers, and shapes. It is often considered the language of science, as it is used to solve real-world problems in a variety of fields, such as engineering, finance, and physics. Mathematics can be broken down into two major categories: applied mathematics and pure mathematics. Applied mathematics is the use of mathematics to solve problems in the real world; it includes such fields as numerical analysis, probability, and statistics. Pure mathematics, on the other hand, deals with abstract concepts and theorems, and includes such topics as algebra, geometry, and calculus.

The nature of mathematics is to use logical reasoning to solve problems and make predictions.

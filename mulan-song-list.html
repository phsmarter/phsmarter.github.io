10. what is the song all about the reflection by mulan


Answer:

The song Reflection in the classic Disney film Mulan, is an expression of the societal and personal struggles women face with in their everyday lives. The song depicts how a woman struggles to break barriers and balance following her dreams and following her parent’s will. Mulan wanted to break free but she is hindered by her fear of disrespecting her family and her country.  

To read the synopsis of the movie Mulan, go to this link: https://brainly.ph/question/404186

Explanation:

Societal Struggles

Gender roles have been construed for women. Society dictates the future of a woman the moment her gender is known, as the lyrics of the song goes,  

• “I will never pass for a perfect bride, or a perfect daughter”

• “Now I see, that if I were truly to be myself I would break my family’s heart”

Because of the ideology of patriarchy, especially in Asia, women as a group have been historically excluded from publicly acclaimed bases of power. Men have had better access to higher education and higher paying jobs. Women are confined to being plain housewives, daughters, and home-makers. Mulan is struggling because she wanted to help her father and her country by joining the military, but she also knew that it is impossible simply because she is a woman.

To know more about gender roles, go to this link: https://brainly.ph/question/1050285

Mulan’s Internal Struggle

Although Mulan is aware of the limitations caused by her gender, she courageously embraced her identity by deciding to join China’s military to fight their oppressors. Mulan knew that she will be lying to herself if she did not pursue her calling.  The lines “How I pray, that a time will come/ I can free myself and meet their expectations” shows that Mulan is torn between her true self and her family’s expectation

After all the hardships, Mulan was able to conquer herself – the limitations that she has set and the expectations of the society. Eventually, she was also accepted and honored not only by her parents, but by her countrymen as well.

The song Reflection and the movie Mulan empower women across the world to conquer their dreams and fears. It is a reminder that no hindrance is too big and nothing is too hard for someone who perseveres.

18. What is the song all about the mulan


Answer:

"Mulan" is a Disney animated film released in 1998. The film features several songs, including the well-known "Reflection".

"Reflection" is a power ballad that serves as the film's main theme song. It is performed by Lea Salonga and tells the story of Mulan, a young woman who disguises herself as a man to take her father's place in the army. The song reflects Mulan's inner turmoil as she grapples with her identity and her desire to bring honor to her family. The lyrics express her fear, uncertainty, and the struggle to find her true self. The song is a poignant and emotional piece that perfectly captures the themes of the film.

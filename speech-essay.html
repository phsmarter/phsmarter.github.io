14. Carl aquino speech essay pls​


CARL AQUINO SPEECH ESSAY

Carl Aquino, 2010 West Hall High School valedictorian, used commonplaces, style, and tone to create a successful address that his audience appreciated. His speech was based on a Rubik's Cube, a multicolored cube with 8 one-colored sides.

Using a Rubik's Cube to explain his high school experience helped his audience relate to the class. He compared a man searching for the proper woman for an extended period, then proposing, to applying to college. You "propose" to back-up women in case "you get refused," indicating he applied to back-up schools in case his first preference school rejected him.

Aquino's manner and tone make his speeches interesting, hilarious, and honest. His companion plays the guitar during his speech to captivate the audience. He employs simple phrases and sentences to avoid becoming "boring" and keep his audience's attention. Graduation is a pleasant time. Therefore his kind tone helps the audience connect with Aquino and his peers. His commonplaces, style, and tone made it a terrific speech altogether.

PROBLEM ANG CONSEQUENCES

Current Problem: The current problem that I am facing is that I do not have the motivation to study for an upcoming big test that has the capacity to change my life.

Possible Choice 1: Push myself to study now so I won't cram later on.

Consequence 1: If I push myself to study now, I might be able to learn a lot but I will get sick of it and laze around later on.Consequence 2: If I push myself to continuously study until the time before the examination, I might be able to pass.

Possible Choice 2: Do what I would rather do now instead of studying

Consequence 1: If I do what I would rather do now instead of studying, I might not have enough time to study other things that might help me pass the examination.Consequence 2: If I enjoy doing other things than studying, I might regret not studying at the time of the examination and lose my spirit during the test.

Possible Choice 3: Ask my friend for a piece of advice in the hope that he/she might help me and give me some motivation

Consequence 1: My friend might be able to tell me that if I study now I can do the things that I would rather do now later in the future since I might have a brighter future if I pass the test.Consequence 2: My friend might joke around and spoil me for being lazy.

Answer:

PROPERTIES OF A WELL WRITTEN TEXT

A writer should know the importance of the properties of a well-written text. Having a well-written text makes it easier for you to express your work to your readers. Listed below are the properties needed to achieve a well-written text.

Unity

Unity is achieved when a composition contains one focused idea. Having unity in your writing helps the readers to easily understand what the composition is about without having to re-read it.

Coherence and Cohesion

Coherence refers to how easy it is to understand the writing and cohesion is the connectivity of ideas in a text. When writing is coherent and cohesive, the readers will not get lost in the point the author is making because of the connected ideas in the text.

Organization

An organized piece of writing is clear and easy in the eyes of the readers.

Language Use

Using clear sentences and vocabularies that can be easily understood, having consistency in using pronouns, avoiding redundancies, wordiness is needed to achieve a well-written text.

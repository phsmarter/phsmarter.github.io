8. Read the quote below and explain what it means. "Failure will never overtake me if my determination to succeed is strong enough."​


Answer:

This quote means that if you are determined and committed to achieving success, you will not be overcome by failure. It suggests that even if you face challenges or setbacks, your determination to succeed will be strong enough to keep you moving forward.

The quote suggests that failure is inevitable and will happen to everyone at some point. However, it also suggests that how you respond to failure is what ultimately determines your success. If you are determined and committed to achieving your goals, you will not be defeated by failure, but rather use it as a learning opportunity and continue to strive for success.

Overall, the quote emphasizes the importance of having a strong determination to succeed and not letting failure hold you back. It encourages persistence and resilience in the face of challenges, and suggests that with a strong enough will to succeed, you can overcome any obstacle on the path to achieving your goals.

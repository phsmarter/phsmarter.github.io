The option B. Reading from manuscript is incorrect answers. Because reading the manuscript is a speech without regard for guidelines and does not cause emotions.

The option C. Formal speech is incorrect answers. Because formal speech is a speech made at a formal event that is usually attended by important people, such as officials, dignitaries, or important figures (which simply needs to use outlines).

The option D. Memorized speech is incorrect answers. Because memorized speech is a method of speech that is carried out without text or is completely memorized. Usually someone who uses this method will be given time and careful preparation until the time to deliver the speech.

EXPLANATION:

There are several methods used in giving speeches, namely memoriter, manuscript, impromptu, and extemporaneous.

The memoriter method is a method of giving a speech by memorizing and writing down the speech first. This method is used by people who are just learning to speak.Manuscript method is a method of giving speeches by bringing and reading the script when giving a speech. This method is used by officials in official state events to avoid mistakes.The impromptu method is a method of making speeches in a spontaneous way and only relying on the experience and insight of the speaker. This means that the speaker in this method is appointed suddenly and is usually the right person to give a speech at an event. This method is used in informal events.The extemporaneous method is a method of giving a speech by bringing a small note as a reminder of the order in which the content of the speech will be delivered. This method is usually used by people who are already proficient in delivering speeches in order to speak systematically.

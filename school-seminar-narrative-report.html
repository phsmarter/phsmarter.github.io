14. There are 18 Davaoenos who participated in a seminar. this is 18% of the total number of the participants. how many attended the seminar?


Answer:

100

Step-by-step explanation:

We need to convert 18% to fraction first.

18% = 0.18 in decimal form0.18 = 9/50 in fraction form

So let x be the total number of participants.

[tex]18 = \frac{9}{50} x[/tex]

To eliminate 9/50, we should multiply both sides  to its reciprocal

[tex]18×\frac{50}{9} = (\frac{9}{50} × \frac{50}{9}) x[/tex]

100 = x

Hence, there are a total of 100 participants who attended the seminar

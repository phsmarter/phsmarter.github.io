6. main purpose to encourage an interchange of information between the patient and the nurse successful interview allows the nurse to achieve the following what is your own understanding by this sentence?​


The main purpose of conducting an interview between a patient and a nurse is to encourage the exchange of information that will help the nurse better understand the patient's health condition and needs. A successful interview allows the nurse to gather essential information about the patient's medical history, current symptoms, lifestyle, and other relevant factors that may impact their health. This information can help the nurse develop an appropriate plan of care, establish rapport and trust with the patient, and provide education and support to the patient and their family. Ultimately, the goal of the interview is to promote better health outcomes for the patient.

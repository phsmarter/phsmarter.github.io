6. Write a one page essay on the state of journalism in Philippines today​


Reporting from home

Reporting from homeCOVID-19, along with the ensuing quarantines, poses challenges to existing journalistic practices that typically require fieldwork, but it also encourages journalists in the Philippines to reimagine news production. We observe that access to information has generally been limited because government offices have not been in full operation while virtual press briefings do not allow for a more open discussion between journalists and officials. To illustrate, Ilagan (2020) reported that most routine requests for information have not been processed since March 2020 when government offices were wholly or partly closed due to the ongoing quarantine. The Philippines is among many governments in the world that had to suspend the processing of freedom-of-information (FOI) requests because of the pandemic (McIntosh, 2020). FOI officers working from home could not address requests because they lacked Internet connection, laptop computers, and scanners, including digital copies of files. They also found it difficult to coordinate remotely with record custodians. While some national agencies have been proactive in providing information on COVID-19, the same cannot be said for many local government units. Ilagan (2020) further noted that ‘[un]like frontline agencies at the national level, local governments do not proactively publish data on their websites’. Information about plans to combat the impact of the virus are usually available, but more prodding is needed to find out how these plans are being implemented and funded

9. reflection about essay,academic journal and academic report​


Answer:

An essay is a piece of writing that presents an argument or a point of view on a specific topic. It usually follows a specific structure, including an introduction, body paragraphs, and a conclusion. An academic journal is a publication in which researchers and scholars publish their research findings and ideas. They are usually peer-reviewed and are considered a reliable source of information. An academic report is a detailed document that presents research findings or information on a specific topic. It is typically written in a formal style and is often used in academic settings to inform others of the results of a study or research project.

Reflecting on these three forms of writing, it can be seen that they all serve different purposes. An essay is typically used to present an argument or point of view, while an academic journal is used to disseminate research findings. An academic report, on the other hand, is used to present research findings in a more detailed and structured format. All three forms of writing are important in academic and research settings as they allow for the sharing and dissemination of knowledge and ideas.

Ang pagkakaroon ng mga katangiang ito ay nagbibigay-daan sa isang tao upang magkaroon ng mga layunin sa buhay, makamit ang mga ito, at magtagumpay sa iba't ibang aspeto ng buhay.

500 words-

Ang pagiging matagumpay ay isang pangarap ng maraming tao. Ngunit, hindi ito madaling makamit. Kinakailangan ng tiyaga, determinasyon, at iba pang mga katangian upang magtagumpay sa buhay.

Ang unang katangian na kinakailangan upang magtagumpay ay ang determinasyon. Ang pagkakaroon ng determinasyon ay nangangailangan ng pagtitiyaga at pagpupursige upang makamit ang mga layunin. Ito ay kailangan upang malagpasan ang anumang mga hadlang at pagsubok na dumating sa ating buhay. Sa pamamagitan ng determinasyon, ang isang tao ay nakakamit ang kanyang mga pangarap at nagkakaroon ng tagumpay.

Ang pangalawang katangian ay ang disiplina. Ang disiplina ay nagpapahiwatig ng pagkakaroon ng kontrol sa sarili at sa mga gawain na ginagawa. Kinakailangan ng disiplina upang magkaroon ng maayos na balanse sa buhay, lalo na kung mayroong mga mabibigat na gawain o responsibilidad na kailangan gampanan. Sa pamamagitan ng disiplina, ang isang tao ay nagiging mas produktibo at nakakamit ang mga layunin nang mas mabilis.

Ang pangatlong katangian ay ang pagiging maparaan. Ang pagiging maparaan ay nagpapahiwatig ng kakayahang mag-isip ng solusyon sa mga problema at mga hamon sa buhay. Kinakailangan ng pagiging maparaan upang magkaroon ng mga alternatibong solusyon sa mga hamon na maaaring dumating sa ating buhay. Sa pamamagitan ng pagiging maparaan, ang isang tao ay nagiging mas handa sa mga pagbabago at nakakamit ang kanyang mga pangarap.

Ang pang-apat na katangian ay ang kakayahang magtitiyaga. Ang pagkakaroon ng kakayahang magtitiyaga ay nagpapahiwatig ng kakayahang hindi sumuko sa gitna ng mga pagsubok. Kinakailangan ng magtitiyaga upang magtagumpay sa buhay dahil may mga pagkakataong hindi agad-agad nakakamit ang mga pangarap. Sa pamamagitan ng magtitiyaga, ang isang tao ay nakakamit ang kanyang mga pangarap at nagiging mas matatag sa kanyang mga desisyon.

Ang panglimang at huling katangian ay ang positibong pananaw sa buhay. Ang positibong pananaw sa buhay ay nagpapahiwatig ng pagiging optimistiko at pagtanggap sa mga bagay na nangyayari sa buhay. Kinakailangan ng positibong pananaw sa buhay upang magkaroon ng matatag na pananaw sa buhay at hindi magpabaya sa mga hamon at pagsubok. Sa pamamagitan ng positibong pananaw sa buhay, ang isang tao ay nakakamit ang tagumpay at nagiging mas maligaya sa kanyang buhay.

Sa kabuuan, ang pagiging matagumpay ay hindi lamang tungkol sa pagkamit ng mga pangarap. Ito ay tungkol sa pagkakaroon ng mga katangian na nakakatulong sa atin upang makamit ang mga ito. Ang determinasyon, disiplina, pagiging maparaan, kakayahang magtitiyaga, at positibong pananaw sa buhay ay ilan sa mga katangian na kinakailangan upang magtagumpay sa buhay. Ang pagkakaroon ng mga katangiang ito ay nagbibigay-daan sa isang tao upang magkaroon ng mga layunin sa buhay, makamit ang mga ito, at magtagumpay sa iba't ibang aspeto ng buhay.

26. reflection about essay,academic journal and academic report​


Answer:

An essay is a piece of writing that presents an argument or a point of view on a specific topic. It is commonly used in academic settings to assess a student's understanding of a subject or their ability to express themselves clearly and coherently. Essays can be written in various formats, such as narrative, descriptive, expository, or persuasive. They can also vary in length, from short to long.

An academic journal, on the other hand, is a publication that contains scholarly articles written by experts in a particular field. These articles are peer-reviewed, meaning they are evaluated by other experts in the same field to ensure their quality and accuracy. Academic journals are an important source of information for researchers and academics, as they provide access to the latest research and ideas in a specific field.

An academic report, also known as a research report, is a document that presents the findings of a research study. It is often written by students or researchers as part of their academic work and is intended to be read by other experts in the same field. It contains a detailed description of the research methods used, the results obtained, and the conclusions drawn from the study.

In summary, while essays are typically used to assess a student's understanding of a subject, academic journals and reports are used to disseminate new research and ideas in a specific field. All three types of writing are important for academic discourse and research. Essays are good for expressing thoughts and ideas, academic journals are good for exposing new research and ideas, and academic reports are good for sharing the results of research studies.

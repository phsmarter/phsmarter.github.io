Non-linear Text

Linear text is the inverse of nonlinear text. Most people think of nonlinear texts as those that include visuals or graphs. Flowcharts, charts, and graphs (for example, pie charts and bar graphs), as well as graphical organizers such as knowledge maps and story maps, are some examples.

Linear Text

Linear text is traditional text that must be read from beginning to end. Texts printed on paper are generally considered linear texts. Novels, poems, short stories, letters, educational texts, and any other text that we read from beginning to end are all linear texts.

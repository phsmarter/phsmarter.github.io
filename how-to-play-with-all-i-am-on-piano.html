25. If you Know How to Play the Piano Then How Can I Know Which finger To Use when playing something?I'm Serious With this question.​


Answer:

Short Answer: Treble Staff is for fingers on right hand and Bass Staff is for left fingers (and left foot, just in case your piano has pedal keys).

Long Answer: Musical scores for beginners on piano lessons normally has numbers for every note from 1 to 5 for each staff, representing the 5 fingers of our both hands. That's easy, right?

However, as your lesson progresses, you will notice that next pieces that you will play will have no numbers like that anymore. That is because you will soon learn the finger placement and the strategy they call 'economy of motion.' This technique allows your hands to play with minimum movement, giving you greater control in playing. By that time, you won't need to ask which finger is to which note.

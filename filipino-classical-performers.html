Answer:

philippine theatrical performance - It seeks to develop performers as well as audiences for classical music performances, both foreign and Filipino.

western classical performance-In technical musical usage this means music composed during the late eighteenth and early nineteenth centuries, characterized by the development of the sonata by such composers as Mozart. In popular use, however, the term is used to mean any serious art music as distinct from jazz, pop, or folk.

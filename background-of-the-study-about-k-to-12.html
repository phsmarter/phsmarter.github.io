3. Background of the study k-12 program: colegio de san jose(CSJ) students' perceptions and experiences as they prepare for college courses and cite the sources ​


Answer:

The K-12 Program is an educational reform initiative in the Philippines that aims to enhance the quality of education in the country by providing a more comprehensive and inclusive curriculum. This program adds two additional years in the basic education system, which is composed of six years of primary education, four years of junior high school, and two years of senior high school.

The Colegio de San Jose (CSJ) is one of the educational institutions that has implemented the K-12 program. This study aims to explore the perceptions and experiences of CSJ students as they prepare for college courses under the K-12 program. The study seeks to examine how the K-12 program has affected the students' readiness for college courses and how it has prepared them for the challenges of tertiary education.

Sources for this study may include published research papers, academic journals, books, and other relevant literature related to the K-12 program and its implementation in the Philippines. The study may also use primary sources such as surveys, interviews, and focus group discussions with CSJ students to gather their perceptions and experiences. The study may also draw on government reports and policies related to the K-12 program to provide context and background information.

Overall, this study seeks to contribute to the understanding of the K-12 program's impact on the education system in the Philippines and its effectiveness in preparing students for college courses. By examining the experiences and perceptions of CSJ students, the study aims to provide insights that may be useful in improving the implementation of the K-12 program and enhancing the quality of education in the country.

14. what i know about background of the study​


Answer:

The background study for a thesis includes a review of the area being researched, current information surrounding the issue, previous studies on the issue, and relevant history on the issue. Ideally, the study should effectively set forth the history and background information on your thesis problem.

Explanation:

The importance of a background study in research can be summarized as three main key points.

Right direction - Doing a background check, is often the most important step in any field. This enables you to move in the right direction with focus and set clear goals. You will be aware of what has already been done in that particular area and what can be contributed into that field from your research point of view.

Better ideas - Most of us start our research with clustered ideas and usually get stuck with how to proceed further. Having a background study in the initial phase help to declutter your thoughts and get them organized. Sometimes, you may even get better ideas than what you intend to do.

Helping Hand - Personally, I feel that background study becomes very useful while writing a technical paper. For most of the technical papers, “Previous work” area is very important and you should be familiar with atleast 10 papers related to your area. If you have already done a background study, you will be saved from this hectic task of finding reference papers for your technical paper.

23. Background of the study about academic procrastination


The background of the study in a thesis should include the following discussion:

A brief review of the research topic Current information or relevant literature that talks about the research topic Relevant history about the research topic

Its basic aim is to lead the readers to be knowledgeable of the purpose of the research topic, and why and how the topic arises for conducting the research.

If your topic is about academic procrastination, make a preliminary research on this.  Read on the current and relevant issues and literature that tackle academic procrastination.  Use the access of books and journals in the library.  Through the information that you have gathered, create a solid research question as the base of your background.  You may use the format provided above.

Here are some studies about academic procrastination that you may read in formulating your background of the study:

1. Reasons of Academic Procrastination: Self-Regulation, Academic Self-Efficacy, Life Satisfaction and Demographics Variables by Mehmet Kandemir, 2014

“Students cannot show their real performances in learning processes and they fail because of procrastination.”

2. Academic Procrastination in College Students: The Role of Self-Reported Executive Function by Laura Rabin, et al., 2010

“Procrastination is increasingly viewed as involving failures in self-regulation and volition, processes commonly regarded as executive functions.”

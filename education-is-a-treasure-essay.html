17. in life what are countable treasure ? what are measurable treasure?if you are to choose which do you prefer to keep countable or measurable treasures?why pahelp po​


Answer:

In life, countable treasures are those that can be quantified and counted, such as money, jewelry, and other physical possessions. Measurable treasures, on the other hand, are those that cannot be counted but can be measured, such as experiences, knowledge, and personal growth.

If I were to choose which type of treasure to keep, I would prefer measurable treasures. While countable treasures can bring temporary satisfaction, they do not necessarily lead to long-term happiness and fulfillment. Measurable treasures, such as personal growth and experiences, can bring lasting satisfaction and can help shape a person's character and values. Additionally, measurable treasures are not affected by external circumstances, such as financial or material loss, and therefore provide a more stable source of happiness and fulfillment.

18. "Millennial Generations:The Roles and Functions of Counselors"-make 200 word Essay​


Counselors play a vital role in the lives of people, more specifically among the students.  They work together with the parents, teachers and school administrators, etc. in order to maintain a healthy environment free of bullying, substance use and abuse, and behavioral issues that are dangerous and deviant towards the self and others.

The counselors are responsible in the management and proper handling of students’ behavior affecting their academics and life in general.  Moreover, aside from dealing with the lives of students, they are expected to improve and empower mental health among individuals, groups and communities.  They create and establish rapport among their clients, and encourage them to voice out their emotions and experiences that are maybe troublesome for the client.  They help process and understand certain experiences and situations.  They examine their client’s existing issues such as dysfunctional families and other relationships, self-image, stress level, suicidal tendencies, career paths, anger management, etc.

They construct interventions fit to their client in order to meet their needs and address the issues with proper guidance.  They can do research or project work, motivational talks, learning sessions about career path and life goals, and mental health wellness.

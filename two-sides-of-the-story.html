22. a high school is planning to put on the musical west side story there are 20 singers auditioning for the musical the director is looking for two singers who could sing a good duet inhow many ways can the director choose two singers from the 20 singers?


Answer:

The director is looking for two singers who could sing a good duet from 20 singers. This is a combination problem because the order of the singers does not matter. The number of ways to choose two singers from 20 singers can be calculated using the combination formula:

nCr = n! / r!(n-r)!

where n is the total number of singers and r is the number of singers to be chosen.

In this case, n = 20 and r = 2. So, the number of ways to choose two singers from 20 singers is:

20C2 = 20! / (2! * (20-2)!) = (20 * 19) / (2 * 1) = 190.

Therefore, there are 190 ways to choose two singers from 20 singers.

10. Gutumin Restaurant started operations on June 1, 2018, with Php 500, 000 from personal savings and Php 200, 000 from a bank loan. During the first year of operations, net income was Php 120, 000. On December 20, 2018, the owner withdrew cash in anticipation of the Christmas day celebration in the amount of Php 10, 000. No additional activities affected the owner's equity in 2018. By December 31, 2018, the restaurant had increased to Php 80, 000. In December 31, 2018 financial statements, how much is the total asset? And how much is the total owner's equity?​


Answer:

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The total assets of Gutumin Restaurant on December 31, 2018 is Php 580,000. This can be calculated from the initial equity (Php 500,000 + Php 200,000) + net income (Php 120,000) - owner's withdrawal (Php 10,000). The total owner's equity on December 31, 2018 is Php 80,000 which is equal to the amount of increased equity as stated in the question.

19. find the actual time from february 20 2018 to december 15 2018


Answer:

The time between February 20, 2018, and December 15, 2018, can be calculated by finding the difference between the two dates.

Counting the days between the two dates:

February has 28 days and March has 31 days, so the number of days between February 20 and March 20 is 28 + 31 = 59.

April, June, September, and November each have 30 days, so the number of days between March 20 and December 1 (excluding December) is 30 x 4 = 120.

December has 31 days, so the number of days between December 1 and December 15 is 15.

Therefore, the total number of days between February 20, 2018, and December 15, 2018, is:

59 + 120 + 15 = 194 days.

So, the actual time between the two dates is 194 days.

22. You are a financial Manager of Chevron Corp. You need to assess the effectiveness of working capital management of the company for 2018 using the following data. What is the 2018 Receivable turnover? 2017 Account Receivable = 15,353,000 2018 Account Receivable = 15,050,000 2017 Inventory = 5,585,000 2018 Inventory = 5,704,000 2017 Accounts Payable = 14,565,000 2018 Accounts Payable = 13,953,000 2017 Sales = 134,674,000 2018 Sales = 158,902,000 2017 Cost of Sales = 95,114,000 2018 Cost of Sales = 113,997,000 2017 Purchases = 95,114,000 2018 Purchases = 123,435,000 10.20 O 10.45 10.60​


Answer:

The formula for receivable turnover is:

Receivable turnover = Sales / Average Accounts Receivable

To calculate the average accounts receivable, we add the beginning and ending balances and divide by 2:

Average Accounts Receivable = (Beginning Accounts Receivable + Ending Accounts Receivable) / 2

Therefore, the average accounts receivable for Chevron Corp. in 2018 is:

Average Accounts Receivable = (15,353,000 + 15,050,000) / 2 = 15,201,500

And the receivable turnover for 2018 is:

Receivable turnover = 158,902,000 / 15,201,500 = 10.45

Therefore, the 2018 Receivable turnover for Chevron Corp. is 10.45.

10. Ergonomic hazards are caused by poor workplace design or setup.( TRUE OR FALSE )- kindly answer if yk tq. ​


Answer:

Yes, that is true.

Explanation:

Ergonomic hazards are caused by poor workplace design or setup that can lead to musculoskeletal disorders, such as carpal tunnel syndrome, and other injuries. These hazards can be reduced or eliminated by designing or arranging the workplace to fit the needs of the workers, using ergonomically designed equipment and tools, and providing training and support to workers to help them use equipment properly.

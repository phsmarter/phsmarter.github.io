Elephants are social animals that live in herds led by a matriarch. They are herbivores and can consume up to 300 pounds of vegetation in a single day. Elephants are also intelligent animals and have been observed exhibiting behaviors such as empathy and self-awareness. Unfortunately, elephants are also threatened by habitat loss and poaching for their ivory tusks. Conservation efforts are underway to protect elephant populations and their habitats.

The elephant, who is initially viewed as a symbol of wealth and prestige, ultimately becomes a threat to the magician's power and is used as a pawn in the magician's attempts to maintain his control over the town.

Solution:

Since

20 : 30 = 360 : X

Then we know

30/20 = X/360

Multiplying both sides by 360 cancels on the right

360 × (30/20) = (X/360) × 360

360 × (30/20) = X

Then solving for X

X = 360 × (30/20)

X = 540

Therefore

20 : 30 = 360 : 540

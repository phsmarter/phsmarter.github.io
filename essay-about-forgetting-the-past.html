13. perfect past tense of make,enjoy,forget??

Had Enjoyed

Had Forgot..

Answer:

made, enjoyed, forgotten

Explanation:

To form the past perfect tense you use the past tense of the verb "to have," which is had, and add it to the past participle of the main verb. For example: subject + had + past participle = past perfect tense.

The past tense of enjoy is enjoyed. The third-person singular simple present indicative form of enjoy is enjoys. The present participle of enjoy is enjoying. The past participle of enjoy is enjoyed.

The past tense of forget is forgot (archaic) or forgat (obsolete). The third-person singular simple present indicative form of forget is forgets. The present participle of forget is forgetting. The past participle of forget is forgotten or fokrgot (archaic).

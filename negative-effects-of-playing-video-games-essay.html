CAUSE

The causes of video game addiction are complex and can vary greatly, but the effects have the potential to be severe. Playing video games can and should be a fun activity for all to enjoy. But just like everything else, the amount of time one spends playing video games needs to be balanced with personal and social responsibilities.

EFFECT

One common effect of video game addiction is isolation and withdrawal from social experiences. 19. Comprehension Questions1. Why are video games popular with children?2. What are the positive effects of playing video games?3. What are the negative effects of playing video games?​


A video game is an interactive electronic entertainment medium that allows players to engage in a virtual environment and experience various game mechanics. It typically involves controlling a character or avatar within the game world and can be played on a variety of platforms including consoles, computers, and mobile devices.

Here are the answers to your activity:

Video games are popular with children for several reasons. Firstly, video games are interactive and can provide a sense of achievement when players progress through levels or complete objectives. They can also offer a form of escapism and allow children to immerse themselves in imaginary worlds or take on roles that they may not be able to in real life. Video games can provide social connections with friends and peers through multiplayer games and online communities.

Playing video games can have positive effects on cognitive and social development. Video games can improve problem-solving skills, spatial awareness, and hand-eye coordination. They can also promote teamwork and communication skills when playing with others. Video games can also be a source of stress relief and relaxation.

Playing video games can have negative effects on physical and mental health. Sitting for long periods while playing video games can lead to a sedentary lifestyle, which can increase the risk of obesity and other health issues. Excessive video game use can lead to addiction, social isolation, and poor academic performance. Some video games may also contain violent or explicit content, which can have a negative impact on a child's behavior and attitudes.

25. brainstorming effects of playing video games.​


Answer:

here are some potential effects of playing video games:

Improved cognitive skills: Playing video games can improve cognitive skills, including memory, spatial awareness, problem-solving, and decision-making.

Hand-eye coordination: Video games require players to react quickly and accurately, which can improve hand-eye coordination and reaction time.

Increased creativity: Some video games involve creativity, such as designing characters, worlds, or storylines, which can inspire and stimulate players' own creativity.

Socialization: Many video games involve playing with others, either in person or online, which can facilitate socialization and teamwork skills.

Stress relief: Playing video games can provide a form of stress relief and escapism, allowing players to relax and unwind.

Addiction: Playing video games can become addictive, leading to excessive screen time and potentially negative effects on mental health and relationships.

Violence: Some video games contain violent content, which has been linked to aggressive behavior in some individuals.

Physical health: Playing video games for long periods of time can lead to physical health problems, such as obesity, back pain, and eye strain.

Sleep disturbance: Playing video games close to bedtime can disrupt sleep patterns and lead to sleep disturbance.

Academic performance: Spending excessive time playing video games can negatively impact academic performance and achievement

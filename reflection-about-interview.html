4. Strategy Process My Reflection Research Read about the topics of your interview using books, websites, or even ask knowledgeable people.


Answer:

To prepare for an interview, it's important to conduct research on the topics you will be discussing. Here are some steps you can take:

1. Strategy: Begin by identifying the goals and objectives of the interview. What do you hope to achieve? What topics will you be discussing? What are the key messages you want to convey? Having a clear strategy in mind will help you focus your research and prepare more effectively.

2. Process: Once you have a clear strategy in mind, it's time to start researching the topics you will be discussing. This may involve reading books, articles, or websites related to the subject matter, or speaking with knowledgeable people who can provide insights and perspectives. Make sure to take notes and organize your research in a way that makes sense to you.

3. My Reflection: As you conduct your research, take time to reflect on what you are learning. What insights or new ideas are emerging? What questions do you still have? What connections can you make between different pieces of information? Reflection can help you deepen your understanding of the topics you are researching and identify areas that may require further exploration.

4. Research: Keep in mind that research is an ongoing process. As you prepare for your interview, continue to seek out new information and perspectives that can inform your thinking. This may involve revisiting sources you have already read, or seeking out new ones. Don't be afraid to challenge your assumptions or consider alternative viewpoints.

By following these steps, you can prepare effectively for your interview and ensure that you are well-informed and confident in your responses. Remember to stay focused on your goals and objectives, and to approach the research process with an open and curious mind.

21. reflection about work immersion interview.​


Answer:

Work immersion interviews can be nerve-wracking for students, especially for those who are inexperienced in the working world. However, it is an opportunity for them to showcase their skills, work ethic, and eagerness to learn in a professional setting.

One of the key takeaways from a work immersion interview is the importance of preparation. Researching the company, practicing responses to commonly asked questions, and being knowledgeable about the position can help students appear confident and prepared during the interview.

Another takeaway is the importance of communication skills. The ability to articulate oneself clearly, express ideas, and actively listen to the interviewer is crucial in creating a positive impression.

Finally, a work immersion interview can provide insights into the working world, such as the skills and qualities that are valued in a professional setting, and the expectations that come with being a part of an organization.

Overall, a work immersion interview is an opportunity for students to gain real-world experience and insights, and to hone their skills in preparation for their future careers.

27. Reflection about interviewing a public market vendor?


Explanation:

A public market is made up of small independent businesses, and each shop or stall is owner-operated. Rather than one company selling every item, like you would find in a supermarket, a public market features dozens of vendors selling food and other products they made themselves.

Most street vendors provide the main source of income for their households, bringing food to their families and paying school fees for their children. These informal workers have strong linkages to the formal economy. Over half of the interviewed workers said they source the goods they sell from formal enterprises. Street vendors and market traders are an integral part of urban economies around the world, offering easy access to a wide range of affordable goods and services in public spaces. They sell everything from fresh vegetables to prepared foods, from building materials to garments and crafts.

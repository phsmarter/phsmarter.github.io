1. indie film movie short summary


Answer:

Kiko Boksingero Movie stars Noel Comia Jr, Yayo Aguila, and Yul Servo. Produced by James Robin Mayo and Ferdinand Lapuz and directed by Thop Nazareno, this Pinoy indie film won 3 awards at the 2017 Cinemalaya Independent Film Festival – Best Original Music Score award for Pepe Manikan, Best Actor award for Noel Comia Jr, and Best Supporting Actress award for Yayo Aguila.

Kiko Boksingero Movie Summary

In the movie, Noel Comia Jr plays Francis “Kiko” Arenas, an 11-year old boy whose mother has passed away and is now living with his Yaya Diday (Yayo Aguila) in Baguio while waiting for his relatives in the US to bring him over there. In school, his classmates tease him for not being circumcised and for not having a father. Unknown to Diday, Kiko frequently visits the abandoned house of his father George (Yul Servo), a retired boxer.

In the said house, Kiko trains himself in boxing using the boxing equipment his father had left behind. One day, George returned home and saw his son. At first, the father and son were quite uneasy with each other. But as days pass by, Kiko and George started to bond together through boxing, jogging and story sharing. George also accompanied Kiko when he gets circumcised. The ending of the movie is very moving.

The film revels in its simplicity. It makes no grand gestures to explain why Kiko’s father is estranged or how his mother died. These are not as important as what it is that Kiko is yearning for now, in the present, which his father can give him and Diday cannot.

3. What makes indie films different from mainstream movies?


INDIE FILMS VS. MAINSTREAM MOVIES

Indie Films

Indie films, are commonly low-budget films, it portrays, current events, social issues and stories that showcases the daily hardships and struggles of a particular Individual or community.

Indie films are faster to film and produce, since it always depicts actual and real-life scenarios and does not require a lot of characters.

Mainstream Movies

Mainstream Movies are usually expensive. It requires complex and sophisticated technologies and a lot of casts and characters, to be successful. Unlike Indie Films, it takes at least a couple of years to accomplish a Mainstream Movie.

12. council of the indies


Answer:

The Council of the Indies; officially, the Royal and Supreme Council of the Indies (Spanish: Real y Supremo Consejo de las Indias, pronounced [reˈal i suˈpɾemo konˈsexo ðe las ˈindjas]), was the most important administrative organ of the Spanish Empire for the Americas and the Philippines. The crown held absolute power over the Indies and the Council of the Indies was the administrative and advisory body for those overseas realms. It was established in 1524 by Charles V to administer "the Indies," Spain's name for its territories. Such an administrative entity, on the conciliar model of the Council of Castile, was created following the Spanish conquest of the Aztec empire in 1521 which demonstrated the importance of the Americas. Originally an itinerary council that followed Charles V, it was subsequently established as an autonomous body with legislative, executive and judicial functions by Philip II of Spain and placed in Madrid in 1561.[1] The Council of the Indies was abolished in 1812 by the Cortes of Cádiz, briefly restored in 1814 by Ferdinand VII of Spain, and definitively abolished in 1834 by the regency, acting on behalf of the four-year-old Isabella II of Spain.[2

14. reflection about Cloudy with the chance of meatballs 2 (2013) movie.​


Answer:

Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs 2" is a 2013 animated movie that tells the story of a young inventor named Flint Lockwood and his friends who travel to an island filled with food-animals, created by one of Flint's inventions.

One reflection I have about the movie is the theme of friendship and teamwork. Throughout the film, Flint and his friends work together to navigate the challenges they face on the island. Despite their differences and occasional disagreements, they ultimately come together to overcome obstacles and save the day. This is a valuable lesson that can be applied to real-life situations where collaboration and cooperation can lead to success.

Another reflection is the movie's message about the consequences of overconsumption and greed. The idea of food falling from the sky may seem like a dream come true, but as the film progresses, it becomes clear that too much of a good thing can have negative consequences. This is a relevant message in today's society where issues such as food waste and overconsumption are becoming increasingly urgent.

Finally, the movie's colorful and imaginative animation style is also worth noting. The vibrant colors and whimsical designs of the food-animals create a visually appealing and entertaining experience that can be enjoyed by audiences of all ages.

Overall, "Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs 2" is an enjoyable movie that offers valuable lessons about friendship, teamwork, and the consequences of our actions.

______3. gintong kutsara - mayaman

______4. ahas - isang uri ng hayop

______5. pula - pagmamahal

Answer:

D - Denotasyon 1. papel - gamit sa paaralan na sinusulatan

D - Denotasyon 2. bakal - matigas (ugali o salitang kilos)

K - Konotasyon 3. gintong kutsara - mayaman

Explanation:

Ang denotasyon ay ang direktang kahulugan. Ito ang kahulugan ng salita sa diksyunaryo. Ang literal o totoong kahulugan ng salita. Habang ang konotasyon ay ang malalim na kahulugan ng salita. Ang kahulugan ay ang personal na kahulugan ng isang salita o pangkat ng mga tao. Ang kahulugan ng konotasyon ay naiiba sa karaniwang kahulugan.

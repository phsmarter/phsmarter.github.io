1. At a store, one doughnut and two éclairs cost $4.00. Two doughnuts and three éclairs cost $6.50. Three doughnuts and one éclair cost $4.50. What is the total cost of one doughnut and one éclair?


Solution:

Let (x) be the price of doughnut
and (y) be the price of eclair

x + 2y = 4
2x + 3y = 6.5

2x - x + 3y - 2y = 6.5 - 4
x + y = 2.5

Answer: $2.5

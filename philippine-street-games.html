Festivals are prevalent in the Philippines. It seems that every province has a festival. Some festivals are even known internationally. Most festivals are related to religion, but there are also non-religious ones. Regardless, festivals are characterized by colorful designs, lively music, and artistic shows.

The SUBLIAN FESTIVAL is a feature of street dancing, harana, games, and lupakan. The SINULOG FESTIVAL is widely celebrated in Cebu City. The ATI-ATIHAN FESTIVAL is known as the wildest festival in the Philippines. “HALA BIRA” is often yelled by the Petitioner during the Ati-Atihan Festival. “PIT SENIOR” is often yelled by the Petitioner during the Sinulog Festival.

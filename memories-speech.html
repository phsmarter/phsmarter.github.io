What type of speech is remarks at the veterans memorial?

The type of speech that is typically given at a Veterans Memorial is a commemorative speech. A commemorative speech is a type of speech that pays tribute to a person, a group of people, an event, an idea, or an institution. In the case of a Veterans Memorial, the speech is usually delivered to honor and remember the sacrifices made by veterans who served in the military and fought for their country's freedom and safety. The speech may also include a call to action or a reminder of the importance of continuing to support and honor our nation's veterans.

28. For a sandy soil, the following are given: emax= 0.66, emin= 0.36 and Kat a relative density of 90% = 0.008 cm/sec. Determine the value of K at a relative density of 50%.​


Answer:

[tex]k_{50}=0.0165[/tex] cm/sec

Explanation:

FORMULAS:

Relative Density:

[tex]D_r=\dfrac{e_{max}-e}{e_{max}-e_{min}}[/tex]

where:

[tex]D_r[/tex] = relative density

e = in situ void ratio

[tex]e_{max}[/tex] = maximum void ratio

[tex]e_{min}[/tex] = minimum void ratio

Kozeny-Carman Equation:

[tex]k=C\dfrac{e^3}{1+e}[/tex]

where:

k = coefficient of permeability

C = constant

e = void ratio

SOLUTION:

k at relative density of 50%, [tex]k_{50}[/tex]:

[tex]D_r=\dfrac{e_{max}-e_{90}}{e_{max}-e_{min}}[/tex]

[tex]0.90=\dfrac{0.66-e_{90}}{0.66-0.36}[/tex]

[tex]e_{90}=0.66-0.90(0.66-0.36)[/tex]

[tex]e_{90}=0.39[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]k_{90}=C\dfrac{(e_{90})^3}{1+e_{90}}[/tex]

[tex]0.008=C\dfrac{0.39^3}{1+0.39}[/tex]

[tex]C=\dfrac{0.008(1+0.39)}{0.39^3}[/tex]

[tex]C=0.1875[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]D_r=\dfrac{e_{max}-e_{50}}{e_{max}-e_{min}}[/tex]

[tex]0.50=\dfrac{0.66-e_{50}}{0.66-0.36}[/tex]

[tex]e_{50}=0.66-0.50(0.66-0.36)[/tex]

[tex]e_{50}=0.51[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]k_{50}=C\dfrac{(e_{50})^3}{1+e_{50}}[/tex]

[tex]k_{50}=0.1875\left(\dfrac{0.51^3}{1+0.51}\right)[/tex]

[tex]k_{50}=0.0165[/tex] cm/sec (ANSWER)

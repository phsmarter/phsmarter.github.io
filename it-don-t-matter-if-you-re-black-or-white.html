15. 4. Which of the following would absorb the most sunlight? A. A white t-shirt B. An orange t-shirtC. A yellow t-shirt D. A black t-shirt ​


Answer:

A black t-shirt would absorb the most sunlight. Darker colors tend to absorb more light than lighter colors, and black is the darkest color. The absorbed light energy is converted into heat energy, which is why black clothing can become much hotter in the sun than lighter colored clothing.

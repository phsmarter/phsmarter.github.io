DEAD STARS SUMMARY

The story of the short story Dead Stars revolves around a man, Alfred Salazar, and his affairs. Alfred Salazar believes in true love and optimism to discover ecstasy in its stir. Esperanza is the first woman he falls in love with.

The families of both of them are acquainted with each other and hence they start a loving relationship. Both get engaged after three years of their relationship. Alfredo is a lawyer who has strong desires and wants warmth and compassion, however, Esperanza is an impassionate woman having strong will and principles. Alfredo’s love for her soon fades away when he meets Julia. Julia, now, becomes a new object of his desire.

Julia Salas is sister in law of the Judge, who is a friend of Alfredo’s father. Julia is an optimistic and enthusiastic person having her own dreams and desires.

When Alfredo comes across her, he is strongly attracted to her. On his visit to her with his father, he engages himself in conversation with her and is attracted to her charm. Even he is so passionate that he doesn’t disclose his engagement to Esperanza.

So as to avoid the discovery of his fiancée, he keeps secrets from Esperanza too. His eyes are doomed when he learns about Julia’s return to his native town. With the fear of losing her, Alfredo decides to declare his true feeling for Julia.

When the Church’s function ends, Alfredo goes to meet her, though his fiancé is waiting for him. When he reaches there, he learns that Julia has already known about his engagement to Esperanza. She wishes him for his marriage and leaves him.

On his return home, he gets a double blow. He finds Esperanza talking to her friend about loyalty and faithfulness. Alfredo senses a desire to communicate. He supports the reason for craving and choice over dishonesty.

Esperanza soon confesses that she knew about his affair with Julia. In pursuit of his lust and heart’s content, she encourages him to cancel the wedding. However, the wedding goes ahead as scheduled and Alfred surrenders to reason.

Near Julia’s native town, Alfred, after eight years, is sent to some work duty. On his visit, he feels nostalgic and cannot resist his lust for Julia and soon finds an excuse to meet her.

Julia is still single that forces Alfred to dream about starting a new life with her; however, he soon realizes that everything is not the same as it were before. Moreover, Julia has also changed lost something.

8. what is the Theme of Dead star?​


The short story "Dead Stars" by Paz Marquez Benitez explores the theme of the fleeting nature of love and the disillusionment that comes with the realization that one's romantic ideals do not always match reality.

The story follows the protagonist, Alfredo Salazar, as he reminisces about his past love affair with a woman named Esperanza. Alfredo initially idolizes Esperanza as the epitome of beauty and femininity, but as he grows older and gains more life experience, he realizes that his feelings for her were based on superficial qualities rather than a genuine connection.

Throughout the story, the theme of the transient nature of love is emphasized as Alfredo reflects on how his feelings for Esperanza have faded over time, and how he has moved on to a new relationship. The story also touches on the theme of societal expectations and the pressure to conform to traditional gender roles and social norms.

In the end, Alfredo comes to the realization that his past ideals of love were unrealistic and that true love must be based on a deeper connection and mutual understanding rather than fleeting infatuation. The story highlights the idea that love, like life itself, is a constantly evolving and changing experience that requires introspection and personal growth to fully understand.

With that, she left and went back to her hometown. For years, Alfredo always thought about Julia and he is convinced that he is still deeply in love with her. For eight years, Alfredo met Julia again. But he found out that his love and attraction for Julia aren't the same as before anymore. The 'Dead Stars' represent a presence that is unrecognized. It speaks of emotions and relationships that may exist but are not realized and lose their real meaning and significance. 19. "Dead Stars" analysis​


Analysis

“Dead stars” by Paz Marquez Benitez is a story about cheating. It emphasizes how men fall into temptation and abandon years of commitment to their significant others for pleasure and lust for another woman that they have just met.

Dead Stars Symbolism

The 'Dead Stars' represent a presence that is unrecognized. It speaks of emotions and relationships that may exist but are not realized and lose their real meaning and significance. In the story, the attraction between Alfredo and Julia is a forbidden and taboo phenomenon.

Main message of the story

The story highlights the futility of chasing momentary gratification over true and gradual emotions. The craving for something forbidden over something moral and legitimate can lead to disastrous results.

Conclusion

Alfonso, finally coming back to his senses, was consumed by guilt and chose his commitment to Esperanza over his intense, but brief affair with Julia. The marriage continued and he never saw Julia again, but some years later, when he visited a place, he saw Julia.

26. ano ang dead stars summary


SUMMARY OF THE STORY "DEAD STARS" BY PAZ MARQUEZ BENITEZ

Ang kwentong Dead Stars ay tungkol kay Alfredo Salazar, isang abogado na ikakasal na sa babaeng nangangalang Esperanza. Tingin sa kanila ng karamihan ay bagay sila sa isa't isa. Sila'y patuloy na nagmamahalan hanggang nakilala ni Alfredo and isa pang babae na nangangalang Julia. Si Alfredo ay nahulog para kay Julia at sa tingin niya ay mas mahal na niya si Julia kaysa sa kinakasama niya ngayon na si Esperanza. Pero, isang araw sinabi ni Julia kay Alfredo na babalik na siya sa kanyang probinsiya at maaring matagal na sila mag kita. Dahil sa takot ni Alfredo na mawala si Julia, inamin niya na ang kanyang totoong nararamdaman niya sa kanya. Subalit, nalaman rin ni Julia ang katotohanan; ikakasal na pala si Alfredo kay Esperanza. Siya'y nasaktan pero sinabihan niya sila na magkaroon ng masayang kasal at pagkatapos nito, siya'y umalis na. Pagkalipas ng ilang taon, palaging iniisip si Julia at 'di niya ito matanggal sa kanyang isip. Naniniwala is Alfredo na mahal niya parin si Julia. Pero, nung sila'y muling nagkita pagkatapos ng ilang taon, napagtanto ni Alfredo na hindi na niya na pala mahal si Julia. Kinompara niya naman ang kanyang pagmamahal kay Julia na matagal nang wala sa isang patay na na bituin o dead star; na ang liwanag ay mananatili parin ng ilang taon at makikita dito sa mundo kahit ito'y matagal nang patay.

3. Identify the good qualities of don francisco mercado as a father


Francisco Engracio Alejandro Mercado, Rizal, also known as Don Francisco Mercado was born in Biñan, Laguna in 1818. He was a Cabeza de barangay and a well-loved and respected man by his constituents. He married Teodora Alonzo and they had eight children, including our national hero, Dr. Jose Rizal.


As a father, he was a good provider. He worked very efficiently as a farmer that her was able to raise his family to be financially comfortable. He also values education very much and he has supported our national hero by sending him to good schools, buying him expensive books and letting him experience things that nurtured his mind and body. His love for his family is very deep and although not much emphasis was given to Don Kiko, Rizal’s love for his father is so big and he considers him a “Model of fathers”.

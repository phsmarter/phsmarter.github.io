Good manners and right conduct are important in all aspects of life. In the workplace, for example, it is important to be professional and to treat others with respect. Treating others with respect is not always easy, but it is always worth the effort. When we show respect for others, we are also showing respect for ourselves. It is a sign of self-respect and maturity.

When we have good manners and right conduct, we are more likely to be respected by others. People are more likely to trust and have confidence in us, and we are more likely to be successful in our relationships and endeavors.

18. "If it is true love that you wait for then, that love will come back at any cost." 1. cite concrete life situations where this lesson can be applied.


Answer:

This lesson can be applied in many situations where people experience separation or distance from their loved ones. For example:

A long-distance relationship where the couple is separated by distance or work, and they are waiting for the right time to be reunited.

A married couple experiencing a rough patch or considering separation. This lesson teaches them to hold on to their love and work to repair their relationship instead of giving up.

A parent who has lost contact with their child due to circumstances beyond their control, such as adoption or custody battles. This lesson encourages them to never give up hope and keep searching for their child.

A friend who has had a falling out with someone they care about. This lesson reminds them to be patient and forgiving, and to wait for the right opportunity to repair their friendship.

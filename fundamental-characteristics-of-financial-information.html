1.a feeling or emotion of respect, awe, fascination, or dread in relation to thesacred.

2.dependence upon a belief system that is usually expressed in the language of myth.

3.is symbolic in relation to its referenceThe self can be described as a ritual being who exhibits a striking parallelbetween their ritual and verbal behavior.

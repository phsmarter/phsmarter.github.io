Answer:

1.Keep hot foods hot [60°C (140°F) or above] and cold foods cold [4°C (40°F) or below].

2.A kitchen is a busy and loud environment that can present a number of risks that could end up harming someone who gets caught in the way. Protective equipment is used in order to limit risks and exposures to injuries or even cut down on major injuries and fatalities.

3.Sharing PPE is not advised. The protective capabilities of single use PPE cannot be assured when it is reused by the same person or used by more than one person. Sharing PPE that is intended for single use may expose another person to infectious materials

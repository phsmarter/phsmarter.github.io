25. uncle Ben spent his vacation in mati city for 155 days. rounded to the nearest tens, about how many days uncle Ben spend his vacation in mati city? ​


Answer:

Explanation:

becaose yes :)

What is 155 rounded to the nearest ten?150

155 rounded to nearest tens: 160
explanation:
• the tens digit is the middle 5 in the number 155.
• remember the rules of rounding off to nearest tens: if the ones digit is below 5 (0,1,2,3,4), retain the number. if the ones digit is 5 and above (5,6,7,8,9), add one to the tens digit and make the ones digit 0.
• in 155, the ones digit is 5, therefore add 1 to tens (5) and make the ones (5) 0.

160

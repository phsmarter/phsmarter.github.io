10. what are the top cyber threats in the Philippines during the pandemic?​


Answer:

The country is facing the COVID-19 pandemic, and amidst all the commotion, cybercriminals may take advantage of the people’s need for updates and guidance on what to do during these times. Let us keep ourselves, and our communities safe from these cyber threats by following these tips:

Be wary of unverified and unproven COVID-19 websites or applications that require you to give your personal data.

These websites and applications might be used by online scammers or might contain viruses, malware, ransomware and other cyber threat intrusions. Cybercriminals will do anything to obtain personal information, especially your financial and banking details. These could come disguised as solicitations or surveys about COVID-19, among others.

Furthermore, unproven apps might give a false sense of security or inaccurately expose you to risk. Keep your data safe and use only trusted websites and applications

Do not open messages or attachments from unknown sources.

Be wary of emails/messages especially those concerning COVID-19. Take time in reviewing the authenticity of the email or message, and do not click on the links or attachments.

Be wary of fake news. Review and confirm information/sources.

Stay tuned in to trusted government and news sites or social media platforms for real and updated information on COVID-19, and do not share unverified information about the situation as not to spread fake news that could incite more panic among the people.

If you have time to spare, help your friends by verifying the information or sources they share. Contact them directly to clarify whatever falsehood they shared and share with them verified facts about the COVID-19.

14. social issue is cyber bullying human sex trafficking and malnutrition why?​


Answer:

There are many reasons why cyberbullying, human sex trafficking, and malnutrition are considered social issues.

Cyberbullying is a social issue because it can have serious negative effects on mental health, self-esteem, and social relationships. It can also lead to anxiety, depression, and even suicide. With the rise of social media and online communication, cyberbullying has become more prevalent and difficult to control.

Human sex trafficking is a social issue because it involves the exploitation and abuse of vulnerable individuals, mostly women and children, for profit. This illegal industry is a violation of human rights and can lead to physical, emotional, and psychological trauma for the victims.

Malnutrition is a social issue because it is a widespread problem in many parts of the world, particularly in developing countries. Malnutrition can lead to stunted growth, cognitive impairment, and weakened immune systems. It is often linked to poverty and lack of access to adequate food and healthcare. Addressing malnutrition requires social and economic interventions to improve access to food, education, and healthcare.

Social issues refer to problems that affect a significant number of people in society. Cyberbullying, human sex trafficking, and malnutrition are all considered social issues because they negatively impact individuals and communities.

Cyberbullying involves using technology to harass, intimidate, or harm others, often through social media platforms. This can lead to emotional distress, depression, and even suicide among victims.

Human sex trafficking involves the illegal trade and exploitation of individuals for sexual purposes, often through force or coercion. This is a violation of human rights and can lead to physical and psychological harm.

Malnutrition occurs when individuals do not have access to or cannot afford nutritious food, leading to health problems and developmental issues. This is a problem that affects millions of people, particularly in developing countries.

Addressing these social issues requires collective efforts from individuals, organizations, and governments to raise awareness, provide resources, and implement solutions to prevent and combat these problems.

The Philippines proudly boasts to be the only Christian nation in Asia. More than 86 percent of the population is Roman Catholic, 6 percent belong to various nationalized Christian cults, and another 2 percent belong to well over 100 Protestant denominations. In addition to the Christian majority, there is a vigorous 4 percent Muslim minority, concentrated on the southern islands of Mindanao, Sulu, and Palawan. 16. as a student how can show that you are proud Filipino proud to be a Filipino 5 minimum​


Answer:

As a student, here are five ways to show that you are proud to be a Filipino:

Participate in Philippine cultural events: Joining events such as the Flores de Mayo, Sinulog, or any cultural events in your region is a great way to showcase your pride in your Filipino heritage.

Learn the national language: The national language of the Philippines is Filipino. Learning to speak and write in Filipino is not only a valuable skill, but it also shows that you have respect for the culture and language of your country.

Display Philippine symbols: Displaying symbols of the Philippines such as the Philippine flag, jeepney models, or traditional Filipino costumes in your room or on your belongings shows your love for your country.

Support Philippine-made products: By supporting locally-made products, you are helping your fellow Filipinos and promoting economic growth. This can also include supporting small businesses, especially those in your community.

Promote Philippine culture and traditions: Share your knowledge of Filipino culture with others. It can be by sharing Filipino songs, dances, food, or anything that can help others understand and appreciate Filipino culture.

By doing these simple things, you can show that you are proud to be a Filipino and help promote the beauty and richness of Filipino culture.

18. How do I know if I can achieve my dream and meet my goal because I can _______?


I know if I can fulfill my dreams and achieve my goals because I can imagine them.

Have you ever realized that your imagination really shapes everything that happens in your life? Imagination can be used in a technique called visualization.

This technique has been taught by all the great masters for centuries. In 1912, Charles F. Hannel, author of The Master Key System, deliberately made a book containing only 2 weeks of written instruction and training for visualization techniques only.

What is visualization?

A visualization technique is a technique involving imagination. In general, the point can be made as follows: if you form an imagination in your head, this is what will happen in your life. If you imagine in your head that you have a stable job, a comfortable home, cute children, a harmonious family, and a comfortable life, that is what is happening in your life.

It doesn't matter what fantasy you have in mind at the time, negative or positive, the same will happen in your reality.

Many people do not know how this power of imagination works. Most people are too busy with various problems at hand. Unknowingly, his imagination continues to work to create more difficulties and bitterness in his life.

But remember that all the imagination you create doesn't have to be in your real life, because there are special ways to apply them. And whatever you are imagining in your head, imagination will not suddenly come into your life right away, because you are living in God's most opportune time and buffer.

In fact, imagination must be accompanied by a sense of belief in the dreams and desires that we imagine will come true one day in our lives.

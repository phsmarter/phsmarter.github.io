1. Essay about how i would like other people to see me​


Answer:

For who I really am on the inside - beyond what they perceive from the outside.

By not judging me through the superficial lenses of gender, status, my job, age, financial status, power or lack of, looks, beliefs, the neighbourhood I live in, the clothes I wear, my struggles, my past, my family or race.

But rather for what is in my heart - my good intentions that seek nothing in return but the good of others.

Seeing “the real me” - my values and my originality.

With eyes that can see the soul

Which never grows old.

That can see an enigma and open book rolled into one conundrum

Which people will choose to take or leave.

Which is okay by me.

As I will never apologise for being real. For caring. For speaking the truth.

For believing in myself without approval or proof.

And neither should you

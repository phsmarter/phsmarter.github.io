Parents, family members, and friends are usually invited to attend the ceremony to share in the students' accomplishments and celebrate their hard work.

18. ESSAYthings to do for us to graduate ​


Answer:

Graduating from high school or college is a momentous occasion, and it requires a lot of hard work and dedication. In order to successfully graduate, there are several things that students must do, including completing coursework, passing exams, fulfilling requirements, and participating in extracurricular activities. Here are some of the key things that students must do to graduate:

Complete all coursework: This is the most fundamental requirement for graduation. Students must attend classes, complete assignments, and earn passing grades in all required courses.

Pass all exams: In addition to completing coursework, students must also pass exams in order to demonstrate their knowledge and mastery of the subject matter. This may include final exams, standardized tests, or other assessments.

Fulfill all requirements: Many schools have specific requirements that students must fulfill in order to graduate. This may include completing a certain number of community service hours, participating in a senior project, or completing a capstone course or project.

Attend graduation rehearsals: In order to ensure that the actual graduation ceremony runs smoothly, students must attend rehearsals to practice walking in their cap and gown, receiving their diploma, and other important aspects of the ceremony.

Participate in extracurricular activities: While not strictly required for graduation, participating in extracurricular activities can help students build skills, gain experience, and develop a well-rounded profile for college or job interviews.

Overall, graduating from high school or college requires a lot of hard work, dedication, and perseverance. By completing coursework, passing exams, fulfilling requirements, attending rehearsals, and participating in extracurricular activities, students can set themselves up for success and achieve their goals of graduation.

5. make a reflection, of what you have learn about purposive communication (essay)​500 words​


Answer:

Purposive communication is a type of communication that is intentional and goal-oriented. It involves the use of language and other forms of communication to achieve a specific objective or purpose. In my studies of purposive communication, I have learned about the importance of effective communication in achieving personal and professional goals.

One of the key things I have learned about purposive communication is that it requires careful planning and preparation. Before engaging in purposive communication, it is important to identify the objectives and goals that you want to achieve. This involves understanding the audience that you are communicating with, their needs, and their expectations. By doing so, you can tailor your message to meet their specific needs and ensure that your communication is effective.

Another important aspect of purposive communication is the use of clear and concise language. Effective communication requires that you use language that is easy to understand and that conveys your message clearly. This means avoiding jargon or technical terms that may not be familiar to your audience. By using simple and straightforward language, you can ensure that your message is understood and that your communication is effective.

I have also learned that purposive communication requires active listening. This means paying attention to the needs and concerns of your audience and responding appropriately. Effective communication requires that you listen to feedback and adjust your message accordingly. By doing so, you can ensure that your communication is relevant and meets the needs of your audience.

In addition to these key principles, I have also learned about the importance of nonverbal communication in purposive communication. Nonverbal communication includes things like body language, facial expressions, and tone of voice. These nonverbal cues can convey important information about your message and can help to reinforce your message.

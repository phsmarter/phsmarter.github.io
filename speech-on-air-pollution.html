Natural resources include substances present in nature, like wind, sunlight, forests, soil, fossil fuels, coal, petroleum, etc. natural resources can be further differentiated into two categories, namely renewable and non-renewable sources.

As the name suggests, renewable resources are the naturally occurring substances that can be replenished, like sunlight, forests, wind and water. These sources can be used to harness energy. Non-renewable resources are commodities that cannot be replenished once they are exhausted, like coal, petroleum.

Hence it is crucial to talk about the depletion of natural resources at a time like this since we need to look for alternate fuels.

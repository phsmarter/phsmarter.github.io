28. trivia about wrinkles​


Answer:

Wrinkles, a natural part of aging, are most prominent on sun-exposed skin, such as the face, neck, hands and forearms. Although genetics mainly determine skin structure and texture, sun exposure is a major cause of wrinkles, especially for people with light skin. Pollutants and smoking also contribute to wrinkling.

30. Why does the skin of a) a newly born baby wrinkled; b) an aged person wrinkled?


Answer:

a.Baby swims in amniotic fluid for 40 weeks. It's this coating that protects an unborn baby's skin from the fluid. Without this protection, a baby's skin would chap or wrinkle in the womb

b.As you get older, your skin naturally becomes less elastic and more fragile. Decreased production of natural oils dries your skin and makes it appear more wrinkled

However, the massive influx of tourists has also had a significant impact on the environment. The increase in tourism has led to overcrowding and pollution, with litter and waste scattered along the beaches and in the water. This, in turn, has led to a decline in the health of the marine ecosystem, affecting the flora and fauna that call the beaches home.

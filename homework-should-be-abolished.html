6. final exam should be abolished(possible question for debate)​


Answer:

The proposition that final exams should be abolished is a possible topic for a debate. Some possible arguments for this proposition could include:

Final exams are a stressful and high-stakes way of evaluating student learning, which can lead to anxiety and other negative mental health effects.Final exams often do not accurately reflect the full range of a student's knowledge and skills, and can lead to unfair or inaccurate grading.There are other forms of assessment, such as projects, papers, and presentations, that can provide a more comprehensive and authentic evaluation of student learning.Abolishing final exams could free up valuable time and resources that could be used for other forms of learning and assessment.

Some possible arguments against this proposition could include:

Final exams provide a consistent and standardized way of evaluating student learning, which allows for fair and accurate grading.

Final exams can serve as a useful review and synthesis of the material learned over the course of a semester or academic year.

Final exams can help students develop important skills, such as time management, organization, and study habits.

Abolishing final exams could result in a lack of accountability and motivation for students to consistently engage with their coursework throughout the semester.

Digital technology makes children's lives better.

Digital technology has had a profound impact on children's lives by providing them with access to a wealth of educational, recreational, and communication opportunities. It has enabled them to explore and discover new things, while also giving them a platform to express themselves and connect with others. From online learning resources to interactive games, digital technology has made it easier than ever for children to learn, create, and engage with the world around them. In short, digital technology has made children's lives better by providing them with the tools to stay educated and connected in a quickly changing world.

Books are better than television.

Books are far superior to television when it comes to providing educational and entertainment value. Not only do books offer the opportunity to engage with complex storylines and characters, but they also allow the reader to challenge their critical thinking skills and expand their knowledge base. In contrast, television simply offers passive entertainment with minimal educational or thought-provoking content. Therefore, books are undeniably the better choice for readers seeking to truly engage with the stories they consume.

School uniforms should be abolished.

School uniforms should be abolished because they limit students' individual expression, create an unnecessary financial burden on families, and do not influence academic performance. Requiring students to wear uniforms goes against the very core of our educational system, which is to nurture independent thought and creativity. Furthermore, the cost of the uniforms can be a significant financial burden on lower-income families, who may be forced to make difficult choices between buying uniforms and other basic necessities. Finally, there is no evidence to suggest that requiring uniforms in schools leads to any improvement in academic performance.

Homework should be banned.

Homework should be banned because it takes away valuable time from students to pursue their interests, practice sports, and spend time with family. Furthermore, research has shown that homework does not necessarily lead to improved academic performance, and it can cause unnecessary stress and anxiety in students. Therefore, it is in the best interest of students to eliminate homework altogether.

We should all be vegetarian.

We should all be vegetarian because it is a more sustainable and healthier dietary choice than eating meat. Not only does it reduce the environmental impact of animal agriculture, but it also reduces the risk of certain diseases and helps to limit the spread of antibiotic-resistant bacteria. Furthermore, plant-based diets are low in saturated fat and cholesterol, making them an excellent choice for those looking to improve their overall health.

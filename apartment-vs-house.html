a pure substance consists only of one element or one compound. a mixture consists of two or more different substances, not chemically joined together.

PURE SUBSTANCE

substance that has a fixed chemical composition throughout is called a pure substance such as water, air, and nitrogen. A pure substance does not have to be of a single element or compound.

MIXTURE

mixture is a material system made up of two or more different substances, which are mixed but not combined chemically. A mixture refers to the physical combination of two or more substances in which the identities of the individual substances are retained.

6. in a part of exciting village, the ratio of private home to apartment house dwellings is 5:3. if all the apartment house dwellings are made of cement and 1/10 of the private homes are wooden structures, what is the maximum portion of houses that may be made of cement?


Answer:

15/16

Step-by-step explanation:

Private Home = 5/8 of all houses

Apt. House Dwellings = 3/8 of all houses

Wooden Portion = 1/10 of the Private Homes = 1/16

The maximum cement portion can be found by assuming that all the other houses are made of cement.

1 - 1/16 = 15/16

Therfore, 15/16 is the maximum portion of houses that may be made of cement.

Explanation:

11. Why important to have commitment in a community action?​.


1. Sense of responsibility: When community members are committed to a cause, they feel a sense of responsibility to contribute their time and resources to make a positive impact. This can lead to increased participation and involvement, which can make the action more effective.

2. Sustainability: Commitment helps to ensure that a community action continues to have support and resources over the long term. Without commitment, it may be difficult to sustain the action and maintain its momentum.

3. Collaboration: Commitment can foster collaboration and cooperation among community members, as they work together towards a common goal. This can help to build stronger, more cohesive communities.

4. Positive change: By working together and committing to a cause, community members can effect real and lasting change in their neighborhoods and beyond. This can lead to improved quality of life and a sense of pride and accomplishment.

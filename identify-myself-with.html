What is the meaning of this question

Over this week I have gained some insight into being a stronger communicator. Communication is affected by our past experiences. There is an old saying, “you can’t judge a book by its cover.” This quote tells us that we cannot always go off of first impressions. 19. Twice myself is 20 more than myself. What am I?


Let x be the number.
2x = 20 + x
2x - x = 20
x = 20
So the answer is 20.ANSWER:

20 years old

SOLUTION:

[tex]2x = 20 + x \\ 2x - x = 20 + x - x \\ 2x - x = 20 \\ 2x - x \\ 2x - 1x \\ (2 - 1) \\ 1x \\ x \\ x = 20[/tex]

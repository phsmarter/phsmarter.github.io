3. Recent technological, social, and cultural changes have influenced Philippine literature in the 21st century by providing new platforms for publication and distribution, as well as shaping the themes and styles of writers.

5. 21st century Philippine literature challenges traditional notions of gender, sexuality, and relationships by exploring themes such as queer identity and non-traditional family structures.

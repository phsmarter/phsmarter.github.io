1a. I agree with the statement that compensation or wages are based on the qualifications set by the management and also based on their performance in doing their tasks. This is because companies set standards and qualifications for employees in order to ensure that they have the necessary skills and knowledge to perform their jobs effectively. Both compensation and non-financial compensation are important from the perspective of an employee. While compensation is important for providing financial security, non-financial compensation such as job satisfaction, work-life balance, and opportunities for career growth can also motivate employees to perform better. A good balance between the two can lead to a more satisfied and motivated workforce.

11. 1. Agree or disagree with the following statements then discuss: a. Compensation or wages are based on the qualifications set by the management and also based on their performance in doing their tasks. b. Compensation pay represents a reward that an employee receives from an employer for his/her good performance in the company. 2. Is compensation a motivational factor for an employee? Defend your answer. 3. Which is important on the perspective view of an employee: Compensation or non-financial compensation? Discuss your answer. (Your Own ANSWER


Answer:

1a. I agree that compensation or wages should be based on the qualifications set by the management and the employee's performance in doing their tasks. It is important for companies to have a standard in determining an employee's salary, and qualifications and performance are good indicators of an employee's value to the company.

1b. I agree that compensation pay represents a reward that an employee receives from an employer for their good performance in the company. It is important for employees to be recognized and rewarded for their hard work, as it can motivate them to continue performing well and contributing to the company's success.

3. Both compensation and non-financial compensation are important on the perspective view of an employee. A good salary and benefits can provide financial security and stability, while non-financial compensation such as recognition, opportunities for growth, and a positive work environment can contribute to an employee's overall job satisfaction and motivation. It is important for companies to consider both financial and non-financial compensation when designing their employee compensation and benefits packages, as they both play a significant role in attracting and retaining talent.

18. how would you describe mcdonald's as a company when it comes to compensation for performance evaluation?​


Answer:

McDonald’s Company Performance Measurement

Words: 4187 Pages: 18

Introduction

Performance management is an essential tool when it comes to the evaluation of the ability of an organisation to remain in business in the short and long term. According to Beechler and Woodward, profit-making organisations deploy profitability levels as measures of their performance (275). In this extent, profitability encompasses a desired organisational output. Performance measurement entails “three specific areas of a firm’s outcomes: financial performance (profits, return on assets and return on investment); product market performance (sales and market share); and shareholder return (total shareholder return and economic value added” (Devinney and Yip 529). The standard approach of measuring performance involves setting targets for the desired outputs and then measuring the actual outputs against the targets.

21. ano pinagkaiba ng income, salary, wage, compensation??​


Answer:

Definition of Wages

Wages are usually associated with employee compensation that is based on the number of hours worked multiplied by an hourly rate of pay. Generally, the employees earning hourly wages will be paid in the week that follows the hours worked.

Definition of Salary

Salary is associated with employee compensation quoted on an annual basis, such as $50,000 per year. Many employees working in a company's general office will be paid a salary. Often the salaries are paid semi-monthly. That is, one pay date will be the 15th day of the month for working from the 1st to the 15th, and the other pay date will be the last day of the month for working from the 16th to the last day of the month.

MOTIVATING A FRIEND

It is true that losing loved one is one of the saddest part in a persons lives. We know that we cannot change the situation and to make our friend feel better but we can do something little by means of giving our time to them.  We need  give our precious time to our friend and we need to give them comforting words and a word of God. We also need to offer hope and a positive outlook toward the future. We need to show them show even a  small gestures like sending a card or flowers, delivering a meal, helping out with laundry or shopping, or making a regular date to listen and offer support. This can be a huge source of comfort to a person who is grieving and can help them to motivate that the life still goes on through the help of God.

3. why did you choose this topic in your research paper? title: The advantage of social to the students​


"The advantage of social media to students."

One reason could be that social media is an integral part of the lives of many young people, including students. Therefore, it is important to study its impact on their well-being, academic performance, and social development. A research paper on this topic could examine the positive aspects of social media use for students, such as the ability to connect with peers and professionals in their field of study, access to educational resources, and opportunities for collaboration and learning.

Another reason could be to explore the potential downsides of social media use for students, such as distraction from academic work, exposure to cyberbullying and other negative online behaviors, and negative impacts on mental health. A research paper on this topic could examine the potential harms of social media use for students and provide recommendations for responsible social media use.

Overall, a research paper on the advantages and disadvantages of social media use for students could help inform policymakers, educators, and parents on how to support young people in using social media in a safe and responsible manner, while also maximizing the potential benefits that it can offer.

PASIG CITY, March 20, 2020– As the entire Luzon and other parts of the country remain under enhanced community quarantine due to the COVID-19 outbreak, the Department of Education (DepEd) launched DepEd Commons, an online platform for public school teachers to support distance learning modalities.

Since its inception last Wednesday, March 17, a total of 151,749 teachers have joined the said online platform, gaining support and praise from teachers as well as parents.

“As a teacher po nila, I can say na napakalaking tulong po sa mga bata nito lalo na po na nakahome quarantine. We appreciate the efforts of Information and Communications Technology Service and Undersecretary Pascua for making this platform available in these trying times,” Briones said.

16. novelty of the research about the advantage and disadvantage of social media to the student in senior high school to the performance of the academic performance senior high school ​


The novelty of the research topic is that it focuses specifically on the impact of social media on the academic performance of senior high school students. While there have been many studies conducted on the general effects of social media on students, this research aims to delve deeper into how it specifically affects senior high school students.

The research problem of this topic could be:

1. What are the advantages and disadvantages of using social media among senior high school students in relation to their academic performance?

2. How does social media use affect the study habits and time management skills of senior high school students?

3. What are the factors that contribute to the positive and negative effects of social media on the academic performance of senior high school students?

The chosen research problem is important because it addresses a relevant issue among senior high school students, who are at a critical stage in their education. The results of the research could provide insights to educators and parents on how to properly regulate the use of social media to maximize its advantages and minimize its disadvantages in relation to academic performance.

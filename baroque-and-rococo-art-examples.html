Rococo, or Late Baroque, is an artistic style that developed in 18th-century in Paris in reaction to the grandeur and strict regulations of Baroque. ... Rococo, therefore, denotes the refined and fanciful style that became fashionable in Europe in the 18th century after the religious and severe style of Baroque.

12. what are the sunset of W? ​


Answer:

Sunset, also known as sundown, is the daily disappearance of the Sun below the horizon due to Earth's rotation. As viewed from the Equator, the equinox Sun sets exactly due west in both Spring and Autumn. As viewed from the middle latitudes, the local summer The sun sets to the southwest for the Northern Hemisphere, and to the northwest for the Southern Hemisphere.The time of sunset is defined in astronomy as the moment when the upper limb of the Sun disappears below the horizon. Near the horizon, atmospheric refraction causes sunlight rays to be distorted to such an extent that geometrically the solar disk is already about one diameter below the horizon when a sunset is observed.

Sunset is distinct from twilight, which is divided into three stages, the first being civil twilight, which begins once the Sun has disappeared below the horizon, and continues until it descends to 6 degrees below the horizon; the second phase is nautical twilight, between 6 and 12 degrees below the horizon; and the third is astronomical twilight, which is the period when the Sun is between 12 and 18 degrees below the horizon.[1] Dusk is at the very end of astronomical twilight, and is the darkest moment of twilight just before night.[2] Night occurs when the Sun reaches 18 degrees below the horizon and no longer illuminates the sky.

Locations further North than the Arctic Circle and further South than the Antarctic Circle experience no full sunset or sunrise on at least one day of the year, when the polar day or the polar night persists continuously for 24 hours.


13. Why there is red sunset​


Answer:

At sunset the light from the sun grazes across the surface of the earth, passing through a long column or pathlength of atmosphere. During its passage through the atmosphere the blue components of the light are reduced in intensity, making the transmitted beam of sunlight more yellow or even red in colour

27. Kahulugan ng outsourcing,near shoring, off shoring at on shoring


Answer:

Outsourcing, Near shoring, Off-shoring at On-shoring

Ito ay mga terminong gamit sa negosyo, kung saan tinutukoy nito kung saan nangagaling ang manpower ng isang kumpanya. Isa isahin natin ang bawat termino sa mga sumusnod:

Outsourcing:

Ito ay ang pagkuha ng mga mangagawa ng isang kumpanya sa ibang lokasyon kung saan mura ang labor ngunit may kalidad ang gawa. Ito ay gngawa upang makatipid sa halaga ng pasweldo ng mga mangagawa. Madalas ang mga kumpanya na may ganitong business model ay mga kumpanya mula sa Europe at America at ang mga bansang pinagkukunan ng manpower ay China, India, Philippines, Indonesia, Guatemala, Brazil

_

Ang mga sumusunod ay klase ng outsourcing:

Near shoring:

Ito ay pagkuha ng mga mangagawa sa karatig na bansa kung saan ang time difference ay kaunit lamang at ang minimum income ng bansang pinagkunan ay hindi rin gaanong mataas.

Ito ay ideal sa mga kumpanya na kritikal ang palagiang komunikasyon, dahil kakaunti lamang ang time difference.

Off-shoring

Ito ay pagkuha ng mga mangagawa sa mas malayong bansa ngunit higit na mas mura ang minimimum income at way of living. Hindi ito ideal sa mga kumpanya na kritikal ang komuniksayon at pagpupulong dahil na din sa malaking agwat ng oras ng mga bansa.

On-shoring

Ito ay pagkuha ng mga mangagawa sa parehong bansa (gaya ng probinsya)  ngunit higit na mas mura ang way of living. Ito ay kadalasan business model ng mga pabrika kung saan ang main office ay nasa siyudad at ang gawaan o pasilidad ay nasa probinsya kung saan mas mura ang raw materials at labor.

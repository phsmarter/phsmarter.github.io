1. How many students are considered themselves that they are computer addicts?

2. What are the software or programs, specifically, names of the software that these respondents are or subjects are playing?

3. To determine the reasons of these subjects or respondents why they are addicted to these software.

Statement of the problem is a part of the research that would determine different problems or questions that is needed for the study to address.

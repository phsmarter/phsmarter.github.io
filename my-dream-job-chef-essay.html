9. What are the character traits of a good chef?Essay:​


Answer:

Passion

"Passion was the first of my 'core values.' Passion is the reason we do what we do...it's what drives us every day. It's a funny thing, because people can tell when you're not being genuine...they know when you are not passionate. It shows. If you love what you do, you put everything into it."

Harmony

"In the restaurant business, everyone has to work together. There needs to be harmony in your team. You may not like someone, you may not hangout with them outside of the kitchen, but we all have to work together to get the job done. Also, harmony in the literal sense--meaning the harmony of food and wine, the harmonious flow of the restaurant, etc."

Humility

"We don't take ourselves too seriously. I want my team to be humble--to appreciate and listen to others. We work as a team at all of my restaurants, and I want people to accept they don't know everything. It's okay to mess up, as long as you learn from it. I also want my team to know that everyone is important, no matter who you are. The dishwasher is just as important as the executive chef--everyone plays a role in how the restaurant runs. Without one person, it doesn't run right."

Imagination

"We like to have fun! We don't take things too seriously, and we try to promote creativity and innovation. We want our chefs to color outside the lines and have a good time. That's where some of our best ideas come from.

Caring

"This is so important--not just for the restaurant, but for life. You need to care for yourself, care for your team, care for your guests, care for your community. If you don't care, why should anyone else?"

Ownership

"Ownership is the latest addition to TAG Restaurant Group's core values. Ownership means to take ownership and to do your job--not just for yourself, but for the company as a whole. We're all working towards the same end goal."

17. Make a Persuasive Speech about YOUR Dream Job as a CHEF ( With Connection And YOUR OWN SPEECH ONLY)


Answer:

Ladies and gentlemen,

I stand before you today to talk about my dream job as a Chef. Growing up, I was always fascinated by the idea of creating new dishes, exploring unique ingredients, and experimenting with flavors. The joy of seeing people relish the food that I had carefully prepared was unmatched. It is this passion and love for food that has driven me to pursue a career as a Chef.

The role of a Chef is not just about cooking food, it is about creating an entire experience for the diner. It requires not just culinary knowledge and skills, but also an artistic flair that can turn an ordinary meal into a work of art. As a Chef, I would have the opportunity to work with a plethora of ingredients, textures, and techniques - combining them to create beautiful and delicious dishes that would tantalize the senses.

My dream job as a Chef would allow me to work in a dynamic and fast-paced environment, where every day is different and unique. It would require hard work, dedication, and attention to detail, but the satisfaction that comes from seeing the smiles on people's faces would make it all worth it. I would also get to work alongside some of the most creative and talented individuals in the industry, constantly learning and growing as a professional.

But beyond just the technical aspects of the job, being a Chef would allow me to make a difference in people's lives. Food brings people together, and it is an essential part of our culture and traditions. As a Chef, I would have the ability to create meals that bring joy, comfort, and happiness to those around me. Whether it is through a formal dining experience or a casual gathering of friends and family, food has the power to connect us all.

In conclusion, being a Chef is not just a job, it is a passion - a passion for creating culinary experiences that bring people together and celebrate the joys of life. My dream job as a Chef would not only allow me to pursue my love for food, but also make a positive impact in the world around me. I am committed to working hard, honing my skills, and fulfilling my dream of becoming a Chef. Thank you.

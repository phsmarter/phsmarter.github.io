2. Lesson 4 Dealing with personal challenges impressive photo essay


Photo Essay (Dealing with Personal Struggles)

The struggle to fit in to society’s norms and standards is like a weed in a wheat field. It is an outcast, it doesn’t belong. When a farmer sees the weed, he doesn’t recognize it as part of the harvest. Instead, it is plucked out away from the rest. But the thing about a weed is that it never fails to sprout again and again. This will point out that a weed was created by God not to do harm but it has a certain purpose in the food chain.

We all have been wonderfully made despite all the personal struggles that we go through. You may be a weed or you can be the wheat and yet you can rely on the fact that even weeds have its purpose given the right place. Just like the wheat. If you will ask the wheat, it has its own struggles, too. You and I may look different, but we all go through our own struggles. It is not about being different but overcoming your weakness by looking into where you do best.

15. Write a 10 sentence essay about your personal challenges in life and the challenges of your chosen character in the story "Les Miserables"​


Answer:

In life, we all face personal challenges that can be difficult to overcome. For me, one of the biggest challenges I've had to face is anxiety. It's something that I've struggled with for years and it's something that can be really tough to deal with on a daily basis. However, I'm slowly learning how to manage my anxiety and I'm hopeful that I'll be able to overcome it completely one day.

The character of Jean Valjean in "Les Miserables" also faces some difficult challenges. He's been imprisoned for 19 years for stealing a loaf of bread and, when he's finally released, he finds it very difficult to start a new life. He's constantly pursued by the police inspector Javert and he's also struggling to overcome his own personal demons. However, Valjean is a strong character and he eventually manages to overcome all of the challenges he faces.

17. example of photo essay in tagalog


Photo Essay (Sanaysay ng Larawan) – Isang koleksiyon ng mga imahe na inilagay

sa isang partikular na pagkakasunod-sunod upang ipahayag ang mga pangyayari,

mga damdamin, at mga konsepto sa pinakapayak na paraan.

Mga Dapat Tandaan sa Pagbuo ng Photo Essay:

1. Maghanap ng isang paksa na ayon sa iyong interes.

2. Magsagawa ng pananaliksik bago isagawa ang photo essay.

3. Hanapin ang “tunay na kuwento.” Matapos ang pananaliksik, maaari munang

matukoy ang anggulo na gusto mong dalhin ang iyong kuwento kahit na ang

bawat ideya ng kuwento ay pareho. Ang pangunahing mga dahilan ng bawat

larawan ay nararapat na lumikha ng isang kapani-paniwala at natatanging

kuwento.

4. Ang kuwento ay binuo upang gisingin ang damdamin ng mambabasa.

Pinakamahusay na paraan upang ikonekta ang iyong sanaysay larawan sa

madla ay ang mga damdamin nakapaloob sa kuwento at gamitin ito sa mga

larawan.

5. Pagpasyahan ang mga kukunang larawan. Magsimula sa paglikha ng isang

listahan ng mga kuha para sa kuwento. Ang bawat “shot” ay tulad ng isang

pangungusap sa isang kuwento sa isang talata. Maaari kang magsimula sa 10 “shots.” Ang bawat “shot” ay dapat bigyang-diin ang iba’t ibang mga konsepto o

emosyon na maaaring pinagtagpi kasama ng iba pang mga larawan.

Death Penalty Pros

- It deters criminals from committing serious crimes. Common sense tells us that the most frightening thing for a human being is to lose their life; therefore, the death penalty is the best deterrent when it comes to discouraging people from carrying out the worst crimes.

-It is quick, painless, and humane. The methods of execution have gradually become more humane over the years, so the argument that the death penalty is cruel and unusual is not valid.

-The legal system constantly evolves to maximize justice. Just because it is feasible that a wrong decision could be made by the legal system doesn’t mean that the death penalty is wrong. Every effort is made in the US to give death row prisoners opportunities to challenge the court's decisions. Modern methods of crime detection, such as DNA testing, also give greater certainty of guilt than existed in the past.

-It appeases the victims or victims' families. The death penalty can provide families of victims with some closure, which may help them to deal with their suffering.

-Without the death penalty, some criminals would continue to commit crimes. It deters prisoners who are already serving life sentences in jail from committing more serious offenses.

-It is a cost-effective solution. The idea put forward by abolitionists that it costs more to execute someone than imprison them for life is simply not true, and there is plenty of evidence to show this.

Ang hiwalayan sa Pilipinas ay hindi lamang isang problema, bagkus, isang kompleks na panjamnam na isyu. Sa kasalukuyan, ang Pilipinas ay isa sa mga bansa na may pinaka-mababang rate ng hiwalayan sa buong mundo, na 1.7 porsyento, na ipinapakita na ang nakatatandaang tradisyon ng pag-iibigan ay napakatibay. Gayunpaman, sa nakaraan, ang Pilipinas ay nakakuha ng pagtulong mula sa international na organisasyon upang pakisamahan ang isang tuntunin na nagbibigay ng pagkakataon sa halos anuman na mag-asawa upang itigil ang kanilang mga relasyon sa pamamagitan ng pagsasalin. Ito ay isang pag-unlad sa pananaw ng Batas sa Family Code 1988, na una ay labag sa mga konsepto ng hiwalayan, at nagbigay ng pagkakataon para sa mag-asawa na hiwalayan.

Ngunit ang paglabas ng hiwalay na mga tuntunin sa Pilipinas ay nagbibigay din ng maraming bagong problema. Una, ito ay binabago ang tatag panlabas ng institusyon ng pamilya sa lipunan. Sa pagdaan ng panahon, may mas maraming patong na ibinibigay para sa mga mag-asawa upang hiwalayan, na maaaring magresulta sa paglaganap ng kultura ng pag-iwas sa responsibilidad sa komunikadong pagsisiyasat at pagsasagawa ng maayos na relasyon.

Ikalawa, ang paglabas ng hiwalay na mga tuntunin ay magdudulot din ng mas maraming mga kaso ng patong sa pagitan ng mag-asawa, na maaaring makaapekto sa pisikal, emosyonal, at ekonomikong kalusugan ng mga mag-asawa at kanilang mga anak. Ang mga Pilipino ay hindi nagpapakita ng pag-unawa sa responsibilidad ng mag-asawa upang mabawasan ang mga ganitong uri ng patong at magsagawa ng maayos upang magkaroon ng isang pagkakaibigan at matagal na relasyon.

Kahit na tinutugunan ng hiwalay na tuntunin ang problema ng matagal na patong sa pagitan ng mga mag-asawa, dapat suriin pa rin ito ng mas malalim at isama ang mga teorya ng relasyon at komunikasyon upang mapalakas ang relasyones sa pagitan ng mag-asawa. Gayunpaman, ang Pilipinas ay hindi pa nakakamit ang isang kalagayan kung saan ang hiwalayan ay naiintindihan at naiiba subalit naiinlab, na nagpapakita na ang solusyon sa patong ay hindi na lamang ang isang simpleng pagpapa-divorce. 27. pros and cons for debate "smoking should be banned on public spaces"​


Answer:

Explanation:

Pros:

Public health: Smoking in public spaces exposes non-smokers to harmful secondhand smoke, which can lead to numerous health problems, including lung cancer, heart disease, and asthma. A ban on smoking in public spaces would help protect non-smokers from the harmful effects of secondhand smoke.

Reduced fire risk: Cigarettes are a common cause of fires in public spaces. Banning smoking in public spaces would reduce the risk of fires caused by discarded cigarettes.

Reduced litter: Smoking in public spaces often leads to litter in the form of cigarette butts. A ban on smoking in public spaces would reduce the amount of litter and help keep public spaces clean.

Cons:

Individual freedom: A ban on smoking in public spaces could be seen as an infringement on individual freedom. People may argue that they have the right to smoke in public spaces, and a ban would be an unnecessary restriction on their personal choices.

Economic impact: Banning smoking in public spaces could have a negative economic impact on businesses such as bars, restaurants, and casinos, which depend on smokers as customers. These businesses may lose revenue if smokers are not allowed to smoke on their premises.

Enforcement issues: A ban on smoking in public spaces would be difficult to enforce, as smokers may ignore the ban or find ways to circumvent it. This could lead to conflict between smokers and law enforcement, and could be a burden on public resources.

Overall, while a ban on smoking in public spaces would have positive effects on public health, fire risk, and litter, it could also be seen as an infringement on individual freedom and have negative economic and enforcement impacts. The debate on whether or not smoking should be banned in public spaces requires careful consideration of these factors.

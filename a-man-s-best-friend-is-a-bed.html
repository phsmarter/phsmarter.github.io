Answer

B. My pillow is a faithful and a comfortable friend.

OR

A. My pillow is a faithful friend that welcomes with comfort.

*Both correctly compares the pillow(thing) to a friend(human) but a prefer B because it describes the similarities between a pillow and friend much better.

Explanation

First, let's understand what a metaphor is. A metaphor is a figure of speech that compares two different things that have something in common.

Thus, in the example, the pillow is described as a friend who is faithful and comfortable to be with. The metaphor is the pillow being compared to a friend, a thing compared to a human.

20. a man wants to encloses circular flower bed with fencing material the flower bed has a radius of 12 dm how much does he need?​


Answer:

The formula for the circumference of a circle is:

Circumference = 2 x π x radius

where π (pi) is a mathematical constant approximately equal to 3.14.

Substituting the given value of radius in the formula, we get:

Circumference = 2 x 3.14 x 12 dm

Circumference = 75.36 dm

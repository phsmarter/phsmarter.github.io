27. tips on how to protect humans health against gadgets​


Answer:

1. Limit your exposure to radiation from electronic devices: Keep your cell phone away from your body when it is in use, and avoid carrying it in a pocket or on a belt. Use the speakerphone or a headset when talking on the phone for long periods of time.

2. Take regular breaks from electronic devices: Make sure to take regular breaks from using electronic devices, such as computers and phones, to give your eyes and body a rest.

3. Avoid blue light exposure at night: Blue light emitted by screens can disrupt sleep patterns, so try to limit screen time before bedtime or use blue-light filtering glasses if you must be on an electronic device late at night.

4. Practice good posture while using electronics: Poor posture can lead to neck and back pain, so make sure you are sitting up straight with your feet flat on the floor while using any type of electronic device for extended periods of time.

5. Clean regularly: Regularly clean all surfaces that come into contact with electronics with disinfectant wipes or sprays to reduce the risk of germs spreading between people who use them frequently

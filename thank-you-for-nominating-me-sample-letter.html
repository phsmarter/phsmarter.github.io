16. Give me an example of nominative case


1) As Subject The merchant purched used a large diamond 2) Subject Complement Rodrigo Duterte is our president SC 3) Appositive Blair, my niece , is in GRADE 1 (niece is the subject) 4) Noun of exclamation Wait! Mr Jolentino (Wait subject) 5) Direct Address Kenneth, go home now (Kenneth subject) The nominative case is the case used for a noun or pronoun which is the subject of a verb.

For example:

> Mark eats cakes.
(The noun Mark is the subject of the verb eats. Mark is in the nominative case.)

> He eats cakes.
(The pronoun He is the subject of the verb eats. He is in the nominative case.)

> They eat cakes.
(The pronoun They is the subject of the verb eats. They is in the nominative case.)

The national government is providing a wage subsidy for affected employees of small businesses to help mitigate the impact of the quarantine in Luzon and various local government units.

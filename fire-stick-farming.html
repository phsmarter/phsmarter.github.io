Answer: Conflict in a short story is the problem that the protagonist must solve. This causes the rising action or the part of the story where the protagonist does something that makes the conflict or problem be resolved. This is paired with the falling action.

Types of conflict are person vs. person, which is a character against another character, person vs. society, which is a character against a society or organization of people, person vs. nature, which is when a character has to fight natural forces, and lastly, person vs. self. This is the conflict in which a character is battling his/herself.

17. using the four types of sentences, write a narrative essay about your interests. tell a story about how you discovered a particular interest and how it influences your everyday life. ​


Answer:

My New-found Interest

A few years ago, I have discovered that I like reading books, especially ones that are more obscure and unique. I discovered this when I visited our school library for some reference material. I do not usually go to the library, as almost all information that I need can already be found on the internet. But during that time, I needed to have a hardcopy reference. Therefore, I spent some time in the library. Who knew that it could actually lead it to my love for reading? I did not realize yet that time it was actually the beginning.

The Magical Moment

I remember that I was looking for a particular science book for my reference. It was crunch time already with my report, so I must find that science reference. However, while I was looking, I came across this old book about a young girl and her desire to have a swimming pool in their backyard. The book cover was nondescript, just an image of a young girl in pig tails. It was hard bound with olive green color and smelled really old and musty. I am not sure why I started reading it, but I actually did! And I was so excited about it! Eventually I was able to find my science reference and borrowed my new-found book together with it.

My Future Career?

Until today I kept asking myself, why did I ever get interested to open that book in the library? Was there some magical moment or some divine intervention leading me to that book? But in any case, that incident got me started into reading. After that, I would visit the library to look for other similar books. Furthermore, it developed in me an inclination to look online for books with good reviews. And I get to spend many days in bookstores in malls. There are bookstores that allow people to stay inside, lounge in the store, and read some books. I like doing that in some of my free time. Also, I have this feeling that I must at least read one physical book for every five online books I read. I also started following online writing platforms and fanfiction websites. I needed to see which ones are new releases and if they are actually good. Nowadays I am getting interested in pursuing creative writing. I want to learn how to be a creative writer. Who knows! One day that might be a career I am willing to take in the future.

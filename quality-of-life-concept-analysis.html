RESEARCH

Research is a process of systematic inquiry that comprises the acquisition of data, the documentation of essential information, and the analysis and interpretation of that data/information in accordance with procedures established by particular professional fields and academic disciplines.

Research is the driving force behind human progress. It is driven by curiosity: become inquisitive, ask questions, and immerse ourselves in learning everything there is to know. Education is booming. Without curiosity and investigation, progress would stall, and our lives as we know them would be drastically altered.

The devices or instruments used to collect data, such as a paper questionnaire or computer-assisted interviewing system, are referred to as data collection or research tools. They are employed to measure a variable or collect the data necessary to address a research topic.

Commonly, quality research refers to the scientific process comprising all parts of the study design. In particular, it relates to the evaluation of the compatibility between techniques and questions, selection of participants, measurement of outcomes, and protection.

23. meaning of concave mirror​


Answer:

A concave mirror, or converging mirror, has a reflecting surface that is recessed inward (away from the incident light). Concave mirrors reflect light inward to one focal point. They are used to focus light.


24. a man 1.30m tall stands 5.20m from a concave mirror. at the image could be formed on a screen 15.0cm.what is the focal length of the mirror​


Answer:

To find the focal length of a concave mirror, we can use the formula 1/f = 1/do + 1/di, where f is the focal length, do is the object distance and di is the image distance.

Given: do = 5.20m, di = -15.0cm = -0.15m

Using the formula: 1/f = 1/5.20 + 1/-0.15

Rearranging: 1/f = (-1/5.20) + (1/0.15)

Solving: 1/f = (-0.1905) + (6.67)

Finally: 1/f = 6.48

And: f = 1/6.48

Therefore, the focal length of the concave mirror is approximately 0.155 m.

30. Meaning/definition of mirror and lenses​


Answer:

The mirror is glass with one side silvery backing that produces an image by reflection on only one surface. The lens is a transparent substance that produces images by refraction in any surface of the two surfaces.

3. Palmolive soap and Safeguard:

Palmolive soap is a brand of soap that is known for its moisturizing properties. It contains natural ingredients like olive oil and aloe vera, which help to hydrate and soothe the skin. Safeguard, on the other hand, is a brand of antibacterial soap that is designed to kill germs and bacteria on the skin.

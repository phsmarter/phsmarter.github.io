21. Explain how technology has changed communication and caused some changes in our behavior and life in general. I need it right now until 7pm ​


Technology has drastically transformed the way we communicate with each other. With the rise of smartphones and social media, we can now send messages, photos, and videos to friends and family members all over the world in an instant. We no longer have to wait days or even weeks to receive a response. This has made communication more convenient and efficient.

However, technology has also caused some changes in our behavior and way of life. For example, the use of smartphones and social media has led to a decrease in face-to-face interaction. Instead of meeting up with friends or having a conversation over the phone, we tend to rely on messaging apps and social media platforms to communicate with each other. This can lead to feelings of loneliness and isolation, especially if we don't make an effort to maintain real-life connections.

In addition, the expectation of immediate response and gratification that comes with technology can be stressful and overwhelming at times. We feel the need to be constantly connected and available, which can make it difficult to disconnect and unwind. This can have a negative impact on our mental health and well-being.

Lastly, technology has also changed the way we consume information and media. We now have access to an endless stream of content, from news articles to TV shows and movies. While this can be a great source of entertainment and education, it can also lead to binge-watching and other unhealthy habits that can affect our productivity and mental health.

In summary, technology has revolutionized the way we communicate, but it has also caused some changes in our behavior and way of life that we should be mindful of. We need to find a balance between our online and offline interactions, and use technology in a way that enhances our lives rather than detracting from it.

25. • Expository Essay - Effects of Technology Write an essay explaining the technology's cause and effect and how it affected our lives.​


Answer:

Technology has had a significant impact on our lives in recent years. From smartphones and social media to advanced medical treatments and transportation, technology has changed the way we live, work, and communicate. However, like everything, technology also has its effects on our lives, both positive and negative. In this essay, we will explore the effects of technology on our lives, focusing on its causes and effects.

The main cause of the effects of technology on our lives is the rapid advancement of technology in recent years. With the rise of the internet and smartphones, technology has become more accessible and integrated into our daily lives than ever before. This has led to a rapid increase in the use of technology in all aspects of life, from education and work to leisure and social interactions.

One of the most significant effects of technology on our lives is the way it has changed the way we communicate. The advent of social media and messaging apps has made it easier than ever to stay connected with friends and family, regardless of geographical distance. This has led to increased socialization and faster communication, but it also has its negative effects such as people becoming more isolated and disconnected.

Technology has also had a profound impact on the way we work. With the rise of automation and artificial intelligence, many jobs that were once done by humans are now being performed by machines. This has led to increased efficiency and productivity, but it has also led to job displacement and loss of livelihood for many people.

Furthermore, technology has also changed the way we learn and access information. The internet and mobile devices have made knowledge and education more accessible than ever, but it also has its negative effects such as students becoming more reliant on technology, lessening their ability to think critically and process information without the use of technology.

In addition, technology has also had a significant impact on our health and well-being. On one hand, technology has made medical treatments more advanced and accessible, leading to a better quality of life for many people. However, on the other hand, the excessive use of technology has also led to an increase in sedentary lifestyles, leading to health problems such as obesity and heart disease. Also, the blue light emitted by electronic devices can cause sleep disturbances. Moreover, constant exposure to the screens can cause eye strain, headaches and even dry eyes, which can lead to more serious eye issues in the long run.

In conclusion, technology has had a significant impact on our lives in recent years. The rapid advancement of technology has led to increased efficiency, productivity, and accessibility in many aspects of our lives. However, it also has its negative effects, from job displacement and loss of livelihood to health problems, and negative effects on education and communication. It is important that as we continue to benefit from the advancements in technology, we also take the time to consider the potential negative effects and take steps to mitigate them. Balancing the usage of technology and finding ways to use it for our advantage without being enslaved by it can be a good way of utilizing the positive aspects of technology.

Technology has revolutionized our lives in many ways. It has enhanced communication easier and faster, it has made transportation quicker and more efficient, and it has allowed working from home a reality for many people. However, technology has also had some negative effects on our lives. It has made us more reliant on computers and less able to think for ourselves. This has made us more isolated from others, and it has made us more vulnerable to identity theft and other cyber crimes.

On the positive side, technology has simplified our lives easier in many ways. For instance, it has given us access to information and knowledge that was previously unavailable. It has also made communication easier and faster, allowing us to connect with people around the world instantly. And, it has made many tasks simpler and more efficient, from doing our taxes to booking a vacation.

On the negative side, technology has also had some harmful effects. For example, it has made us more reliant on machines, which can lead to feelings of anxiety and loneliness when we're not around them. It has also contributed to a rise in cyberbullying and internet addiction. And, it has made some jobs obsolete, which can lead to unemployment.

Overall, the impact of technology on our lives has been both positive and negative. While it has made many things easier, it has also created some new problems that we need to address.

1. argumentative essaystudents should do community service if they come to school late habitually ​


Community service is a valuable opportunity for students to give back to their communities while also learning important life skills and values. One group of students who could benefit greatly from participating in community service are those who habitually come to school late.

First, community service can serve as a form of punishment for students who are habitually late to school. Instead of solely receiving disciplinary action, such as detention or suspension, students could be required to complete a certain number of community service hours as a way to make amends for their actions. This approach would not only hold the student accountable for their behavior, but it would also give them the opportunity to give back to their community in a positive way.

Additionally, community service can help to instill a sense of responsibility and accountability in students who habitually come to school late. By participating in community service, these students will learn the importance of being reliable and punctual, as well as the value of giving back to their community. Through their experiences, they will understand that their actions have consequences and that they have a responsibility to make things right.

Furthermore, community service can be an excellent way for students to develop important life skills. For example, students who participate in community service can learn teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills. These skills will not only help them in their academic and personal lives but also in their future careers.

Finally, community service can be an excellent way for students to learn empathy and compassion. By volunteering their time and energy to help others, students will learn the importance of serving and supporting others. They will learn the importance of taking care of their communities, and they will develop a sense of civic responsibility.

In conclusion, community service is a valuable opportunity for students who habitually come to school late to give back to their communities, develop important life skills and learn the importance of responsibility and civic duty. It is a more productive and beneficial way to handle the situation than solely punishing the student.

The Effects of Soil Erosion

Erosion comes from the Latin erosion (meaning to gnaw) or also called erosion is an event that occurs naturally by the erosion of solids (sediment, soil, rock, and other particles) due to transportation by wind, soil, and other materials under the influence of gravity or force. A carrier that moves over the earth.

Soil erosion is a process or event of the loss of the topsoil, either caused by the movement of water or wind. This erosion process can cause a decline in soil productivity, soil carrying capacity, and environmental quality. Generally, erosion involves three processes, namely detachment, transformation, and sedimentation.

Erosion that occurs can form many interesting natural features such as mountain peaks, valleys, and coastlines. Erosion is actually an easily recognizable natural process, but in most places, it is exacerbated by human activities in poor land use, such as :

deforestation,mining activities,plantations and farms,construction or construction activities that are not well organized,road construction.

The effects of soil erosion are:

Reduce soil fertility, which can have a negative impact on crop yields.Floods are due to the buildup of thick layers of sediment that clog the flow of rivers.

As for what can protect the community from the impact of erosion, namely the behavior of the community itself. Conduct socialization activities regarding erosion, both the factors that cause erosion and their impacts so that the community will understand more about the process of erosion. In addition, finding the right method for better land management will certainly help maintain the land landscape, so that it can help conserve soil or reduce erosion.

7. What are the different sources of Research Problems?


Answer:

- Personal or professional experience

- Theory

- Literature and the media

Explanation:

Personal or professional experience

Everyday personal or professional experience could lead us to recognize a problem that we would like to solve. Alternatively, we may have a topic or question that we would like to try to resolve.

For example, on a personal level, you might prefer the taste of organically produced vegetables and so wonder whether people usually prefer the taste of organically produced vegetables to non-organic ones.

The research subject is an analysis of taste tastes and the question 'do people usually favor the taste of organically grown vegetables to non-organic ones? 'Alternatively, for example, as a knowledgeable nature reserve guard, you may want to promote the establishment and distribution of a specific plant species, since you know that it is a food source for rare butterflies. The study issue may be, 'How do I facilitate the spread of plant species of interest?

Theory

This is a study about the relationship between situations or events. The hypotheses may be universal, widely held views (like domestic cats are the cause of a reduction in the number of birds in the UK gardens) or more technical ideas (for example, that global warming is causing a time change of the seasonal responses of the flowering cherry tree in the UK).

Literature and the media

There are a variety of sources of literature, such as novels, journal articles and journals. It is possible to find gaps in understanding and expertise and issues about which there is actually no solution in the search and reading of literature. They can provide a strong base for analysis.We are often filled with knowledge from media sources, such as TV, that could contribute to your study.

Other different sources of research problems:

Past researches and literature reviewCasual observationCurrent social and economic issues Replication of previous studies.Clarification of contradictory research results

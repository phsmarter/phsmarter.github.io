22. make a short essay aboutWhy Students Eat Breakfast Everyday​


Answer:

Eating breakfast every day can have a significant impact on students' academic and overall health. Research has shown that students who eat breakfast regularly tend to have better memory and concentration, which can improve their performance in school. Breakfast also provides the necessary nutrients and energy to start the day, which can help students stay focused and alert throughout the morning.

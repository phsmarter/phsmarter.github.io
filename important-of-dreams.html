12. What is the importance of dreams and aspirations in the play raisin in the sun?​


Answer:

Dreams and aspirations are of paramount importance in "A Raisin in the Sun" as they represent the characters' desires for a better life and the pursuit of happiness. They highlight the struggles faced by African-Americans during that era and explore themes of social mobility, identity, and family. Dreams provide motivation and hope, serving as a driving force for the characters to overcome obstacles and envision a brighter future. The play showcases the clash between different generations and their aspirations, reflecting the evolving perspectives within the African-American community. Ultimately, dreams and aspirations symbolize the resilience, determination, and longing for progress in the face of adversity.

16. What is the summary of the story "Baucis and Philemon" own words pls thanks


Baucis and Philemon were an elderly couple. They did not have much, but loved each other greatly. One day two travelers showed up at their ‘shack and poverty’. They did not know that the guests were actually mortal forms of Jupiter and Mercury, but as Baucis and Philemon were a very kind and pious couple, they invited the travelers inside. These walking forms of Jupiter and Mercury had already been turned away from one thousand homes. However, Baucis and Philemon began to prepare the best meal they could for the strangers, even though they didn’t have much. Hospitality was very important to the Romans, so, in return for their kindness, Jupiter gave them what they wanted most: to die at the same moment. He turned the old man and woman into two trees growing from the same trunk so that they would live and die together.

21. What are the books of titus philemon hebrews james 1st and 2nd peter all about?


Answer:

The Epistle of Paul to Philemon, known simply as Philemon, is one of the books of the Christian New Testament. It is a prison letter, co-authored by Paul the Apostle with Timothy, to Philemon, a leader in the Colossian church. It deals with the themes of forgiveness and reconciliation. Paul does not identify himself as an apostle with authority, but as "a prisoner of Jesus Christ", calling Timothy "our brother", and addressing Philemon as "fellow labourer" and "brother." (Philemon 1:1; 1:7; 1:20) Onesimus, a slave that had departed from his master Philemon, was returning with this epistle wherein Paul asked Philemon to receive him as a "brother beloved."(Philemon 1:9–17)

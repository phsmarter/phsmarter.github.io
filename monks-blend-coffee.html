9. Arthur has blended coffee worth 95 pesos per kilogram with coffee worth 115 pesos per kilogram to make 50 kilograms of coffee that will be sold at 107 pesos per kilogram. How many kg each kind did he blend?


Let's let the amount of coffee worth P95/kg be X.

Then since he makes 50kg of coffee, the amount of coffee worth P115/kg is 50-X.

Now, for the equation, on the right side:

The cost of the 50kg is P107/kg x 50kg

For the left, we will still use the cost of the 50kg of coffee, but, using each kind:

P95/kg x X + P115/kg (50-X)

Putting these together:

95X + 115(50-X) = 107(50)

Simplifying:

95X + 5750 - 115X = 5350

Combining like terms:

- 20 X = - 400

X = 20

So that means, he used 20kg of the P95 coffee, and 50-20= 30kg of the P115 coffee.

We can check:

20(95) + 30(115) = 5,350 = 50(107)

Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the experimental probability that the next cup sold will not be a blended beverage, we need to calculate the ratio of the number of non-blended cups to the total number of cups sold.

The number of non-blended cups sold is:

80 - 35 = 45

So the experimental probability that the next cup sold will not be a blended beverage is:

45/80 = 0.5625 or 56.25%

Therefore, the experimental probability that the next cup sold will not be a blended beverage is 56.25%.

24. Trins runs a coffee shop. Her best seller is a personal blend that was mixed using 2 types of ground coffee beans. One kg of the mix costs 225 pesos. How much of each type does Tris use?


let 'x' be the amount of coffee which costs 190/kg
    'y' be the amount of coffee which costs 275/kg
since you know that 1 kg is 225 then you'll the equations as:
(since the mix is 1 kg then you will have the idea that x + y =1)
x + y = 1 ----equation 1
(since you have the cost and the price you can have the second equation)
190x +275y = 225  ---equation 2
rearranging equation 1
x + y = 1
x = 1 - y   ----equation 3
substitute equation 3 to equation 2
190x + 275y = 225
190(1-y) + 275y = 225
190 - 190y + 275y = 225
85y = 225 -190
85y = 35
y = 7/17 kg
substitute to equation 3
x = 1 - 7/17
x = 10/17 kg

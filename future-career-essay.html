13. Can you make me an essay wit the theme of "Follow the guide, Tag a career, Like the future"...


“Follow the guide, Tag a career, Like the Future”


This was the theme of National Career Advocacy Congress, with the collaborative efforts of the DOLE. DSWD, DepEd, CHED, TESDA, PRC and DOST. The program was established to raise concerns of some graduate students having a hard time getting a job, or those who are employed but landed on a different career path.


Job mismatch is really common in our country. It could be that the person is in a rush to find a job and grab the first application to respond to earn money for the family, or, there really is no vacancy matched for their major and get the available one instead as a starting job.


However, there are some instances where some will really land to a job that they, even for a little, did not expect to happen, and turned out to be a success in that path. A good example is this: a graduate of Information Technology, dreamt of having a career in programming, however landed in Human Resource Management. The person may have performed well in the HR field, so some companies would offer a proper education and accreditations for that person to be qualified, in terms of credentials, performing HR duties.


It really is important to love what you do, and chose what you like. For university/college students who have really chosen the right major for them, if you want to have a good career in the future, study harder and aim to get your dream job. For those who are having second thoughts, you still have a choice to shift to the course/major you really like. Follow the guide, follow your choice, and land on the dream job that you’re looking forward to, so that the future will be what you want it to be.

17. a degree from a university provides invaluble lesson on life and a future career?essay​


A degree from a university provides invaluable lessons on life and a future career. Earning a degree from a reputable university is not only a great accomplishment but also a valuable investment in one's future. A degree opens doors to new opportunities, exposes individuals to a diverse range of perspectives, and equips them with essential life and career skills.

One of the most important lessons that a university degree provides is the importance of hard work and dedication. Pursuing a degree is no easy feat, and it requires a significant amount of time, effort, and determination. The process of earning a degree teaches individuals the value of perseverance and the power of setting and achieving goals. It also helps them develop a strong work ethic and a commitment to excellence that will serve them well in their personal and professional lives.

A university degree also exposes individuals to a diverse range of perspectives and ideas. In a university setting, students have the opportunity to engage with individuals from different backgrounds and cultures. They learn to appreciate the value of diversity and to recognize that people from different walks of life can offer unique perspectives and valuable contributions. This exposure to diverse perspectives helps individuals develop critical thinking skills and a broader understanding of the world around them.

Finally, a university degree provides individuals with essential life and career skills. A university education is designed to develop a range of skills, such as communication, problem-solving, and leadership. These skills are essential for success in both personal and professional settings and are highly valued by employers. Additionally, a university degree provides individuals with a network of contacts and resources that they can draw upon throughout their careers.

In conclusion, a degree from a university provides invaluable lessons on life and a future career. It teaches individuals the importance of hard work and dedication, exposes them to a diverse range of perspectives, and equips them with essential life and career skills. Earning a degree is a valuable investment in one's future and is well worth the time, effort, and dedication that it requires.

28. Write a creative essay on your strategic management leanings and how you will apply them to your future career path.​


Strategic management is an essential aspect of any successful business. It involves setting goals, developing strategies, and implementing plans to achieve those goals. Throughout my academic journey, I have learned various strategic management concepts and principles that have influenced my future career path.

One of the essential leanings I have gained is the importance of creating a strong mission statement. A mission statement outlines the organization's purpose, values, and goals. It provides direction and guides decision-making processes. In my future career path, I plan to apply this leanings by ensuring that every organization I work for has a well-crafted mission statement. The mission statement will help me align my goals with those of the organization and provide a clear focus for all stakeholders.

Another strategic management leaning that I find valuable is the SWOT analysis. SWOT analysis is a tool used to evaluate an organization's strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. It is a valuable tool that helps organizations identify their competitive advantages and areas that require improvement. In my future career path, I plan to apply this learning by conducting SWOT analyses regularly to help organizations understand their market position, identify new opportunities, and address challenges proactively.

Additionally, strategic management has taught me the importance of continuous improvement. In today's dynamic business environment, organizations must constantly evaluate their processes and systems to stay competitive. I plan to apply this learning by continuously monitoring the performance of the organizations I work for and looking for opportunities to optimize processes, reduce waste, and improve efficiency.

Lastly, strategic management has taught me the value of effective communication. In any organization, communication is essential to ensure that all stakeholders are informed and aligned. I plan to apply this learning by improving my communication skills and ensuring that I keep all stakeholders informed and engaged throughout the decision-making process.

In conclusion, strategic management is an essential aspect of any successful business. The leanings I have gained have provided me with a solid foundation to apply to my future career path. I plan to apply these learnings by ensuring that every organization I work for has a strong mission statement, conducting regular SWOT analyses, focusing on continuous improvement, and improving communication skills to ensure that all stakeholders are informed and engaged.

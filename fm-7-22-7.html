2. 7. In FM, frequency of modulated wave varies with A. amplitude B. time C. wavelength D. energy​


FREQUENCY

A. Amplitude

In FM, Frequency of modulated wave varies with AmplitudeFM modulation, the carrier frequency and amplitude remain constant, while the instantaneous frequency of the signal varies according to the modulating wave. This variation in frequency occurs due to the process of phase shifting. The frequency deviation (the amount by which the carrier wave deviates from its original frequency) is proportional to the amplitude of the modulating wave.

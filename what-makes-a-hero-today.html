11. 1. What do we mean by heroes of the past and heroes of today?​


Answer:

Heroes of the past are people who did something really amazing a long time ago, like a hundred or even a thousand years ago. They might have been brave warriors who fought in battles, or clever scientists who discovered new things, or kind leaders who helped their people. They are people who made a difference in the world and people still talk about them today. Just like how we still talk about King Arthur or Julius Caesar. They are like super-heroes from the history books.

Heroes of today are people who are doing something amazing right now, like today or in recent years. They could be people who help others in their communities, like doctors or firefighters, or people who speak up for what they believe in, like activists or politicians. They could also be people who achieve great things in their field like Athletes or even actors. They are making a difference in the world today and we can learn from them and be inspired by them. Just like how we look up to LeBron James or Malala Yousafzai,

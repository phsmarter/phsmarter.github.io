1. What is the significance of studying culture,society,and politics


What is the significance of studying Culture, Society, and Politics?

Culture  

The significance of studying culture is its way of reminding us who we really are as a people. As the seemingly unstoppable western culture pushes forward around the world, looking into our past and knowing about our own culture becomes quite interesting.  

Points to remember:

It is important for our identity to realize who and what we are, and to feel that quiet inner pride and contentment of really belonging to these islands and region of the world. Knowing one’s culture may free someone from constantly dreaming of a life in the west which is sadly a prevailing preoccupying goal among our people.

Society  

The significance of studying society has to do with teaching us how we react and adjust to it.  

Reasons to be aware of our society:

Many life decisions are based on our knowledge of how our society functions. How we interact and behave should not stray too far from the norms accepted by the people around us.  

Politics

The significance of studying politics is vitally important in the direction of a town, region or nation. It opens our eyes to the reality of how our politicians work.  

Why should we study politics?

You begin to know how public funds are spent, how new laws are passed and whether they are just. The study of politics will hopefully help the individual in choosing the right leaders in the future.  

The reality of culture, society, and politics are intertwined, the curiosity and study in one of them lead to the other two.

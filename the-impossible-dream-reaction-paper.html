27. write your reflection about the short film impossible dream​


A Reflection on the short film ‘The Impossible Dream”

The Impossible Dream is a short animated film from 1983. Its main purpose is to show the problem every woman is facing, which is the tiring job of being a housewife, who does all the house chores and a full-time job.

In the short film, it was clearly shown that there was inequality between men and women. Men will always have more privilege over women in almost all aspects of life. The short film depicted the situation of women in growth in their career was very hard to achieve since the obligation of managing the house only falls on the women. A woman is expected to be a wife and mother which makes it hard for them to establish their own careers. Men, however, are not expected of these which is why they can focus on their careers and the reason why most professions are dominated by men.

The short film showed this issue from a comedic point of view, and yet, we must not laugh at this. Despite being made back in 1983, this is still relevant in modern society. Women of today are still experiencing this. This is something we must solve as a society. Women are just as capable of doing successfully in their respective chosen careers as men.

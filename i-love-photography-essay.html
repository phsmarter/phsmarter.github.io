26. Tell the difference of the following in an essay. Photography, Film, Animation and Print Media.​


Answer:

-Photography is the art, application, and practice of creating durable images by recording light, either electronically by means of an image sensor, or chemically by means of a light-sensitive material such as photographic film.

-Film is a sequence of images of moving objects photographed by a camera and providing the optical illusion of continuous movement when projected onto a screen.

-An animation is a method in which figures are manipulated to appear as moving images. In traditional animation, images are drawn or painted by hand on transparent celluloid sheets to be photographed and exhibited on film. Today, most animations are made with computer-generated imagery.

-Print media is one of the oldest and basic forms of mass communication. It includes newspapers, weeklies, magazines, monthlies and other forms of printed journals. A basic understanding of the print media is essential in the study of mass communication.

29. write an essay about photography pa answer please need na po​


Answer:

Photography

Explanation:

Photography is an art form that has been around for over a century and has undergone many changes since its inception. With advancements in technology, the equipment used for photography has become more sophisticated and the possibilities for capturing images have expanded. Despite these changes, the essence of photography remains the same - it is a medium for capturing and preserving moments in time.

Photography can be used for many purposes, from capturing memories of family events to documenting history and current events. It can be used to create art, tell a story, or communicate a message. With the rise of social media, photography has become even more important in our lives as we share our experiences and memories with others online.

One of the most important aspects of photography is composition. Good composition is essential for creating an effective image that draws the viewer's attention and communicates a message. This involves considering elements such as framing, perspective, lighting, and color. The photographer must also consider the subject of the photograph and how it relates to the overall message they are trying to convey.

In addition to composition, understanding and controlling the technical aspects of photography is also important. These include shutter speed, aperture, and ISO, which all affect the exposure of the image. Understanding how to adjust these settings can help the photographer achieve the desired effect and create a well-exposed image.

Post-processing is also an important part of modern photography. Editing software such as Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom allow photographers to fine-tune their images and achieve the desired effect. This can involve adjusting color, contrast, and sharpness, as well as removing unwanted elements or adding new ones.

Overall, photography is a versatile and powerful medium that can be used to capture and communicate a wide range of experiences and emotions. Whether used for personal or professional purposes, it is an art form that requires skill, patience, and creativity to master. With the advent of new technologies and techniques, photography will continue to evolve and capture our imaginations for generations to come.

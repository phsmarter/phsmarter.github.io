Why sociology, anthropology, and political science important in understanding the dynamics of society and culture?​


Basically, these three fields study how human groups (from families to large communities) form and interact. They attempt to explain how they evolve, develop a culture and create a form of government where leadership roles turn into positions of power and authority. As these groups grow they become more and more complex and as they go through their stages of evolution and development, they also build their history. Therefore, these three fields of study offer the framework and perspectives that will allow us to better understand society, its origins, needs, beliefs, interactions, functions, conflicts, and ideologies.

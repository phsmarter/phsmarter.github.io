2. Techniques in writing Reader-Response Criticism​


Answer:

Reader-response suggests that the role of the reader is essential to the meaning of a text, for only in the reading experience does the literary work come alive.

the purpose of a reading response is examining, explaining, and defending your personal reaction to a text.

Your critical reading of a text asks you to explore:

1.why you like or dislike the text;

2.explain whether you agree or disagree with the author;

3.identify the text’s purpose; and

4.critique the text.

it is important that you demonstrate an understanding of the reading and clearly explain and support your reactions.

3. technique in writing of reader response criticism​


Answer:

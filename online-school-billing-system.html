Answer:

There are two ways:

1. You can pay via Meralco Online. Meralco Online is accessible through our website - just click on the Meralco Online Beta button.  

Step 1. Login to your Meralco Online Account via www.meralco.com.ph and click "Pay Now".

Step 2. Choose the bill or service that you want to pay for and click "Pay Now".

Step 3. Review your payment summary. Once you are sure your payment details are correct, click "Proceed".

Step 4. You will be redirected to our payment partner's pages. Choose you payment method (Cards - Visa, Mastercard, JCB). Complete your payment credentials and click "Pay".

2. You can pay via Meralco Mobile App. This is a mobile application available for mobile phones operating in Android and iOS.

Step 1. Open your Meralco Mobile App and Login to your Meralco Online Account.

Step 2. Choose the bill that you want to pay for and click "Pay Now".

Step 3. Enter the amount that you want to pay for and click "Next"

Step 4. Review your payment summary. Once you are sure your payment details are correct, click "Proceed".

Step 5. You will then be redirected to our payment partner's pages. Choose you payment method (Cards - Visa, Mastercard, JCB). Input your payment credentials and click on "Pay".

1. no man is an islandESSAY. ​


"No man is an island." A sentence we always hear, a sentence already too cliche. We tend to hear this in situations when someone feels alone or when someone wants to be alone or freed. We also hear this at times when we want to express to someone that we need other people.

This saying simply means that no one can live alone. Living alone means having no life because you don't have interactions. We are all different. This is because we have purpose for each other. We need to share everybody's skills to live in an exceptional world. We need to share everybody's strength to overcome everybody's weaknesses. We need the world.

Whenever you feel alone, always remember, no man is an island. There will always be someone there for you. If you want to be totally independent, you won't be for long because soon, you'll realize how important other people are.

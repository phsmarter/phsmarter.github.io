12. toast message for wedding speech​


Ladies and gentlemen, I would like to raise a toast to the happy couple. Today, we are here to celebrate the beginning of their new journey together. They say that marriage is not just about finding the right person, but also about being the right person. And I have no doubt that both [Bride's Name] and [Groom's Name] are the right people for each other.

May your love for each other continue to grow stronger with each passing day. May your marriage be filled with laughter, joy, and happiness. May you always be there for each other through the good times and the bad.

So, here's to the bride and groom! May your love story be as beautiful as your wedding day, and may your future be filled with endless love and happiness. Cheers!


13. why is a toast speech commonly done on wedding receptions?


If only the groom gives the toast, he can first thank the guests, and then his parents. He can not only thank his new spouse's parents but also express his happiness at being a part of their family. He will often finish by turning to his bride, saying how proud he is to be her husband and how much he loves her.

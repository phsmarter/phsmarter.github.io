Answer:

Definitely learning from your own mistakes is sensible, rather than focusing on others for quite a few reasons

You'll waste your time waiting for others to try out something first(waste of time), why not go ahead and do it yourself.

In the process of observing others' mistakes, you will yourself fall in a pit without knowing how you got there, neither the way out, and won't learn anything from your mistakes as well.

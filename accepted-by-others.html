WHY IS IT IMPORTANT FOR EVERYONE TO ACCEPT THEMSELVES AND ACCEPT EACH OTHER?

Self-acceptance helps you see yourself in a different light, both good and bad, and relieves guilt and unhappiness. You will be able to recognize and understand your own personality characteristics and tendencies, which will help you to stop comparing yourself to others.

Acceptance of where others are implied letting go of your judgments and expectations of how others should behave. It also entails letting go of the inferiority complexes we have when we are spiritually, cognitively, and emotionally mature. We will never be able to fully comprehend what is going on in someone else's life. We don't have all of their tales or the whole truth about them. We try to write our own versions of their stories to fit our ego's best interests at times.

We offer people the space to find their own way and discover their own truth when we accept them as they are. We do not have to engage in others' tales or mentalities just because we accept and love them as they are. Healthy limits come with compassion and acceptance.

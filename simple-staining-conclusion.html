2. What is the advantage of the gram stain over the simple stain?


Answer:

What is the advantage of the Gram stain over a simple stain such as methylene blue? Gram staining highlights different bacteria types through the use of special dyes. It aids in the diagnosis of a specific organism and tells the difference between gram negative and gram positive bacteria.


3. Why is gram stain more used than simple stain?


Answer:

Gram staining is considered more valuable than simple staining procedure due to the following reasons: It tells about the type of bacterial cell. It is used for distinguishing the gram-positive and gram-negative bacteria. Helps in the diagnosis of the specific organism, in case of disease.

Answer: Why is the size more accurate in a negative stain than in a simple stain? Because it's not subjected to heat fixing or other harsh chemicals- ensuring that cell-size does not change or be distorted by the stain. Air-drying helps keep the size as well.

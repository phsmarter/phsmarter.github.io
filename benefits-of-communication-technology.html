23. 1. Determine at least three (3) global issue affecting information communication and technology. Explain. 2, Give and explain five (5) benefits or advantages of technology today. 3. Give two (2) most important technological development invented that we are using today.​


Answer:

1. One global issue affecting information communication and technology is the lack of access to technology and the internet in many parts of the world. This can create inequalities and limit opportunities for individuals and communities in those areas. Another issue is cyber security and the increasing frequency of cyber attacks, which can compromise personal and sensitive information. A third issue is the potential negative effects of excessive technology use and screen time on individuals, such as sleep disruption and problems with social interaction.

2. Some benefits or advantages of technology today include increased productivity and efficiency, improved communication and collaboration, access to a vast amount of information, enhanced education and learning opportunities, and improved healthcare through the use of medical technology.

3. Two of the most important technological developments that we are using today are the internet and smartphones. The internet has transformed the way we communicate, access information, and do business, while smartphones have revolutionized personal computing and made it possible for people to stay connected and access a wide range of services and information on the go.

Explanation:

1.

Cybersecurity threats: With the increasing reliance on technology and the internet, there has been a rise in cyber attacks and data breaches. This has resulted in the loss of sensitive personal and corporate information, causing financial and reputational damage.

Digital divide: There is a growing gap between those who have access to the internet and modern technology, and those who do not. This creates unequal opportunities and disadvantages for certain populations, leading to social and economic inequality.

E-waste: The rapid advancement of technology has led to a high rate of electronic waste, which poses environmental and health hazards. Improper disposal of e-waste can contaminate soil and water, and release toxic chemicals into the air.

2.

Improved communication: Technology has greatly improved communication by allowing people to connect with others around the world quickly and easily. This has facilitated the exchange of ideas and information, and has made it possible for people to collaborate on projects and work together more efficiently.

Increased productivity: Technology has made it possible for people to be more productive in their work. With the help of computers and other forms of technology, people can complete tasks faster and with greater accuracy, allowing them to produce more in less time.

Enhanced education: Technology has also had a profound impact on education. With the help of computers and the internet, students can access a vast array of educational resources and materials, which can help them learn more effectively and efficiently.

Improved healthcare: Technology has also had a major impact on the healthcare industry. With the help of advanced medical technology, doctors can diagnose and treat patients more effectively, leading to improved patient outcomes and lower healthcare costs.

Enhanced entertainment: Technology has also made it possible for people to access a wide range of entertainment options, from streaming movies and music to playing video games and more. This has made it easier for people to find ways to relax and enjoy themselves in their free time.

3.

The internet: The internet is one of the most important technological developments of the past several decades. It has revolutionized the way we communicate, access information, and do business, and has made it possible for people around the world to connect with each other in ways that were previously unimaginable.

The smartphone: The smartphone is another important technological development that has had a major impact on our daily lives. It has made it possible for people to access the internet, make phone calls, send messages, and perform a wide range of other tasks from a single device, which has greatly increased convenience and efficiency.

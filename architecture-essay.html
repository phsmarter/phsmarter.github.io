essay about the Importance of Architecture in Asia.

People need places to live, work, play, learn, shop and eat. Architects are responsible for designing these places and protect the public health, safety, and welfare. This second responsibility requires architects to be licensed by the state, which they work. The job begins with learning what the client wants. The architect takes many factors into consideration, including local and state building and design regulations, climate, soil on which the building is to be constructed, zoning laws, fire regulations, and the client’s financial limitations.

After discussing and agreeing on the initial with clients, architects develop final construction plans that show the building’s appearance and details construction. The final design shows the exact dimensions of every portion of the building, including the location and size of columns and beams, electrical outlets and fixtures, plumbing, heating and air-conditioning facilities, windows, and doors. The architect’s job is very complex. He or she is expected to know construction methods, engineering principles and practices, and materials. Architects must be up to date on new designs, construction techniques and procedures. Computer-aided design and drafting (CADD) and building information modeling (BIM) have replaced traditional drafting paper and pencil as the most common methods for creating designs and construction drawings. However, hand-drawing skills are still used, especially during the conceptual stages of a project. As construction proceeds, architects may visit building sites to ensure that contractors follow the design, keep to the schedule, use the specified materials, and meet work-quality standards. The job is not complete until all construction is finished, required test are conducted, and construction costs are paid.

Competition to enter these programs is high. Grades, class rank, aptitude and achievement scores count heavily in determining who will be accepted. Most schools of architecture offer degrees through either a five-year bachelor’s program or a three-or four-year master’s program

6. IT Architecture concepts and What is Software Architecture. ​


Answer:

Software architecture is a set of concepts and principles that indicate a blueprint or plan for a software system. It refers to the fundamental elements of a software system, including its components, modules, and interfaces, as well as the relationships and connections between these elements. Software architecture provides an overall framework and high-level model for building software, helping to understand the system's workings and to make decisions about client and business needs.

IT architecture, on the other hand, refers to the concepts and principles that describe a blueprint or plan for aligning technology with business. It refers to the fundamental elements of an IT system, including hardware, software, networks, and data, as well as the relationships and connections between these elements. IT architecture provides an overall framework and high-level model for aligning technology with business, helping to understand the system's workings and to make decisions about business technology needs.

What have you discovered about the golden ratio?

Two numbers are in the golden ratio if the ratio of the sum of the numbers (a+b) divided by the larger number (a) is equal to the ratio of the larger number divided by the smaller number (a/b). The golden ratio is about 1.618, and represented by the Greek letter phi, Φ.

How is the Golden Ratio derived?

You can find the Golden Ratio when you divide a line into two parts and the longer part (a) divided by the smaller part (b) is equal to the sum of (a) + (b) divided by (a), which both equal 1.618. This formula can help you when creating shapes, logos, layouts, and more.

How do you calculate Golden Ratio?

The Golden Ration Defined

Algebraically, if you have two numbers, A and B, it has to be such that (A + B) divided by A = A divided by B. In most cases, this is going to be a comparison result in a ratio of 1:1.618. This appears naturally all over your body.

Ancient Greek architecture is distinguished by its highly formalised characteristics, both of structure and decoration. These included amphitheatres, aqueducts, baths, bridges, circuses, dams, domes, harbours, temples, and theatres.

Romanesque architecture is an architectural style of medieval Europe characterized by semi-circular arches. There is no consensus for the beginning date of the Romanesque style, with proposals ranging from the 6th to the 11th century, this later date being the most commonly held.

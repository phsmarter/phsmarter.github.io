25. PERSONAL HEALTH CAREER PLANCAREER EXPLORATIONHealth Careers-engineer-police-medtech-nursingDescription Of The Health Career----​


Certainly! Here are brief descriptions of the health careers for engineer, police, medtech, and nursing:

1. Health Careers for Engineer: Biomedical Engineer

Biomedical engineering combines biology, medicine, and engineering to create new technologies and develop innovative applications that improve human health, such as prosthetics, biomedical imaging, and medical devices.

2. Health Careers for Police: Forensic Pathologist

Forensic pathologists are medical doctors who specialize in investigating the cause of death in cases of sudden, unexpected, or violent deaths. They work closely with law enforcement agencies to help solve crimes by conducting autopsies and analyzing evidence.

3. Health Careers for Medtech: Medical Technologist

Medical technologists are responsible for running complex medical tests and analyzing the results. They work in hospitals, clinics, and laboratories, using specialized equipment to study cells, tissues, and bodily fluids to diagnose diseases and monitor treatment.

4. Health Careers for Nursing: Nurse Practitioner

A nurse practitioner is a registered nurse who holds an advanced degree in nursing and is licensed to diagnose and treat patients. They work in a variety of healthcare settings, from primary care clinics to hospitals to nursing homes, and provide a range of services, including physical exams, medication management, and patient education.

29. what is career plan?


Career planning is an ongoing process that can help you manage your learning, interest and development.

There are 4 steps in Career planning
1. Knowing Yourself (what do i want to be?)
2. Finding Out (what skills do i need?)
3. making decisions (What can i do about it?/What will hinder me?)
4. taking action (Where can i get help?)Career Planning is the continuous process of: thinking about your interests, values, skills and preferences; exploring the life, work and learning options available to you; ensuring that your work fits with your personal circumstances.

25. what is the correct counting of kuradang step


What is the correct counting of kuradang step

The festival Kuradang is lively in market music, weddings, christening and other festival activities.The Kuradang may have been derived from the archaic Waray word  kurandang or kudangdang that literally means "overdressed." The word is possibly applicable to the old-fashioned dancers.  

East Visayas is believed to be the birthplace of the Kuradang. A number of Kuradang variants have been  found in the provinces of Samar and Leyte. The richest production of Kuradang dances are town parties or  weddings in the northern part of the province of Eastern Samar.

The counting of Kuradang step Counts 1, 2, 3 by count (2 M.). Switch step forward obliquely R (L) (cts. 1 and 2), step L (R)  by R (L) in front (ct. 3); shift step forward obliquely R (L)

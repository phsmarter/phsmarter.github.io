6. True or False (Business Finance)1. The objective of personal finance is like that of corporate or business finance 2. Recording of business transactions is required in business finance but not in personal.3. There is no distinct difference between personal goals and personal objectives.4. The personal net worth of a person or family is reflected in the personal income statement. 5. The personal balance it is usually prepared when using the actual approach or basis.6. Personal budget is a personal financial plan that has been expressed quantitatively.7. When preparing the personal budgeted income statement for a particular month only personal expenses must be included.8. During the monitoring or evaluation stage of the personal financial planning adjustments are made on the projected amounts. 9. Personal saving spending and investing activities are interrelated ​


Explanation:

1.False. The objective of personal finance is to manage an individual's or a family's financial resources in order to achieve their financial goals, while the objective of corporate or business finance is to manage the financial resources of a business in order to achieve the goals of the business.

2.False. Recording of business transactions is required in both personal finance and business finance.

3.False. Personal goals and personal objectives are distinct and may be different from each other. Personal goals are broad and long-term, while personal objectives are specific and short-term.

4.False. The personal net worth of a person or family is reflected in the personal balance sheet, not the personal income statement.

5.False. The personal balance sheet is usually prepared using the historical approach or basis, not the actual approach or basis.

6.True. A personal budget is a personal financial plan that has been expressed quantitatively and includes estimates of personal income and expenses for a specific period of time.

7.False. The personal budgeted income statement should include both personal income and personal expenses for a particular month.

8.True. During the monitoring or evaluation stage of the personal financial planning process, adjustments may be made to the projected amounts in order to ensure that the personal financial plan is on track to achieve the financial goals.

9.True. Personal saving, spending, and investing activities are interrelated and can affect each other. It is important to consider all of these activities when managing personal finances.

3. Why is the word “globalization” given emphasis or importance in today`s contemporary world?​


ROLE OF GLOBALIZATION IN CONTEMPORARY WORLD

Globalization it so important cause it changes the way nations, businesses and people interact. Specifically, it changes the nature of economic activity among nations, expanding trade, opening global supply chains and providing access to natural resources and labor markets. Globalization allows people, goods, services, ideas, languages, information, and commodities to flow across national borders all the time.

The effect of globalization is that the world is becoming increasingly smaller as we all become more and more connected. Some of the challenges faced in contemporary world include migrations of refugees, new thecnologies, ethnic and religious conflicts, terrorism, poverty, literacy, etc.

The contemporary world is an ever changing mix of social and political changes. While religious, political, and ethnic conflicts continue, we are currently living in one of the most peaceful eras in the history of the planet. Globalization in contemporary world is the word used to describe the growing interdependence of the world's economies, cultures, and populations, brought about by cross-border trade in goods and services, technology, and flows of investment, people, and information.

Globalization is a social phenomenon with wide implications. The term globalization is widely used and names the shape of our contemporary world. It means we are now living in a deeply and increasingly interconnected, mobile, and hasty society driven by technology.

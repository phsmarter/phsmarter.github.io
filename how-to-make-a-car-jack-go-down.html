1. Jack bough a used car for 4,555. He made a down payment of 1,500 for the car, & he will pay the remaining balance in 10 equal payments. How much will jack still owe on the car after he makes seven payments?


First, subtract his down payment from the full price.

4555 - 1500 = 3055

Divide this value by 10.

3055 ÷ 10 = 305.50

After seven payments, he will have three more before he completes the payment. Multiply 305.50 to three.

305.50 x 3 = 916.50

After seven payments, Jack will still owe 916.50.

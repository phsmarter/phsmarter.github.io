The study could explore whether the frequency and severity of cyberbullying incidents affect students' grades and academic progress.

The study could explore whether cyberbullying affects students' mental health and how this, in turn, affects their academic performance.

5.The Effectiveness of Cyberbullying Prevention Programs on Students' Academic Performance: This study could examine the effectiveness of cyberbullying prevention programs on students' academic performance.

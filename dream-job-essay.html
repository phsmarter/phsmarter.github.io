Descriptive – My dream job

My dream job is to become a veterinarian. I have always loved animals and have a passion for their well-being. A veterinarian’s job is to diagnose and treat illnesses in animals, provide routine check-ups and surgeries, and help prevent diseases through vaccinations. As a veterinarian, I would be able to work with a variety of animals, including dogs, cats, horses, and even exotic animals. I would be able to provide care for animals in need and help make a difference in their lives. Overall, my dream job as a veterinarian combines my passion for animals with my desire to make a positive impact in the world.

Compare-contrast – Study habits of students then and now

Back then, students used to rely on books, notes, and libraries to study. Nowadays, students have access to a wealth of information through technology. With the internet, students can access online resources, digital textbooks, and even virtual classrooms. However, while technology has made studying more convenient, it has also made students more distracted. With social media and mobile games, students find it harder to stay focused on their studies. In contrast, students in the past had fewer distractions and were able to devote more time to studying. Thus, while technology has brought many advantages to students, it has also created new challenges in terms of study habits.

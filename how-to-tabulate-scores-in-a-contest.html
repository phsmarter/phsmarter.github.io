13. 25 - 34 23 2. There are 30 students from a certain secondary school who took an examination. One of these students is Daisy whose score is 36. Given the tabulated score of the students below, determine the percentile rank of Daisy.​


Answer:

To determine the percentile rank of Daisy, we need to first find out how many students scored lower than her.

Looking at the table, we see that there are 5 students who scored lower than 25, 9 students who scored between 25 and 34, and 11 students who scored between 35 and 44. Therefore, there are a total of 5 + 9 = 14 students who scored lower than 34.

Since there are 30 students in total, we can now calculate the percentile rank of Daisy:

percentile rank = (number of students with scores lower than Daisy / total number of students) x 100

percentile rank = (14 / 30) x 100

percentile rank = 46.67

Therefore, the percentile rank of Daisy is approximately 46.67%.

24. Paul got in the contest although hegot a low score. its is compound sentence or complex sentence?​


Answer:

complex sentence po :)))

Answer:

Complex Sentence

"Paul got in the contest although he got a low score." is a complex sentence.

Explanation:

✅  Why this IS a Complex Sentence.Complex sentences consist of one dependent clause and one independent clause.independent clause in given sentence: "Paul got in the contest"dependent clause in given sentence: "although he got a low score"These kinds of sentences are linked together by subordinating conjunctions (whenever, while, although, even though, etc.). Examples of compound sentences: Even though Ysa was tired, she still managed to listen to her teacher in class.  and  Raven heated her coffee because it was cold.❎ Why this IS NOT a Compound Sentence.Compound sentences consist of two independent clauses.These kinds of sentences are linked together by coordinating conjunctions (FANBOYS - for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so). Examples of compound sentences: Mika wanted to sleep, but she still needed to finish her homework.  and  Princess decided to film a video, and she posted it on MeTube.

19. art's content of Fernando Amorsolo's Planting Rice​


Answer:

Fernando Amorsolo's painting, Planting Rice, depicts a traditional Filipino rural scene of farmers planting rice in a field. The painting is set in a lush green landscape, with a river in the background and a mountain range in the distance. The farmers are working together, with one man in the foreground planting the rice while two others in the background are preparing the soil. The painting is filled with warm colors and a sense of peacefulness, emphasizing the beauty of the Filipino countryside. The painting captures the hard work and dedication of the farmers, who are essential to the country's agricultural industry. Planting Rice is a reminder of the importance of hard work and the beauty of the Filipino countryside.

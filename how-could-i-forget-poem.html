29. if you have a friend who is the victim of rape? how could you advise her to forget it? even if it is hard to?


If you have a friend who is a victim of rape, it is best not to advise her to do anything. Just listen and be a friend. It is hard opening up about rape to someone, especially that most of the rapists are either their fathers, uncles or brothers. So if they open up to you, listen well. Do not tell them what to do, unless they ask you specifically.  

Apart from this, make your friend feel that you are trustworthy and will not spill their secrets to anyone. Never force them to share details of what happened. It is painful to recall what happened during that time. Your friend will take time to get over it. When they do become more open, you can advise them to seek professional and even legal help so that the perpetrator could be apprehended.

7. invisible hand theory


It is the unobservable market force that helps the demand and supply of goods in a free market to reach equilibrium automatically is the invisible hand. Description: The phrase invisible hand was introduced by Adam Smith in his book "The Wealth of Nations".



8. invisible organisms ​


A microorganism or microbe is an organism that is so small that it is microscopic (invisible to the naked eye). Microorganisms are often illustrated using single-celled, or unicellular organisms; however, some unicellular protists are visible to the naked eye, and some multicellular species are microscopic.


9. Matter is composed of atom in which Democritus believe were invisible particle


2,500 years ago, Democritus suggested that all matter in the universe was made up of tiny, indivisible, solid objects he called "atomos." However, other Greek philosophers disliked Democritus' "atomos" theory because they felt it was illogical. All matter is composed of extremely small particles called atoms.

14. meaning of invisible ​


Answer:

The term "invisible" generally refers to something that is impossible or very difficult to see or perceive. For example, the term may be used to describe something that is hidden or obscured from view, such as an invisible seam in a garment. It may also be used in a more abstract sense, such as to describe a concept or idea that is not immediately apparent or visible, such as the "invisible hand" in economics, which refers to the way that the free market can operate without a central controlling force. Entertainment Speech

An entertainment speech has the purpose of entertaining the audience. It brings relaxation and enjoyment to the people who hear the speech. Also, this kind of speech should have a theme or primary focus.

Entertainment speech: If I Am a Millionaire

Every one of us wanted to be a millionaire. We have thoughts that we strive to be one of them. But not all the people on this planet has the capacity to become a millionaire. Sometimes, some individuals are lucky enough to enjoy those moments. And you could buy everything you want for yourself and your family.

If I could have a chance to be a millionaire, I wanted to buy a house for my family. All I have will also be shared with them and make them happy. Moreover, becoming a millionaire is not just for me. Still, I wanted to share my blessings with other people. I also wanted them to be happy and enjoy what I gained and achieved in life.

I wanted to have a feeding program for all the people in need in these current times. Also, giving grocery bags to people experiencing poverty is one idea in my plan if I had a chance to become a millionaire. Then, I was also planning to give children and students some school supplies they need in order to continue their education despite hard times in their life. A simple gift to them will make their day, and that's what I want.

Being a millionaire is a dream to many. In other words, they mainly pursue having more money these days. However, we should have a good motive and kindness if we gain something other people cannot obtain. Always look to the bright side and make a habit of helping others and not just focusing on ourselves.

Do not let greed be the first one to happen. Decide what makes us happy and others also. In short, always give and do not be selfish.

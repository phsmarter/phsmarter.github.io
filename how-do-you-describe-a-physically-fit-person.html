12. How do you describe physically fit and unfit person? Please answer this.ASAP


Physical fitness

The state of a physical being of a person

Physically fit person

The following are some of the descriptions of a physically fit person

Cardiorespiratory Endurance - people who are physically fit could do activities such as running, biking, walking, etc without getting exhausted easily. The heart rate, endurance, and breathing rate are measured in order to properly evaluate the fitness of an individual Muscular Endurance - they can do push ups or pull ups in one sitting with a high rate.  Muscular Strength - physically fit people could easily exert muscle force in doing activities Flexibility - a physically fit person has flexibility in doing a range of movements.

Physically unfit person

The following are some of the descriptions of a physically unfit person

Have a hard time doing extensive activities Easily gets tired after exercising for some time Having unstable heartbeats and breathing rate after doing a certain activity

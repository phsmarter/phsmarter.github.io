12. Ibigay ang kahulugan ng bawat isa 1. Verbal Talent 2. Numerical Talent 3.Scholastic Talent 4.Abstract Talent. 5. Mekanikal Talent 6.Spatial Relation Talent 7. Clerical Accuracy Speed Talent 8.Kakayahan sa wika (linguistics)​


Answer:Verbal Talent

Ang talentong pandiwa ay isang espesyal na kakayahan na mayroon ang isang tao tungkol sa mga konsepto o bagay na may kaugnayan sa mga anyo ng salita.

.Numerical Talent

Ang talento sa numero ay isang espesyal na kakayahan na mayroon ang isang tao tungkol sa mga konsepto o bagay na may kaugnayan sa mga numero o matematika.

.Scholastic Talent

Ang talentong iskolastiko ay isang kumbinasyon ng mga kasanayang may kaugnayan sa mga salita (lohika) at mga kakayahan na may kaugnayan sa mga numero. Ang isang taong may ganitong talento ay magkakaroon ng mahusay na kakayahang magsunud-sunod, lumikha ng mga hypotheses, mag-isip gamit ang mga pattern ng sanhi, pangangatwiran, at maghanap ng mga conceptual regularities o numerical patterns. Ang talentong eskolastiko ay karaniwang taglay ng mga siyentipiko, accountant, at computer programming.

.Abstract Talento

Ang abstract talent ay isang espesyal na kakayahan na mayroon ang isang tao tungkol sa mga pattern, disenyo, sukat, diagram, hugis, at posisyon.

.Talento Mekanikal

Ang talentong mekanikal ay isang espesyal na kakayahan na mayroon ang isang tao tungkol sa mga pangkalahatang prinsipyo ng agham, iba't ibang kasangkapan, at mga pamamaraan sa paggawa ng makina.

.Spatial Relation Talent (spatial)

Ang talento para sa spatial na relasyon ay isang espesyal na kakayahan na mayroon ang isang tao sa pagmamasid, pagsasabi ng dalawang-dimensional na pattern, at pag-iisip sa tatlong dimensyon. Ang isang taong may ganitong talento ay may matalas na kahulugan ng visual na detalye sa pamamagitan ng kakayahang ilarawan ang mga bagay sa isang tunay at malinaw na paraan. Ang talentong ito ay karaniwang pag-aari ng mga artista, arkitekto, piloto, photographer, mechanical engineer, at ilang iba pang propesyon.

.Clerical Accuracy Speed ​​Talent

Ang katumpakan ng klerikal ay isang espesyal na kakayahan na mayroon ang isang tao sa larangan ng pagsulat, pag-concoct para sa mga laboratoryo, opisina, at iba pa.

Kakayahan sa wika (linguistics)

Ang talento sa wika ay isang espesyal na kakayahan na taglay ng isang tao sa pagsasagawa ng pagsusuri sa wika na pangangatwiran (linggwistika). Ang talentong ito ay karaniwang ginagamit sa larangan ng pamamahayag, pagsasahimpapawid, batas, pag-edit, stenography, mga tindero, at iba pa.

Vegetable Gardening: Applying Fertilizer

Fertilizer is a powerful tool that can help plants thrive if used appropriately. If applied incorrectly, it can not only harm plants but also the environment.

When to Fertilize

Regular fertilizer applications keep plants vigorous and productive. When plants grow reluctantly or start turning yellow, fertilizer may help. If plants are vigorous and green, you can wait a little bit before applying more fertilizer. Too much fertilizer can burn plants. Tomatoes and beans gave too much fertilizer grow lots of foliage but little fruit.

Vegetables growing in porous, well-drained soil should be fed frequently. Usually, a balanced fertilizer is applied every three to four weeks throughout the growing season. Don't stop applications when the fruit appears—continue to apply fertilizer as needed to ensure continued production.

vegetables growing in clay soils will need less fertilizer than those in sandy soils. One application every four to six weeks after planting is typically enough. Crops growing in organic soils may need little additional fertilizer—again, just use foliage color and plant vigor as guides. In gardens where the soil is sand enriched with organic matter, one or two additional applications at intervals or three to four weeks is usually enough.

Types of Fertilizer:

Dry fertilizer - can be applied in many ways. Scatter it over the entire garden, down a row, or ring individual plants.

Animal Manures - Composted animal manures used in place of inorganic fertilizer are best applied as a side dressing—this means they’re placed next to rows.

Water-soluble Fertilizer

Water-soluble fertilizers are often useful as a quick boost for vegetables. Liquids or crystals mixed with water are applied as frequently as once a week. The nutrients, easily distributed by a gardener with a sprinkling can, are readily available to plants. These fertilizers are especially handy for container-grown plants.

Foliar feeding -  is a technique of spraying plants with dilute liquid fertilizer, is rarely part of regular maintenance. Instead, use it to provide a special boost or to supplement micronutrients like iron, manganese, or zinc.

Preventing Pollution

To prevent water pollution from nutrient leaching and runoff, always follow these steps when fertilizing your vegetable garden.

30. 2. Music caters to all strata of people, not only to the few talented but to the unwashed amateur. - Tibor Bachmann Make essay this quote.​


Answer:

Music is an art form that has the power to touch people from all walks of life. From the highly trained and talented professionals to the everyday individual who simply enjoys listening to music, it caters to everyone. Tibor Bachmann, a renowned music composer, has aptly stated that music caters to all strata of people, not only to the few talented but to the unwashed amateur. This quote highlights the universal appeal of music and its ability to transcend social, economic, and cultural barriers.

One of the most significant aspects of music is that it is an inclusive art form. Unlike some other art forms that require a certain level of education or skill, anyone can enjoy music. Whether you are highly educated or have no formal education, music has the power to resonate with you. It can evoke emotions, memories, and even physical reactions in people, making it a truly universal language.

Furthermore, music has the ability to bring people together. It can create a sense of community and foster social connections. Music festivals and concerts attract people from all walks of life, providing a shared experience that transcends differences in culture, language, and background. In this way, music can break down barriers and bring people closer together.

Lastly, the quote by Bachmann also highlights the importance of amateur musicians. While highly trained and talented musicians are undoubtedly important, amateur musicians also have a significant role to play in the world of music. They can bring a unique perspective and fresh ideas, and their passion for music can inspire others to take up the art form. Amateur musicians can also benefit from the inclusive nature of music, as it provides them with an opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals and grow as musicians.

In conclusion, Tibor Bachmann's quote about music catering to all strata of people is an accurate representation of the universal appeal of music. Music has the power to unite people, break down barriers, and provide a sense of community, making it an art form that truly caters to everyone, regardless of their background or skill level.

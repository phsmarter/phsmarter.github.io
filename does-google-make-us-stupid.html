5. What are the sources of google’s competitive advantage? 2. what value does google create for customers and advertisers 3. what business-level strategy is google pursuing?


1. What is Google's source of competitive advantage?

The source of Google's competitive advantage is the database infrastructure that has been programmed by Google for many years, the relevance of search results and product offerings from AD words to social networking sites to mobile application platforms being the main source of Google's competitive advantage.

2. What value does Google create for customers and advertisers?

Google delivers Google Ads by aggregating and anonymizing data for ecommerce advertisers to identify profiles of customers who frequently buy or visit certain products based on network traffic. Google is also making it easier to see customer value by age, gender, or audience characteristics

3. What business level strategy is Google pursuing?

Google uses a generic strategy. This strategy involves a wide market scope. The company will offer products to everyone around the world. A generic competitive differentiation strategy involves developing capabilities that create a competitive business atmosphere.

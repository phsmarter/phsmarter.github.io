1. The effect of the hallyu wave on buying behavior of K-pop fans in Taytay senior high​


Answer:

The Hallyu wave, also known as the Korean Wave, refers to the global popularity of South Korean culture, including music, television dramas, and movies. This phenomenon has a significant impact on people's buying behavior, particularly among K-pop fans in Taytay Senior High. One of the most notable effects of the Hallyu wave on buying behavior is the increased demand for K-pop merchandise, such as albums, posters, clothing, and accessories. K-pop fans in Taytay Senior High tend to purchase these items as a way of showing their support for their favorite Korean idols.

Furthermore, the Hallyu wave has also led to an increase in the popularity of Korean skincare and beauty products. Many K-pop idols are known for their flawless skin and makeup, and this has prompted fans to purchase Korean beauty products in the hopes of achieving a similar look. Additionally, the Hallyu wave has also influenced the food choices of K-pop fans in Taytay Senior High. Korean cuisine has become more accessible, with Korean restaurants and food stalls popping up in various places. As a result, K-pop fans are more likely to try Korean food and incorporate it into their daily diets. In conclusion, the Hallyu wave has had a significant impact on the buying behavior of K-pop fans in Taytay Senior High, from the increased demand for K-pop merchandise to the popularity of Korean beauty products and cuisine.

The Korean wave or K wave

The increase in global popularity of South Korean culture since the 1990s. First driven by the spread of K-dramas and K-pop. This dissertation research examines the Korean Wave phenomenon as a social practice of globalization, in which state actors have promoted.

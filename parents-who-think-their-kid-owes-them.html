While some Filipino parents may use aswang stories as a scare tactic to discipline their children, it is important to note that such stories are also used to pass down cultural values and lessons. The cultural functions of aswang for parents who use them to discipline their children are twofold. Firstly, it is a way to instill fear in their children, in order to get them to respect and obey their parents. Secondly, it serves as a reminder to children that certain behaviors are not acceptable and can have dire consequences. Aswang can also be used to teach children to be aware of their environment and to take heed of warnings. Ultimately, aswang stories can be used to promote good behavior and reinforce the importance of respecting authority.

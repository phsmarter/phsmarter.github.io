A survey is one of the activities that are usually executed to improve or develop the system in an institution.

Detail explanation:

There are actually many things that an institution can execute to develop or improve its system. However, one of the very first things to do is a survey. This survey aims to obtain data. From the survey, an institution can find out and evaluate what they need to improve, improve, and develop in the future.

The survey is carried out in the form of a questionnaire which will be distributed to and filled up by people at the institution. Depending on the purpose, questions and questionnaire forms can vary. It can be a yes or no choice, a scale of 1 - 5, or a question that must be filled with personal opinion. The data from the completed questionnaire will be processed to then draw conclusions from it. After that, the institution will develop a plan for the development or improvement of its system.

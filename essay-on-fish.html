8. Why is it important to consider the quality of meat fish and poultry? Essay. need it now.


Answer:

There are several reasons why it is important to consider the quality of meat, fish, and poultry:

Food safety: High-quality meat, fish, and poultry are less likely to contain harmful bacteria or parasites that can cause foodborne illnesses.

Nutritional value: Fresh, high-quality meat, fish, and poultry are rich in essential vitamins and minerals that are important for overall health and well-being.

Taste and texture: Meat, fish, and poultry that are fresh and of high quality have better taste and texture, making them more enjoyable to eat.

Public health: Poor quality meat, fish, and poultry that contain high levels of pathogens or antibiotic-resistant bacteria can contribute to the spread of illnesses and the development of antibiotic-resistant bacteria, which can be a public health concern.

Environmental impact: The way meat, fish and poultry are produced can have an impact on the environment, choosing products from sustainable and responsibly sourced can help reduce the impact on the environment.

Overall, considering the quality of meat, fish, and poultry is important for ensuring a safe, nutritious, and enjoyable food supply, as well as protecting public health and the environment.

20. ESSAY what is the role of the gin/alcohol in the curing process to curing process of fish fillet?​


Answer:

Fish fillets have been around for thousands of years and are one of the most basic and desirable dishes. In their current fresh form, these delicacies must be cured. This process is generally done by applying a chemical mixture to the fish outside as well as inside, which includes soaking it in salt water or alcohol. But what role does alcohol play? Is it a necessary component in the curing process?

First of all, you can think about fish fillets as a type of food that has been processed via drying rather than cooking. This means that they have been dried and then frozen, which basically means that the ingredients are cooled.

Salting has been used for hundreds of years in curing fish and meat, but it was only discovered by the ancient Romans. The fish were left to dry out in shallow pits covered with salt for a few days and then moved to deeper pits with a specific mix of salt, sulfur and ferrous chloride (FeCl3). The result is the browning of the outside and it can be preserved for a long time by salt curing.

But why does salt curing work? Actually, we cannot use the word “salt” for any salt substance of sodium chloride, and in general, there are two ways to explain the process. The first explanation is that it is closely related to the amount of water inside the meat. Salt attracts and absorbs water from the meat through osmosis, and at the same time, sodium (Na+) ions from salt react with the organic material in meat to make them more solid.

So basically, the role of the gin/alcohol in the curing process of fish fillet is to decrease the amount of water in the meat.

But is the alcohol really necessary? As it turns out, this question is not simple to answer. In general, meat products can be cured by a number of methods including salting and smoking. The last method is used as a technological process that involves heating the product in an oven at high temperature and then cooling it down quickly before storing it to preserve the food.


21. essay from 100- 150 words 1.What is the importance of cleaning and scaling fish?​


Cleaning and scaling fish is an important step in preparing them for cooking or consumption. It helps to remove any debris or bacteria that may be on the fish, as well as to improve its overall appearance and texture.

One of the main reasons for cleaning and scaling fish is to remove any potential contaminants that may be present on the fish. Fish are often caught in areas where there may be pollutants or other harmful substances, and these can be transferred to the fish through their scales and skin. By removing the scales, we are able to reduce the risk of these contaminants entering our bodies when we eat the fish.

In addition to removing contaminants, cleaning and scaling fish also helps to improve the overall appearance and texture of the fish. When a fish is cleaned, its scales are removed, which can make it look smoother and more appealing. This is particularly important when it comes to presentation, as a clean and well-scaled fish is more likely to be enjoyed by those who are eating it.

Furthermore, scaling fish helps to improve the cooking process. When fish scales are left on, they can become hard and difficult to chew. By removing the scales, the fish becomes easier to cook and to eat, making it a more enjoyable dining experience.

In summary, cleaning and scaling fish is important for several reasons. It helps to remove potential contaminants, improve the appearance and texture of the fish, and make it easier to cook and eat. By taking the time to properly clean and scale fish, we can ensure that we are consuming safe and delicious food.

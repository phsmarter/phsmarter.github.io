8. pasagotDirections: Apply the concepts that you have learned about proportions in solving the following problems. Write your answers on separate sheet/s of paper. 1. A copier machine at Alegria National High School uses 150 grams of toner to produce 100 copies of module. How many grams are required to produce 2500 copies? 2. A printer can print 16 pages of colored module in 3 minutes. How many pages of module can the printer print in 15 minutes? 3. A girl who is 5 ft. tall can cast a shadow on the ground that is 10 ft. long. How tall is a girl who can cast a shadow that is 12 ft. long? 4. Eryl ran 3 miles in 30 minutes. At that rate how far would he run in 1 1/2 hours? 5. Edith's car can travel 12 kilometers on 1 liter of gasoline. How far can it travel on 5 liters?​


Answer:

1.) copies.

We can set up a proportion: 150 grams / 100 copies = x grams / 2500 copies

Cross-multiplying, we get: 100(x) = 150(2500)

Simplifying, we get: x = 3750 grams

Therefore, 3750 grams of toner are required to produce 2500 copies.

2.) Let x be the number of pages of module the printer can print in 15 minutes.

We can set up a proportion: 16 pages / 3 minutes = x pages / 15 minutes

Cross-multiplying, we get: 3(x) = 16(15)

Simplifying, we get: x = 80 pages

Therefore, the printer can print 80 pages of module in 15 minutes.

3.) Let x be the height of the girl who can cast a shadow that is 12 ft. long.

We can set up a proportion: 5 ft. / 10 ft. = x ft. / 12 ft.

Cross-multiplying, we get: 10(x) = 5(12)

Simplifying, we get: x = 6 ft.

Therefore, a girl who is 6 ft. tall can cast a shadow that is 12 ft. long.

4.) Let x be the distance Eryl would run in 1 1/2 hours.

We know that Eryl ran 3 miles in 30 minutes, which is equivalent to 1/2 hour. So, in 1 1/2 hours, he would run three times that distance.

Therefore, x = 3(3) = 9 miles

Therefore, Eryl would run 9 miles in 1 1/2 hours.

Let x be the distance Edith's car can travel on 5 liters of gasoline.

We can set up a proportion: 12 km / 1 liter = x km / 5 liters

Cross-multiplying, we get: 1(x) = 12(5)

Simplifying, we get: x = 60 km

Therefore, Edith's car can travel 60 km on 5 liters of gasoline.

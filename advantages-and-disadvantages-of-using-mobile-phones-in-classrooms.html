WHAT IS MODERN CLASSROOM?

In a Modern Classroom, where teacher-created instructional videos replace lectures, students advance at their own paces, while teachers spend class time providing targeted support. Differentiation becomes the very essence of instruction. Modern Classrooms are data-driven.

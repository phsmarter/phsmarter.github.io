27. definition of terms about "Exploring of work ethics or school utilities​


Answer:

Work Ethics - Work ethics refers to the set of moral principles and values that guide an individual's behavior and actions in the workplace. It includes traits such as reliability, honesty, responsibility, and accountability.

Professionalism - Professionalism is the conduct, behavior, and attitude that reflects the high standards and expectations of a particular profession. It encompasses qualities such as competence, integrity, and respect for others.

Workplace Culture - Workplace culture refers to the shared values, beliefs, behaviors, and attitudes that exist within an organization. It includes the norms and expectations for how employees interact with each other, with customers, and with their work.

Time Management - Time management is the process of organizing and prioritizing tasks and activities in order to make the most efficient use of time. It involves setting goals, establishing priorities, and developing a schedule to ensure that tasks are completed on time.

School Utilities - School utilities refer to the resources and facilities provided by educational institutions to support the learning and development of students. These may include libraries, laboratories, technology resources, sports facilities, and student support services.

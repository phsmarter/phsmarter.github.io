You can start with the telegraph. Before that, only local news was available in the newspapers in a timely fashion.

The next big step was radio. With radio you could hear the news soon after it happened, i.e.; before the next edition of the paper hit the streets.

With television, you could see the news soon after it happened.

With communication satellites, you could see and hear the news as it happens.

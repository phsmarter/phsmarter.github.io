[tex]\large\sf\pink{CHOICES:}[/tex]

ThusSecondFirstLastlySoAs a result

[tex]\large\sf\pink{ANSWER:}[/tex]

"A good parent reads stories to their children every day."

I have three reasons for believing the quote. First, I can proudly say that my parents read to me bible stories before going to bed. As a result, I grow up relating my personal experiences with the stories I listened to. Second, stories widen my perspective in life. So when life is hard, I would most of the time ponder on the lessons I have learned from stories that my parents read to me. And lastly, stories are my guide in making difficult choices. It is like hearing my mother or father giving me advices even if they are not around. Thus, I can say that I have a good parent.

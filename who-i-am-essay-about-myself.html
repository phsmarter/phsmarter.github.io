6. Who am I? If you multiply me by myself, the product is equal if you double me once?


Probably 2.

If you multiply me by myself...

Let's replace that statement with [tex]x[/tex]

The product is equal if you double me once.

That would now be an equation, which is:

[tex]x^2 = 2x[/tex]

If [tex]x = 2[/tex], then:

[tex]2^2 = 2 (2) \\ 4 = 4[/tex]

Answer:

Media such as radio, television, internet, and recording play a significant role in the development and evolution of different musical genres. These platforms provide a way for musicians and artists to share their music with a wider audience, and they can help to expose listeners to new sounds and styles that they may not have encountered otherwise. In addition, media platforms can provide a way for musicians to connect with other artists and collaborate on new projects, which can lead to the creation of new genres or sub-genres. The media can also serve as a way for musicians to receive feedback and criticism from listeners, which can help them to refine and improve their work.

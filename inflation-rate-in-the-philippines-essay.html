5. The five effect of the Philippines inflation rate 1 to 5 answer


Answer:

1. Increased Prices: Inflation increases the general price level of goods and services in an economy, which leads to an increase in the cost of living for consumers.

2. Reduced Purchasing Power: Inflation reduces the purchasing power of consumers as their money can buy fewer goods and services.

3. Lower Saving: Inflation also impacts savings as the purchasing power of savings declines over time due to inflation.

4. Decreased Investment: As inflation increases, investors are less likely to invest their capital in an economy as the returns will not be worth the risk.

5. Increased Unemployment: Higher inflation is often associated with increased unemployment as businesses may not be able to afford to increase wages to keep up with the higher prices.

11. The five effects of the Philippine's inflation rate. 1 to 5 answer


Answer:

1. Increased cost of living: The most direct effect of inflation is an increase in the cost of living. This means that prices of goods and services rise, leaving consumers with less purchasing power.

2. Decreased savings: Inflation erodes the real value of savings, and high rates of inflation can make saving money difficult. As the cost of living increases, the purchasing power of saved money decreases.

3. Reduced purchasing power: As the cost of living rises, so does the cost of goods and services. This makes it more difficult for consumers to purchase the same quantity of goods and services as before.

4. Increased unemployment: High inflation leads to higher production costs, which can result in businesses reducing their workforce or shutting down altogether. This leads to higher unemployment, as workers are laid off or unable to find new jobs.

5. Reduced national competitiveness: High inflation rates can make it difficult for a country to compete in the global marketplace. As prices of goods and services increase, other countries may be able to offer more competitive prices. This can result in a loss of market share for domestic producers.

21. Ano ang mga dahilan ng pagkakaroon ng inflation? a. Cost push inflation b. Core inflation c. Headline inflation d. Demand pull inflation e. Inflation rate


Explanation:

Ang mga dahilan ng pagkakaroon ng inflation ay ang mga sumusunod:

a. Cost push inflation - Ito ay nagaganap kapag tumaas ang mga gastos sa produksyon tulad ng mga raw materials at labor costs, na nagdudulot ng pagtaas ng presyo ng mga produkto at serbisyo.

b. Core inflation - Ito ay tumutukoy sa pagtaas ng presyo ng mga bilihin at serbisyo na hindi kasama ang volatile components tulad ng pagkain at enerhiya. Ito ay nagsisilbing indikasyon ng pangkalahatang pagtaas ng presyo.

c. Headline inflation - Ito ay tumutukoy sa pangkalahatang pagtaas ng presyo ng mga bilihin at serbisyo, kabilang ang mga volatile components tulad ng pagkain at enerhiya.

d. Demand pull inflation - Ito ay nagaganap kapag tumataas ang demand sa mga produkto at serbisyo, na nagdudulot ng pagtaas ng presyo. Ito ay kadalasang nagaganap sa panahon ng mataas na ekonomikong aktibidad at low unemployment rates.

e. Inflation rate - Ito ay tumutukoy sa rate ng pagbabago ng pangkalahatang presyo ng mga bilihin at serbisyo sa loob ng isang panahon, karaniwang binabatay sa consumer price index (CPI) o producer price index (PPI).

27. paano naging konektado ang inflation rate sa implasyon o inflation​


Ang inflation rate ay isang measure ng pagtaas ng average na presyo ng isang basket ng mga binebenta na mga produkto at serbisyo sa loob ng isang specific na panahon. Sa ibang salita, ito ay nagpapakita ng rate kung paano nag-aasenso ang cost of living sa isang bansa.

Ang inflation o implasyon naman ay isang sitwasyon kung saan may nagaganap na pagtaas ng average na presyo ng mga produkto at serbisyo sa loob ng isang ekonomiya.

Ang inflation rate ay isang indicator ng inflation, na nagpapakita ng degree o magnitude ng inflation na nangyayari sa isang bansa. Sa pamamagitan ng pagmonitor sa inflation rate, maaaring malaman ng mga policy makers, economists, at iba pang stakeholders kung anong aksyon ang dapat gawin upang mapababa ang inflation o upang mapaigting ang economic growth.

Sa madaling sabi, ang inflation rate ay isang numerical representation ng inflation, na ginagamit upang mas madaling maunawaan ang sitwasyon sa ekonomiya.

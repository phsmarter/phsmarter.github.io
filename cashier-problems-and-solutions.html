Problem 1⃣:

Nancy bought alcohol for Php95.75. She gave a Php100 to the cashier. How much did she get back from the cashier?

What is asked? The change Nancy received after paying.

Given facts: Nancy's money = Php 100

                     Alcohol price = Php 95.75

Plan : Subtract the price to her money.

Determine the operation to be used: subtraction

Number sentence: Php 100 - Php 95.75 = N

Solve :

Php 100 - Php 95.75 =  Php 4.25

Nancy's change is Php 4.25

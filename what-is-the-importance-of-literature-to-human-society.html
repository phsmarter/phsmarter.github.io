20. 1. What is the importance of literature to human society?​


The Importance of Literature in Modern Society

Humanity is reflected in literature, which helps us understand one another better. Reading someone's words can provide us insight into how they are feeling. Because of its aim and influence on a culture that is becoming increasingly disengaged from human connection, literature is important in today's world.

Literature in education

The bulk of ‘us encounter literature for the first time in a meaning’ful way in school. All of ‘us have had reading and writing hammered into us since we were little, and the commencement of exams sets this in motion.

From the viewpoint of a learner, it is crucial and essential to be able to empathize with a cast of people on a page. Additionally, being able to discern themes and messages allows us to think differently. Literature serves as a vehicle. The 130 million books that have been written worldwide serve as guides for readers and build a pathway for them to discover new information.

Literature within history

Through literature, one may travel through time and learn about life on Earth from those who came before us. We may learn more about different cultures and develop a deeper appreciation for them. We get knowledge via the methods used to record history, including manuscripts and verbal communication.

Influence of literature

There is no disputing literature's influence on contemporary culture. Each author uses literature as a medium for expression. Some novels serve as social mirrors that help us comprehend the culture we live in.

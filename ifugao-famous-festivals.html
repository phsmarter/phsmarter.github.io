5. The Ifugao’s wedding with the festival dance is called ____.


Uyaoy Dance is an Ifugao wedding festival dance accompanied by gongs and is performed by the affluent to attain the second level of the wealthy class. Wealthy people who have performed this dance are entitled to the use of gongs at their death.

9. what are the ifugao's famous artwork?


Ifugao is located in Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR) in Luzon. The term Ifugao means people from the hills.

 

Their arts and culture revolves around rice. They weave cloths called Ikat which is composed of blue, red and white stripes.

They are also known for amulets that are made of beads, gold and bronze which are called lingling-o or dinumug.

 

Although they have different crafts and artworks, the two most famous are the Ifugao Hut and Banaue Rice Terraces. The Banaue Rice Terraces is known to be carved by hand by the Ifugaos.

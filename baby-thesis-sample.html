Reservation status codes are attached to the guest reservation to indicate the status of the booking made by the guest. Eg:- Booking confirmed by credit card, tentative bookings which has to be released by 6 PM, Travel Agent Voucher confirmed etc.

Confirmed: Once payment method and/or billing instructions are confirmed a reservation status can be selected as confirmed. A reservation should only be confirmed upon receipt of a credit card or deposit payment.

Tentative: If payment method and/or billing instructions have not been guaranteed then the reservation should be set to the status of Tentative.

Waitlist: If the hotel or room type is fully booked then the customer should be given the option to waitlist their reservation.

Company Guarantee: If a reservation is made by a company and also billed to the same company then reservation status 'Company guarantee' may be used.

Travel Agent Guarantee: A travel agent booking with a valid IATA number and Travel agent voucher may be selected as code Travel Agent Guarantee'.

6 PM Release: A reservation is not guaranteed with payment or voucher and the hotel will hold it till 6PM in the evening.

11. 1. Ano ang kaugnayan ng population sa sample size. 2. Bakit mahalaga na alamin ang sample size sa usapin ng paggawa ng thesis? 3. Sa usapin na probability sampling, ano ang gusto mong gamitin sa iyong thesis kung ang iyong titulo na napili ay quantitative at bakit? 4. Ipaliwanag yong konsepto ng BIAS sa pagpili ng respindents para sa iyong thesis?.


Answer:

1. Ang sample size ay isa sa mga importanteng factor na tinatawag sa pagpapasiya kung gaano kalaki ang population na maaaring pagkunan ng data. Ang sample size ay tumutukoy sa bilang ng mga indibidwal o item na pinili mula sa population upang magamit sa pag-aaral o pagtataya.

Ang population size ay nakaugnay sa sample size dahil mas malaki ang sample size na kinakailangan kung mas malaki ang population size. Sa isang malaking population, ang pagpili ng sample na maaaring maaring representatibo ng kabuuan ay mas mahirap, kaya mas malaking sample size ang kinakailangan upang makamit ang sapat na pagkakakilanlan sa population. Sa kabilang dako, kung mas maliit ang population size, mas maliit ang sample size na kinakailangan upang makamit ang sapat na pagkakakilanlan sa population.

Ang pagpapasiya sa tamang sample size ay nangangailangan ng trade-off sa pagitan ng kahusayan at kakayahan. Ang maliit na sample size ay maaaring magdulot ng hindi sapat na pagkakakilanlan sa population at maaaring magdulot ng hindi tumpak na pagtataya, samantalang ang malaki na sample size ay maaaring magdulot ng mahal na pag-aaral at hindi epektibo sa pag-optimize ng resources.

Ang tamang pagpapasiya sa sample size ay nangangailangan ng pag-unawa sa mga layunin ng pag-aaral, sa pagkakakilanlan sa population, sa disponibilidad ng resources, at sa iba pang mga kadahilanan na maaaring magdulot ng epekto sa pagtataya.

2.Ang sample size ay mahalaga sa pagpapasiya sa pagpili ng mga indibidwal o item na maaaring pagkunan ng data sa pag-aaral, lalo na sa usapin ng paggawa ng thesis. Ang pagpapasiya sa tamang sample size ay nakakaapekto sa kahusayan at kakayahan ng pag-aaral at sa kwalidad ng mga resulta na makakalap.

3.Kung ang iyong titulo na napili ay quantitative at gusto mong gamitin ang probability sampling sa iyong thesis, maaaring gamitin ang Simple Random Sampling o SRS. Ang Simple Random Sampling ay isa sa mga uri ng probability sampling na ginagamit upang mapili ang mga indibidwal o item na magiging pagkakakilanlan sa pag-aaral. Ang layunin ng SRS ay upang makamit ang representatibong pagkakakilanlan sa pag-aaral sa pamamagitan ng pagpili ng mga indibidwal o item sa population gamit ang random selection process.

4. Ang bias ay isang kahalagahan na kadahilanan na nagdudulot ng hindi tumpak na pagkakakilanlan sa pag-aaral. Sa pagpili ng mga respondents para sa iyong thesis, ang bias ay maaaring mangyari kung hindi tumpak na mapapili ang mga indibidwal na magiging pagkakakilanlan sa pag-aaral. Ang hindi tumpak na pagpili ng mga respondents ay maaaring magdulot ng hindi tumpak na resulta sa pag-aaral at hindi tumpak na pagkakakilanlan sa population.

Ang iba't ibang uri ng bias sa pagpili ng mga respondents ay kinabibilangan ng selection bias, response bias, at non-response bias. Ang selection bias ay nangyayari kung hindi tumpak na mapapili ang mga respondents na magiging pagkakakilanlan sa pag-aaral dahil sa anumang kadahilanan tulad ng pagpapabor sa mga indibidwal na mayroong kaugnayan sa pag-aaral. Ang response bias naman ay nangyayari kung hindi tumpak na ibinibigay ang mga sagot ng mga respondents dahil sa anumang kadahilanan tulad ng pagkahiya o pagkakahiya. Ang non-response bias naman ay nangyayari kung hindi tumpak na ibinibigay ang mga sagot ng mga respondents dahil sa hindi pagpapaliwanag sa mga tanong o hindi pagpapaliwanag sa pagkakahalintulad.

Upang maiwasan ang bias sa pagpili ng mga respondents para sa iyong thesis, kailangan mong magpakalma sa pagpili ng mga indibidwal na magiging pagkakakilanlan sa pag-aaral sa pamamagitan ng pagpili ng mga indibidwal gamit ang tumpak na random selection process. Kailangan din na magpakalma sa pagpapaliwanag sa mga tanong upang hindi magdulot ng hindi tumpak na sagot mula sa mga respondents.

14. sample interview for jeepney operators (thesis)​


Answer:

Interviewer: Good day! Thank you for agreeing to this interview. Can you tell us your name and how long have you been operating a jeepney?

Jeepney Operator: Good day, my name is Juan dela Cruz, and I have been operating a jeepney for over 15 years now.

Interviewer: Thank you for that. Our study focuses on the impact of modernizing jeepneys on the livelihood of operators. What do you think are the advantages and disadvantages of modernizing jeepneys?

Jeepney Operator: Well, the advantage of modernizing jeepneys is that it can improve the riding experience of passengers. With modern features such as air conditioning and GPS navigation, passengers can enjoy a more comfortable and convenient ride. Also, modern jeepneys can help reduce air pollution, which is a significant problem in the city. On the other hand, the cost of modernizing jeepneys is quite expensive, and not all operators can afford it. Also, some jeepney operators may not be familiar with the new technologies, making it difficult for them to operate.

Interviewer: I see. Can you tell us about the current challenges you face as a jeepney operator, and how do you cope with them?

Jeepney Operator: One of the significant challenges we face is the increasing cost of fuel, which affects our daily earnings. Another challenge is the competition among jeepney operators, which leads to fare cuts and reduced income. To cope with these challenges, we try to maximize our daily trips and maintain our jeepneys to ensure that they are in good condition.

Interviewer: Lastly, how do you think the government can support jeepney operators in their livelihood?

Jeepney Operator: The government can help by providing financial assistance to jeepney operators who want to modernize their units. Also, they can regulate the fare and provide incentives to those who comply with the regulations. Lastly, they can provide training and education to jeepney operators to help them adapt to new technologies and become more competitive in the market.

Interviewer: Thank you so much for your insights, Mr. Dela Cruz. Your inputs will be a valuable contribution to our study.

25. make baby thesis about The effect of different levels of chicken name on survival growth of pomelo seedling​


Answer:

Title: The Effect of Different Levels of Chicken Manure on Survival and Growth of Pomelo Seedlings

Introduction:

Pomelo (Citrus maxima) is a popular fruit tree in many parts of the world due to its delicious fruit and health benefits. However, like any other plant, pomelo seedlings require proper nutrients and care for optimal growth and survival. One of the important nutrients needed by plants is nitrogen, which is essential for growth and development. Chicken manure is an excellent source of nitrogen, and it also contains other essential nutrients that can promote the growth and health of plants.

Objective:

The objective of this study is to determine the effect of different levels of chicken manure on the survival and growth of pomelo seedlings.

Methodology:

The study will be conducted in a randomized complete block design (RCBD) with four treatments and five replications. The treatments are as follows: T1 (Control), T2 (2% chicken manure), T3 (4% chicken manure), and T4 (6% chicken manure). The pomelo seeds will be germinated in a nursery for 3 weeks before transplanting to plastic pots filled with soil and chicken manure mixtures. The growth parameters such as plant height, stem diameter, and leaf area will be measured weekly for a period of 8 weeks. The data collected will be analyzed using analysis of variance (ANOVA) and means separated using Duncan's multiple range test.

Expected Results:

It is expected that the use of chicken manure as a source of nitrogen will have a positive effect on the growth and survival of pomelo seedlings. It is also expected that the growth and survival rate of the pomelo seedlings will increase as the level of chicken manure increases.

Conclusion:

The results of this study will provide valuable information on the use of chicken manure as a source of nitrogen for pomelo seedlings. The findings will be beneficial to farmers and gardeners who are interested in growing healthy pomelo trees. The study will also highlight the importance of proper fertilization and care for the growth and survival of pomelo seedlings.

30. please can someone help how to make baby thesis my topic is all about “The effect of different levels of chicken name on survival growth of pomelo seedling"​


Sure! I can provide you with some guidance on how to make a baby thesis with the topic "The effect of different levels of chicken manure on survival and growth of pomelo seedlings." Here's a basic outline that you can follow:

1. Introduction

- Provide a brief overview of pomelo and its significance as a fruit tree.

- Introduce the concept of using chicken manure as a potential fertilizer for pomelo seedlings.

- State the purpose of your study and your research question, such as "What is the effect of different levels of chicken manure on the survival and growth of pomelo seedlings?"

2. Literature Review

- Review relevant literature on the topic, including previous research and studies related to the effects of different types of fertilizer on plant growth and survival.

- Discuss the potential benefits and limitations of using chicken manure as a fertilizer for pomelo seedlings based on existing literature.

3. Methodology

- Describe the materials and methods you used in your study, including the experimental design, sample size, and data collection procedures.

- Explain how you applied different levels of chicken manure to the pomelo seedlings, and any controls or treatments that you used.

- Provide details on how you measured survival and growth of the pomelo seedlings, including any data collection tools or techniques used.

4. Results

- Present your findings in a clear and organized manner, using tables, graphs, or other visual aids to display your data.

- Interpret your results and discuss any trends, patterns, or statistical significance that you observed.

- Address any limitations of your study and potential sources of error.

5. Discussion

- Discuss the implications of your findings and how they relate to your research question.

- Compare your results with previous research and studies in the field.

- Provide explanations for any unexpected results and suggest possible areas for further research.

- Conclude with a summary of your main findings and their significance.

6. Conclusion

- Provide a concise summary of your study's main findings and their implications.

- Restate your research question and state whether your results support or refute your hypothesis.

- Offer recommendations for future research or practical applications of your findings.

7. References

- List all the sources you cited in your thesis using an appropriate citation format, such as APA or MLA.

Remember to follow the guidelines and requirements set by your institution or supervisor for formatting, structure, and other specific details for your baby thesis. Good luck with your research!

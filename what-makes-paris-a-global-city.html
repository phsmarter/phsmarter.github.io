Answer:

5 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

Convert 900,000 meters to kilometers

900,000 m = ___ km

900,000 m ÷ 1,000 = ___ km

900,000 m = 900 km

Then divide 900 by 180

900 km ÷ 180 km/hr = 5 hrs

Lola takes 5 hours traveling from

Paris to Nice

Answer:

5 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

First, covert the distance of meters (m) to kilometers (km).

We know that 1000 m = 1 km.

Therefore, 900,000 / 1,000 = 900 km

Since the train travels 180 km/h. We can divide it from 900 to get the time.

So, 900 / 180 = 5 hours.

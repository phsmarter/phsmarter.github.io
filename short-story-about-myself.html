17. short essay about how do i see myself 10 years from now​


I See Myself 10 years From Now:​

Maybe I can make myself in the next 10 years as a person who has achieved all dreams. Or at least close to the realization of dreams. My imagination runs wild and becomes.

EXPLANATION:

Long enough I was silent. It's not that I don't have goals or dreams. Not that I failed to visualize. Fate is often a hindrance. I can't get out of the shadows. It's not that I don't want to and I can't, but I really can't, maybe there's a reason. Excuses are easy to make. There's only one thing that can't: family. It's a cliche excuse for my inability to conquer some challenges.

We don't have the right to choose who we are born with, who we raise and how many we give birth to and raise. They are the powerful fabric that made us who we are today. So unique that no one human being in this world is exactly the same. Imagination is formed. Well I immediately wrote a letter to him before it dispersed.

Dear Me,

This is me, my hopes, my dreams, my thoughts, on December 29, 2022. If the postal arrangements are correct and according to the rules, then you will receive it in 2032. It doesn't matter the date. No. I'm not that tight in thinking.

I imagine you are sitting in a cabin under a tree looking out at the river near you. A river with bends and rocks that are still not colored by the rainbow. Your house is made of wood with your sacred architecture. In your hand is a device whose image moves dynamically, showing the image of a large university. There are your two children. The middle of college at his brother's work. I have a hard time imagining the two kids apart. I envision myself being a successful entrepreneur.

13. Write the happiest moment of your life​


Feelings of pleasure or the happiest moment in my life for me is actually in general in simple things. I don't have a complicated definition of pleasure. Because for me, pleasure is something that is very cheap and should be accessible to anyone, not only me or those who have certain strengths or abilities. So that everyone should be equally happy.

Even though it's not complicated, that doesn't mean I don't have special moments that make me feel good. These 3 particular moment certainly won't be the same for anyone else. So the list of 3 happiest moment in my life are:

1. Being in the wild

I am a person who really enjoys moments of solitude that are separated from the hustle and bustle of social interactions among human beings. Being isolated in an environment I didn't recognize at all. And those moments often lie in the wild. Where I will be able to interact directly or indirectly with the inhabitants of nature. Animals, plants, sounds, sunlight, or the twinkling stars at night. All those natural elements always manage to make me happy and smile.

2. Seeing and hearing children playing

I only realized this moment when I was growing up, when I was often gloomy and silent playing with my head. The children who can often be found in the playgrounds have a unique atmosphere that is contagious for those around them, including me. Seeing these children is what made me love child-friendly places. Because that's where my friends will always be able to see me smiling alone.

3. Remembering, thinking and praying for friends

For this last point, it is a new fun and happy moment that is starting to become a habit. Because you could say recently I have a friend that I care so much about. In my travels so far, I don't really have many close friends. This habit then made me addicted. I feel so happy even though it's only an indirect mental activity about friends who then unconsciously continue to make me happy and carve lines of smiles at every moment of the chant.

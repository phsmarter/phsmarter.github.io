Since he only has 14 eggs to distribute, and he needs to give two eggs to each person, he would need a total of 2 x 12 = 24 eggs. Therefore, he does not have enough eggs to give two eggs to each person.

In this case, he could either decide to give one egg to each person, which would require a total of 12 eggs, leaving him with 2 eggs left over. Or, he could choose to give 2 eggs to each father and 1 egg to each son, which would require a total of 18 eggs, leaving him with 4 eggs left over.

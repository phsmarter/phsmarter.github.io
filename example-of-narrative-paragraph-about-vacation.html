28. essay about Christmas vacation? example ​


My Essay About Christmas Vacation

My last Christmas Vacation was considered to be the best of all. I have to spend the holidays with my family. We normally doesn't get a chance to stay together since we are busy to our personal, career, academic lives. I have two siblings whom both are working already. My parents are handling a family business. Seeing them all present for the holiday is a huge thing for me. We started our day with a mass. After we said thanks to our Lord, we directly go to our house to prepare for the Christmas Eve. Me and my sister accompanied our Mom to the market and shop for the ingredients she needed for the foods. Dad and my brother stayed at home to bring up Christmas decors.  

Night time, everybody is at the table. We ate together. Everybody has their own personal stories to share while we eat. Mostly about their lives for the year. Mom shared how the business is growing. Sister shared about her work and brother shared how he is becoming good when it comes to his new hobby - biking. He wanted me to join him if only I have a bike. We exchanged gifts for each member. I got a new shirt and a cellphone. Mom had a new rice cooker. Dad, he got a shaver. While bro have a new bike helmet. This is the best Christmas ever.

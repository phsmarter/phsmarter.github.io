10. Could you imagine a day without technology? What would you do? Do you think we rely on technology too much? Has technology replaced the face to face communication? Take time to discuss the strengths and weakness of not having technology in your life.​


Answer:

A day without technology may be challenging for some people as we rely on it for many aspects of our lives such as communication, work, entertainment, and daily tasks. Without technology, we may need to resort to more traditional methods, such as reading books, writing letters, and doing things manually. We may also need to rely more on face-to-face communication and social interactions.

In terms of whether we rely on technology too much, it depends on the individual and their circumstances. Technology has certainly made our lives easier and more efficient in many ways, but it is important to find a balance and not let technology consume our lives.

Regarding whether technology has replaced face-to-face communication, it is true that technology has provided us with new forms of communication such as texting, video calls, and social media. However, it has not entirely replaced face-to-face communication as it is still important for building and maintaining relationships, and for effective communication in certain situations.

The strengths of not having technology in our lives may include more time spent engaging in physical activity, more time spent with family and friends, and the opportunity to disconnect from the constant distractions of technology. On the other hand, the weaknesses may include difficulties in communication and productivity, reliance on manual methods for daily tasks, and limited access to information and entertainment.

Overall, technology has both strengths and weaknesses, and it is up to individuals to find a balance that works for them in their personal and professional lives

21. ENGLISH ESSAY: Too much dependence on computer and technology. Atleast 350 wordsBrainliest ko sino makasagot ​


Answer:

In today's world, it is undeniable that computers and technology play a crucial role in our daily lives. From the moment we wake up and check our phones to the time we go to bed, we are constantly surrounded by and interacting with technology. While this reliance on technology has brought many benefits and conveniences, it has also created a situation where many people are overly dependent on computers and other forms of technology.

One of the main problems with too much dependence on computer and technology is that it can lead to a lack of critical thinking skills. When we rely on technology to do things for us, we can become passive and lose the ability to think for ourselves. For example, if we always use a GPS to navigate, we may not develop a good sense of direction and may become lost without it. Similarly, if we always use spell check and grammar check, we may not develop good writing skills.

Another issue with excessive reliance on technology is that it can lead to isolation and disconnection from the world around us. With the rise of social media and other forms of online communication, many people have become more isolated from their friends and family. They may spend hours each day scrolling through their feeds, but not actually interacting with others in person. This lack of real-world interaction can lead to feelings of loneliness and isolation.

Additionally, too much dependence on computer and technology can also have negative effects on our physical health. Spending long hours sitting in front of a computer screen can lead to problems like back pain, eye strain, and even obesity. It can also disrupt our sleep patterns and lead to fatigue and other health issues.

Overall, while computer and technology have brought many benefits, it is important to be aware of the potential downsides of relying on them too much. It is crucial to strike a balance between using technology to make our lives easier and not becoming overly dependent on it. By developing critical thinking skills, staying connected to the world around us, and taking care of our physical health, we can avoid the negative effects of too much dependence on computer and technology.

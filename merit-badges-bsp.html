17. the game gave a red badge if you svored near 60 and blue badge if you scored near 70. what badge would carlos get if he scored 65? round 65 to the nearest ten.​


Answer:

Carlos will get the blue badge.

Step-by-step explanation:

To know whether 65 is nearer to 60 or 70, you must round it off. To round it to the nearest ten:

*Analyze the digit before the tens place value, (5)

If it is 4 and below, do nothing.

If it is 5 and above, add 1 to the tens place value

The 65 will be 75.

*Next, make the numbers before the place value 0.

The 75 will be 70.

Therefore, Carlos will get the blue badge.

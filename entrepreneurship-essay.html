9. write an essay how important the salient features in entrepreneurship to become a successful entrepreneur.​


There are a few key things that are essential for any entrepreneur to have in order to be successful. These include: strong business acumen, creativity, and determination. Additionally, entrepreneurship is often defined by the ability to take risks and be innovative. Therefore, these three qualities are essential not only for becoming an entrepreneur, but also for continued success.

If these qualities are not in place, it can be difficult to maintain a successful business. For example, many entrepreneurs who have high levels of creativity and determination often find themselves struggling when it comes to taking risks. This is because they may not know how to apply their knowledge and skills in a new way.

In conclusion, it is important for any entrepreneur to have strong business acumen and creativity as well as determination. These qualities will help the individual to be able to take risks and come up with innovative ways to improve their businesses. Additionally, it is important for entrepreneurs to have a clear understanding of what they are trying to achieve when starting their business. This will help them be more successful in achieving their goals.

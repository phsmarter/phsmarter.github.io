27. When ministry of micro small and medium enterprises in india was established?

Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (M/O MSME), Government of India was formed after merging of the erstwhile Ministry of Small Scale Industries and the Ministry of Agro and Rural Industries on 9 May 2007.

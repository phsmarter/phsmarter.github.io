28. 1. It is the way of examining pieces of literature using different approaches. A. Book review C. Short Story analysis D. Character sketch B. Literary criticism​


Answer:

B. Literary Criticism

Explanation:

Literary criticism is the practice of studying, evaluating, and interpreting works of literature. Similar to literary theory, which provides a broader philosophical framework for how to analyze literature, literary criticism offers readers new ways to understand an author's work.Literary criticism is the comparison, analysis, interpretation, and/or evaluation of works of literature. Literary criticism is essentially an opinion, supported by evidence, relating to theme, style, setting or historical or political context.

26. creative writing vs. scientific writing​


Answer:

What is difference between technical writing and creative writing?

Technical writing focuses on factual and straight forward content. Creative writing focuses on imaginative and symbolic content. Technical writing has its specific reader/audience. Creative writing has general reader/audience.

Explanation:


27. pa help po creative writing vs technical writing


Forms: Technical writing is primarily used to provide instructions, explain complex concepts, and convey factual information in a clear and concise manner, while creative writing focuses on self-expression and artistic storytelling.Objectives: The objective of technical writing is to convey information and instructions clearly and accurately, while the objective of creative writing is to engage and entertain the reader with imaginative and artistic expression.Content: Technical writing is focused on conveying information and data, while creative writing is focused on storytelling, character development, and emotional content.Audience: The audience for technical writing is usually professionals or individuals who need specific information or instructions, while the audience for creative writing is usually general readers who are looking for entertainment or emotional connection.Diction: Technical writing uses precise and technical language that is specific to the field or topic, while creative writing uses descriptive and expressive language to create mood, tone, and atmosphere.Structure: Technical writing is structured logically and systematically, while creative writing is structured to evoke emotional responses and create a sense of narrative flow.

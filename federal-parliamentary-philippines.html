2. ano ang federal parliamentary republic​


Answer:

In english:A federal parliamentary republic refers to a federation of states with a republican form of government that is, more or less, dependent upon the confidence of parliaments at both the national and sub-national levels. It is a combination of the government republic and the parliamentary republic.

24. ang ang katangian ng constititional monarchy,absolute monarchy,parliamentary democrasy,constitutional hereditary monarchy,presidential parliamentary,federal parliamentary and islamic theocratic republic at ibigay ang mga bansang nakapa loob dito.PLEASE EXPLAIN ONE BY ONE^_^​


Answer:

Constitutional monarchy

A constitutional monarchy is a form of monarchy in which the sovereign exercises authority in accordance with a written or unwritten constitution.

Absolute Monarchy

Absolute monarchy is a form of monarchy in which the monarch holds supreme autocratic authority, principally not being restricted by written laws, legislature, or customs.

Parliamentary Democracy

Parliamentary democracy is a system of government in which citizens elect representatives to a legislative parliament to make the necessary laws and decisions for the country.

Constitutional Hereditary Monarchy

A constitutional monarchy is a form of monarchical government established under a constitutional system which acknowledges an elected or hereditary monarch as head of state.

Presidential Parliamentary

Under the president-parliamentary system, the prime minister and cabinet are dually accountable to the president and the parliament.

Federal Parliamentary

A federal parliamentary republic refers to a federation of states with a republican form of government that is, more or less, dependent upon the confidence of parliaments at both the national and sub-national levels.

Islamic Theocratic Republic

An Islamic republic is the name given to several states that are officially ruled by Islamic laws, including the Islamic Republics of Afghanistan, Iran, Pakistan, and Mauritania.

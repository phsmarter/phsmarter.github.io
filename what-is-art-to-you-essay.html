7. What do you consider art to be? List some of the benefits of arts in terms of society's social structure. Having facts to back up your claims will make your essay​


Answer:

Art can be defined as a form of creative expression that is typically appreciated for its beauty, emotional impact, or aesthetic value. While the definition of art can be subjective and vary across cultures and individuals, it is widely recognized as a fundamental aspect of human culture and society.

One of the primary benefits of art in terms of society's social structure is its ability to promote cultural understanding and communication. Art serves as a medium for sharing ideas, values, and experiences across different communities and can help to bridge cultural divides. Additionally, art has the potential to foster empathy and understanding by providing a unique perspective on different aspects of life.

Art also plays a significant role in promoting social change and advocacy. Through visual or performance art, artists can raise awareness about important social issues and inspire positive change. For example, the Black Lives Matter movement has utilized art as a means of expressing their message and advocating for social justice.

Moreover, art has been shown to have a positive impact on mental health and wellbeing. Art therapy has been used to treat a variety of conditions, including depression, anxiety, and post-traumatic stress disorder. By engaging in creative expression, individuals can tap into their emotions and process difficult experiences.

In terms of education, art can provide numerous benefits to students. Studies have shown that exposure to art can improve critical thinking skills, creativity, and academic achievement. Art education can also promote self-expression, confidence, and self-esteem in students.

Finally, the art industry contributes significantly to the economy, creating jobs and generating revenue. Artistic endeavors such as music, film, and visual arts contribute to cultural tourism and provide entertainment for individuals worldwide.

23. What I Have Learned In 250 words, make an essay/letter on the different artworks of the respective periods with the elements and principle used of art style of a western and classical art. You will be assessed through a rubric.​


Answer:

Visual art manifests itself through media, ideas, themes and sheer creative imagination. Yet all of these rely on basic structural principles that, like the elements we’ve been studying, combine to give voice to artistic expression. Incorporating the principles into your artistic vocabulary not only allows you to objectively describe artworks you may not understand, but contributes in the search for their meaning.

The first way to think about a principle is that it is something that can be repeatedly and dependably done with elements to produce some sort of visual effect in a composition.

The principles are based on sensory responses to visual input: elements APPEAR to have visual weight, movement, etc. The principles help govern what might occur when particular elements are arranged in a particular way. Using a chemistry analogy, the principles are the ways the elements “stick together” to make a “chemical” (in our case, an image). Principles can be confusing. There are at least two very different but correct ways of thinking about principles. On the one hand, a principle can be used to describe an operational cause and effect such as “bright things come forward and dull things recede”. On the other hand, a principle can describe a high quality standard to strive for such as “unity is better than chaos” or “variation beats boredom” in a work of art. So, the word “principle” can be used for very different purposes.

Another way to think about a principle is that it is a way to express a value judgment about a composition. Any list of these effects may not be comprehensive, but there are some that are more commonly used (unity, balance, etc). When we say a painting has unity we are making a value judgment. Too much unity without variety is boring and too much variation without unity is chaotic.

The principles of design help you to carefully plan and organize the elements of art so that you will hold interest and command attention. This is sometimes referred to as visual impact.

In any work of art there is a thought process for the arrangement and use of the elements of design. The artist who works with the principles of good composition will create a more interesting piece; it will be arranged to show a pleasing rhythm and movement. The center of interest will be strong and the viewer will not look away, instead, they will be drawn into the work. A good knowledge of composition is essential in producing good artwork. Some artists today like to bend or ignore these rules and by doing so are experimenting with different forms of expression. The following page explore important principles in composition.

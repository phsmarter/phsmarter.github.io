Body:

*Discuss individual actions that can help reduce global warming, such as using energy-efficient appliances and transportation, reducing energy consumption, and conserving water

When the United States ceased their Philippine colonization, they did not only leave the legacy of basketball, burgers, and, Hollywood movies. They left us a universal language, which is English.

Personally learning the language made it possible to read American classics, British tales, and, other kinds of foreign literature. Plus, it gave me confidence that I could easily communicate with other nationalities from online chatting to vacationing and studying abroad. In these places, there are more well-off people, and, you can find international citizens in the locale.

Moreover, I noticed that there is a common connotation with speaking Taglish, which means a person is sosyal or associated with being rich and well-educated. This could equally mean that English fluency represents class and education. This is alarming yet no surprise for me. After all, the colonial mentality is strong in the Philippines.

However, despite the fact that English could threaten our Filipino pride, I believe that is key to achieving globalization because you cannot speak Tagalog in international diplomatic talks. You cannot woo investors with Cebuano or study abroad with only Hiligaynon in mind. You cannot order food in Ilocano when you're vacationing in France. In other words, we can vocally make our presence worldwide.

Nevertheless, as we speak in American slang, we must not forget to keep the Filipino pride burning through our dialects and traditions.

11. Essay Writing - Problem Solution Pattern 1. Climate change and global warming is a serious threats the world is facing, which is reducing the average life expectancy. What are some causes of this? How can governments and healthcare sectors solve this problem? You will make 3 paragraphs (introduction, body, and conclusion)​


Answer:

Introduction:

Climate change and global warming are two interrelated phenomena that are posing a serious threat to our planet. The impact of these two problems is so significant that they are gradually reducing the average life expectancy of individuals. While the root causes of these problems may be complex and multifaceted, some significant factors include industrialization, deforestation, and overpopulation. In this essay, we will discuss the causes of climate change and global warming, as well as the measures that governments and healthcare sectors can take to solve this problem.

Body:

One of the primary causes of climate change and global warming is the increasing level of industrialization across the globe. The more factories and production facilities are established, the more fossil fuels are burnt, and consequently, the more greenhouse gases are released into the atmosphere. This, in turn, leads to the greenhouse effect, causing a rise in global temperatures. Another significant factor is deforestation, as trees play a crucial role in regulating the climate. When forests are cleared to make room for human activities such as agriculture and urbanization, the carbon-absorbing trees are destroyed, contributing to global warming. Finally, overpopulation is another significant cause of climate change and global warming, as the rising number of people on the planet leads to increased demand for resources and energy, which often results in environmental degradation.

To solve the problem of climate change and global warming, governments and healthcare sectors can take several measures. First, governments can implement regulations and policies that aim to reduce carbon emissions and promote cleaner energy sources, such as wind and solar power. Second, healthcare sectors can develop and implement programs to educate individuals about the importance of reducing their carbon footprint and taking small steps towards environmental conservation. Finally, governments and healthcare sectors can work together to promote sustainable development and environmental conservation, which can help to curb the negative impact of climate change.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, climate change and global warming are serious threats that the world is facing, and they are reducing the average life expectancy of individuals. Industrialization, deforestation, and overpopulation are some of the primary causes of these problems. However, by implementing regulations and policies that aim to reduce carbon emissions, promoting sustainable development, and educating individuals about the importance of environmental conservation, governments and healthcare sectors can play a crucial role in solving this problem

Answer:

Media and information literacy enables people to interpret and make informed decisions as users of information and media, as well as to become skilled creators and producers of their own informationgiphy and media messages. MIL is a term that combines media literacy and information literacy under one umbrella and refers to a set of competencies. Media literacy emphasizes the ability to understand media functions, evaluate how they are performed, and use them for self-expression, whereas information literacy emphasizes the importance of access to information as well as its evaluation and ethical use. It acknowledges the centrality of information and media in our daily lives.

It emphasizes free expression and information because it empowers citizens to understand the functions of media and other information providers, evaluate their content, and make informed criticism as users and producers of information and media content.

Traditionally, information literacy and media literacy have been considered separate fields. It is the strategy that combines these two fields as a set of competencies (knowledge, skills, and attitude) required for today's life and work. MIL takes into account all forms of media and other information providers, including libraries, archives, museums, the Internet, and other technology.

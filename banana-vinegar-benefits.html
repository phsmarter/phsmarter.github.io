12. 4. What process is involved in the Banana vinegar activity?​


BANANA VINEGAR PRODUCTION

What process is involved in the Banana vinegar activity?

ANSWER

The process involved in the Banana vinegar activity/production are mainly enzymolysis, fermentation, acetic acidization and filtration.

Enzymolysis is the process of biochemical decomposition, it is associated with fermentation. Filtration on the other hand is the process of filtering or separating two or more substances.

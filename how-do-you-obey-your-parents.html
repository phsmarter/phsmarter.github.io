2. how do you respect and obey your parents​


Answer:

Explanation:

So, you can respect your parents at any age. It’s a little easier when you’re a kid, but as you grow older, you want to be more independent and show your parents you can do it yourself. As a teenager, respect might include listening to your parents and offering to help them. It may mean not demanding your own way all the time, and even being courteous.

Ask them how they are from time to time. This shows that you care by checking if they are OK, really OK. Sometimes they do not want children to get upset about them, so they say they are fine.

No matter how old, or ugly, or whatever undesirable looks your parents have, be proud of them and do not humiliate them. Walk beside them and if they cannot walk properly due to illness or old age, assist them. When you were young and were beginning to walk, they gave you their hands.

Never shout at them, if they commit mistakes or if they forget many things, or if they move slowly. Be more tolerant, more loving, more compassionate and more understanding than before. When they were young, they sacrificed their best years for you and/or your siblings. Talk to them in a very calm and caring way, because it is what they need.

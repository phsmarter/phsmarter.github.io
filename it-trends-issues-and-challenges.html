18. I realized that health trend, issues and concerns in the global level:I discovered that health trend, issues and concerns in the global level:​


There are several health trends, issues, and concerns in the global level, including:

COVID-19 pandemic: The ongoing pandemic has affected people worldwide, leading to numerous deaths, overwhelmed healthcare systems, and economic downturns.

Non-communicable diseases (NCDs): NCDs, such as cardiovascular diseases, cancer, and diabetes, are responsible for the majority of deaths worldwide, with risk factors including unhealthy diets, physical inactivity, and tobacco and alcohol use.

Mental health: Mental health concerns, including depression, anxiety, and substance abuse, are prevalent globally, with the COVID-19 pandemic exacerbating these issues for many individuals.

Climate change: The effects of climate change, such as extreme weather events, air pollution, and food and water insecurity, have significant impacts on global health.

Aging population: As populations continue to age globally, health concerns related to age, such as dementia, become increasingly prevalent.

Access to healthcare: Despite efforts to improve access to healthcare globally, many individuals still lack access to essential healthcare services, particularly in low- and middle-income countries.

Health disparities: Health disparities, including differences in health outcomes based on race, ethnicity, and socioeconomic status, continue to be a significant issue globally

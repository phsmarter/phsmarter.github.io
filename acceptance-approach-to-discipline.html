30. How do you apply the 5s approach (Sorting, systematizing, sweeping sanitizing, and self-discipline) in your home?​


Answer:

The 5S approach is a method used to organize and maintain a clean and efficient workplace. However, it can also be applied in a home setting to help improve efficiency and reduce clutter. Here are some ways you can apply the 5S approach in your home:

Sorting: Go through all your belongings and categorize them into things you use regularly, things you use occasionally, and things you no longer need. Get rid of items you no longer need by donating or selling them.

Systematizing: Create a designated spot for everything in your home. This will help you find things easily and reduce clutter. Use storage solutions such as shelves, containers, and drawers to help organize your belongings.

Sweeping: Clean your home thoroughly,

Answer:

The 5s approach is a great way to keep your home clean and organized. To sort, simply put things in order so that they are easy to find and use. Systematize by creating a place for everything and putting things back in their place after use. Sweep by regularly cleaning surfaces and removing dust and debris. Sanitize by disinfecting surfaces to kill germs. And self-discipline by following these steps on a regular basis to maintain a clean and organized home.

2. Sino si elizabeth barreth brownning


Born in County Durham, the eldest of 12 children, Elizabeth Barrett wrote poetry from about the age of six. Her mother's collection of her poems forms one of the largest extant collections of juvenilia by any English writer. At 15 she became ill, suffering intense head and spinal pain for the rest of her life. Later in life she also developed lung problems, possibly tuberculosis. She took laudanum for the pain from an early age, which is likely to have contributed to her frail health.

Answer:

5 cups

Step-by-step explanation:

If each cup of brown sugar is accompanied by a cup of white sugar, then the total amount of sugar used in making caramel pande coco is 1 cup brown sugar + 1 cup white sugar = 2 cups of sugar.

To find out how many cups of white sugar are needed for 5 cups of brown sugar, we can set up a proportion:

1 cup white sugar / 1 cup brown sugar = x cups white sugar / 5 cups brown sugar

Simplifying this proportion, we get:

x cups white sugar = 5 cups brown sugar * (1 cup white sugar / 1 cup brown sugar)

x cups white sugar = 5 cups white sugar

Therefore, 5 cups of white sugar are required for 5 cups of brown sugar to make caramel pande coco.

1. 1. Differentiate between invasion games from modern games.​


GAMES

INVASION GAMES

Invasion games, refers to the sort of team games in which the main objective is to invade the opponents' area or zone in order to score or make a point. The opposing teams will try to defend their territories to prevent the other team from scoring.

» Examples are Basketball, Soccer and Rugby.

MODERN GAMES

Modern games on the other hand, are games that mainly requires gadgets and are mostly technology related or computer based. These are virtual and usually doesn't require physical exertion unlike any other games.

Explanation:

The Olympic motto “Citius, Altius, Fortius” (“Faster, Higher, Stronger”) was coined by Father Henri Didon, who was a close friend of Baron Pierre de Coubertin. It was adopted by the IOC in 1894.

Answer:

Citius, Altius, Fortius” is the motto of the Olympic Games; an event that symbolizes unity and exuberance of the human spirit.

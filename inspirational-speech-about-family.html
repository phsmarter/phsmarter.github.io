1. inspiration in life speechabout family, friends, etc​


SPEECH

Dear friends,

I am honored to speak to you today about inspiration in life. For me, inspiration comes from many sources, but most importantly, it comes from my family and friends.

My family has always been a constant source of support and love for me. From the moment I was born, they have been there for me, cheering me on and helping me through all of life's challenges. They have taught me the value of hard work and determination, and have instilled in me a sense of resilience and perseverance.

My friends have also been a source of inspiration for me. They have helped me to see the world in a different light, and have challenged me to think outside of the box. They have supported me through the highs and lows, and have always been there to lend an ear or a helping hand.

But my inspiration doesn't just come from those closest to me. It also comes from the world around me - from nature, from art, and from people who have overcome great challenges and achieved great things.

In short, my inspiration comes from all around me - from the people I love, from the world I live in, and from the dreams and goals that drive me forward.

I hope that, as you go through life, you too can find inspiration in the people and things that matter most to you. Thank you.

Answer:

Dear fellow students,

I stand before you today to talk about something that is incredibly important to me: the people who inspire me in my life. For me, that includes my family, my friends, and the mentors and role models I've had the privilege of encountering along the way.

First and foremost, I am constantly inspired by my family. My parents have always been my biggest supporters and have taught me the value of hard work, determination, and unconditional love. Whether we're laughing together, exploring new places, or simply having a heart-to-heart conversation, my friends bring joy and positivity into my life every single day.

Finally, I am grateful for the mentors and role models who have shaped me into the person I am today. These are the people who have challenged me, guided me, and believed in me even when I didn't believe in myself. In conclusion, my family, friends, and mentors are the people who inspire me the most in my life. They are the ones who have helped me to become the person I am today and who continue to push me to be the best that I can be. Thank you for being a part of my journey.

6. Speech about nutirion month inspirational


In recognition of Nutrition Month, I rise today to talk about and acknowledge something our school is taking very seriously – the health and wellness of our studentry, faculty and staff, and those who are concerned of healthy living and lifestyle.


An inspiring nutritious morning to all of us.

One should never underestimate the importance of eating healthy food. The benefits of eating a healthy diet include living a longer life, feeling happier, gaining vitality, weight loss, and a healthy appearance. Eating healthy foods means we can better fight diseases and live longer. When it comes to happiness, studies have shown that eating healthy foods is an excellent natural anti-depressant. When you eat healthy your energy levels tend to rise.

As we celebrate today this year's Nutrition Month celebration, with the theme"Pagkain ng gulay ugaliin, araw-araw itong ihain!" The theme encourages us to increase consumption of fruits and vegetables to address micronutrient deficiencies and prevent non-communicable diseases such as cardiovascular disease, cancer and diabetes. You should try to eat as much fruits and vegetables as possible throughout the day.

As an institution, we can and we will provide actions and applications for children to become better equipped to lead healthier lives. Let’s surge forward of achieving a healthy-living school.

Today, I would like to issue a challenge to everyone to infuse a healthy culture in life – inside and outside the school premises. We all have the power to take real actions to improve our health. I encourage you to look for opportunities each and every day to make an improvement of your own health.

In addition, I would like to extend my warmest congratulations to everyone for a job well done. The success of our celebrations marks a milestone of history.

May you have a healthy living and continue living healthy.

Thank you very much and God bless us more!

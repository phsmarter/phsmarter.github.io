Answer:

MY GREATEST CHALLENGE

"As a teen, I am used to see my friends almost daily which is five days in a week because we go to the same school and most of them are my classmates. Recently, what I am used to is not happening anymore because of the pandemic, a common health problem that is experienced by everybody in the world. It is a great challenge to me, not seeing my friends.I really miss my friends because they are all benevolent especially to elders, they never forget to be kind at all times. In class, my friends and I were reserved but the other classmates are noisy. Today, I need to wake up early and do the chores like sweeping the floor, fixing my bed and washing my own clothes.

TYPES OF CONTEXT CLUES

DEFINITION. The meaning of the word is written next to it.

Pandemic, a common health problem that is experienced by everybody in the world.

SYNONYM. The word having the same meaning of a certain word is also found in the same sentence.

I really miss my friends because they are all benevolent especially to elders, they never forget to be kind at all times.

ANTONYM. So you can get the meaning of a certain word, an antonym appears in the later part of a sentence as a clue.

In class, my friends and I were reserved but the other classmates are noisy.

EXAMPLE. Some clues appear as examples for an easy grip of the meaning of an unfamiliar word.

Today, I need to wake up early and do the chores like sweeping the floor, fixing my bed and washing my own clothes.

2. WHAT IS BIODIVERSITY AND HEALTHY SOCIETY?


Answer:

ambot lang dimanko Earth

Answer:

Biodiversity refers to the variety of life on earth, including the diversity of species, ecosystems, and genetic diversity. Biodiversity is important because it provides a wide range of services that are essential for human well-being, such as the provision of food, clean air and water, and medicinal resources. It also plays a critical role in supporting the earth's ecosystems and regulating the planet's climate.

A healthy society, on the other hand, is one that is characterized by the well-being and overall health of its members. This includes not only physical health but also social, emotional, and mental health. A healthy society is one in which individuals have access to the resources they need to live fulfilling and satisfying lives, including access to health care, education, and social support.

Biodiversity and healthy societies are interconnected because healthy societies rely on the services provided by biodiversity to maintain the health and well-being of their populations. Biodiversity can provide important resources for human health, such as the development of new medicines, while also contributing to overall ecological stability and sustainability. Conversely, when biodiversity is lost or degraded, it can have negative impacts on human health and well-being, such as through the spread of infectious diseases.

Therefore, protecting and preserving biodiversity is crucial for maintaining a healthy society, as it ensures the continued provision of essential resources and services for human well-being. At the same time, building healthy societies that promote the well-being of their populations can help to ensure that the impacts of human activity on biodiversity are minimized and that the natural systems that support life on earth are protected for future generations.

25. what is the relationship between a healthy watershed and rich biodiversity


Watershed is an area of land that catches rainfall and other precipitates, and drains the water off to rivers, lakes, streams and other bodies of water.

Meanwhile, biodiversity refers to the variability among all living organisms in a certain area. An area with a rich biodiversity contains a high number of different species with high genetic variability. A rich biodiversity ensures the survival of a given ecosystem. Environmental changes or diseases can be overcome by a diversed population since there is a higher likelihood of having organisms with genetic traits that can adapt to such changes, thus lesser chance of extinction and more chance of adaptation.

A watershed is an ecosystem composed of living organisms including plants and animals. As explained above, a rich biodiversity indicates a healthy ecosystem. The richer the diversity is in a watershed, the healthier it becomes.

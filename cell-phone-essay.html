3. should cell phone allowed in school(200 word essay)​


Answer:

The use of cell phones in schools has been a controversial topic for many years. Supporters argue that cell phones can be used as educational tools, while opponents believe that they can be a source of distraction for students.

While it is true that cell phones can provide access to educational resources, such as online textbooks and research databases, their potential to distract students is a significant concern. Cell phones can be a source of temptation for students to engage in non-educational activities, such as playing games or scrolling through social media. This can lead to a decline in academic performance and a disruption of class.

Additionally, the use of cell phones in schools can create inequality among students who may not have access to the latest technology or may not be able to afford a cell phone. This could lead to a sense of exclusion and unfairness among students.

In conclusion, while the use of cell phones in schools can have some benefits, such as access to educational resources, the potential for distraction and inequality outweigh these benefits. Therefore, it is recommended that schools establish clear policies on the use of cell phones in the classroom to minimize their negative impact on students' academic performance and social dynamics.

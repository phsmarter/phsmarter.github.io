19. pepsi have 5 letters now spell that in 4 letters


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Brain Teaser

What is a brain teaser?

Brain teaser is a form of puzzle. It requires logical and lateral thinking, or thinking “outside the box” to figure out the answer.

Let us analyze the given brain teaser above.

"Pepsi have 5 letters now spell that in 4 letters."

Some answered P-E-P-C thinking that we need to spell "Pepsi" in four letters but that is wrong.

The answer here is T-H-A-T because it is simply asking us to spell the word "that".

Rephrase the sentence.

"Pepsi has 5 letters, now spell that in 4 letters."

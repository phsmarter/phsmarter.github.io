2. Explain the definition and importance of courtship, dating and behavior that promote healthy relationship in marriage and family life​


Courtship is the real process of wooing someone by letting them see who you truly are as they do the same for you. If done correctly, courtship can lead to the fulfilling and loving relationship you may be seeking, and it can even prevent a lifetime of unexpected consequences and emotional turmoil. Courtship offers a trial period to determine whether or not the couple is really ready for marriage, if they really love each other. It is a discussion period to test each others character and personality. It was during this period that the couple should be able to determine whether it is wise for them to marry.

Abstract

Marx and Weber devoted little space to the discussion of class, but the importance of that theme in their work is well known. The present paper contrasts the Marxian argument concerning the foundation of class in the organization of production with Weber's greater emphasis upon status-differences and organized collective action. 14. 90% of high school students are not interested in a career-involving STEM. If there are 30 students in a class, how many of them are NOT interested in pursuing a career STEM?


Answer:

27 students

Step-by-step explanation:

n is 90% of 30

Given:

n = percentage

90% or 0.9 = rate

30 = base

Formula and Solution:

Percentage = Base x Rate

Percentage = 30 x 0.9

Percentage = 27

27 students are not interested in pursuing a career in STEM.

In my research, I found that many universities and colleges have limited the number of face-to-face classes due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. This is being done to reduce the risk of transmission and keep students, faculty, and staff safe. Many institutions have shifted to a hybrid model of instruction, where classes are held both online and in-person, or have transitioned entirely to online learning. However, some institutions have found that certain types of classes, such as lab courses or hands-on training, are more challenging to conduct remotely and have therefore limited the number of in-person classes offered. Additionally, I found that universities and colleges have implemented strict safety protocols, such as mask-wearing and social distancing, to ensure the safety of those who attend in-person classes.

29. what are the effects of k-12 basic education program in the Philippine collegiate level​


Answer:

Graduates of the K–12 program will be seen as more capable and will have better global opportunities. The decongested and focused basic education curriculum has been improved. Students will have more time to study core academic subjects, resulting in improved comprehension.

There will be fewer units in the College General Education curriculum. Subjects that were covered in Basic Education will no longer be included in the College General Education curriculum.

The new GE Curriculum is described in detail in CHED Memorandum Order No. 20, series of 2013.

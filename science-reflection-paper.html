Answer:

The difference between Society and Nature is that Nature is greater than Society and it precedes Society in the sense that Nature is pristine, whereas Society is a Human Construction i.e. Society belongs to Nature, but Nature does not belong to Society in that a Society cannot exist without Nature, but Nature can exist without Society (Far in the uninhabited jungle, for example). Nature and Society are Teleologically related.

Society is the Social Construction of a collection of Homo Sapiens living or existing in close proximity, so that a particular physical territory might accommodate various different Societies, Groups or Collections of Humans. The term Society has also been lent to Groups of other Sentient Beings such as Primates, but less so to the Lesser Developed Species such as dogs, lions or cats or insects, such as ants — where the term Colony seems more apt.

In certain parts of the world there is an existence of a biosphere supporting Life other than Homo Sapiens and we do not expect to find a Society there. Primates exist in the jungle and their Groups have often been referred to as Societies of Apes or Monkeys but that is strictly not a true Society per se, because it is less highly developed or differentiated in comparison to that of Homo Sapiens or Man and for that reason it is more apt perhaps to describe such Primate gatherings as Troops or Groups.

Human Society is a highly differentiated collection of Individuals coexisting usually in peace and co-operation by following an evolved body of fixed rules, as even found to exist among Primitive Man i.e. Natural Equitable Rules of Living.

Nature per se is Primary and Pristine and Existence is based on the “Survival of the Fittest” and (Darwinian) Natural Selection and Adaptation, where the Law is based on “Tooth and Claw” superiority (Lex Talionis). In contradistinction, Living in a Society is a more sophisticated existence governed by consensual Social Conventions, Rules and Law.

All Societies are part of Nature but not all Nature is Society — Nature is the greater.

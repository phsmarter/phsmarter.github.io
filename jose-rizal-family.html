24. is it practical to have a big family like Jose Rizal's Family?​


Answer:

Having a big family like Jose Rizal's family may seem idealistic and romantic, but it is not practical in today's world.

- In the past, having a large family was necessary for economic reasons as children were needed to help with farming and other household chores. However, times have changed, and having many children can be financially burdensome.

- Raising a large family requires a significant amount of resources such as food, clothing, education, and healthcare. The cost of living has increased significantly over the years; it is difficult to provide for a big family without compromising on quality of life. Additionally, raising many children can be emotionally taxing as parents may struggle to give each child adequate attention.

- Moreover, overpopulation is already an issue in many countries worldwide.

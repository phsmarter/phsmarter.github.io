27. What is political consensus ?


Answer:

There is Political consensus  when a large proportion of the members of a society agree about how decisions regarding the distribution of values should be made. It is common in most countries who are practicing democracy. Political consensus, in  any  country and organization aims to avoid more criticisms and disagreement from people or various parties regarding certain agreements. It aims to maintain civil peace among the society. Further is a major generating factor for the following:

Establishment of State and its FunctionPolitical FrameworkStrategic Decision-makingEstablishment and Maintaining Relationship with International Organizations

Running a country is not always easy. It takes brilliant ideas from brilliant minds to come-up with best strategies for economic progress, strong ideas for political framework and good decision-making skills of all people involved. It is in this progressive work that political consensus must come in. Without political consensus, there would be chaos in the various institutions promulgating laws for the benefit of everyone and most especially in the society.

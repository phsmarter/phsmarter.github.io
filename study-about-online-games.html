The study on the effects of online games on academic performance of students is significant for several reasons.

Firstly, online games have become an increasingly popular form of entertainment among young people, and the potential impact of these games on academic performance is an important concern for parents, educators, and policymakers.

Secondly, the study could help to identify the specific ways in which online gaming might affect academic performance. For example, it could reveal whether excessive gaming leads to a decrease in study time, increased distraction during class, or decreased motivation to do well in school.

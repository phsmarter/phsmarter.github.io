Impact Of Playing Online Games In Academic Performance

oleh d4728395

On academic performance The academic performance of a student who was addicted to gaming suffered. Since the student is too engrossed in the game to finish their assignments or prepare for class, they found that gaming addiction has a negative physical impact on academic performance. Others have shown a connection between playing online games and performing worse in school. Even if the respondents play online games, their academic performance is unaffected. Even though they spent time playing, they nevertheless received good grades. Overall, the respondents’ academic performance is strong despite their interest in playing online games, according to the study’s findings. This can be seen as the compromise made when playing video games. There won’t be any points deducted from the student’s grade if they elect not to participate (Dennis O. Dumrique and Jennifer G. Castillo, 2018). According to university of Syracuse reported that being addicted to games may lead to decrease sleep, inference with the demands of society life including socializing and academic work. These online games have a big factor that affects the students in their academic performances in school.

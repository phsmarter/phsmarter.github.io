20. why is piscine patel so concerned at his name?


Piscine Molitor "Pi" Patel was so concerned with his name because "Piscine" sounds like "p i s s i n g", that is, urinating. His classmates used to tease him about it all the time.

That is why he later changed his nickname to "Pi" since it has no negative connotations.

It's also one of the first ways we see him take control of his fate, which he does later on while stuck at sea.

Answer:

The Author:

Yann Martel, (born June 25, 1963, Salamanca, Spain), Canadian author best known for Life of Pi (2001), the story of the eponymous Indian teenager adrift at sea, after a shipwreck, in a lifeboat shared with a Bengal tiger.

Piscine Molitor “Pi” Patel:

Piscine Molitor Patel (Pi) is an Indian boy, turned man, who survives 227 days on the Pacific after a shipwreck claims the life of his loved ones. Despite being named after a glorious French swimming pool, Piscine changes his name to Pi due to relentless teasing and mispronunciations from others.

Richard Parker:

Richard Parker is an adult Bengal tiger who is stranded on the lifeboat with Pi when the ship sinks. Richard Parker lives on the lifeboat with Pi and is kept alive with the food and water Pi delivers. Richard Parker develops a relationship with Pi that allows them to coexist in their struggle.

Ravi Patel:

Ravi is ''a fearsome bowler and a powerful batter, the captain of the town's best cricket team. '' In other words, he is a jock, and a popular one too.

Mr. Santosh Patel :

Pi's father and the owner of the Pondicherry Zoo. Santosh is a reasonable and affectionate father; he seeks to cultivate well-rounded children and humanely cares for his animals. His outlook on life and his focus on the survival and well-being of his family completely define him.

Mrs. Gita Patel:

Gita is raised a Hindu and had a Baptist education, but she is nonreligious as an adult and questions Pi's faith. Gita encourages Pi to read books as a youth. In Pi's first story Gita dies when the Tsimtsum sinks, but in his second story she takes the place of Orange Juice the orangutan.

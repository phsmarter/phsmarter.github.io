5. what is the meaning of the center of inclusive education in building discipline and good character for god and humanity explain​


Here is the meaning behind the center of inclusive education in building discipline and good character for god and humanity

This is most likely comes from an education institution's motto. Motto is used to explain the main goal or the mission of a certain institution. The institution details why it is called the center of inclusive education in such goals (building discipline and good character). Whereas, God and humanity here are the parties who will be benefited by the goal achieved (discipline and good character).

Serving God and humanity is a good mission and goal to achieve. By studying in that institution, it is hoped that the output (alumni) will at least possesses self-discipline and a good character. Usually, this is present in a religious institution. Meanwhile, the use of "inclusive" here emphasizes the facilities in that education institution, and how these facilities help in achieving those goals.

12. "a man without education is like building without foundation."make an essay


Education is an essential part of human life. It is not only important for personal development but also for the growth and progress of society. A person without education is like a building without foundation. The foundation of a building is what keeps it standing and strong, and without it, the building is weak and unstable. Similarly, education provides a strong foundation for an individual's growth and development.

Education is not only about gaining knowledge and information but also about developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and decision-making skills. Education helps people to understand the world around them and to make informed decisions. It equips individuals with the necessary skills to contribute to society and to be productive members of their community.

In today's world, education is more important than ever. With the rapidly changing world and advancements in technology, it is crucial to have a good education to keep up with the pace of change. Those without education are at a disadvantage and are likely to be left behind in today's fast-paced world. Education is also essential for personal growth and development. Without education, people are limited in their ability to achieve their full potential.

Furthermore, education is not just for individuals but is also essential for the growth and development of society. It is through education that we can address social and economic issues, promote equality, and create a better future for generations to come. Without education, society is likely to be stagnant, and progress will be slow.

In conclusion, a man without education is like a building without foundation. Education is a fundamental aspect of human life and is essential for personal growth, development, and the growth and development of society. It provides a strong foundation for individuals to achieve their full potential and to contribute positively to society. Therefore, it is crucial that we invest in education and ensure that every individual has access to quality education.

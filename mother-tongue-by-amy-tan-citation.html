18. How much does the writer know of the subject?(writer knowledge),mother tongue by amy tan.


Mother Tongue by Amy Tan talks about her experience with her mother. It shows how language use affect the perception of the person. This is a story about how she developed the language she used in the book, The Joy Luck Club.

Amy Tan clearly knows this subject as this is a non-fiction autobiographical essay detailing the difference between her mother's "broken" English and what is usually considered as the normal but limited English. 

This is clearly an essay that the author is very familiar with the subject since it talks about her own family and her experience growing up with a mother who speaks a very different kind of English.

20. In mother tongue by Amy Tan, Who is the target reader of the text?​


Answer:

The intended audience of Amy Tan's essay "Mother Tongue" is the people who criticize and judge Tan's mother's version of spoken English. Tan uses the essay to convince her audience that her mother's version of English communicates effectively and is a powerful language.

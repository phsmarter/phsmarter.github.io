25. Consider that both force and displacement is parallel, what will happen to the work done on a push cart if the force is doubled while the displacement is still the same?


FORCE AND DISPLACMENT

  The distance and direction that an object is moved are known as displacement. While force and displacement are indirectly proportional, work and force are directly proportional. W= Fd is the equation illustrating the connection. A force is applied to an item, and the more force is applied, the farther the thing will move. Displacement is a verb that denotes movement or displacement of an item. The definition of displacement is the modification of an object's location.

  The displacement behaves like a constant term if it can be seen. As a result, the force and work are directly proportionate. We can also observe that when the force is twice, the amount of work done is also doubled.

Moment of force is affected by the following variables:

The size of the force that was used. The mass and displacement of the item have an impact on how much force is applied. The amount of force needed to push or pull a body would increase with its mass.The distance of the force's path of action from its rotational axis. The force will cause the item to revolve around the pivot in a clockwise manner. It is crucial to keep in mind that the distance d is the separation between the pivot and the force's line of action, measured perpendicularly.

1. what is the rationale for probability sampling in quantitative research​


Answer:

The reason is that, in most cases, researchers who use probability sampling techniques are aiming to identify a representative sample. from which to collect data. A representative sample is one that resembles the population from which it was drawn in all the ways that are important for the research being conducted.

Title: The Effects of Social Media on Mental Health Among Adolescents in the United States

Introduction:

Social media has become an integral part of daily life for many adolescents in the United States. While social media has many benefits, it has also been linked to negative mental health outcomes such as depression, anxiety, and low self-esteem. This research aims to investigate the effects of social media on mental health among adolescents in the United States.

Rationale:

The increasing prevalence of social media use among adolescents has raised concerns about its impact on mental health. Despite numerous studies exploring this topic, there is still limited knowledge about the specific effects of social media on mental health among adolescents in the United States. This research will help fill this gap in knowledge and provide insights for parents, educators, and mental health professionals.

Seriousness of the Problem:

Mental health is a significant public health concern in the United States, with approximately one in six adolescents experiencing a mental health disorder. Social media use among adolescents has been linked to negative mental health outcomes, such as anxiety, depression, and low self-esteem. Therefore, it is crucial to understand the relationship between social media and mental health to identify ways to promote positive mental health outcomes among adolescents.

Setting:

This study will be conducted in the United States, with participants recruited from schools and online platforms. The study will involve both qualitative and quantitative methods, including surveys and interviews.

General Objective:

The general objective of this research is to investigate the effects of social media on mental health among adolescents in the United States.

General Purpose or Overall Purpose:

The overall purpose of this research is to provide insights into the relationship between social media use and mental health among adolescents in the United States.

Literature foundation:

Several studies have explored the relationship between social media use and mental health among adolescents, but the findings have been mixed. Some studies have found a significant positive relationship between social media use and mental health, while others have found negative associations. This research will build on the existing literature and provide a more nuanced understanding of the relationship between social media use and mental health among adolescents in the United States.


5. Sino may sample codes ng reservation hotel? I need it for thesis.


Reservation status codes are attached to the guest reservation to indicate the status of the booking made by the guest. Eg:- Booking confirmed by credit card, tentative bookings which has to be released by 6 PM, Travel Agent Voucher confirmed etc.

Confirmed: Once payment method and/or billing instructions are confirmed a reservation status can be selected as confirmed. A reservation should only be confirmed upon receipt of a credit card or deposit payment.

Tentative: If payment method and/or billing instructions have not been guaranteed then the reservation should be set to the status of Tentative.

Waitlist: If the hotel or room type is fully booked then the customer should be given the option to waitlist their reservation.

Company Guarantee: If a reservation is made by a company and also billed to the same company then reservation status 'Company guarantee' may be used.

Travel Agent Guarantee: A travel agent booking with a valid IATA number and Travel agent voucher may be selected as code Travel Agent Guarantee'.

6 PM Release: A reservation is not guaranteed with payment or voucher and the hotel will hold it till 6PM in the evening.

6. sample interview for jeepney operators (thesis)​


Answer:

Interviewer: Good day! Thank you for agreeing to this interview. Can you tell us your name and how long have you been operating a jeepney?

Jeepney Operator: Good day, my name is Juan dela Cruz, and I have been operating a jeepney for over 15 years now.

Interviewer: Thank you for that. Our study focuses on the impact of modernizing jeepneys on the livelihood of operators. What do you think are the advantages and disadvantages of modernizing jeepneys?

Jeepney Operator: Well, the advantage of modernizing jeepneys is that it can improve the riding experience of passengers. With modern features such as air conditioning and GPS navigation, passengers can enjoy a more comfortable and convenient ride. Also, modern jeepneys can help reduce air pollution, which is a significant problem in the city. On the other hand, the cost of modernizing jeepneys is quite expensive, and not all operators can afford it. Also, some jeepney operators may not be familiar with the new technologies, making it difficult for them to operate.

Interviewer: I see. Can you tell us about the current challenges you face as a jeepney operator, and how do you cope with them?

Jeepney Operator: One of the significant challenges we face is the increasing cost of fuel, which affects our daily earnings. Another challenge is the competition among jeepney operators, which leads to fare cuts and reduced income. To cope with these challenges, we try to maximize our daily trips and maintain our jeepneys to ensure that they are in good condition.

Interviewer: Lastly, how do you think the government can support jeepney operators in their livelihood?

Jeepney Operator: The government can help by providing financial assistance to jeepney operators who want to modernize their units. Also, they can regulate the fare and provide incentives to those who comply with the regulations. Lastly, they can provide training and education to jeepney operators to help them adapt to new technologies and become more competitive in the market.

Interviewer: Thank you so much for your insights, Mr. Dela Cruz. Your inputs will be a valuable contribution to our study.

Title: The Impact of Social Media on College Students' Academic Performance: A Comparative Study

Introduction: Social media has emerged as an integral part of our daily lives. The widespread use of social media platforms has brought a significant shift in how we interact, communicate and share information. While social media has the potential to connect people and foster new relationships, there are growing concerns about its negative impact on academic performance. As more students spend an increasing amount of time on social media sites, academic standards may be compromised, leading to a decline in student grades and academic achievement.

Rationale: The rationale for this study lies in identifying the relationship between social media usage and academic performance among college students. The findings of this study could assist educators and policymakers to understand the effects that social media has on academic achievement.

Seriousness of the Problem: The seriousness of the problem lies in the potential negative impact of social media on academic performance. However, the current study aims to provide a comparative analysis of social media use and academic performance among college students to provide a more comprehensive understanding of the subject matter.

Title: The Effects of Online Learning on the Academic Performance of Senior High School Students in the Philippines

Introduction:

Rationale:
The ongoing COVID-19 pandemic has forced educational institutions to shift to online learning as a means of continuing education despite the restrictions on face-to-face classes. While online learning has been implemented in some schools prior to the pandemic, its widespread use has brought about questions on its effectiveness, particularly in terms of academic performance. This study aims to investigate the effects of online learning on the academic performance of senior high school students in the Philippines.

Seriousness of the Problem:
The senior high school level is a crucial stage in the education of Filipino students as it prepares them for higher education or the workforce. The shift to online learning has disrupted the traditional classroom setup and has challenged both students and teachers to adapt to new learning methods. Understanding the effects of online learning on academic performance is important in ensuring that students receive quality education despite the limitations brought about by the pandemic.

Setting:
The study will be conducted in selected senior high schools in the Philippines that have implemented online learning as a means of continuing education during the pandemic. The study will cover students from different regions in the country to ensure a diverse sample.

General Objective:
The general objective of this study is to determine the effects of online learning on the academic performance of senior high school students in the Philippines.

General Purpose or Overall Purpose:
The overall purpose of this study is to contribute to the understanding of the effectiveness of online learning in the Philippine education system and to provide insights for educators and policymakers in improving the delivery of education during times of crisis.

Literature Foundation:
Previous studies have shown mixed results on the effectiveness of online learning on academic performance. Some studies have shown that online learning can be as effective as traditional classroom learning, while others have shown that it may have a negative impact on academic performance. This study aims to contribute to the literature by focusing on the Philippine context and by investigating the effects of online learning on senior high school students, a crucial stage in the education of Filipino students.

Title: The Effects of Social Media on the Mental Health of Adolescents in the Philippines

Introduction:

Rationale:

Social media has become an integral part of the lives of many people, particularly adolescents. It has transformed the way people communicate, share information, and interact with one another. However, there is growing concern about the negative impact of social media on mental health, especially among young people. With the increasing use of social media among adolescents in the Philippines, it is important to investigate the effects of social media on their mental health.

Seriousness of the Problem:

Mental health problems such as depression, anxiety, and low self-esteem have been linked to social media use among adolescents. The constant exposure to unrealistic beauty standards, cyberbullying, and comparison to peers can contribute to feelings of inadequacy, isolation, and anxiety. The seriousness of this problem is underscored by the fact that mental health problems can have long-lasting effects on an individual's well-being, relationships, and future opportunities.

Setting:

This study will be conducted in the Philippines, a country with a rapidly growing social media user base, particularly among young people. The study will target adolescents aged 13 to 19 years old who are active social media users.

General Objective:

The general objective of this study is to determine the effects of social media on the mental health of adolescents in the Philippines.

General Purpose or Overall Purpose:

The overall purpose of this study is to contribute to a better understanding of the relationship between social media use and mental health among adolescents in the Philippines. The findings of this study will provide insights into the impact of social media on mental health and inform the development of interventions and policies that promote healthy social media use among adolescents.

Literature Foundation:

Previous studies have explored the effects of social media on mental health among adolescents in other countries. Some studies have found a positive correlation between social media use and mental health problems, while others have found no significant association. However, there is limited research on this topic in the Philippine context. This study aims to build on the existing literature by investigating the effects of social media use on the mental health of adolescents in the Philippines.

Title: Examining the Effectiveness of Mindfulness-Based Intervention in Reducing Symptoms of Anxiety among University Students

Explanation:

Introduction:

Rationale:

Anxiety is a common mental health concern among university students, which can lead to a range of negative outcomes including impaired academic performance, social functioning, and overall well-being. Mindfulness-based interventions have gained increasing attention as a potential treatment for anxiety due to their effectiveness in improving cognitive control and emotional regulation. However, there is still limited empirical evidence on the effectiveness of mindfulness-based interventions among university students.

Seriousness of the Problem:

According to the American College Health Association, anxiety is the most common mental health concern among university students. General Objective:

The general objective of this study is to examine the effectiveness of a mindfulness-based intervention in reducing symptoms of anxiety among university students.

General Purpose:

The general purpose of this study is to contribute to the development of evidence-based interventions for addressing the mental health concerns of university students.

Literature Foundation:

Previous research has demonstrated the effectiveness of mindfulness-based interventions in reducing symptoms of anxiety among various populations. However, few studies have examined the effectiveness of such interventions specifically among university students. This study will build on the existing literature by examining the effectiveness of a mindfulness-based intervention program in reducing symptoms of anxiety among university students, contributing to the growing body of evidence on the potential benefits of mindfulness-based interventions for mental health.

17. w a t e r give meaning every word​


Answer:

W - Wellspring of Life

A - Agriculture

T - Transportation

E - Energy

R - Recreation

Explanation:

Water is undeniably one of the most vital resources known to man. The letters in the word WATER can be used as an acronym to describe its significance in our lives and the environment.

The first letter W stands for "Wellspring of Life" since water is essential for all forms of life to exist, including plants, animals, and humans.

A stands for "Agriculture" as water is necessary in the growth of crops, which provides food and jobs for people.

T represents "Transportation" since waterways like rivers and oceans have been used for centuries as a means of travel and trade.

E symbolizes "Energy" since water is used to generate hydroelectric power through dams and turbines.

Lastly, R signifies "Recreation" as water offers numerous recreational activities like swimming, skiing, and boating.

The importance of water cannot be overstated, and it is vital that we conserve and protect this precious resource for future generations.

20. are you familiar with "canon rock" and "canon in D by pachelbel"?​


Answer:

Pachelbel's Canon in D is a canon (a musical composition technique where portions of the music are repeated over and over again) composed by  German Baroque composer Johann Pachelbel and is popularly used in weddings and even in funeral ceremonies in the western world.

Canon rock is a rock rendition of Canon in D by Jerry Chang, a Taiwanese guitarist. Canon rock became popular in 2005 after a cover of the piece was made by South Korean guitarist funtwo.

Multiple artists and guitarists have been YouTube covers of both Pachelbel's Canon and Canon rock, further driving the popularity of both the classic piece and its modern counterpart.

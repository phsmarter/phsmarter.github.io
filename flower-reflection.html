21. VII. Reflection Write a paragraph with 3-5 sentences that appreciating the importance of flowers to human, animals and environment.


Directions: Write a paragraph with 3-5 sentences that appreciating the importance of  flowers to human, animals and environment.

Flowers are important in nature because they can feed insects, birds, animals, and humans; they can also give natural remedies for humans and certain animals; and they can help a plant reproduce by attracting pollinators from the outside. Plants would be only green without flowers, and the world would be a duller place.

Planting trees, shrubs, and flowers is a terrific method to help make the environment a better place, since studies have consistently proven that an increase in the quantity of plants leads to an improvement in air quality. When pollination kicks off the reproductive cycle and seeds are formed, many plants reproduce through their blooms. Humans gather the seeds and plant them elsewhere, or birds, bats, and other creatures transport them from one location to another. The more seeds that are created and end up in the ground, the more plants there will be.  So, in providing the seeds that make it possible to grow more plants, flowers benefit the environment by creating more carbon dioxide absorbing and oxygen-radiating plants.

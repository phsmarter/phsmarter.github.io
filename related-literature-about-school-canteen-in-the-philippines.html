By using a case study approach that included interviews with canteen managers and staff, as well as observations of canteen operations, the researcher was able to identify the key factors that influence the effectiveness and efficiency of school canteen management. One of the main findings of the study was that effective communication and collaboration among canteen staff and other school stakeholders is crucial to the success of school canteen operations.

4. give me an specific problem about Extreme poverty and lack of basic health education in parents. Pa help po sa research ​


Answer:

One specific problem related to extreme poverty and lack of basic health education in parents is the increased risk of child mortality due to preventable diseases. Children born into families living in extreme poverty are more likely to suffer from malnutrition and a lack of access to basic healthcare, which can lead to illnesses such as pneumonia, diarrhea, and malaria. Without proper education on preventative measures and access to medical treatment, these preventable diseases can often be fatal for young children.

17. The pros and cons of using video games to students informative essay​


Answer:Playing video games has both pros and cons associated with it. On the one hand, video games can have negative effects on physical and mental health, including depression, social anxiety, lack of motivation, poor emotional regulation, poor sleep hygiene, muscle loss, fat gain, dehydration, and exhaustion [1]. On the other hand, playing video games can also have several positive effects. For instance, video games can help children learn math, spelling, resource management, and problem-solving skills [2]. Moreover, certain video games can also serve as a brain booster and painkiller [3]. However, it is important to note that excessive gaming can lead to obesity, "Nintendo thumb" (a type of repetitive strain injury), and cyber addiction [3]. Therefore, it is important to maintain a balance between playing video games and engaging in other activities to reap the benefits while minimizing the negative effects.

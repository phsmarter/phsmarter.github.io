2. drafting an outline of informative speech example​


Answer:Informative Speech Outline Format

Introduction. Grab the audience's attention. Include the speaker's opinion or hypothesis. Present thesis statement.

Body. Outline the main points with real-world examples and supporting facts. Use transition between main points.

Conclusion. Summarize the main points. Restate the thesis statement.

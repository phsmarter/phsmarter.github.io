12. Performance Task 1 SHORT ESSAY Direction. Write a short paragraph with at least 100 words that explains your answer to this question. Question: "What do you think is the role of media like radio, television, internet and recording play in the development of different musical genres?"​


Answer:

Media such as radio, television, internet, and recording play a significant role in the development and evolution of different musical genres. These platforms provide a way for musicians and artists to share their music with a wider audience, and they can help to expose listeners to new sounds and styles that they may not have encountered otherwise. In addition, media platforms can provide a way for musicians to connect with other artists and collaborate on new projects, which can lead to the creation of new genres or sub-genres. The media can also serve as a way for musicians to receive feedback and criticism from listeners, which can help them to refine and improve their work. Overall, the role of media in the development of different musical genres is important because it helps to facilitate the exchange and dissemination of new ideas and sounds, and it allows for the evolution and growth of different musical styles.

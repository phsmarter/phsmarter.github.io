The COVID-19 pandemic has led to a dramatic loss of human life worldwide and presents an unprecedented challenge to public health, food systems and the world of work. The economic and social disruption caused by the pandemic is devastating: tens of millions of people are at risk of falling into extreme poverty, while the number of undernourished people, currently estimated at nearly 690 million, could increase by up to 132 million by the end of the year.

Millions of enterprises face an existential threat. Nearly half of the world’s 3.3 billion global workforce are at risk of losing their livelihoods. Informal economy workers are particularly vulnerable because the majority lack social protection and access to quality health care and have lost access to productive assets. Without the means to earn an income during lockdowns, many are unable to feed themselves and their families. For most, no income means no food, or, at best, less food and less nutritious food.

In the COVID-19 crisis food security, public health, and employment and labour issues, in particular workers’ health and safety, converge. Adhering to workplace safety and health practices and ensuring access to decent work and the protection of labour rights in all industries will be crucial in addressing the human dimension of the crisis. Immediate and purposeful action to save lives and livelihoods should include extending social protection towards universal health coverage and income support for those most affected. These include workers in the informal economy and in poorly protected and low-paid jobs, including youth, older workers, and migrants. Particular attention must be paid to the situation of women, who are over-represented in low-paid jobs and care roles.

26. essay of ginger pasta 1000 words​


Answer:

Ginger pasta is a delightful dish that combines the warm and spicy flavor of ginger with the comforting texture of pasta. This dish is a great way to introduce new and exciting flavors into your cooking repertoire, and it is sure to impress any dinner guests with its unique and bold taste.

One of the best things about ginger pasta is its versatility. This dish can be made with a variety of pasta shapes and sizes, from spaghetti to linguine to penne. Additionally, the sauce can be customized to suit your preferences. For example, if you prefer a spicier dish, you can add more ginger or chili flakes to the sauce. If you prefer a milder flavor, you can reduce the amount of ginger and garlic in the sauce.

To make ginger pasta, you will first need to cook your pasta of choice according to the package instructions. While the pasta is cooking, you can prepare the ginger sauce. To do this, you will need to finely chop a fresh ginger root and a few cloves of garlic. In a large skillet, heat up some sesame oil and sauté the ginger and garlic until they are fragrant.

Once the ginger and garlic are fragrant, you can add in some soy sauce and continue to cook the sauce until it has thickened slightly. At this point, you can add in your cooked pasta and toss everything together until the pasta is coated in the ginger sauce. Garnish with some green onions or sesame seeds for an extra pop of flavor and texture.

One of the great things about ginger pasta is that it can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a variety of proteins and vegetables. For example, you can add some sautéed mushrooms, bell peppers, and onions to the dish for added flavor and nutrition. Alternatively, you can serve the pasta alongside some grilled chicken or shrimp for a complete meal.

Another benefit of ginger pasta is its health benefits. Ginger is known for its anti-inflammatory properties, which can help reduce pain and inflammation in the body. Additionally, ginger has been shown to aid in digestion and improve overall gut health. By incorporating ginger pasta into your diet, you can enjoy these health benefits while still indulging in a delicious and satisfying meal.

In addition to its health benefits, ginger pasta is also a great option for those following a vegetarian or vegan diet. The dish can easily be made without any animal products, and the sauce is packed with flavor and nutrition.

Overall, ginger pasta is a delightful and unique dish that is sure to impress any dinner guests. With its bold and spicy flavor, this dish is a great way to introduce new and exciting flavors into your cooking routine. Whether enjoyed on its own or paired with your favorite proteins and vegetables, ginger pasta is a versatile and delicious option for any occasion.

1. Real-life Examples of Projectile Motion give 10 examples


Answer:

The projectile motion refers to the motion of an object which is in flight along the horizontal and vertical direction simultaneously. The projectile motion is always in the form of a parabola. There is only one acceleration acting during projectile motion and that is the acceleration due to gravity in the vertical direction.

Real-Life Examples

In real life, the projectile motion finds applications in sports. Playing basketball, football is examples of projectile motion in real life.

While throwing a basketball into the basket, the player shoots the ball in such a way that the flight taken by the ball is in the form of a parabola. A curve is generated by the ball such that the distance of the ball from the fixed point is equal to the distance it travels on to the other axis. This is how the projectile motion is used in real life.

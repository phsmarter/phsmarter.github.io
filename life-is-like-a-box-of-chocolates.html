5. Life is like a box of chocolate. you never know what you're going to get.


Answer:

This saying was popularized in the movie Forrest Gump. It means that in life, you never know what unexpected thing will happen next.

Explanation:

A box of chocolates does not always contain the same type of chocolate. It can contain several different kinds, so you will never know until you open and eat each one of the chocolates.

Sometimes a piece of chocolate will look plain, but it will have fillings that are not chocolate or candy. Some chocolates will look very fancy because they have different things in them such as nuts, gold foil, or fruit. Some chocolate is oily, while some are made of dark chocolate.

Life is compared to a box of chocolates. You will not have the same experiences throughout your life. It will be full of surprises and unexpected twists and turns.

27. explain the analogy:life is like a box of chocolate - you never know what your gonna get​


Answer:

Source: https://www.quora.com/What-did-Tom-Hanks-mean-in-Forest-Gump-when-he-said-Life-is-like-a-box-of-chocolates-You-never-know-what-youre-going-to-get

When you open a box of chocolates, there is a variety of flavors available. Problem is, since they are covered in chocolate, you can't really tell what any given piece of chocolate is going to taste like. You're going to eat a chocolate (you know you are! admit it! you want chocolate!), but you won't know what you are getting until you bite into it - and then it's done. Like it or not, you've tasted it.

You never know what life is going to give you. But you live nevertheless. So every day - every new experience - is something you really don't know about until you actually live it. It may not be an experience you like. It may be the greatest experience ever. But you don't know until you've lived it, and by then it's too late to not live it.

The real lesson: you take what life gives you and you learn to deal with it, because - as they say - that's life.

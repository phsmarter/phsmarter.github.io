Specific Purpose: To inform the audience about the causes and consequences of teenage pregnancy

Topic: Teenage Pregnancy

Pattern: Problem-Solution

Introduction: Introduce the topic of teenage pregnancy, discuss its prevalence, and explain why it is important to understand the causes and consequences.

Body: Discuss the causes of teenage pregnancy, such as peer pressure, lack of access to contraception, and poverty. Describe the consequences of teenage pregnancy, such as health risks, educational challenges, and social stigma.

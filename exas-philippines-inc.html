16. what is the vision of ARTISTS RESOURCE NETWORK ASSOCIATION INC. (ARNAI) a theater play in the philippines?​


What is the vision of ARTISTS RESOURCE NETWORK ASSOCIATION INC. (ARNAI) a theater play in the philippines?

Artists Resource Network Association Inc. (formerly ACTION or Activating Communities For Inter Cultural Orchestration Network) was established in  1978 during his tenure with the celebrated MSU-Sining Kambayoka in Mindanao by the Theater Artist Frank G. Rivera.  Its acronym and corresponding context reflect the vision of the organization of  a True Filipino National Theater through the participation of communities of different cultures.

Trailblazing the inclusion of traditional, ethnic and regional theaters in the mainstream and national consciousness, the Artists Resource Network has unprecedently pioneered the integration of various regional theater groups into a major national power, as the Cultural Center of the Philippines (CCP) and the National Commission for Culture and the Arts (NCCA) have now done jointly.  

Over the years , the group has quietly and gradually produced participation from regional theater groups and individuals in the country by  involving and then moving into action promoting distinctly Filipino cultural arts, which is the only path to national upliftment and advancement as individuals.

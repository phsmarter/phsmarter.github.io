1. who is ivan dmitrich aksionov?​


Answer:

Ivan Dmitrich Aksionov is a fictional character from the novel "The Death of Ivan Ilyich" by Russian author Leo Tolstoy. He is a wealthy man who, upon his deathbed, realizes the error of his ways and the shallowness of his life. He is a symbol of the human search for meaning and redemption.

16. God Sees the Truth, But Waits In the town of Vladimir lived a young merchant named Ivan Dmitrich Aksionov. He had two shops and a house of his own. Explain this​


Answer:

"God Sees the Truth, But Waits" is a short story by Russian author Leo Tolstoy. The story is about a young merchant named Ivan Dmitrich Aksionov who lived in the town of Vladimir. Aksionov was a successful businessman with two shops and a house of his own. However, his life takes a dramatic turn when he is falsely accused of a crime and sent to prison for many years. Despite the unjust circumstances, Aksionov maintains his innocence and endures the harsh conditions of prison life. The story explores themes of justice, faith, and morality, as Aksionov's perseverance is tested by the hardships of his situation. Ultimately, the story demonstrates that the truth will always be revealed, even if it takes time, and that a person's moral character is the most important factor in determining their worth.

28. who is ivan dmitrich aksionov from the story GOD SEES THE TRUTH BUT WAITS?​


Answer:

Ivan Dmitrich Aksionov is the main character in the short story "God Sees the Truth But Waits" by Leo Tolstoy. He is a merchant from Vladimir who is falsely accused of murdering a fellow merchant, and is sentenced to twenty-five years in prison. Despite his innocence, Ivan must endure years of harsh treatment and unjust punishment in prison. During his time in prison, he is able to maintain his faith in God, and eventually finds redemption when the real murderer confesses. After his release, Ivan leads a peaceful life and dedicates himself to helping others.

10. Do you see yourself as an entrepreneur or becoming? Why or why not?​


Yes, I will become an entrepreneur. Entrepreneurs are one of the jobs that are loved by young people today. There are various reasons for choosing to become entrepreneurs behind those who want to work rather than work for other people. Because being an entrepreneur is more free to manage time, free from boredom, and free from superior boss orders. Apart from the reasons above, there are many other reasons that encourage someone to choose to become an entrepreneur.

Here are some reasons to choose to become an entrepreneur:

Freedom at Work

By choosing to be an entrepreneur means that you are free from all the boring work rules. For example wearing work uniforms, boring room designs, having age restrictions, and limited time for me time. Through freedom at work, it is guaranteed that you will be happier enjoying life.

More control over the future

As an entrepreneur, you decide when and where to work, with whom to do business, and determine how to network. Indirectly you have formed the future that you hope and desire. So the success of the company depends on how you manage it.

Want to have time with family

Half of our lives on average are dedicated to making money, one of which is by working for other people. While working for other people must comply with burdensome rules, such as giving up time to gather with family. So it's not surprising that many employees decide to become entrepreneurs so they can have more time with their families.

No Options

In the beginning, becoming an entrepreneur was a last resort because of the intense competition in the world of work. Therefore, you are forced to rack your brain to be able to get creative ideas in making money. Then you become an entrepreneur and earn more than when you work in other companies. And finally you decide to become a successful young entrepreneur.

Want to have a bigger source of income

The main reason for choosing to become an entrepreneur is that you want to generate greater profits. Because by having a large income you can enjoy a pleasant life, free from debt bondage, have a sufficient life, and can even travel abroad.

Ideas That Can Be a Source of Income

While still an employee, sometimes many ideas come to mind to be able to advance the company. However, not all of our ideas can be accepted by the company. So the idea is better to run yourself until it becomes your daily source of income.

Like Challenges

Some people do not like to pass the days with a job that is saturated and monotonous. This trait is able to trigger them to do things differently from the work they normally do. This sense of challenge is what they can't get in jobs that require routines for long periods of time. This reason makes someone prefer to be an entrepreneur because it can provide its own satisfaction.

Entrepreneurs Have Clear Life Goals

Someone who wants to be an entrepreneur usually has a plan A and a plan B. So, even if they fail, they still have other plans. Most entrepreneurs have a clear life path, have a vision and mission, as well as determination and belief in changing lives for the better.

Want to Help Many People

An entrepreneur certainly needs the help of others in advancing the company. Therefore, he recruited competent people to help him in various activities. In this regard, being an entrepreneur has helped many people to get a job.

Doesn't Like To Be Ruled And Ordered By Others

Some people do not like to be governed and ordered by others, especially if their superiors are superior. They prefer to live independently and take decisive decisions. This is one of the reasons for choosing to become entrepreneurs that triggers them to open their own business without having to be tied to other people.

Well, those are some of the reasons for choosing to become an entrepreneur as the right choice for a bright future. Becoming an entrepreneur is certainly everyone's desire because it is free from binding rules and is freer in regulating the direction of the company's success.

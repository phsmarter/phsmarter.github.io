30. Why do overlapping niches lead to competition?


Overlapping niches mean that some resources are common among two species.

Niches are simply considered as resources that correspond to a group of species. That being said, what happens when niches overlap? Yes, competition. A conflict will arise between the species involved, as they fight for the resources that overlap.

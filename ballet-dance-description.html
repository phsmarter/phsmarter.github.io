5. ano ang ballet dance​


Answer:

Ballet is a type of performance dance that originated during the Italian Renaissance in the fifteenth century and later developed into a concert dance form in France and Russia. It has since become a widespread and highly technical form of dance with its own vocabulary.

13. The motion of dancing (Ballet)is​


Answer:

Ballet movement, in classical ballet, any of the formalized actions of a dancer that follow specific rules regarding the positions of the arms, feet, and body. Ballet choreography is based on combinations of these fundamental movements. Other movements are steps in a ballet.

30. ilarawan ang ballet dance


Answer:

Ang ballet (bigkas: /ba-ley/) o baley ay isang uri ng sayaw na itinatanghal na nagsimula sa mga korte ng Renasimyentong Italyano noong ika-15 dantaon. Umusbong ito upang maging isang anyo ng sayaw na pangkonsiyerto sa Pransiya at Rusya. Magmula noon, ito ay naging isang anyo ng sayaw na malaganap at mayroong mataas na teknikalidad, na nagkaroon ng sarili nitong talasalitaan. Naging pandaigdigan ang impluwensiya nito at nailarawan nito ang pansaligan o pundamental na mga teknikang ginagamit sa maraming iba pang mga henero o genre ng sayaw. Ito ay karaniwang sinasaliwan ng musikang pamballet.

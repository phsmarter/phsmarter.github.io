1. Impact of online class and gadgets to students


Answer:

The impact of online classes and gadgets on students can be both positive and negative. On the positive side, online classes and gadgets can provide students with more flexibility, access to more educational resources, and increased motivation. Technology can also provide more engaging and interactive learning experiences. On the negative side, the use of online classes and gadgets can lead to increased stress, decreased physical activity, and increased sedentary behavior. Additionally, the use of technology can lead to an over-reliance on gadgets, which can have a negative impact on critical thinking and problem-solving skills. In order to maximize the positive effects of online classes and gadgets, it is important for students to practice healthy technology habits, including setting boundaries and taking regular breaks from digital activities.

7. Define the Study of the Impact of gadgets in learning among Senior High School students?​


Answer:

"Define the Study of the Impact of gadgets in learning among Senior High School students"

The study of the impact of gadgets on learning among Senior High School students is an investigation that aims to explore how the use of gadgets such as smartphones, tablets, laptops, and other electronic devices affect the academic performance of senior high school students. This study typically examines the extent to which gadgets are used by students, how they are used, and whether their use has a positive or negative impact on learning outcomes.

The study may also explore the potential benefits and drawbacks of using gadgets in the classroom, as well as the attitudes of students and teachers toward their use. Additionally, it may investigate how factors such as socioeconomic status, access to technology, and cultural norms influence gadget to use and its impact on learning outcomes.

Overall, the study of the impact of gadgets on learning among Senior High School students seeks to provide insights into how technology is transforming education and to identify best practices for integrating gadgets into the learning process in a way that maximizes their potential benefits while minimizing any negative effects.

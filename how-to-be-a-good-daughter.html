9. Short Story English Tittle: A good daughter​


Once upon a time, there was a young girl named Sarah. She was the youngest of three children and the only daughter of her parents. Sarah was a good daughter. She always helped her mother with chores, took care of her siblings, and did well in school.

One day, Sarah's father became very ill. He needed to go to the hospital for treatment, and Sarah's mother had to go with him. This left Sarah and her brothers alone at home. Sarah knew she had to take care of her brothers and keep the house running while her parents were away.

Sarah woke up early every day to make breakfast for her brothers and helped them with their homework after school. She also did the laundry and cleaned the house, making sure everything was in order before her parents came back.

Days turned into weeks and weeks turned into months. Sarah's father's condition improved and he was able to come home. Sarah's mother was very grateful and proud of her daughter for taking care of her family while they were away. She praised Sarah and thanked her for being such a good daughter.

From that day on, Sarah's family became closer than ever before. They realized how much they needed each other and how important it was to have a good daughter like Sarah. They were grateful for her and knew they could always count on her.

Sarah learned that being a good daughter is not only about doing chores and taking care of others but also about being responsible and reliable. She was proud of herself for being able to help her family in their time of need and would always be there for them in the future.

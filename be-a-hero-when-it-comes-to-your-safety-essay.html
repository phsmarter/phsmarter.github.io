16. Write an informative essay that explains the qualities of a hero and how to recognize heroes in your life.


Answer:

A hero is someone who embodies the qualities of courage, selflessness, and integrity. They are individuals who are willing to put themselves in harm's way to help others, stand up for what is right, and make a positive difference in the world. Heroes can be found in all walks of life, from ordinary citizens to public figures and historical figures. In this essay, we will explore the qualities of a hero and how to recognize heroes in your life.

One of the most important qualities of a hero is courage. Heroes are often faced with difficult and dangerous situations, and it is their courage that allows them to face these challenges head-on. Courageous heroes do not shy away from adversity, but instead, they confront it with bravery and determination. They are willing to take risks, speak out against injustice, and stand up for what they believe in, even when it is not the popular or easy thing to do.

Another key quality of a hero is selflessness. Heroes put the needs and well-being of others before their own. They are motivated by a desire to help others and make a positive difference in the world, rather than personal gain or recognition. Selfless heroes are often driven by a strong sense of compassion and empathy, as they seek to alleviate the suffering of others and promote the greater good.

Integrity is also an important quality of a hero. Heroes are honest, trustworthy, and ethical individuals who hold themselves to high standards of moral conduct. They do not compromise their values or principles for personal gain or convenience, but instead, they uphold their convictions, even in the face of opposition or criticism. Heroes who possess integrity are respected and admired for their unwavering commitment to doing what is right.

Now that we have explored the qualities of a hero, how can we recognize heroes in our lives? Heroes can be found in many different places, from our families and communities to public figures and historical figures. One way to recognize heroes is to look for individuals who exhibit the qualities of courage, selflessness, and integrity. These may be people who have overcome adversity, helped others in need, or made a positive impact in their community or the world.

Another way to recognize heroes is to learn about historical figures and public figures who have made significant contributions to society. These may include civil rights leaders, political activists, or humanitarian workers who have dedicated their lives to promoting justice, equality, and human rights. By studying the lives and legacies of these heroes, we can learn about the qualities that make them extraordinary and inspire us to emulate their example.

In conclusion, heroes are individuals who embody the qualities of courage, selflessness, and integrity. They are willing to face adversity, put others first, and uphold their values and principles, even in the face of opposition. By recognizing heroes in our lives, we can learn from their example and be inspired to make a positive difference in the world ourselves.

19. nation arts hero essay​


Answer:

A national hero of the Philippines is a Filipino who has been recognized as a national hero for his or her role in the history of the Philippines. Loosely, the term may refer to all Filipino historical figures recognized as heroes, but the term more strictly refers to those officially designated as such. In 1995 the Philippine National Heroes Committee officially recommended several people for the designation, but this was not acted upon. As of 2007, no one had ever been officially recognized as a Philippine national hero.

One example is Norman Borlaug, an American biologist who is widely regarded as the father of the Green Revolution.

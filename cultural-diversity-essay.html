4. make 250 words essay about how oral communication in context activities useful to cultural diversity ​


As we know, in this world there are many different cultures. One culture is not the same as another and of course, there are many differences between them. In the current era of globalization, interaction can occur more easily.

The way to communicate with anyone is wide open, of course with the help of technological developments that are increasingly practical. In this way, communication between different cultures finally occurs.

In the Cultural Studies of Communication, it is taught that cultural diversity is learned, not eliminated. That is, avoiding conflicts between different cultures, does not mean we have to equate all existing cultures. This might be able to minimize conflicts that could occur due to cultural differences, but it could also lead to other conflicts.

Therefore, cultural diversity cannot be eliminated just like that. Not feeling oneself and one's own culture as superior and demeaning other people's cultures, as well as wanting to learn to build better relationships between many cultures, is an attitude that is found by studying Cultural Studies of Communication.

As social beings, humans need to interact, whether in any form it is unavoidable, as well in inter-cultural interactions. We will always interact with other people from different cultures.

For example, as a student myself, I met friends from various islands, with different cultural backgrounds. I myself am a native of Sulawesi with a rather fast speech tempo and also a bit louder, while my friends are mostly Javanese who are famous for their calm nature.

There are differences here, but I understand my friend's culture and try to respect it. So, even though we have differences, we can still be friends and interact smoothly.

For this reason, studying Communication Cultural Studies can help us to interact better because Communication Cultural Studies is not only studying culture but also respecting the existing cultural diversity.

Globalization has had a significant impact on cultural diversity. While it has created opportunities for cultural exchange and understanding, it has also led to the homogenization of cultures. It is important for individuals and governments to actively preserve and celebrate cultural diversity to ensure that it is not lost in the face of globalization. By promoting cultural education and supporting local artists and traditions, we can ensure that the world remains a rich and diverse place.

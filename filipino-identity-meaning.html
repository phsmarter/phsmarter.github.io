Classification of Research Topics According to the Five Types of Qualitative Research Design

Grounded theoryPhenomenologyPhenomenology or NarrativeGrounded theoryEthnographyGrounded theoryNarrative or Case StudyEthnography or Case StudyNarrative or Ethnography Narrative

Explanation on the Classification of Research Topics According to the Five Types of Qualitative Research Design

Grounded theory

This is a qualitative research design that starts with the gathering of facts or data. Then, new theories are formed based on the collected information. Social processes, such as behaviors, are studied using this method.

Phenomenology

It is a descriptive type of research that interprets the lived experiences of a particular group of individuals dealing with the same experience or event. This applies to #2 and #3.

Case Study

It is a thorough investigation of an event using multiple sources of evidence. The researchers can only observe and study the context without doing an experiment or involving themselves. #s 7and 8 can also use this design.

Ethnography

This is used to study socio-cultural groups and gain a deeper understanding of their behaviors, culture, beliefs, practices, language, and many more. Items 5, 8, and 9 may use this qualitative method.

Narrative

This type of research gathers first-hand information that can be a personal account or biography of a person or group of people. Such as the case in #7 and #10.

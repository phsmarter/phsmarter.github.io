3. reflection about the success story of John Gokongwei.​


Answer:

From peanuts to billions

Based in Cebu, he started out as an entrepreneur at the tender age of 13, forced to earn money for his family after the untimely passing of his father. He started very small, selling fried peanuts, and worked his way up to selling thread, soap, and candles on a bicycle.

15. how ' john gokongwei BECAME SUCCESSFUL WITH THEIR CHOSEN FIELD AS ENTREPRENEUR?​


Answer:

John Robinson Lim Gokongwei Jr. was a Filipino billionaire businessman and philanthropist. He had holdings in telecommunications, financial services, petrochemicals, power generation, aviation, food, beverage, and livestock farming.

From living in a stylish home, 15-year-old John became a market vendor. He sold peanuts and just whatever he could make money out of. Even with little cash, he allotted some to send to his family in China. He was eventually able to buy a bicycle which enabled him to sell goods in nearby towns.

John Gokongwei Jr. died in November 2019 at the age of 93. He was the third-richest person in the Philippines. He was the founder of JG Summit which has interests in airlines, telecom, banking, food, power, and property. His brother James Go chairs JG Summit.

To those who don't know who John Donne is, he's the clergyman/ Dean of Saint Paul's Cathedral. He is a great English poet and one of his masterpiece is the NO MAN IS AN ISLAND poem. 28. Gawing repleksyon ang quotes at palawakin ito: “Don’t be afraid to give up the good to go for the great.” — John D. Rockefeller


Ang sabi ni John D. Rockefeller, huwag matakot na iwan ang maganda upang makamit ang mas magandang bagay. Sa buhay, hindi sapat na maayos lang ang mga bagay-bagay; kailangan natin ng mga pagbabago at pagsisikap upang maabot ang mga pangarap natin.

Kadalasan, tayong mga tao ay nagsasama-sama sa hindi malaking bagay upang hindi malubog sa mga pagsubok ng buhay. Ngunit sa mga pagkakataon na kailangan nating magsakripisyo ng isang bagay na maganda upang umunlad, mahalaga na magpakatatag tayo.

Sa bawat desisyon na ginagawa natin sa buhay, kailangan nating isipin anong magandang bagay ang ibubuwis natin upang magkaroon tayo ng mas magandang pagkakataon. Ang pagiging handa na iwan ang mga bagay na hindi na kailangan ay magbibigay sa atin ng malaking tyansa upang tumungo sa kahulugan ng buhay.

Kaya baonin mo ang lakas ng loob upang iwan ang mga nakasanayang bagay na hindi na nakakabuti. At habang patuloy tayong magpapakasigasig, mas marami pang magagandang bagay ang darating sa buhay natin.

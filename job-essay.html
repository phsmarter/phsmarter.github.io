1. essay about applying job ​


Answer:

Many employers now require a writing sample, or job application essay, to accompany all applications or résumés — even if writing is not a significant part of the position. The goal of the job application essay is to ensure that applicants have the right communication skills for the position offered. Sometimes, potential employers will provide a specific topic or series of questions for your essay to respond to. However, you may also be asked to provide an essay with no guidance whatsoever. Either way, approach the essay seriously so that it highlights the skills and assets you could bring to the company.

Explanation:

Read the job listing and essay description carefully. Your essay should respond directly to any instructions provided or questions asked by the employer. Part of the "test" with job application essays is to see how well you respond to questions and follow instructions.[2]

As you read, write down keywords or phrases. Focus on traits or skills the employer is looking for in an ideal applicant. The hiring manager will likely scan for these words, so you'll want to highlight them in your essay.

If you don't know much about the company, do a little research on it before you start writing. You might look at their website or do a general internet search with the name of the company to see if any news articles or other reports come up. Go beyond the four corners of the job listing so that you understand who will likely be reading your essay.

If there's anything in the job listing or essay requirements that you don't understand, contact the employer and ask about them. Employers are often impressed by applicants who clarify the employer's intent rather than making assumptions.


2. essay about job preparation​


Answer:

Job preparation is the process of getting ready for the job market and securing employment. It involves several steps and activities to increase one's chances of landing a job and being successful in it.

Self-assessment: Before starting the job search process, it is important to have a clear understanding of one's skills, interests, values, and goals. This can be done through self-assessment tools such as career aptitude tests or by reflecting on past experiences and accomplishments.

Resume and Cover Letter: A well-written resume and cover letter are essential in the job search process. The resume should highlight one's education, work experience, and skills, while the cover letter should explain why one is interested in the job and how they would be a good fit for the company.

Networking: Networking is a crucial part of the job search process. It involves building and maintaining relationships with people in one's industry, connecting with alumni, and attending job fairs and networking events.

Skill Development: Developing new skills can help increase one's chances of being hired. This can be done through online courses, workshops, or on-the-job training.

Interview Preparation: The interview is the final stage of the job search process, and it is important to be well prepared. This includes researching the company, practicing common interview questions, and dressing appropriately.

In conclusion, job preparation is a comprehensive process that involves self-assessment, resume and cover letter writing, networking, skill development, and interview preparation. By taking these steps, one can increase their chances of landing a job and being successful in it.

My primary responsibilities will involve analyzing user needs and designing computer systems and applications that meet those needs.

One of the key aspects of my future job will be staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in computer technology. This will require me to continuously learn and develop new skills, and to keep up-to-date with emerging trends and technologies. As a computer engineer, I will need to be creative and innovative, and to be willing to think outside of the box to develop new and exciting computer systems and applications.

In addition to designing and developing computer systems and applications, I will also be responsible for ensuring that these systems are functioning properly and are secure from outside threats. This will require me to be knowledgeable about cybersecurity and to take steps to protect computer systems from hackers and other cyber threats.

Another important aspect of my future job will be collaborating with other professionals, such as software developers, hardware engineers, and project managers. By working closely with these individuals, I will be able to develop new computer systems and applications that meet the needs of businesses and organizations.

In my future job as a computer engineer, I will also have the opportunity to make a positive impact on the world. By developing new computer systems and applications, I will be contributing to a more efficient and sustainable future for our planet. This is because computer systems can help businesses and organizations to operate more efficiently and reduce their environmental impact.

In conclusion, my future job as a computer engineer will involve designing and developing new computer systems and applications, collaborating with other professionals, and staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in computer technology. By doing so, I will be able to make a positive impact on the world and contribute to a more efficient and sustainable future.

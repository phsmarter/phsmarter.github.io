8. what do you mean by freedom as fact and freedom as achievement?


Answer:

There are three types of freedom. The first kind of freedom is “freedom from,” a freedom from the constraints of society. Second, is “freedom to,” a freedom to do what we want to do. Thirdly, there is “freedom to be,” a freedom, not just to do what we want, but a freedom to be who we were meant to be.

23. What does freedom mean?


Answer:

What does freedom mean?

Freedom is a broad concept that could have varied, but the same connotative meaning.  

In general view, it is about providing every citizen or every person regardless of economic, social and academic status, the equal rights and opportunity to live life to the fullest, without any hesitation and judgment, but with all the liberty to their pursuit of happiness and self-actualization.

In some situations’ specific view, just like what happened to the Philippines perhaps long way before, the most significant meaning of freedom for the Filipinos was when they were being freed from years of colonization. Since then, they were able to speak out and acknowledged their reasons and intuitions freely and independently.  

Another relevant meaning of freedom is most significant to the Africans, during the time when black slavery was condemned by Abraham Lincoln. From then on, they were given equal opportunity to exercise every privilege as members of the state.

No matter how varied the meaning of freedom could be, may it be freedom from the state, relationship, or racial and economic status, it all connotes the same verdict, TO BE FREE, TO BE INDEPENDENT.

26. 1. Discuss: Freedom does not mean doing everything you want. What is the difference between freedom and license? Give examples of license.​


Answer:

In moral and legal philosophy, there exists a distinction between the concepts of freedom and license. The former deals with the rights of the individual; the latter covers the expressed permission (or lack thereof) for more than one individual to engage in an activity.

An example of a license is what drivers carry showing they are legally able to drive. An example of a license is the document a married couple has saying they are legally married.

I. Introduction (10%)

- Briefly define globalization and its impact on industries

- Introduce the specific industry that the essay will focus on

- Provide a thesis statement that outlines the key points that will be discussed

II. Body Paragraphs (60%)

A. Content (50%)

- Discuss the ways in which globalization has impacted the industry in terms of trends

- Provide examples to support the argument

- Analyze the effects of these trends on consumer behavior and preferences

Since the dawn of time, people have been curious in the composition of the universe. The early alchemists were encouraged to practice such a transformative skill since solids could turn into liquids and liquids into gases. The philosopher's stone which could turn base metals into gold, was the main goal of their hunt. Even if all of the attempts were unsuccessful, many ineffective strategies produced crucial insights.

Utilizing nature's building blocks to produce wholly new substances is another aspect of chemistry. It allows for the combination of elements and molecules that, in the absence of clever laboratory technology, would never have been possible. It was one of the pioneers of contemporary applied chemistry.

He experimented with fertilizers for farming and created extracts for baby food and seasonings. And he was one of the pioneers in the effort to make chemistry approachable for a broad audience. His show lectures have become a legend. Students and famous people from their era couldn't stop wondering at how powerfully it thumped, buffered, and smoked.

Conclusion

Therefore, a living lab is the human body. Because chemical reactions are constantly happening inside of us. The building blocks of life can be found throughout the cosmos. Stardust is the source of everything on earth. The sequence of events allowed life to emerge from chemical building blocks here of all places.

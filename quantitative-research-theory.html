8. Pretend you’re the manager in charge of a loan processing department. Describe how you would research this issue using each of the following management approaches or theories: scientific management, general administrative theory, quantitative approach, behavioral approach, systems theory, and contingency theory.


Answer:

Here's how I would approach the issue of loan processing using each of the management approaches or theories you mentioned:

1. Scientific Management: I would first analyze the current loan processing procedures to identify inefficiencies and ineffectiveness. Then I would use the principles of time and motion study to develop a more efficient and effective method of loan processing. I would also break down the loan processing task into smaller, specialized tasks to increase productivity.

2. General Administrative Theory: I would apply this theory by looking at the loan processing department as a whole system and examining the interrelatedness of its components. I would also examine the relationships between the loan processing department and other departments within the organization to ensure coordination and cooperation.

3. Quantitative Approach: I would use data analysis to evaluate the loan processing department's performance, including the time it takes to process a loan, the number of loans processed per day, and the number of errors made during processing. Based on this data, I would determine whether the loan processing procedures need to be revised and make any necessary changes to improve efficiency.

4. Behavioral Approach: I would take into consideration the attitudes and behaviors of employees in the loan processing department. I would conduct employee surveys to determine how employees feel about the current loan processing procedures and whether they feel they have the resources and support needed to perform their job effectively. I would also look at ways to motivate employees and improve job satisfaction.

5. Systems Theory: I would view the loan processing department as a system and analyze it to identify the inputs, processes, outputs, and feedback mechanisms. I would also look at how the loan processing department interacts with other systems within the organization, such as the finance and marketing departments. By doing this, I would identify areas where the loan processing system can be improved to increase efficiency and effectiveness.

6. Contingency Theory: I would use this theory to prepare for potential problems that could arise in the loan processing department. I would identify potential risks and develop contingency plans to minimize their impact on the loan processing procedures. I would also look at ways to respond to unexpected events and changes in the loan processing department to maintain its efficiency and effectiveness.

By using these management approaches and theories, I would be able to comprehensively evaluate the loan processing department and make any necessary changes to improve its efficiency and effectiveness.

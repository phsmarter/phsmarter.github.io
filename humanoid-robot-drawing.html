27. can you imagine humanoids robots replacing human jobs?​

Explanation:

There is ongoing debate among experts and society about the potential impact of humanoid robots on the workforce. While robots have already replaced humans in certain jobs, such as in manufacturing and assembly lines, humanoid robots could potentially replace human workers in a variety of fields, such as hospitality, retail, and even healthcare.

Advocates for humanoid robots argue that they could lead to greater efficiency, productivity, and cost savings for businesses, as well as improve overall safety and reduce workplace injuries. However, critics argue that widespread adoption of humanoid robots could result in widespread job loss, income inequality, and societal unrest.

It is important to note that while humanoid robots have the potential to replace certain jobs, they are not a complete replacement for human workers. Robots still lack the creativity, emotional intelligence, and problem-solving skills that humans possess. Additionally, the ethical implications of using humanoid robots in certain jobs, such as caregiving, still need to be considered.

Overall, the potential impact of humanoid robots on the workforce remains uncertain, and it will require careful consideration and planning to ensure a balance between technological advancements and human employment.


28. What do you understand by, "humanoid robot"?​


Humanoid robots are professional service robots built to mimic human motion and interaction. Like all service robots, they provide value by automating tasks in a way that leads to cost-savings and productivity. Humanoid robots are a relatively new form of professional service robot.

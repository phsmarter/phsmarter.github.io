4. Hand washing commercial script​


Answer:

Title: Clean Hands, Healthy Life

Opening shot: Close up of a pair of hands, covered in dirt and grime.

Voiceover: Our hands are the most essential tools we use in our daily lives.

Cut to: A busy street, where people are going about their daily routine.

Voiceover: We use them to shake hands, hold things, and touch different surfaces.

Cut to: A close-up of a person holding a door handle.

Voiceover: But these same hands can also carry germs and bacteria that can make us sick.

Cut to: A woman sneezing into her hand.

Voiceover: Germs and bacteria can spread quickly from person to person.

Cut to: A man shaking hands with another man.

Voiceover: That's why it's important to wash your hands properly.

Cut to: A person turning on the tap and washing their hands.

Voiceover: Washing your hands with soap and water can help remove germs and bacteria.

Cut to: Close up of soap being lathered onto the hands.

Voiceover: It's a simple, yet effective way to prevent the spread of illnesses.

Cut to: A person drying their hands with a towel.

Voiceover: So remember, wash your hands frequently throughout the day, especially before and after eating, after using the bathroom, and after being in public places.

Closing shot: A group of people with clean hands, smiling and looking healthy.

Voiceover: Clean hands mean a healthy life.

11. Make a script about baby shampoo (English) ​


Answer:

Title: Gentle Care for Your Baby's Hair

Explanation:

Hello and welcome to our program about baby shampoos. As parents, we want nothing but the best for our babies, and that includes their hygiene. Using regular shampoos can be too harsh for their delicate scalp, which is why baby shampoos are formulated differently.

Baby shampoos are mild and gentle, made with ingredients that won't irritate or dry out your infant's scalp. They are also tear-free, ensuring your baby won't be distressed during bath time. Additionally, baby shampoos often have a pleasant scent and can help prevent cradle cap, a common scalp condition in newborns.

It's important to note that not all baby shampoos are the same. Some have added fragrances or detergents that may cause allergic reactions or dryness. It's wise to check the label and do your research before choosing a baby shampoo that's right for your little one.

In conclusion, baby shampoos are essential for maintaining good hygiene for your baby's hair. With their mild and gentle formula, they provide the perfect balance of cleanliness and care. So next time you're shopping for baby essentials, be sure to add a baby shampoo to your cart. Thank you for listening!

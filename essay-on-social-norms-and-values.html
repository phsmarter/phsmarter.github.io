Answer:

As a Filipino student, there are several social norms and values that I want to uphold and preserve. One of these is the concept of "pakikipagkapwa," which is the idea of treating others with respect and empathy. This value is important to me because it promotes a sense of community and helps to build strong relationships with others.

Another norm that I want to uphold is the importance of family. In Filipino culture, family is highly valued and is seen as a source of support and love. This is important to me because it helps to create a sense of belonging and provides a sense of security and stability.

There are also some norms and values that I would like to replace. One of these is the traditional gender roles that are still prevalent in Filipino society. I believe that these roles are limiting and do not allow for individuals to fully realize their potential. I would like to see a society where people are free to pursue their goals and dreams regardless of their gender.

Overall, the social norms and values that are important to me are those that promote equality, respect, and empathy towards others. These values are crucial for creating a more harmonious and inclusive society.

30. How Do Social Media Effect The Values And Norms Of The People Around You? ​


Answer:

In the individual effect, media information about new norms may persuade individuals to accept them.

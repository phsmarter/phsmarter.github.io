What are the Multiple Intelligences of Howard Gardner?

Who is Howard Garner?

He is an American developmental psychologist who theorized the Multiple Intelligences. According to Howard Gardner, people are born unique and possess intelligence in a specific area.

What are the Multiple Intelligences of Howard Gardner?

1. Visual-Spatial Intelligence- an ability to recognize and manipulate patterns. A person with visual-spatial intelligence is good at visualizing things such as the direction of a road, maps, puzzles, and charts.

2. Logical-Mathematical Intelligence- Good in logical reasoning and critical thinking. They can analyze the problem and find solutions. They are also good with numbers and patterns.

3. Musical-Intelligence- Have a strong interest in music. A person with this intelligence performs well in singing activities and playing musical instruments.

4. Linguistic-Verbal Intelligence- They are good with words such as memorizing, spelling, reading, writing, and speaking.

5. Bodily-Kinesthetic Intelligence- They are good with body movement. They perform well in dancing, sports, and physical activities.

6. Naturalistic Intelligence- They have a green thumb. They love exploring and are good at nurturing the environment. They have a passion for taking care of animals.

7. Intrapersonal Intelligence- They are aware and understands their own emotion, feelings and can motivate themselves.

8. Interpersonal Intelligence- They are good with socialization. They create more friends and build good relationships with others.

Answer:

This study aimed to investigate the relationship between study anxiety and academic performance in senior high school students. A total of 120 students from Talisay National High School in Gingoog City were randomly selected as respondents. An adaptive questionnaire was used to assess the level of anxiety, and academic performance was evaluated using School Form 138 to obtain the students' overall GPA. Descriptive statistics, including frequency, mean, and percentage, were used to analyze the data, and standard deviation was utilized to measure the spread of scores within the dataset. Person correlation was then used to determine the significant relationship between the level of study anxiety and academic performance of the respondents.

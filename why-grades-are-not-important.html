Notes and rests are important because music is a combination of sound and silence.

Silence in music comes in the form of “rests”, which are little symbols that tell the player to be silent for a number of counts. Just like notes, rests can last for different durations. This goes a long way towards creating satisfying music, with good dynamic balance between musical activity and rest. Even within a phrase, rests can be used with great effect to add strength to the rhythm of the melody.

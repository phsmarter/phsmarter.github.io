11. The present age of the father is double of the age of his son. If the age of the son after 10 years is equal to the age of the father before 15 years, how old is the son?


Answer:

The son is 25 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Let the present age of son = a

The present age of father = 2a

The age of son after 10 years:

⇨ a + 10

The age of father before 15 years:

⇨ 2a - 15

According to the Question:

⇨ a + 10 = 2a - 15

⇨ 10 + 15 = 2a - a

⇨ 25 = a

Present age of son = a = 25 years.

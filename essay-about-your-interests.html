The effects of climate change are numerous, ranging from rising sea levels and temperatures to more frequent and intense natural disasters such as hurricanes, floods, and wildfires. In this essay, I will discuss how climate change will affect me as a student in today's generation.

The first way that climate change will impact me as a student is through the availability and quality of education. Use the Progressive Forms of Verbs

I have learned about:

The progressive form is used to express an ongoing action in progress at some point in time. Verbs can appear in any one of three progressive forms: present progressive, past progressive, and future progressive.

€ Present Progressive

The present progressive shows a continuing action, or an action that is going on at the time of speaking. it is formed with the present be verb (am, is, are) plus the present participle (-ing ending).

The be verb am is used if the subject is |.

EXAMPLE: I am doing my task now.

The be verb is used if the subject is singular.

EXAMPLE: Jessa is collecting the books

She is woalking towards the teacher's table.

The verb are is used if the subject is plural and if the subject is you.

EXAMPLE: Troy and Edna are opening the windows.

€ Past Progressive

The past progressive expresses an ongoing action at a particular moment in the past. The action started before that moment but nas nor fnished at that moment.

EXAMPLES: Sheryl was riding her bike yesterday.

Michoel and Randy were playing basketball a whle ago.

The past progressive is offen used with the simple past tense. You use the past progressive to express a long acion, and you use the simple past to express a short action that happens in the middle of the long action. The two ideas can be joined with when or while.

EXAMPLES: He was watching tv when the telephone rang.

We were walking when a stranger knocked on the door.

The past progressive is formed with the past be verb (was, were) plus the present participle (-ing ending).

The be verb was is used if the subject is singular.

EXAMPLES: Terence was wating at the bus stop.

Louella was writing in her diary.

The be verb were is used if the subject is plural and if the subject is you.

EXAMPLES: Ruel and Mandy were sketoching in the Art room.

They were listening to the radio when they heard a crash.

You were hiding in the kitchen.

€ Future Progressive

The future progressive expresses an action that will be happening on going on at some point in the future. It is formed with the modal will or shall plus be plus the present participle (-ing ending).

EXAMPLES: I shall be visiting the place next year.

My sister will be joining the quiz bee sometime in October.

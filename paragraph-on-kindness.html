5. what are the four kinds of paragraph​


Answer:

Because there are four paragraph types — narrative, descriptive, expository, and persuasive—the paragraph can be used to describe or explain an endless variety of things.

1. Narrative - tells us the details of one main event or the series of events.

2. Expository - gives/present information, opinions or ideas.

3. Descriptive - paints a picture, only using words, about what a person or a thing is like.

4. Persuasive - expresses an opinion of a specific topic. It is developed with reasons based on facts.

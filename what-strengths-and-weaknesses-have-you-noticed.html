19. what strengths and weaknesses have you noticed oe identified​


Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths:

Enthusiasm. Creative thinking. Task prioritization. Discipline. Determination.

Weaknesses:

Not taking criticism well. Impatient. Lazy. Easily bored. Procrastinate.

Knowing your top strengths gives you a filter to use when deciding what you will and will not do with your time.

Knowing your greatest weaknesses allows you to focus on the areas where you need to improve in order to avoid being limited.

Everyone enjoys knowing their strengths, but it takes a mature person to deliberately seek out and address their faults. While your strengths will help you succeed, focusing on your top shortcomings will help you live a more stress-free life.

How to Recognize Your Weaknesses and Strengths?

Make two lists. I propose that you spend about 30 minutes alone generating two lists before using any outside sources to help you discover your strengths and weaknesses. Speak with someone you can trust. Take a personality test to find out more about yourself. Experiment with new ideas.

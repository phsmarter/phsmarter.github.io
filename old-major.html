24. who were the major prophets of the Old Testament, and what were their key message?​


Answer:

There were four major prophets of the Old Testament:

Isaiah- Isaiah prophesied about the coming of Messiah, the judgment upon Israel and Judah for their sins, and the restoration of Israel.

Jeremiah- Jeremiah prophesied about the coming exile of Jerusalem, the judgment upon Israel for their sins, and the future restoration of Israel.

Ezekiel- Ezekiel prophesied about the judgment upon Israel and the restoration of Israel, and he had vivid and symbolic visions.

Daniel- Daniel prophesied about the coming Messiah, the rise and fall of empires, and the judgment upon Israel for their sins.

The key message of these prophets was to call God's people to repent of their sins and turn back to God, to warn of coming judgment and exile if they did not, and to prophesy about the coming of the Messiah and the restoration of God's people. They also spoke of the sovereignty and justice of God, and the importance of obedience to His ways.

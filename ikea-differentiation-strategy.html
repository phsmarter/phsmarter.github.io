3. what are the benefits to IKEA of shifting so much of its global production to china?​


Answer:

Explanation:

- Low production costs: Cost of manufacturing in China is much cheaper due to lower labor costs and other raw material costs. Local sourcing of material and labor helped IKEA in keeping the cost low and price its products competitively in the market against local players.

- Lower maintenance costs: Quality checks and audits done locally helped IKEA to keep its maintenance cost cheaper and still provide global standards in quality.

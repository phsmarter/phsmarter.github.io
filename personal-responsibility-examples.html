Be a good pollutant 8.Which of the following is NOT an example of physical change? A. Dissolving of sugar in water B. Explosion of dynamite D. Blown by wind 9.It is a change in the form, appearance, and composition of a material. A. Chemical change B. Physical change C. Neutron D. Population 10. It is the proper way of waste sergregation. A. Responsible waste disposal B. Responsible parenthood C. Responsible pupil D. Responsible person


Answer:

7. C. Be a good steward

8. B. explosion of dynamite

9. A. Chemical change

10. A. Responsible waste disposal

Explanation:

Materials undergo changes all the time. It can be a physical or chemical change. Physical change happens when there is a change in form or appearance but the structure, composition, and identity of the material remain the same. An example is a change in the phase of the material, such as the melting of ice. On the other hand, a chemical change is a kind of change wherein the structure, composition, or identity of material changes. Examples of this type of change are burning, corrosion, or any change that involves a chemical reaction. Changes can either be useful or harmful. These changes may also be done by man. Useful changes are those that benefit the community or the environment. One example of useful changes is practicing composting. On the other hand, harmful changes are those that can harm the community or the environment. An example of this is the burning of plastic. As members of the community, we have to do our part in minimizing or stopping the harmful changes in the environment so that we can maximize the benefits we get from the environment. We need to be good stewards of our environment such as being responsible of the wastes we produce.

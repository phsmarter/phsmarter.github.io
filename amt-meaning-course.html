12. 1. Chlorine Gas occupies a volume of 1.2L at 720tor. What volume will it occupi at 1.0 amt. pressure​


Answer:

1.1364 L

To determine the volume of chlorine gas at a different pressure, we can use Boyle's law, which states that the product of the initial pressure and volume is equal to the product of the final pressure and volume.

P1 * V1 = P2 * V2

where:

P1 = Initial pressure

V1 = Initial volume

P2 = Final pressure

V2 = Final volume

In this case, we have:

P1 = 720 torr

V1 = 1.2 L

P2 = 1.0 atm (we'll convert torr to atm)

V2 = ?

To convert torr to atm, we divide by 760 (since 760 torr = 1 atm):

P1 = 720 torr / 760 = 0.947 atm

Now we can rearrange the equation and solve for V2:

V2 = (P1 * V1) / P2

= (0.947 atm * 1.2 L) / 1.0 atm

= 1.1364 L

Therefore, the volume of chlorine gas at 1.0 atm pressure would be approximately 1.1364 L.

22. A 400ml sample of a gas is a at 10 atm what wii be the vol.i the pressure become 40 atm.with a fixed amt if gad and temperature?


Given:

[tex]P_{1} = \text{10 atm}[/tex]

[tex]V_{1} = \text{400 mL}[/tex]

[tex]P_{2} = \text{40 atm}[/tex]

Unknown:

[tex]V_{2}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]P_{1}V_{1} = P_{2}V_{2}[/tex]

[tex]V_{2} = V_{1} × \frac{P_{1}}{P_{2}}[/tex]

[tex]V_{2} = \text{400 mL} × \frac{\text{10 atm}}{\text{40 atm}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{V_{2} = \text{100 mL}}[/tex]

29. AMT AT KITA" 1.) Reaction Summary on 2) Answer the following: Ani at kita 1) what is the importance of forming industry? to support 2) Why do we need the forming industry?​


The forming industry refers to the sector of the economy that specializes in shaping and manipulating raw materials, such as metals, plastics, and composites, into useful products and components. The importance of the forming industry can be seen in several ways:

1.Economic growth: The forming industry plays a crucial role in driving economic growth by creating jobs, generating income, and contributing to the gross domestic product (GDP) of a country. The industry provides employment opportunities for skilled workers, engineers, designers, and managers, and creates a demand for raw materials and machinery.

2.Innovation and technology: The forming industry is often at the forefront of innovation and technology development, as companies compete to create more efficient, durable, and sustainable products. Advancements in the forming industry can lead to new materials, production processes, and applications that benefit a wide range of industries.

3.Productivity and efficiency: The forming industry enables the mass production of complex shapes and designs with high precision and accuracy, which can improve the productivity and efficiency of manufacturing processes. This, in turn, can lead to cost savings and higher quality products.

4.Infrastructure and supply chain: The forming industry is closely tied to other industries, such as transportation, logistics, and energy, and requires a robust infrastructure and supply chain to function effectively. The industry can help drive investments in infrastructure and logistics, leading to improvements in transportation networks, energy systems, and supply chain management.

In summary, the forming industry is important for economic growth, innovation and technology development, productivity and efficiency, and infrastructure and supply chain development. Without the forming industry, it would be difficult to create the products and components that we rely on in many industries, from aerospace to automotive to electronics.

1. Speech for running as an SSG grade 10 representative


In making your speech, introduce yourself. Not only your names, but your personality and values as well. State the purpose why you would want to become their representative and what are the things you can do for them as a leader. Also, take into consideration of what they are looking for in a representative. try to be courteous and avoid saying negative words against your opponent. State your plans and platforms and what changes do you want to achieve for the benefit of the students.

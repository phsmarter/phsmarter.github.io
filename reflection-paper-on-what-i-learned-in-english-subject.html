26. REFLECTION PAPER FOR ENGLISH LEARNINGS


A reflection paper is a personal response to what you have learned in your English class or through your English language journey. It is an opportunity to reflect on your own thoughts and feelings about your progress and growth in the language. Here are some steps to follow when writing a reflection paper:

Start by introducing the topic or experience you are reflecting on. In this case, it would be your English learning journey.

Describe the specific learning experience or lesson that made an impact on you. Explain why this experience was important and how it has contributed to your understanding of the English language.

Analyze your personal thoughts and feelings about the learning experience. How did it affect you emotionally, mentally, or even socially? Did it change your perception of the language, or how you approach English in your everyday life?

Reflect on your own strengths and weaknesses in English. What are your strengths and weaknesses in speaking, writing, or understanding English? What are some areas you still need to improve on?

Conclude your reflection by summarizing the main points of your paper and offering some insight on how you plan to continue your English learning journey.

25. how can we reverse the process for each of the following?a. drying leaves to make them fresh againb. fixing an eggshell to make it wholec. keeping the foods fresh from the spoilaged. heating of watere. flowing the water back to the top


Answer:

Reversible and Irreversible processes:

The given processes in the question are Reversible and Irreversible processes.

A Reversible process is process where the state of an object can be returned back to its original state or condition.An Irreversible process is a process that can not be returned back to its original state or condition.

Let's discuss one by one the scenarios:

Drying leaves to make them fresh again :

This process is irreversible. Once the leaves are detached from the branches of the tree the nutrients from the tree will not flow through the veins of the leaves. Hence the leaf will die. The leaf can not be brought back to its original state because the composition of the molecules of the leaf are also change, in this case have dried already and can no longer absorb nutrients from outside source.

Fixing an eggshell to make it whole

There is no way where a broken eggshell may be returned back to its original shape, once the shell has cracked there is no way that the pieces will once again be bonded to each other. Hence, this is an irreversible process.

Keeping the foods fresh from the spoilage

The chemical composition of the spoiled food can not be returned back to its original condition. The decomposition of the food has already taken place hence this is an irreversible process.

Heating of water

Water can be heated and cooled down, hence the process is reversible.

Flowing the water back to the top

By using of a water pump, Water can continuously flow from top. Therefore this is reversible.

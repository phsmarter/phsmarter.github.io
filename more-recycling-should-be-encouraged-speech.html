5. 1. How can you explain recycling to your younger sibling or friend? 2. How can you encourage people in your community to recycle more often?


Answer:

1. Recycling is a way to turn old things into new things again. Instead of throwing things like plastic bottles, paper, and cans in the garbage, we can collect them and take them to a special place where they will be made into new things. This is good for the environment because it saves energy and resources, and it also helps to keep our planet clean and healthy.

2. Here are some ways to encourage people in your community to recycle more often:

- Set a good example: Make sure that you are recycling at home and in public places like parks and schools. When others see you recycling, they may be more likely to do it too.

- Educate others: Share information about the benefits of recycling and how to do it properly. You can make posters or flyers, or even give a presentation at school or in your community.

- Make it easy: Provide bins for recycling in public places and encourage businesses to do the same. This makes it easier for people to recycle on the go.

- Offer incentives: Consider offering rewards or incentives for people who recycle, such as discounts at local businesses or recognition in a community newsletter.

- Get involved: Join a local environmental group or volunteer for a community clean-up event. This shows your commitment to recycling and encourages others to get involved too.

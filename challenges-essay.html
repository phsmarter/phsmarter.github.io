9. PERSUASIVE ESSAY ABOUT TURNING CHALLENGES INTO OPPORTUNITY


Challenges are opportunities. For the pessimistic people, the statement may sound weird but for the optimistic ones, the statement is a reality. Challenges are part of our daily lives. We struggle the moment we woke up, when we rode the jeepneys to school or work. Every single action is a struggle in our part but only our attitude towards this struggle can make the difference. Let us not forget that our attitude matters. If we consider this struggle as opportunities then these will become opportunities because not all bad things are bad sometimes it is the path towards attaining the best. What comes first in our minds is that challenges are problems and nothing else. Battling them is a brilliant idea. The reality regarding challenges is they're not actually a problem nor difficulties but they are sometimes opportunities instead. Challenges were not meant to destroy us or to let us feel weak but it provides us with strengths and lessons in which we can use for the rest of our lives. They're simply blessings in disguise.

11. Essay about turning challenges into opportunies


Answer:

As long as there is life, there will always be challenges to overcome. A challenge can be turned into an opportunity when we try to overcome it. It may be the challenge of learning to walk, or challenge of learning to speak, we have to keep trying to make any challenge an opportunity. Covid 19 pandemic lockdown came as a very big challenge to all the mankind; there has not been any continent, country, society, and city that has not been affected by this pandemic.  

Life came to a standstill; factories, offices, schools, colleges, were closed down by the orders of Governments. People became confined to their houses. I tried to take this bull of lockdown by its horns. After having spent a few days of lockdown in trivial activities, I realized I could not afford to waste my precious time in idle activities.  

I set certain goals for me viz. health goal, knowledge goal, and self-improvement goal.  

Health Goal

Health is wealth. In order to take care of this invaluable health during the lockdown, I made a physical exercise regime. In the evening I would do physical exercises consisting of skipping, jumping, stretching exercises along with a few yoga asnas. When the relaxations in the lockdown were allowed, I began going for 5 kilometer long an evening walk. This improved my health considerably. Not only was my physical health at its best, but mental health also.  

Knowledge Goal

Covid 19 pandemic lockdown posed a serious threat to my knowledge and studies; however, I prepared to keep this threat at bay. Though the schools were providing online education, yet I planned to corroborate my online learning with self-study time-table. Since lockdown provided me considerable amount of leisure time, I resolved to make best use of this time in self-improvement. I made a list of some classic and self-help books to read them in my free time. I didn’t allow myself to be tempted by TV or mobile phones. I felt a unique confidence and contentment after I had read just a few books.  

Self-improvement  

Apart from reading classic books, I practiced meditation and mental yoga. I read a book on meditation. I practiced all the breathing, mind-calming, and tranquility inducing techniques. I was amazed at the wholesome effects I began experiencing. As a result of meditation and mind-calming activities my life was filled with hope and new purpose. First time in my life realized how meditation and yoga cleanse our mind of negative thoughts and feelings.  

I would not have been able to extract so much out of life, if the challenge of Covid 19 had not come. I have realized this truth that in every challenge is the opportunity to achieve greater things in life. We should not get scared or cowed down by the challenge looming in front of us. Challenges are blessings in disguise; they bring the best out of us.

18. turning challenges to opportunities essay examples ​


Turning challenges into opportunities is a real challenge. It is also one of the most important skills for entrepreneurs.

One of the ways I've found to turn challenges into opportunities is by using my business as an example. I'm always looking for ways to make the most of my business, and one way is to look at what my customers are telling me about their problems and how I can help them overcome those problems.

When I see a customer who needs help with a problem, I don't just ask them what their problem is — I ask them how they would solve it if they had time and money to devote to it themselves. And then we talk about how we can combine our resources in order to make solving that problem easier, faster or cheaper.

29. Turning challenges into opportunities essay


When writing an essay, you have to make an outline about the topic. Plan how you will write your introduction, what you will be pointing out in the body, and how you will end your essay using the conclusion. Make sure that every main point has supporting details as well. Here's a sample essay on turning challenges into opportunities:

"Facing challenges is no easy feat for many. It may set us back from our goals or it may even make us feel unmotivated. Although there are little challenges and big challenges, it is how you look at them that you'll be able to overcome them. It is when we see challenges as opportunities that we will be able to properly face them and overcome them.

People face challenges in their everyday lives--from small inconveniences to large setbacks. But instead of looking at them solely as enemies, we can try to shift our perspective and ask ourselves how these challenges can benefit us. Just like one fresh graduate who kept getting turned down at numerous job interviews. When he looked at these challenges as an opportunity for him to learn what he's lacking and how he can improve, he overcame the obstacle and finally got a job.

Challenges may make us feel bad about ourselves, but always remember that it's okay to feel bad temporarily and use challenges as opportunities to improve yourself."

Tips on How to Write an EssayBe conciseAlways support your main ideas in the succeeding sentencesSupport your ideas by using facts, experiences, or statisticsUse transitionsMake sure that the conclusion restates your thesis statement in the introduction

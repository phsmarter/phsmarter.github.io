24. what are social issues of philippine revolution?


During the 19th century life was transformed by the Industrial Revolution and for ordinary people, life become more comfortable. It was a period of great social and political changes.

1. SOCIAL CHANGES OCCURRED IN THE 19TH CENTURY

The major social changes in the 19th centuary is listed below:

1)Industrial Revolution

2) Slavery Abolishment

3) Massiver Urbanization

4) Increase the profit and productivity

2. POLITICAL CHANGES OCCURRED IN THE 19TH CENTURY

The major political changes in the 19th centuary is listed below:

1) Individual individual rights and freedom of the press

2) Representative government

3) Democratic elections

4) Open trade.

3. PROBLEMS OF INQUILINO SYSTEM

An inquilino is a labourer indebted to a landlord who allows him to form a farm in parts of his property and who in exchange works without pay for the landlord. Some of the major problems of this system is:

1)The land market opens with each landlord deciding how much land to buy

2) The workers are not being paid the wages that they are expecting.

3) Increse the numbers of landless agricultural labourers.

4) Increase the income distribution problem

4. NEGATIVE IMPACT OF BOURBON REFORMS

The Bourbon Reforms played an important role in the long-term effects that caused independence. The objectives of the reform were to increase agricultural production, improve military defense, increase production and collection of tax revenue, reduce contraband, and streamline the administration of the colonies. Some of the negative impacts of bourbon reforms is:

1) Losing political control

2) Independence movement restricted.

3) Forcing the colonies to become more dependent

4) Limit the power of manufactured goods

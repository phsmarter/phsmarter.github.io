Practical Research A research study conducted by Torrendon (2017) entitled Difficulties Encountered by the Student Researchers and the Influences on their Research Output revealed that most student researchers have difficulties focus on conflicts in time management. They cannot proceed if most of the time their minds are pre-occupied. When doing a research paper, you must have an enough time to work on. Additionally, having not enough background knowledge is also a factor that affects them since choosing for a research topic will be easy if you have observed well and been listening to the discussions. Lastly, not all of them are having their own media devices that can help them improve their output. There are a lot of ways on how are you going to find those. What can you say about the findings of the conducted research of Torrendo?


Answer:

Finding a good topic is essential to producing quality research output. This can be difficult for student researchers, who may lack the background knowledge to identify good topics. Additionally, time management is crucial for research papers. Student researchers may have difficulty focusing on their work if they are preoccupied with other things. Lastly, having access to media devices can improve research output. However, not all student researchers have their own media devices. The findings of the Torrendo study suggest that these factors can influence the quality of student research output.

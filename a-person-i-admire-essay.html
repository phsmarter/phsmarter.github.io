Explanation:

One person who I can think of that many people admire is Malala Yousafzai. Malala is a young woman from Pakistan who became an advocate for girls' education at a very young age. Despite facing threats and violence, she continued to speak out for the rights of girls to receive an education.

One of the reasons why many people admire Malala is her bravery and determination. At the age of 11, she began speaking out against the Taliban's ban on girls' education in her home region of Swat Valley in Pakistan. She wrote a blog for the BBC about her experiences, using the pseudonym "Gul Makai" to protect her identity. Despite the risks, she continued to speak out and advocate for girls' education, even after the Taliban targeted her and shot her in the head in 2012.

Another reason why people admire Malala is her commitment to her cause. Despite the dangers she faced, she never wavered in her belief that all girls have the right to an education. She has since become a prominent figure in the global education movement and was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2014 for her efforts.

Moreover, Malala's work has also demonstrated that young people can make a real difference in the world. At just 18 years old, she became the youngest ever Nobel laureate, and her story has inspired countless young people to take action and speak out for what they believe in.

Malala's determination, bravery, commitment, and inspiration are all admirable qualities that have made her an inspiration to many people around the world. Her work and dedication to education have shown that one person can make a significant impact on the world, and her story serves as a reminder that each individual can make a difference.

27. Describe a person you admire essay. Contain phrases, Dependent clause and Independent clause with 6 or more sentences. ​


Answer:

Identifying Independent and Dependent Clauses

When you want to use commas and semicolons in sentences and when you are concerned about whether a sentence is or is not a fragment, a good way to start is to be able to recognize dependent and independent clauses. The definitions offered here will help you with this.

Independent Clause

An independent clause is a group of words that contains a subject and verb and expresses a complete thought. An independent clause is a sentence.

Jim studied in the Sweet Shop for his chemistry quiz.

Dependent Clause

A dependent clause is a group of words that contains a subject and verb but does not express a complete thought. A dependent clause cannot be a sentence. Often a dependent clause is marked by a dependent marker word.

When Jim studied in the Sweet Shop for his chemistry quiz . . . (What happened when he studied? The thought is incomplete.)

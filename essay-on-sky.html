QUANTITATIVE OBSERVATION

Which of the following statement is a quantitative observation?

A. The sky is blue.

C. The sky is dark

B. The sky is 2000 km high.

D. The sky is very high

ANSWER

Amongst the given choices above the correct answer is letter B. The sky is 2000 km high.

When we say quantitative observation, it is the sort of observation or analysis, that is based on statistical data, mathematical modeling, measurements and things like that, so the answer is B.

The other three choices are not quantitative observation, because those are just pure observations and perception of human eye.

Therefore, blue light is scattered more than red light and the sky appears blue during the day.

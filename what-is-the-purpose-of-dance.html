2. What are the Purposes ofDance?​


Answer:

ceremonial, recreational and artistic

Explanation:

CEREMONIAL DANCE –

dances created or performed for rituals or celebrations (dances of Native Americans and  West Africans to celebrate life events such as harvest, ritual dances associated with worship, etc.)

RECREATIONAL –

dancing for entertainment and fun, to support recreational activities, social dancing (ballroom, line dancing, aerobic dance, dance as a hobby).

ARTISTIC EXPRESSION –

dance created with the intent to express or communicate emotion, feelings, ideas (ballet, tap dance, modern dance, dance created and performed in a concert and/or theatrical  setting or an audience)

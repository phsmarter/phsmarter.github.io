9. Please help me answer this , I really need it for tomorrow "HOPE OF THE FLOWERS"​


Answer:

Theme: Search for meaning and purpose in life.

Where: In a meadow

Conflict: Internal struggle of the two main characters, Stripe and Yellow, as they search for their purpose in life.

(Explanation)Far on the story, They begin to feel that there must be more to life than just climbing up the stem, and they become determined to find it. This leads to a conflict between their own desires and the expectations of the other caterpillars, who believe that they should just continue climbing without asking questions.

Ending: Reaching the top of the stem and become pretty butterflies

Life lesson,(in my opinion) :

The value of patience and perseverance, Stripe and Yellow faced many challenges and obstacles on their journey, but they continued to move forward and never gave up on their search for meaning. This teaches the value of being patient and persistent in pursuing one's goals and dreams.

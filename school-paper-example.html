27. You are the editor-in-chief of your school's publication. The school paper adviser requires you to write a 2-3 paragraph opinion article about the Jeepney Phaseout for a special edition paper. You need to take side and be specific in explaining your point of view by provingclear examples and illustrations to support your arguments.​


Answer:

As the editor-in-chief of our school's publication, I believe that the Jeepney Phaseout should be reconsidered. While it is true that these traditional vehicles contribute to air pollution, their removal would have significant consequences for our country's transport system and the livelihoods of thousands of Filipino families.

Jeepneys are not just a mode of transportation; they are a cultural icon that symbolizes Filipino ingenuity and resourcefulness. By phasing them out, we risk losing a piece of our national identity. Moreover, Jeepneys are often the only means of transportation for low-income Filipinos who cannot afford private vehicles or ride-sharing services. If they are removed from the roads, many people may struggle to get to work or school, particularly in rural areas where public transport options are limited.

Rather than simply getting rid of Jeepneys, we should invest in upgrading them with more eco-friendly engines or electric motors. This would not only help reduce air pollution but also provide an opportunity to support local businesses and create jobs in the automotive industry. By finding a sustainable solution, we can preserve our cultural heritage and support the livelihoods of Filipino families while also protecting the environment.

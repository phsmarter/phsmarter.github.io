1. Molecule/Compound: H2OA. LinearB. BentC. Trigonal PlanarD. Trigonal PyramidalE. TetrahedralF. Octahedral​


Answer:

B. Bent

Explanation:

Water has 4 regions of electron density around the central oxygen atom (2 bonds and 2 lone pairs). These are arranged in a tetrahedral shape. The resulting molecular shape is bent with an H-O-H angle of 104.5°.

3. if there are four electron pairs around the central atom of a molecule this electron pairs are in a _________ arrangementa.linearb.trigonal planarc. tetrahedral d. octahedral​


Answer:

Tetrahedral molecule

Explanation:

A Tetrahedral molecule has four electron pairs around the central atom. They are arranged so that the repulsion between the electron pairs is minimum. Electron pairs are arranged at a 109.5° angle in a tetrahedral molecule.


4. explain the difference between voidable contract and void or in existence contract.​


Answer:

Essentially, the difference between void and voidable contracts is enforceability: a void contract is illegal and unenforceable; a voidable contract is legal and enforceable. A contract that is void is unenforceable, meaning that neither party has legal recourse against the other for a breach.

14. 9. Which of the following molecules is tetrahedral


Answer:

Molecules of methane, CH4, ammonia, NH3, and water, H2O, all have four electron groups around their central atom, so they all have a tetrahedral shape and bond angles of about 109.5°. For CH4 molecules, all four groups are bond groups.

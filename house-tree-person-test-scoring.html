19. the scores of a students in three test are 19 ,17, 15 what must be the 4th score to gain average of 17?​


The 4th score must be 17 in order to gain an average of 17.

Solution:
Convert the worded problem into algebraic expression and find for the value of x.

(19 + 17 + 15 + x)/4 = 17
(51 + x)/4 = 17
51 + x = 4 (17)
51 + x = 68
x = 68 - 51
x = 17

Verification
(19 + 17 + 15 + 17)/4 = 17
68/4 = 17
17 = 17

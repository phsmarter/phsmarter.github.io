2. ESSAY: explain how science, technology and society (STS) subject helps you become better as a student and a successful individual in the future. ​


Answer:

Science, technology and society (STS) subjects help me become better as a student because they give me an understanding of the context in which scientific and technological innovations take place. This context includes the social, economic, political and cultural factors that influence the development of science and technology. Then, as a result of this contextual understanding. I can more effectively engage with scientific and technological texts and understand their meaning. Additionally, by studying STS subjects. I can develop critical thinking skills that will be useful in all areas of life.

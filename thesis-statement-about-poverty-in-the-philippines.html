17. Formulate an effective thesis statement about challenges that the Philippine society is facing today.​


A thesis statement is the main idea of your thesis or paper. It sums up the points you will be raising and your stand on the issues that will be discussed in your paper.

Example of effective thesis statement about the challenges that the Philippine society is facing today:

The challenges that the Philippine society is facing today reveal the deep problems rooted in the corruption and power greed of the people in the government.

How to formulate an effective thesis statement?

Begin with a question that would tackle your chosen topic.Draft your initial response to the question you have formulated.Elaborate on your initial answer by adding details as to "why" you have decided on this answer and "how" you can convince your readers to side or agree with you.Review and refine your answer. There you have your thesis statement!

21. write a thesis statement in his question to inform readers about the causes of flash flood in key cities in the Philippines​


Answer:

Depends where in the Philippines you are. Much of the country experiences heavy rains and lots of typhoons every year, so flooding is practically guaranteed. Whether it’s destructive or not depends on several reasons. If you’re in Manila, congestion and geography are the main reasons. Manila has always been prone to flood. It’s low-lying and the drainage is not good (there used to be canals and channels - called esteros, but they’ve either been built over or have been backed up with garbage) - plus a lot of it is paved over so there’s no ground absorption. Also everything drains to only one place, Manila Bay, via flood drains (which are inevitably clogged with garbage) or the Pasig River, which itself floods. Outside Manila, heavy rainfalls turn into disastrous and destructive floods and landslides (or mudslides) because of deforested hills and mountains.

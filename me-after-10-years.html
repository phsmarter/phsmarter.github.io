2. your age is thrice my age 10 years after you will be twice as old as me ​


Answer:

The present ages are 10 and 30.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the age of one person

y be the age of the other person

x = 3y  ---equation 1

x + 10 = 2(y+10)    ------equation 2

Equate equations 1 and 2

3y + 10 = 2y + 20

3y - 2y = 20-10

y = 10

Substitute y=10 to equation 1

x = 3y

x = 3(10)

x = 30

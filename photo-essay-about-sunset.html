Ang larawang sunset ay isang kahanga-hangang tanawin ng kalikasan kung saan ang araw ay nagtatapos sa kanilang arawang paglalakbay sa kalangitan. Sa larawang ito, nakikita natin ang magandang kulay na ginagawa ng araw sa kalangitan at nagbibigay ito ng kalma at kapayapaan sa ating mga damdamin.

20. make an inductive and deductive reasoning about sunset​


Answer:

Inductive reasoning: We observe that the sun sets every day at a specific time. We can conclude that the sun will likely set at a specific time tomorrow as well.

Deductive reasoning: If the Earth rotates on its axis, and the rotation causes the sun to rise in the east and set in the west, then we can deduce that the sun will set in the west at a specific time every day.

27. interpret data about sunrise and sunset to tell when daytime is long and when daytime is short.​


Answer:

Daytime is shorter in winter than in summer. As the Earth moves around the sun during a year, the northern half of the Earth is tilted towards the sun in the summer, making daytime longer than night. In winter, this reverses; the earth tilts away from the sun and night time becomes longer.

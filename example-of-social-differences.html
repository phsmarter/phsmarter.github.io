30. Write five example o different key parts of socialization? ​


Answer:

primary, secondary, developmental, anticipatory and resocialization.

Explain:

1.Primary socialization

This type of socialization happens when a child learns the values, and behaviors that should be in order to live a specific culture.

2.Secondary socialization

This type of socialization occurs when a person learns an appropriate behavior within a smaller group which is still part of a larger society.

3.Developmental socialization

This type of socialization involves a learning process and the focus indeveloping our social skills.

4 Anticipatory socialization

This type of socialization refers to the process in a person practices for future social relationships.

5.Resocialization

This type of socialization rejecting previous behavior and accepting new ones from one part of his life to another.

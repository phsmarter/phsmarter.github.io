Padre Faura Witnesses the Execution of Rizal

Poem that has become a written witness of Rizal's death. It was written by  Danton Remoto. A free - verse poem relaying what had happened on the 30th of December, 1896. It uses some words which are appealing to the heart. It conveys the message of looking back and paying respect to our national hero.

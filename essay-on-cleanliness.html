9. IV. Make an Essay about on how you can value our world and how you can help spread the cleanliness, order, and its hazardous waste. Minimum of 1000 words.​


The natural world is an incredible wonder that inspires us all. It underpins our economy, our society, indeed our very existence. Our forests, rivers, oceans and soils provide us with the food we eat, the air we breathe, the water we irrigate our crops with. We also rely on them for numerous other goods and services we depend on for our health, happiness and prosperity.

These natural assets are often called the world's 'natural capital'. These benefits are also hugely important to the economy – from farming and forestry to leisure and tourism. If you add them all up, the total value of these benefits is phenomenal – at least US$125 trillion every year.

Because nature is free, we often take it for granted and overexploit it. We clear forests, overfish oceans, pollute rivers and build over wetlands without taking account of the impact this will have. By not taking into account the benefits we get from nature, we create huge social and economic costs for ourselves.

We need to look at the value of nature in economic and social terms to help us better understand the full implications of the choices we make. Instead of making decisions based on short-term financial interests, we can look at the longer-term benefits for people and the economy – and of course nature itself. Using this argument, we’re persuading governments and businesses to take better care of the natural world, so that it can continue to sustain us all into the future

21. Write an essay about the topic “ Sanitation and Cleanliness for a Healthy Environment”. The essay should be composed of at least two paragraphs.


Answer:

Sanitation and cleanliness play an important role in the betterment of the physical environment. Sanitation refers to the maintenance of hygienic conditions and improvement and promotion of human health through different practices including safe disposal of human waste, waste water and waste solid, garbage collection and health and hygiene management. Cleanliness and sanitation is not only about keeping clean, removing dirt and dust but also about protecting the environment and its local resources, We must reduce the harmful effect of inadequate sanitation practices in order improve health and hygiene and promote sustainable development.

There are many people in our country who do not have access to sanitation and cleanliness facilities. Several children and adults belonging to the impoverished sections of the society stay and grow up in an unsafe, unhygienic environment, which causes a number of serious diseases in them. Responding to and fighting against inadequate sanitation and cleanliness conditions, Buddy Care Foundation intends to provide the poor and unprivileged access to safe, essential sanitation facilities. Through our different proposed programs, we wish to help spread awareness about cleanliness and sanitation needs and improve the health status and native physical environment of the poor and unprivileged people and communities.


22. "ESSAY" As an executive housekeeper/ manager of the hotel, how will you maintain the cleanliness of the hotel in this time of pandemic?


Answer:

Wash all parts of your hands frequently (at least 20 seconds if using an alcohol-based hand rub, and at least 40 seconds with soap and water), including after exchanging objects such as money or credit cards with guests.

Cover a cough or sneeze with a bent elbow or tissue and throw away the tissue in a closed bin.

Maintain at least a 1 metre distance from other staff and guests. This includes avoid hugging, kissing, or shaking hands. If you can’t guarantee the distance, wear a fabric mask. Be sure to check local and national guidelines on the use of masks.

Staying behind plexiglass boards at reception and concierge desks can prevent droplet transmission.

Teleworking may be a possibility for some jobs, which can help reduce physical contact with others.

HOW WE CAN VALUE OUR WORLD

In this world I, you and all other living beings. All the people we care about are like family, friends, lovers and all life in today's world. The world is a place where we will spend the rest of our lives until we die. Therefore we should be able to appreciate the world in which we live and time.

Value our world can be done by spreading kindness to all living beings. It is important to remember once again that we are not alone in this world. There are billions if not trillions of other living things that live with us in the world. By loving each other and spreading love, we actually appreciate this world. Doing good and helping each other, not hurting animals and preserving plants is a form of love between living things.

Another example of how to value this world is to take care of this planet, planet earth. With various issues such as global warming and climate change which are assumed to destroy the planet, we should take precautions to prevent this from happening. If the earth is damaged, it will certainly have an impact on all life in it, including us and the people we care about.

Planting trees and restoring forest functions, preventing the use of excess energy, helping the stability of the human population, conserving animals in nature are various activities that we must do to save the earth as our home. Of course we can't do all these things alone, we need synergistic support from all the inhabitants of the earth to be able to work together to create a better world. A better world can also be realized by condemning all forms of human rights violations. War, violence and racism must be abolished so that the world can become a comfortable place for all human beings who live in it.

Keeping clean is one way for us as humans to be able to appreciate the earth where we live. As inhabitants of this planet, we should have an awareness of the cleanliness and orderliness of our homes. If the world is full of garbage then the feasibility of the earth as a planet where humans live will get worse.

In the era of the development of industry 4.0 as it is today, there are many revolutions. Almost all fields have rapid development and progress. Almost all businesses such as technology, textiles, retail, and various creative economies are developing because they are supported by improving conditions and economic stability of the community.

But of course the development of the industry is also in line with the waste generated. Every large-scale company to home industry certainly produces waste or residual waste from production. Of course, if not managed properly, this waste and garbage can pollute the earth, especially large-scale factories or companies which of course can produce several tons of waste in one day. If not handled, this will certainly be a new problem in the world that can threaten the health of our earth.

So why should we manage waste? If the waste is not managed and only dumped directly into the environment, it will reduce the quality of cleanliness and beauty, reduce comfort, become a medium for disease transmission (flies, mice, mosquitoes, cockroaches), reduce environmental quality, the impact of pollutants such as industrial waste, mining, paint, sewage motor vehicle gas, used batteries, cans.

Then how to manage waste properly. Waste management can be done by reducing and handling it. Waste reduction is carried out through the 3R (Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle) principles, namely waste reduction, waste reuse, and waste recycling.

Limitation of waste generation/reduction of waste. For example, by bringing your own shopping bag from home, serving food and drink with plates and glasses.Recycling of waste, for example plastic waste is recycled into handicraft productsReuse of waste is utilizing waste that can still be reused, for example, used paint cans are used as plant pots.

Especially for industrial waste that contains several hazardous chemicals, it will certainly become a special threat if it is not managed properly and correctly. Especially for people who live around industrial areas. There are many ways that can be done to destroy industrial waste, so that the waste does not have a negative impact on the environment, but can have a positive impact, namely being a useful source of alternative raw materials and alternative fuels (Alternative Fuel & Materials - AFR). for the community if managed properly and effectively.

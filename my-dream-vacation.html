7. essay about my christmas vacation


               My Christmas Vacation

Christmas is the most awaited season of almost all people in the world especially the children. We expect receiving and giving gifts during this time. Christmas vacation also means time out from classes and temporarily goodbye to early waking up in the morning.

I feel very satisfied and happy about my Christmas vacation. We had family reunion and I was able to see my cousins from far and near again. We ate delicious food and we played games. In Christmas eve, Mother and Ate cooked food for media noche. There were ham, spaghetti, chicken salad, chicken joy and ice cream on our table. I think all my favorites were served during that night. Mother and father also gave us gifts and we opened the gifts together after eating. Ate, kuya and I also gave our gifts to Mama and Papa. We also offered them songs and dance for entertainment. In some other days, we went to different beaches everyday and felt the fresh air while enjoying the scenery. We visited grandma and grandpa in the province and we had our new year there. I could say that we’ve done and enjoyed many activities during the Christmas vacation.  

Starting today,I will save money in my piggy bank so I can buy more gifts when Christmas will come again this year. Mama said, it is a season for giving though everyday is also okay. If I have money in December, I can also give something to my cousins if we meet in the reunion again.

The second paragraph tells about what happened or the narrative of your chosen topic. The last paragraph is composed of the wrap up/conclusion about your topic or your future plans regarding what you have written. My ideal vacation Hello, everyone. I'd like to tell you about my ideal vacation. To make my vacation perfect, certain important things need to be taken into consideration. These things include who I spend my vacation with, where I want to go, what I want to do, and the sort of weather the place has. It can be an ideal vacation when all these things are met. First of all, I like to spend my vacation with people I love and I feel comfortable with. They could be my family, friends or even my future boy friend. Secondly, I love traveling to other countries. I love visiting new places because I can experience different cultures and meet different people who I wouldn't normally get to meet. When I go abroad, I really get the feeling that I'm living in a big world and that all my worries are so small and pointless. It would be great to go to a country I have never been to before. I've heard from many people who've been to Europe that Barcelona was the best place they'd ever visited. Barcelona is a city that has beautiful beaches and is known for having sunny and clear weather, so if I had a chance of going to any country I wanted to, I'd choose Barcelona as my ideal vacation location. On my vacation, I'd like to relax on a beautiful blue beach. I want to sunbathe and get a tan, too. Most people think of this sort image in their heads when they come up with their idea of an ideal vacation, and I agree with them. I know it's because the media influences people's ideas. But, I've always wanted to experience a vacation like that at least once in my life.

20. write a 365 words essay about "my dream vacation", tysm​


Answer:

Everyone has a dream vacation destination that they've always wanted to visit. For me, that place is Japan. Japan has always been a fascinating country to me with its unique blend of traditional culture and modern technology. My dream vacation would be to spend a couple of weeks exploring Japan's cities and countryside, immersing myself in their rich culture, sampling their delicious cuisine, and experiencing everything that Japan has to offer.

Firstly, I would love to visit Tokyo, Japan's bustling capital city. Tokyo is known for its vibrant energy, futuristic architecture, and bustling streets filled with neon lights and skyscrapers. I would visit famous landmarks like the Tokyo Tower and the Imperial Palace, and explore the bustling streets of Shinjuku and Shibuya, two of Tokyo's most vibrant districts.

Next, I would travel to Kyoto, Japan's ancient capital city, known for its traditional temples, gardens, and geisha districts. I would love to stroll through the famous bamboo groves of Arashiyama and visit iconic landmarks like the Kiyomizu-dera temple and the Fushimi Inari Shrine.

Of course, no trip to Japan would be complete without sampling its delicious cuisine. I would love to try traditional Japanese dishes like sushi, ramen, and tempura, as well as explore the unique street food scene in Tokyo and other cities. I would also love to participate in a traditional tea ceremony and learn more about Japan's tea culture.

In conclusion, my dream vacation to Japan would be a perfect blend of traditional and modern culture, food, and natural beauty. It would be an incredible opportunity to learn more about this fascinating country, meet new people, and create unforgettable memories. Although it may be a while before I can make this dream a reality, I know that someday I will travel to Japan and experience all the wonders that it has to offer.


21. my summer vacations with signal words​


Answer:

Oh man, let me tell you about my summer vacation. It was wild, guys. I mean, I knew it was going to be a good time, but I had no idea it was going to be this good. I'm still blown away by everything I saw and did.

So, we started out on this beautiful island in the Caribbean. It was like something straight out of a movie, you know? White sandy beaches, crystal-clear water, sunshine - the whole shebang. I spent my days lounging in the sun, taking dips in the ocean, and chowing down on some seriously amazing food. And get this - we even went snorkeling and saw all sorts of cool stuff like bright fish and coral formations. I was like a kid in a candy store.

Next, we hit up this crazy city in Europe. It was non-stop action, I'm telling you. We visited all the big tourist spots like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre. And then we went to a classical music concert - can you believe it? I mean, who goes to a classical music concert on vacation? But it was actually really cool. And shopping? Oh my gosh, I had a blast.

And finally, we ended up at this serene mountain resort. It was the perfect way to wrap up the trip, you know? I got to go on some seriously gorgeous hikes, soak in hot springs, and just relax. It was a chance to reflect on everything we'd seen and done over the past few weeks, and it was really special.

In conclusion, my summer vacation was an absolute blast. I saw so many incredible things, met some cool people, and had the time of my life. I feel so grateful to have had the opportunity to escape my regular life and experience all these different environments and cultures. And let me tell you, I'll never forget it.

The signal words included in this essay are: "Oh man," "wild," "good time," "seriously," "get this," "non-stop action," "cool," "blast," "gorgeous," "special," "absolute blast," "incredible," "cool people," "time of my life," "grateful," "escape," and "never forget."

13. Ethical behavior under political system​


Answers:

Honesty, fairness, transparency, accountability, and respect for human rights.

Explaination:

Ethical behavior in a political system refers to the moral principles and standards of conduct expected of individuals and institutions within a society's political framework. This can include honesty, fairness, transparency, accountability, and respect for human rights. Ethical behavior helps to maintain the integrity and legitimacy of the political system and protects the rights of citizens.

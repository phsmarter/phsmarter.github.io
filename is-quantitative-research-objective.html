5. Explain the line, “Quantitative research is objective rather subjective”.​


Quantitative research is a research approach that is objective, includes the collection and analysis of quantitative data and uses statistical testing methods. Research is a scientific activity that aims to gain knowledge about a problem.

EXPLANATION:

The objective approached or often called quantitative approach. This approach is often said to be a systematic scientific approach to the parts and phenomena and their relationships based on the view that objects, behaviors and events exist in a world that can be observed by the five senses (sight, hearing, touch, taste, etc.) , and odorants), can be measured and predicted.

The objective approach views that truth can be found, if one can get rid of human intervention when conducting research, in other words taking a distance from the object under study, because this approach is more systematic, controlled, empirical about the assumed relationship between natural phenomena.

The objective approach also tends to regard the humans they observe as passive and their changes are caused by social forces outside of themselves. This approach also argues, to a certain degree human behavior can be predicted, although the predictions are not as precise as the predictions of the behavior of nature. In other words, the laws that apply to human behavior are possible (probabilistic). For example, if students study more diligently, they will (perhaps) get better grades. So if it is understood that this objective approach considers human behavior can be divided into independent parts, each of which works systematically.

In research, the objective or quantitative approach aims to develop and use mathematical models, theories and/or hypotheses related to natural phenomena. In objective or quantitative research, that is, research that measures both the influence and the relationship between variables. The influence of X on Y, the relationship of X with Y and so on.

Research Example: "The effect of watching soap operas on TV "A" on the level of intelligence of elementary school students in the city of Manila"

6. wht type of compound word is bittersweet​


Answer:

Copulative compounds

In these compounds there is no semantic head, the relation between the constituent is a relation of coordination. For example is the word bittersweet.

Explanation:

Is Bittersweet a compound word?

OED defines bittersweet as "sweet with an admixture or aftertaste of bitterness. fig. agreeable or pleasant with an alloy of pain or unpleasantness." As you can see from this definition, the parts, bitter and sweet, are on different hierarchical levels; thus, "bittersweet" is not a co-compound.Mar 21, 2012

27. AMADO V. HERNANDEZ said that a poem is a scent, bittersweet memories, and a murmur of flowing water, the pen is powerful and according to him, even a king can be bent by the pen. Why do you think he made that statement?​


Answer:

"A poem is a scent, bittersweet memories, and a murmur of flowing water, the pen is powerful and according to him, even a king can be bent by the pen."

I think that in this statement, Amado V. Hernandez wanted to show us the power of literature. He described poetry as a beautiful but also possibly sad thing. In the first part of the quote, he is showing us how good poetry can be when it comes to bringing out our emotions. It is a good way to use our imagination to experience something by reading about it.

There is more to literature than just beauty, however. It is can also be used to make political and societal changes. A king is the most powerful person in a monarchy. Most of the time, people will bow to him. However, Hernandez wanted to illustrate his point by reversing the situation.

Of course, we should not take his words literally. No pen in the world will ever make a king bow. However, writing can be used as a way to change the way that we think. People might realize that the king is taking advantage of them by reading the words of a writer. Or perhaps the king himself will realize that he does not deserve to be in power simply because he inherited the throne. ​In the end, the point is that literature is beautiful and powerful and should not be taken for granted.

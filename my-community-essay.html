I Understand and Appreciate the Culture of My Community

As humans who live in various communities, we must respect each other's cultural diversity. There are many things or ways to appreciate our own culture and the culture of others. Some attitudes to mutual respect for other people's cultures are:

Watching performances or performances of other regional culturesLearn about other regional cultures.Avoid regionalismDo not interfere with other cultures or demean them.

Don't keep our cultural wealth just for ourselves. One way is to teach our culture to others, both at home and at school. The more people who teach or tell, the more people who know about the culture of their own region or other regions. The more cultural knowledge we have, the greater our sense of respect for other people's cultures.

Apart from not being influenced by foreign cultures, we should still sort out foreign cultures to study. Do not just choose and eliminate your own culture. We can study foreign cultures but must be careful. Taking the positive side that can develop us, without losing our own cultural identity.

To preserve our own culture, we should not be influenced by the culture of other countries. In the current era of globalization, foreign cultures very easy to enter the midst of society. With many foreign cultures entering, we should make our own culture our identity. It is an opportunity to introduce our community culture. Although foreign culture is considered more modern and more slang, our culture is no less good to be introduced.

In addition to introducing our own culture at home, we should also introduce our culture abroad. Especially with the increasingly sophisticated social media technology. By utilizing social media, we can post photos and videos of the local arts and culture of our community. Posting this, indirectly introduces our culture abroad. This is because those who use social media or the internet are not limited to just us, but everyone in the world.

16. essay about "my role in the community"​


        Every individual has a community, both in their immediate surroundings and in the larger external community. Being in the community and being a member will certainly make us have responsibility and empathy to create a better atmosphere for our community. The participation of every member of the community is important and very much needed to maintain a healthy and comfortable climate in a community. I am talking about my role in the community, namely within the environment where I live. The people around my house, including myself, are a community where each individual has a responsibility for our community. Because many immigrants migrated to the city and settled around my house, our society is very diverse in terms of religion, race, and different languages. Our responsibilities include obeying the rules and regulations that have been mutually agreed upon and being a good member of the community; keeping the environment clean; participating in activities that aim to revive the community; mutual respect and appreciation between community members; preserving and reforesting the environment; doing mutual cooperation in activities that involve all community members. All of these steps are aimed at creating a good community that is able to grow and bring benefits because we can carry out our functions as humans in a sphere called community.

Let us briefly discuss the meaning of community and its benefits.

What is a community?

A community is a group of organisms (people, and so on) that live and interact with each other in a certain area. A community can be defined as a community group or an association.

Benefits to the community:

-Information facility

A community can serve as a source of information for its members because information can circulate swiftly there.

-Build connections

Humans are social creatures that require other people, so the presence of a large community will undoubtedly make this a way to forge lasting relationships.

-Support each other.

Due to shared hobbies or interests, community members can support one another, which benefits others outside the group. Of course, doing this has a lot of advantages, especially when trying to empower the community.

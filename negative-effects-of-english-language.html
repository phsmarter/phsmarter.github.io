1. What is the Negative effect of adopting English language in Philippines Culture?​


Answer:

They keep dropping English into their sentences, but that’s not “forgetting” anything: it’s a well-understood linguistic phenomenon known as “code-switching”, and indicates a fundamental fluency in both languages. That’s not “disappointing”; it’s encouraging!

14. B. Rene can type a research paper in 8 hours. Mario helped Rene in typing, and together theyfinished in 6 hours. How long will it take Mario to finish the job alone?A. 20 hoursB. 22 hoursC. 24 hoursD. 26 hours​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in solving word problems about linear equation and fractions.

Given

Rene can type a research paper in 8 hours.

Rene and Mario together they finished in 6 hours.

Problem

How long will it take Mario to finish the job alone?

Solution

Use the formula 1/x + 1/y = 1/t in solving work word problems, where x is the time consume by the first person and y is for the second person.

x = 8 hours

y = ?

t = 6 hours

1/8 + 1/y = 1/6

Solve for y

1/8 + 1/y = 1/6

LCD of 8, y and 6 is 24y

1/8 + 1/y = 1/6 → 3y + 24 = 4y

3y + 24 = 4y

4y - 3y = 24

y = 24 hours

Answer24 hours

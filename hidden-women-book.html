22. about Hidden Figures Formalism Book Review Marxism Historical Moralism Feminism Reader Response, pahelp po kaylngan na po ngayun â€‹


Explanation:

"Hidden Figures" is a book that tells the story of three African-American women who worked at NASA during the space race of the 1960s. The book has been analyzed through several different critical lenses, including Formalism, Marxism, Historical Moralism, Feminism, and Reader Response.

Formalism is a literary theory that focuses on the formal elements of a work of literature, such as its structure, language, and style. When analyzing "Hidden Figures" through a formalist lens, one might focus on the book's use of language and imagery to convey the experiences of the three women. Formalists might also analyze the book's structure and how the different narratives of the women are intertwined.

Marxism is a critical theory that focuses on social and economic inequality. When analyzing "Hidden Figures" through a Marxist lens, one might focus on the women's experiences as African-Americans and women in a predominantly white and male-dominated workplace. Marxism might also analyze how the women's struggles relate to broader societal issues.

Historical Moralism is a critical theory that focuses on the historical context of a work of literature. When analyzing "Hidden Figures" through a historical moralist lens, one might focus on the social and political context of the space race and how it impacted the experiences of the three women. Historical moralism might also analyze how the book's message of perseverance and overcoming obstacles relates to broader historical narratives.

Feminism is a critical theory that focuses on gender inequality. When analyzing "Hidden Figures" through a feminist lens, one might focus on the women's experiences as women in a male-dominated workplace. Feminism might also analyze how the book's message of empowerment and breaking down gender barriers relates to broader feminist narratives.

Reader Response is a critical theory that focuses on the reader's subjective response to a work of literature. When analyzing "Hidden Figures" through a reader response lens, one might focus on how the reader's personal experiences and beliefs impact their understanding of the book. Reader response might also analyze how the book resonates with different readers and how their responses shape the meaning of the book.

Overall, "Hidden Figures" can be analyzed through several different critical lenses, each providing a unique perspective and interpretation of the book's meaning and significance.

2. compare fatty acid synthesis with fatty acid degration​


Answer:

(1) Fatty acid synthesis takes place in the cytosol whereas the degradation takes place primarily in the mitochondrial matrix. (2) All the intermediates in fatty acid synthesis are linked to the-SH group of acyl carrier protein (ACP) but in the degradative pathway the intermediates are linked to -SH group of coenzyme-A.

8. 1. Define the following a. protein synthesis b. nucleic acid c. nucleotides d. DNA, RNA​


A. Protein synthesis is the process of creating proteins from amino acids. This process occurs in the cells of living organisms and is essential for cell growth and reproduction.

B. Nucleic acids are molecules composed of nucleotides that serve as the primary information-carrying molecules in cells and viruses.

C. Nucleotides are the building blocks of nucleic acids and consist of three parts - a sugar, a phosphate group, and one of four types of nitrogenous bases - adenine, guanine, cytosine, and thymine.

D. DNA (deoxyribonucleic acid) and RNA (ribonucleic acid) are the two types of nucleic acids found in cells. DNA contains the genetic information of organisms and is used to make RNA, which carries out the instructions encoded in the DNA.

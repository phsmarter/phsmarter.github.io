18. You are asked to develop a loyalty program for a hotel or restaurant. Go on the internet and find out what information you can find out about loyalty programs, including existing hotel or restaurant loyalty programs. Write up a summary of your findings.​


Answer:

Loyalty programs are a great way to reward customers for their repeat business and engage them in a deeper relationship with a brand. These programs can be a powerful tool for customer retention and long-term customer loyalty.

Hotel and restaurant loyalty programs typically offer their members exclusive rewards, discounts, and special offers. This could include free nights, free meals, VIP upgrades, free upgrades, free drinks, free gifts, or discounts on services. Some loyalty programs also offer exclusive deals or discounts on products or services from partner companies.

In addition to the rewards, loyalty programs also offer members access to exclusive events or experiences. These can be anything from special culinary experiences, to wine tastings, to VIP access to events.

In the era of digital technology, loyalty programs are often tied to online accounts. Customers can access their accounts online and view their rewards, check their points balance, and redeem their rewards. This makes it easy for customers to keep track of their rewards and take advantage of them.

Overall, loyalty programs are a great way to reward customers for their loyalty, and to build a strong and lasting relationship with them. With the right loyalty program, businesses can drive customer engagement, loyalty, and retention.

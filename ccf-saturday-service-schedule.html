6. janice is a bridesmaid for her cousins wedding in New Delhi the wedding is scheduled at 6:00 on the Saturday afternoon and is scheduled to take 6 hours if the time in Manila is 2.5 hours ahead of new delhi and assuming at will take her an extra hour to get ready, will janice be ready for the wedding on time​


To determine whether Janice will be ready for the wedding on time, we need to convert the time of the wedding in New Delhi to the time in Manila.

The wedding in New Delhi is scheduled for 6:00 on Saturday afternoon. Since Manila is 2.5 hours ahead of New Delhi, the wedding in Manila would be at:

6:00 PM New Delhi time + 2.5 hours = 8:30 PM Manila time

So the wedding in Manila is at 8:30 PM on Saturday evening. If the wedding is scheduled to take 6 hours, it would end at 2:30 AM on Sunday morning.

Assuming it will take Janice an extra hour to get ready, she would need to start getting ready at 1:30 AM on Sunday morning to be ready for the wedding at 8:30 PM Manila time on Saturday evening.

Therefore, Janice would not be able to make it to the wedding on time as it would not be possible for her to start getting ready at 1:30 AM on Sunday morning and be ready for the wedding at 8:30 PM Manila time on Saturday evening.

29. janice is a bridesmaid for her cousin's wedding in new delhi the wedding is scheduled at 6:00 p.m. on a saturday jenny's flight leaves naia at 1:00 p.m. saturday afternoon and is scheduled to take 6 hours if the time in manila is 2.5 hours and of new delhi the assuming it will take her an extra are to get to the ceremony location from the airport and another are to get ready wilderness be ready for the wedding on time?​


Answer:

She will not arrive for the wedding on time since she will will arrive at 6:30 p.m. (New Delhi time) which is 30 minutes after the scheduled wedding.

Step-by-step explanation:

Start with the initial time which is 1:00 p.m. Then count the number of hours it will take to reach the airport (New Delhi) from Naia (Manila)(6 hours) which is 7:00 p.m.

                (6 hours)

1:00 p.m. ---------------------> 7:00 p.m.

It will take her an extra hour to get to the ceremony location from the airport. From, we now have to count an hour from the time of arrival which will give us 8:00 p.m.

                  (1 hour)

7:00 p.m. ---------------------> 8:00 p.m.

It will take her another hour to get ready for the ceremony location. From, we now have to count an hour from the time of arrival to the wedding location which will give us 9:00 p.m.

                  (1 hour)

8:00 p.m. ---------------------> 9:00 p.m.

Since there is a 2.5 hr-time difference between Manila and New Delhi, the final step is to just take away the time difference from 9:00 p.m.

9:00 p.m. - 2 hours and 30 minutes = 6:30 p.m.

30. What does the acronym CCF stand for? (Math)​


Answer:

The most common units are centum cubic feet (CCF) and the gallon. A CCF also called an HCF (hundred cubic feet), represents one hundred cubic feet of water. The first "C" comes from the Roman word for hundred, "centum.” This is the most common unit used by both water and natural gas utilities.

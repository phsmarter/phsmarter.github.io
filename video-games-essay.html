1. The symptoms brought by video games essay​


Answer:

Video games are a popular form of entertainment enjoyed by millions of people around the world. With the rise of technology, video games have become more advanced and immersive, allowing players to experience virtual worlds and engage with others online. While video games can be a fun and engaging pastime, they can also have negative effects on our health if we are not careful. In this essay, we will explore the symptoms brought by video games and how they can impact our well-being.

One of the most common symptoms associated with video games is eye strain. This occurs when you stare at a screen for an extended period of time, causing your eyes to become tired and strained. Symptoms of eye strain can include dry eyes, headaches, and blurred vision. This is especially prevalent among gamers who spend hours playing games without taking breaks or resting their eyes. To prevent eye strain, it is recommended to take breaks every 20 minutes to look away from the screen and focus on something in the distance.

Another symptom brought by video games is poor posture. Gamers often sit in front of their screens for extended periods of time, leading to bad posture and potential back pain. This can be exacerbated by the use of chairs that do not provide adequate support, or by sitting in a cramped or uncomfortable position. It is important to take regular breaks and stretch to avoid these issues, and to invest in a comfortable chair that promotes good posture.

In addition to physical symptoms, video games can also have negative effects on our mental health. One symptom that is commonly associated with gaming is addiction. When playing games, it is easy to become immersed in the virtual world and lose track of time. This can lead to neglecting other responsibilities, such as school, work, or personal relationships. Addiction to video games can also cause mood swings, anxiety, and depression.

Another symptom brought by video games is aggression. Some games are designed to be violent and aggressive, and players can become desensitized to this behavior over time. This can lead to an increase in aggressive behavior, both online and offline. While not all gamers will experience this symptom, it is important to recognize the potential for violent games to impact our behavior.

Finally, video games can also have an impact on our sleep patterns. Many gamers will stay up late into the night playing games, leading to a lack of sleep and disrupted sleep patterns. This can cause fatigue, decreased productivity, and a range of physical and mental health issues. To avoid these symptoms, it is important to establish a healthy sleep routine and limit gaming to a reasonable amount of time each day.

In conclusion, video games can bring a range of symptoms that can impact our physical and mental well-being. These symptoms can include eye strain, poor posture, addiction, aggression, and disrupted sleep patterns. While not all gamers will experience these symptoms, it is important to be aware of the potential risks associated with gaming and take steps to protect our health. This can include taking regular breaks, investing in comfortable seating, setting limits on gaming time, and seeking professional help if necessary. By taking care of our bodies and minds, we can enjoy the benefits of gaming while minimizing the risks.

Video games can cause negative psychological effect such as self-control, anxiety, depression, aggressiveness and confusion. These effects could endanger the future of adolescence as they’re still part of the growth and development stage. The addictiveness of video games could further increase these negative benefits. Playing video games supports teenagers to do crimes as they are rewarded for doing so. For example, in Grand Theft Auto or GTA, the player is awarded for doing missions, missions which instructs the player to steal, to drive recklessly, to fight, to shoot and to kill. After a mission is accomplished, they would get rewards such as points, or more health, or a more violent option such as a weapon like a gun or a tank.

If you see a violent video game player assault another person, it is difficult to know the direct cause of the assault. Was it playing violent video games for hours on end, or was it something else? The psychological process by which playing violent video games produces this result is not as intuitive to most people as are biological processes. People are probably more accepting of the idea that smoking causes lung cancer, for example, because it is much easier to grasp the idea that smoke going into the lungs, damages cells, and starts tumour growth. According to Brad bushman, a communications and psychology professor at Ohio State University, the link between video games and the aggressive behaviour is clear: “Over 130 studies have been conducted on over 130,000 participants around the world.” Bushman said that these studies proves that violent video games can lead to an increase in aggressive thoughts, angry feelings, physiological arousal, including increased heart rate.

CAUSE

The causes of video game addiction are complex and can vary greatly, but the effects have the potential to be severe. Playing video games can and should be a fun activity for all to enjoy. Answer:Playing video games has both pros and cons associated with it. On the one hand, video games can have negative effects on physical and mental health, including depression, social anxiety, lack of motivation, poor emotional regulation, poor sleep hygiene, muscle loss, fat gain, dehydration, and exhaustion [1]. On the other hand, playing video games can also have several positive effects. For instance, video games can help children learn math, spelling, resource management, and problem-solving skills [2]. However, it is important to note that excessive gaming can lead to obesity, "Nintendo thumb" (a type of repetitive strain injury), and cyber addiction [3]. Therefore, it is important to maintain a balance between playing video games and engaging in other activities to reap the benefits while minimizing the negative effects.

30. evidences and opinions of why video games shouldn't be banned (in a form of an essay)​


in Essay

There are several reasons why video games should not be banned.

First and foremost, video games are a form of expression and a legitimate art form. Just like movies, books, and music, video games can be used to tell stories, convey emotions, and explore various themes. Banning video games would be a violation of freedom of expression and would deprive people of the opportunity to experience and create a wide range of artistic works.

Secondly, video games have been shown to have numerous benefits for both children and adults. For children, video games can improve problem-solving skills, spatial awareness, and hand-eye coordination. They can also help children develop social skills by allowing them to interact with other players online. For adults, video games have been shown to improve cognitive function and reduce stress.

Thirdly, blaming video games for societal issues is often a simplistic and misguided approach. Many factors contribute to problems such as violence, and it is unfair to single out video games as the sole cause. In fact, numerous studies have found that there is no significant link between video game violence and real-world aggression.

In conclusion, video games should not be banned as they are a form of expression, have numerous benefits, and are not a significant cause of societal issues. Instead of banning video games, we should focus on finding ways to use them responsibly and address any negative effects they may have on a case-by-case basis.

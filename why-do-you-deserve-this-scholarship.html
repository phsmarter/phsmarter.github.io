2. essay about scholarship,why do you deserve to be a scholar?​


Essay about scholarship

You can answer why do you deserve to be a scholar by example below:
I deserve this scholarship not because I deserve anything, but because I may provide a great return on your investment. I work hard, am motivated by myself, have achievable goals, and want to contribute to the advancement of society as a whole. Fundamentally, it is about others rather than myself. What can I do to make the world a better place? How can I improve things for others in my own way? Your scholarship will assist me in achieving my goals. My dream is to grow up to be a valuable person who makes a significant contribution to society. Through this scholarship, I will be able to continue my studies and expand my attitude for the improvement of the society.

I have so far gained knowledge about community involvement through volunteer work. My desire to make a greater social contribution in the future with my expertise is stimulated by these activities.

Here are some guidelines for preparing an essay on why you should award you a scholarship:

-Describe how the scholarship funds would help you achieve your long-term objectives.

-Concentrate on the scholarship's purpose. Consider the purpose of the scholarship and its funding source when you write your essay. By demonstrating your prior accomplishment, you might inspire hope for your future success.

-Use a thesis statement in the same way that you would in any other essay. All throughout the essay, you should make references to your argument and weave it into your summary.

-Focus on how you overcame challenges in your life when sharing them. Show your ability to solve problems

-Avoid making generalizations; statements like "I deserve this because I work hard" are insufficient. What distinguishes you as unique, noteworthy, and special?

-Support your argument with a personal success from your life and upbringing.

Communicative Styles

- The habitual mode of interaction of an individual, variously typified (usually in the context of communication skills training and often in relation to personality types). For example: aggressive, passive-aggressive, passive, or assertive.

Communication variation

- Communication Variations. Communication difference/dialect is a variation of a symbol system used by a group of individuals that reflects and is determined by shared regional, social, or cultural/ethnic factors.

Language register

- In sociolinguistics, a register is a variety of language used for a particular purpose or in a particular communicative situation.

10. Ano Ang life metaphor


Ang mga metapora ay mga pigura ng pananalita na nagsasaad na ang isang bagay ay talagang isa pang bagay. Ang mga ito ay isang paraan ng paglikha ng paghahambing na bagama't hindi literal na totoo, ay nagbibigay ng matalinghagang kahulugan. Ang mga metapora para sa buhay ay isang paraan ng paghahambing ng buhay sa iba pang mga bagay sa paraang maaaring makatulong sa iyong pag-isipan ang iyong buhay at mga problema sa ibang paraan.

11. How is the sea used as a metaphor in life


Answer:

Metaphor: The ocean lifted its arm to touch the shore.

Explanation:

A wave from the ocean rose up before it crashed down on the shore, and looked like an arm.

As waves crash in some places, the tide ebbs softly and gently in others. It seems the ocean is the perfect metaphor for life. Life has its crashing waves, that we have to learn to ride. Life also has tranquil walks in the soft sand, left behind by the ebbing tides.

19. ang hangin ay dumadampi sa aking pisngi simile metaphor hyperbole personification metonymy synecdoche​


Answer:

metaphor

Explanation:

Metaphors

A metaphor is one of the important figures of speech in the English language. A metaphor is used for comparing two things indirectly. Unlike a simile, the words 'like' and 'as' are not used, in fact no direct words are used for comparison. One might then wonder how to recognize a metaphor. Usually, the comparison is made between an inanimate and animate object through which we can understand that the inanimate object is compared to an animate object.

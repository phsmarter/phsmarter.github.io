Answer:

What data analysis is used for qualitative research?

-Content analysis: This is one of the most common methods to analyze qualitative data. It is used to analyze documented information in the form of texts, media, or even physical items. When to use this method depends on the research questions. Content analysis is usually used to analyze responses from interviewees.

What is the purpose of qualitative data analysis?

-Qualitative data analysis aims to make sense of the abundant, varied, mostly nonnumeric forms of information that accrue during an investigation

18. Title thesis and justification example​


Answer:

A potential thesis statement and justification for a paper could be:

Thesis: The use of solitary confinement in prisons has a detrimental effect on the mental health of inmates and should be banned.

Justification: Solitary confinement, also known as isolation, is a form of punishment used in prisons that involves isolating inmates in a small cell for extended periods of time. Studies have shown that this type of confinement can have severe psychological effects on prisoners, including depression, anxiety, and even psychosis. It can also lead to self-harm and suicide. Therefore, the use of solitary confinement in prisons should be banned to protect the mental health of inmates.

This thesis is supported by research on the psychological effects of solitary confinement and aligns with the principle of protecting the well-being of prisoners.

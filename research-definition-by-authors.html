The Meaning Of Research

Research is a study or observation that aims to find answers to a problem through a scientific approach and gathering facts.

Research purposes

Research conducted has a general purpose to resolve or solve the problem being studied. But not just one, there are several goals to be achieved in research, namely:

Get new information

New information is obtained by researchers while collecting data or facts to conclude something in their research. This new information can also be in the form of theories that emerge as research conclusions that have not previously been concluded or obtained by other researchers.

Develop and explain

Aims to explain and develop the theories found based on the appropriate theory or literature so that the research conducted can be developed.

Predict and conclude

Aims to predict something and conclude it to be the result of research that must be relevant to the analysis.

implement

Aims to be implemented in a community or community environment that experiences a problem raised in the research conducted.

2. The population of a town is 10000. Out of these, 5400 persons read newspaper A 4700 persons read newspaper B. If 1500 persons read both newspapers,then how many do not read either newspaper A or B?


Answer:

No. of people read newspaper A is n(A) = 5400

No. of people read newspaper B is n(B) = 4700

No.of people read both A and B is n(A ∩ B) = 1500

No. of people read either of news paper A or B is n(A U B) =?

From

n(A U B) = n(A) + n(B) - n(A ∩ B)

n(A U B) = 5400 + 4700 - 1500

n(A U B) = 10,100 - 1500 = 8600

Therefore no. of people neither reading newspaper A & B = 10,000 - n(A U B)

= 10,000 - 8600

= 1400

Answer:

1500

Step-by-step explanation:

5400 + 4700 = 10100

10100 - 1500 = 8600

10000 - 8600 = 1400

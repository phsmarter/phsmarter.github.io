3. What are the 5 important things you had in mind in considering these three topics?

-Relevance: Is this a topic that is currently highly relevant and will be of interest to the wider academic community?

-Accessibility: Do I have access to the relevant data and materials to conduct a robust research project on this topic?

-Appeal: Does the topic have enough of an appeal to capture the attention of my target audience?

-Originality: Is the topic novel and has it not already been fully explored?

-Credibility: Do I have the credibility and qualifications to pursue this topic and be taken seriously by my peers?

1. Who is Donato di Niccolo di Betto Bardi?


Donato di Niccolo di Betto Bardi is an  Italian sculptor during the Renaissance. He lived in Florence. He  worked in stone, bronze, wood, clay, and wax with several assistants. His work was inspired by ancient sculptures.

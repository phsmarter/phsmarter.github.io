10. please help me write a letter for my teacher for teacher's day​


This is a letter for you, my Teacher.

I have no words to say other than sincere prayers for your health and safety, my Teacher.

Through this simple letter, I would like to express my apologies for any unfavorable attitudes, actions, and behavior. I realize that while being taught and educated by the teacher, I often argue with words, make noise in class, even intentionally not doing my assignments.

My dear teacher, for me these things are very trivial, but from these trivial things I can learn about the importance of disciplined behavior, responsibility, and carrying out the mandate.

My beloved teacher. There is no word as beautiful as a prayer for you, because you are an unsung hero. Truly a hero who taught me to read and write arithmetic, introduced me to the vastness of the world, also taught me about true sincerity.

O my beloved teacher, you have taught us by example, with behavior that is not just words and theory, and about the importance of patience and sincerity. Truly, you are an unsung hero that we cannot match you.

Dear my dear teacher, as your student who sometimes skips class and is reluctant to do homework, I apologize profusely for your careless actions, for your laziness, and yelling at you who are willing to work tirelessly to teach me. Truly, you are a candle that is willing to burn yourself in order to illuminate us students.

To my beloved teacher, this letter I convey. Welcome your happy day, National Teacher's Day with full of joy. May God reward you with endless rewards

6. Script for role play about Counseling po please yung may introduction na din po and conclusion ​


Answer:

Sure, here's a script for a counseling role play with an introduction and conclusion:

Introduction:

Counselor: Hi, welcome to our counseling session today. My name is Jane, and I'm here to listen and support you. How can I assist you today?

Client: Hi, thank you. I'm feeling really anxious and overwhelmed lately.

Counselor: Okay, let's talk about that. Can you tell me a little more about what's been causing these feelings of anxiety and overwhelm?

Client: Well, I've been having trouble sleeping, and I'm having a hard time concentrating on my work. I just feel like there's so much to do, and I can't keep up with everything.

Counselor: I see. It sounds like you're feeling a lot of pressure. Have you experienced these feelings before?

Client: Yeah, I have. But it's been worse lately, and I don't know how to cope.

Counselor: Okay, well, we can work on that together. Let's start by exploring some strategies to manage your anxiety and prioritize your tasks.

(Role play conversation continues, exploring coping strategies, addressing any underlying issues, etc.)

Conclusion:

Counselor: It's been great working with you today. Remember, you don't have to face these challenges alone. If you're feeling overwhelmed or anxious again, please don't hesitate to reach out and schedule another session.

Client: Thank you so much. I really appreciate your help and support.

Counselor: Of course. Remember, taking care of yourself is important. Don't forget to practice self-care and reach out for support when you need it. Take care, and I'll see you soon.

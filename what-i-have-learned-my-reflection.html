12. what i have learned my reflection ​


Answer:

Making a reflection paper on a topic will help both the student and the instructor. Reflection papers will help the instructor gauge the level of understanding of the student on a certain topic. While the student will be obliged to read and study the topic to be able to comply.

Explanation:

Requiring reflection papers is a tool of assessment used by teachers and instructors, the point is to include your thoughts and reactions to the reading or the topic at hand. Here are some tips for students who have no idea what a reflection paper should consist:

Introduction

Make a brief summary of the topic. This should give the reader a clear overview of the topic. Identify the setting, the problem, the issue at hand, and all relevant information

Body

Includes presentation of facts, the positive and negative sides of the topic, presentation of fallacies and claims and negating them with facts. The student’s opinion and his explanation and discussion for it is also included here. You may answer the following guide questions:

How did the article/ topic affect you? How does this article/ topic catch the reader’s attention? Has the topic changed your mind about something? If so, explain how. Has the topic left you with any questions?

Conclusion

This is the summary of what you have learned. This is where you will tie and stitch all your ideas together and restate your stand.

18. 2 movie that shows rational response and imotional response essay​


Answer:

"The Martian" is a film that follows an astronaut named Mark Watney, who becomes stranded on Mars after his crew believes him to be dead and evacuates the planet without him. In this film, Mark demonstrates rational thinking as he uses his knowledge and skills as a botanist and engineer to survive on the planet for years. He takes a scientific approach to his situation, rationing his food and water, growing crops, and improvising solutions to the challenges he faces. His calm and logical thinking enables him to stay alive and even attempt a daring escape plan. Mark's rational response to his situation is what allows him to survive and eventually be rescued.

On the other hand, "The Notebook" is a romantic drama that centers on the emotional response of its characters. The film follows the story of two lovers, Noah and Allie, who are separated by societal and family pressures. The film shows how emotions can drive individuals to make irrational decisions, as both Noah and Allie are willing to give up everything in order to be together. Allie is torn between her love for Noah and her obligation to her family, while Noah is willing to do whatever it takes to win her back. The film demonstrates how emotions can cloud judgment and lead to impulsive actions that have long-lasting consequences.

"The Martian" and "The Notebook" are two examples of films that demonstrate rational and emotional responses. While "The Martian" highlights the importance of rational thinking and problem-solving skills in challenging situations, "The Notebook" showcases the power of emotions and the impact they can have on one's decisions and actions. Both films serve as a reminder of the complexity of human responses, and how both rational and emotional thinking play a role in our daily lives.

21. What is Citizenship ( the right and responsibility ? essay​


Citizenship is a legal status defining the relationship between an individual and the state, defining both rights and duties each bears to the other. The notion of citizenship is in sharp contrast to the historically rival notion of being a subject. In countries ruled by a powerful monarch, subjects owed their sovereign unquestioning obedience and allegiance, and could even be said to be his property. Citizenship is entirely the province of the granting power which can impose whatever requirements it chooses. Broadly speaking there are two forms of citizenship: jus solis and jus sanguinis. In the first case anyone born on the territory of the granting power is considered a citizen. In the second case the child takes the citizenship of the parents regardless of birthplace. As usual its not really that simple and there are a bunch of caveats. For immigrants there is some form of naturalization process. Rules are varied and depend on the individuals status (e.g. refugee, landed immigrant, whatever). Normally this includes at least some means of support and a period of residence on the national territory.

The rights and responsibilities for each country are enshrined in their constitution. Those constitutions can result from a democratic process, revolution or imposition by a foreign power. The other answers outline what those rights and responsibilities are for the Philippines.

One of the driving forces of immigration and emigration throughout history has been individuals wanting to move themselves and their families to a jurisdiction whose constitution reflect their personal values.

Our first and most important responsibility is to acknowledge those same rights in our fellow citizens. Second, we must defend those rights in others even if we find the manner in which they are exercised uncomfortableable.

26. Essay “ Your responsibility as an individual "​


As an individual, it is our responsibility to take ownership of our actions and make a positive contribution to our society. We must strive to be the best versions of ourselves, and use our abilities and talents to help those around us. Each and every one of us has something special to offer, and it is our responsibility to use that to make the world a better place.

The most important responsibility we have as individuals is to be respectful to one another. Respect and kindness go hand in hand, and we must strive to treat everyone with dignity and respect, regardless of any differences we may have. We must also be mindful of our words and actions, as these have a powerful impact on those around us.

We must also take responsibility for our own actions and decisions. We are all responsible for the choices we make, and we must be prepared to accept the consequences of our actions. We must be honest and accountable for our decisions and strive to learn from our mistakes.

It is also our responsibility to stay informed and engaged in the world around us. We must take the time to educate ourselves on current events and important issues, and take part in conversations that can make a difference. We must also do our part to help those in need, whether it be through volunteering or donating to charity.

In addition, we must strive to be good role models for the younger generations. We must be positive influences on those around us, and show them what it means to be responsible and compassionate citizens. We must also encourage them to be active participants in their communities and to take part in activities that can benefit the greater good.

Finally, we must take responsibility for our own well-being. We must take care of ourselves, both physically and mentally, and ensure that we are getting the proper rest, nutrition, and exercise that we need. We must take the time to relax and enjoy life, and never forget to take care of our mental health.

Being a responsible individual is an important part of living a fulfilling life. We must all strive to be the best versions of ourselves, and take responsibility for our actions and the world around us. We must strive to be respectful and kind, stay informed and engaged, and take care of our own well-being. By doing all of these things, we can make a positive impact on our society and the world.

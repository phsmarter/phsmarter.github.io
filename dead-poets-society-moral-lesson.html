The problem of dead poet society, an issue being raised in the story: Keating way vs. Welton way is the conflict between two different teaching methods. The Welton Academy, where the story takes place, is a prestigious all-boys preparatory school that follows a traditional and conservative teaching style. Mr. Keating, a new English teacher at the school, encourages his students to think for themselves and to pursue their passions. This creates a conflict between the two teaching styles, which ultimately leads to the tragic events of the story.

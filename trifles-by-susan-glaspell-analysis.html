26. Susans father is 3years more than twice as old as susan. The product of their ages in years is 560. How old is susan and her father?


Susan's age: x

Father's age: 2x + 3

Product of their ages: 560

Equation:

(x) (2x + 3) = 560

2x² + 3x - 560 = 0

Factor the quadratic equation:

(2x + 35) (x - 16) = 0

2x + 35 = 0

x = -35/2

x - 16 = 0

x = 16

Substitute 16 to x:

Susan's age: x = 16 years oldFather's age: 2(16) + 3 = 35 years old

Check the product of their ages:

(16) (35) = 560

560 = 560  (True)

3. differentiate objective vs non- objective truth​


Answer:

An objective claim is a statement about a factual matter-one that can be proved true or false. For factual matters there exist widely recognized criteria and methods to determine whether a claim is true or false. A subjective claim, on the other hand, is not a factual matter; it is an expression of belief, opinion, or personal preference. A subjective claim cannot be proved right or wrong by any generally accepted criteria.

18. give some description that can best that can best address to the traffic problem.1. anthropology vs. political science2. economics vs. history3. psychology vs. sociology4. geography vs. demography​


Answer:

1. Anthropology focuses on the study of human cultures, while political science focuses on the study of governmental structures and policies.

2. Economics looks at how resources are distributed and allocated, while history examines past events and their effects on the present.

3. Psychology studies the way in which the mind works, while sociology looks at the behavior and interactions of individuals and groups.

4. Geography studies physical features of the Earth and the distribution of its population, while demography looks at the size and composition of populations and the factors that influence them.

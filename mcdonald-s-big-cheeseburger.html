2. In a fast food chain, four cheeseburgers and two large soft drinks cost ₱180. Two soft drinks cost ₱5 more than one cheeseburger. What is the cost of cheeseburger and soft drink?​


Answer:

Let x be the cost of one cheeseburger in pesos.

Then, 2x + 5 is the cost of one large soft drink in pesos.

From the given information, we can form two equations:

4x + 2(2x + 5) = 180 (the cost of 4 cheeseburgers and 2 large soft drinks is ₱180)

2x + 5 = cost of one large soft drink

Simplifying the first equation, we get:

8x + 10 = 180

8x = 170

x = 21.25

Substituting x = 21.25 into the second equation, we get:

2(21.25) + 5 = 47.5

Therefore, the cost of one cheeseburger is ₱21.25 and the cost of one large soft drink is ₱47.5.

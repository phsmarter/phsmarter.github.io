THE IMPACT OF ICT IN COMMUNICATION

ICT (Information and communication technologies) has primarily had a beneficial impact on how we communicate since it has made it easier and less expensive. Because there are so many various means to connect without meeting in person, many people prefer to speak over the internet or texting because it is now easier than planning a meeting. ICT has enabled a wide range of new communication options, allowing you to communicate with others almost instantly, for less money, and across longer distances than ever before. It allows users to instantly share expertise and assistance, as well as set up a low-cost online shop or website, substantially lowering the obstacles to beginning a business. As a result, it is a critical change enabler, and ICT maturity is intimately tied to economic growth. ICT has made significant contributions to the transformation of our daily lives, such as the conversion of letters to e-mail, market shopping to online shopping, classroom learning to e-learning, and so on. The effects of ICT on home and domestic activities, social networking, education, health, commerce, banking, and employment are discussed in this study.

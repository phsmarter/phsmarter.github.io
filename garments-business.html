1. What is the business venture of Carpentry and Garments?​


Answer:Carpentry and garments are two separate industries with distinct business ventures.

Carpentry involves the crafting and construction of wooden structures and products such as furniture, cabinetry, and buildings. A business venture in carpentry could involve offering custom woodworking services, creating and selling handmade furniture, or providing carpentry services for construction and renovation projects.

Garments, on the other hand, involve the manufacturing and sale of clothing and textile products. A business venture in garments could involve designing and producing a line of clothing, providing alterations and tailoring services, or selling wholesale clothing to retailers.

While there may be some overlap in terms of the materials and tools used (such as the use of wood for clothing racks or hangers), the two industries generally have distinct business ventures and target markets.

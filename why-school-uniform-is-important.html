Why do you need to wear a proper uniform to school?They encourage children to dress appropriately for school, and the school uniform will boost their confidence. Students will understand that they are all treated equally by the educational institution. Students who appropriately wear their school uniforms will quickly understand the value of treating others with respect.

All pupils are required to wear the same attire at school, which helps level the playing field. By doing this, all preconceived notions about what to dress are eliminated, resulting in increased group cohesiveness and commonality. Uniforms provide an unequivocal message of equality as a result.

School uniforms are advantageous for both off-site study and field excursions because of the increased safety they provide.

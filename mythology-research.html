Mythology (from the Greek mythos for story-of-the-people, and logos for word or speech, so the spoken story of a people) is the study and interpretation of often sacred tales or fables of a culture known as myths or the collection of such stories which deal with various aspects of the human condition: good and evil; the meaning of suffering; human origins; the origin of place-names, animals, cultural values, and traditions; the meaning of life and death; the afterlife; and the gods or a god. Myths express the beliefs and values about these subjects held by a certain culture.Myths tell the stories of ancestors and the origin of humans and the world, the gods, supernatural beings (satyrs, nymphs, mermaids) and heroes with super-human, usually god-given, powers (as in the case of Heracles or Perseus of the Greeks). Myths also describe origins or nuances of long-held customs or explain natural events such as the sunrise and sunset, the cycle of the moon and the seasons, or thunder and lightning storms.

1. RESEARCH TOPIC: The Socioeconomic Characteristics of Candonians in their Respective Baranggay

RESEARCH DESIGN: Descriptive Research

EXPLANATION: Descriptive research is used to describe the characteristics of a population, in this case the socioeconomic characteristics of Candonians in their respective baranggays. The research will gather data through surveys, interviews, and observation, to provide a detailed description of the population's characteristics.

2. RESEARCH TOPIC: The Effectiveness of Melatonin Supplements to Improve Sleep

RESEARCH DESIGN: Experimental Research

EXPLANATION: Experimental research is used to test the cause-and-effect relationship between two variables. In this case, the research aims to test the effectiveness of melatonin supplements in improving sleep. The research will involve a control group and an experimental group, where one group will take melatonin supplements while the other group will not. The researcher will then compare the sleep quality of both groups to determine the effectiveness of the supplements.

3. RESEARCH TOPIC: the relationship between english proficiency and professional success rate.

RESEARCH DESIGN: Correlational Research

EXPLANATION: Correlational research is used to establish a relationship between two variables. In this case, the research aims to establish the relationship between English proficiency and professional success rate. The research will gather data on the English proficiency and professional success rate of a sample population, and use statistical methods to determine the strength and direction of the relationship between the two variables.

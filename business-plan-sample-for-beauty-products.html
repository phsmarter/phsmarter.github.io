15. what is the component of production or technical plan in business plan​


Answer:

All this production doesn’t happen by magic. Much of an operation manager’s time is spent planning the production process. Think about hosting a large party for your parents’ anniversary. The first thing you have to do is find a location that is large enough to accommodate all the people you will be inviting. Once you have identified the location, you then need to visit the site and decide how it will be laid out. Where should the tables and chairs go, where will you set up refreshments, and what about a gift table? Once you’ve decided on the layout, then you need to start making a list of the materials you’ll need for the party. This includes everything from plates, cups, and napkins to hiring a DJ and a caterer. Lastly, based on the number of guests, you’ll need to calculate how much of everything—food, drinks, etc.—to order.

Operations managers engage in similar planning, but they use different terminology to describe the different parts of the plan. In production planning, the components are facility location, facility layout, materials-requirement planning (MRP), and inventory control.

Answer:

In production planning, the components are facility location, facility layout, materials-requirement planning (MRP), and inventory control.

30. essay from marketing plan business of beauty salon


Beauty salon business plan

The purpose of this business plan is to outline the strategies, objectives and actions required to successfully launch and operate a beauty salon.

Mission

Our mission is to provide quality beauty services in a friendly, professional environment, with a focus on client satisfaction. We will provide our clients with a wide range of services that are tailored to meet their individual needs and budgets.

Objectives

1. To establish a loyal customer base by delivering exceptional customer service.

2. To offer a variety of quality beauty services at competitive prices.

3. To create a welcoming atmosphere that promotes relaxation and comfort.

4. To maintain a safe and sanitary environment at all times.

5. To build a team of highly skilled professionals who can deliver quality services with professionalism.

Market Analysis

The beauty salon industry is highly competitive and there are a large number of competitors in the local area. The majority of these competitors are well established and offer a wide range of services at competitive prices. In order to be successful, we will need to differentiate ourselves from our competitors by offering superior customer service and a more relaxing atmosphere. We will also need to ensure that our prices are competitive.

Product/Service Line

We will offer a wide range of beauty services including hair styling, hair colouring, nails, waxing, skin care, makeup, massage, and other body treatments.

Marketing Plan

1. Advertising: We will use a combination of online and offline advertising to reach our target market. This will include targeted newspaper and magazine ads, online ads, and social media campaigns.

2. Promotions: We will offer regular discounts and special offers to attract new customers.

3. Word of Mouth: We will build a positive reputation in the local area by providing exceptional customer service and quality beauty treatments.

Location

We will be located in a high-traffic area with easy access to public transportation. The salon will be well-lit and inviting, with comfortable furniture and modern décor.

Financial Plan

We will use a combination of personal funds, bank loans and private investments to finance the launch of the beauty salon. We estimate that the initial start-up costs will be approximately $50,000. This will cover the cost of equipment, supplies, rent, and other miscellaneous expenses. We anticipate that it will take approximately six months for the business to break even and begin generating a profit.

Conclusion

We believe that by following the strategies outlined in this business plan, we will be able to successfully launch and operate a profitable beauty salon. We have a clear mission, objectives, and a detailed marketing plan, as well as a strong financial plan. We are confident that with hard work and dedication, we will be able to achieve our goals and provide our customers with quality beauty services.

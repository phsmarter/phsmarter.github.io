29. why is civil society is a major component in local and global communities​


Explanation:

Without an active and thriving civil society, without its collaboration and a close relationship with institutions, no local or global project to improve the quality of life or to preserve the planet will ever be successful. Therefore, every "realistic" vision of the future must include:

projects and plans for strengthening the civil society and local communities;the recognition of the role that civil society organisations perform and can carry out for peace, for an economy with justice and for democracy;the growth of cooperation between civil society and institutions at all levels, from the local to the global one, spreading a culture of reciprocity;respect for the autonomy of the civil society and its non-subordination to the political or economic system.

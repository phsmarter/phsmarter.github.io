Answer:

Statement A is accurate: Jose Rizal was a Filipino writer who wrote the novels "Noli Me Tangere" and "El Filibusterismo." These two books are considered some of the most important works in Philippine literature, and they were instrumental in inspiring the Philippine Revolution against Spanish colonial rule in the late 19th century.

Statement B is somewhat accurate, but it lacks specific information about which books Jose Rizal wrote. While it is true that Rizal was a great writer, it is important to note that his literary works were not just interesting, but also had a significant impact on Philippine history and culture.

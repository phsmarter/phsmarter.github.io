One type of interactive whiteboard is the SMART Board. An interactive whiteboard, at its most basic level, lets you project an image and 'interact' with it by writing on it or moving it around. The SMART Board is used with a projector and is connected to a computer. The projector shows what's open on the computer, and the SMART Board has a touch screen, so you can manipulate anything on the screen with your fingers rather than using a mouse or keyboard (though you can). It's comparable to how you'd use an iPad or tablet. A SMART Board comes with special pens that make writing in different colors quick and easy.

Answer:

Wireless sensors are a key component of smart English classroom design based on artificial intelligent systems. These sensors can be used to monitor and analyze various aspects of the classroom environment, such as temperature, humidity, light levels, and sound levels. By collecting and analyzing this data, an AI system can optimize the classroom environment for optimal learning conditions. For example, the system can adjust the temperature and lighting based on the number of students in the room, or the time of day. Additionally, the AI system can use the data collected from wireless sensors to identify patterns in student behavior and adjust lesson plans accordingly. This can lead to more personalized and effective teaching, and ultimately, better academic outcomes for students. Overall, the application of wireless sensors in smart English classroom design has the potential to revolutionize the way we teach and learn.

Depending on the subject and the amount of original material a teacher produces, multimedia lessons or components of lessons delivered through video or photo include computers, projectors and other electronic devices. 30. Mrs. Jacinto asked a carpenter to construct a rectangular bulletin board for her classroom. She told the carpenter that the board’s area must be 18 square feet. What are the possible dimensions of the bulletin board? Give at least 2 pairs of possible dimensions.


Answer:

The possible dimensions of the bulletin board are, 3ft x 6ft and 2ft x 9ft.

Step-by-step explanation:

       Given:

 Area of bulletin board = 18 square feet

 Shape is rectangular

Required:

 Dimension of the rectangular bulletin board. (Length and Width)

 Give at least two pairs of possible dimension.

Solution:

 Let,

  A = area of the rectangular bulletin board

  L = Length of rectangular bulletin board

  W = Width of rectangular bulletin board

 A = L x W

Since, A= 18 ft²

 L x W = 18

If L is twice as long as the W, L = 2W

Then,

 2W(W) = 18

 2W² = 18

 W = √(18/2)

 W = 3, thus L = 2W = 2(3) = 6

Checking,

 A = L x W = 18

       6 x 3 = 18

             18 = 18, checked

If L is 7 ft longer that W, L = W+ 7

Then,  

 W(W+7) = 18

 W²+7W = 18

 W²+7W-18 = 0, roots are, W = 2 & W = -9

 Real root is positive for this application, thus, W = 2

 L = W+ 7

 L = 9

Checking,

 A = L x W = 18

        9 x 2 = 18

  18 = 18, checked

Therefore,  

  Dimensions are: 6ft x 3 ft  and,

         9 ft x 2 ft

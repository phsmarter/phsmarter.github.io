20. Give the general knowledge on speech writing


Answer:

Understanding the audience: Before writing a speech, it is important to understand the audience you will be speaking to. Knowing the age group, their interests, and cultural backgrounds can help in tailoring the speech to fit their needs and beliefs.

Purpose of the speech: The purpose of the speech needs to be clear. It could be to persuade, inform, entertain, or inspire.

Structure: A well-structured speech consists of an introduction, body, and conclusion. The introduction should grab the attention of the audience, outline the purpose of the speech, and provide an overview of the topic. The body should provide relevant facts, examples, and anecdotes to support the main points of the speech. The conclusion should summarize the main points of the speech, restate the purpose of the speech, and end with a call to action.

Language: The language used in the speech should be clear, concise, and easy to understand. It is important to avoid using jargon or complex technical terms that the audience might not understand.

Practice and delivery: Practice delivering the speech several times to ensure that you are comfortable with the flow and timing of the speech. Eye contact, body language, tone, and pace are important factors to consider when delivering the speech.

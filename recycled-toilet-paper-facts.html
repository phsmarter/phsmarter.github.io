24. saang bansa naimbento ang unang toilet paper​


Answer:

Toilet paper (sometimes called toilet tissue)[1] is a tissue paper product primarily used to clean the anus and surrounding anal region of feces after defecation, and to clean the perineal area and external genitalia of urine after urination or other bodily fluid releases. It also acts as a layer of protection for the hands during these processes. It is usually supplied as a long strip of perforated paper wrapped around a paperboard core for storage in a dispenser near a toilet. The bundle is known as a toilet roll,[2] or loo roll[3] or bog roll[4] in Britain.

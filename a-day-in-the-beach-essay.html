4. make me an travelogue essay in 150 words beach​


As I walked along the sandy shore of the beach, the waves crashed against my feet and the salty sea breeze filled my lungs. The warm sun was shining down on me, and I could feel the sand between my toes. The ocean stretched out before me, with its shades of blue and green blending into each other.

I looked around and saw people of all ages enjoying the beach - some sunbathing on the sand, others playing beach volleyball, and still others taking a dip in the cool water. The sound of laughter and the smell of sunscreen filled the air.

I couldn't help but feel a sense of peace and contentment as I gazed out at the endless expanse of the ocean. The beach is a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, a place to relax and unwind. Whether you're looking to soak up the sun, take a swim, or simply enjoy the beauty of nature, the beach has something for everyone.

Not only for the beauty and relaxation but for the sheer entertainment. That is the three reasons I enjoy it as much as I do.
I go to the beach to get away from all my troubles that are bothering me. To feel the grainy sand all under your toes. To sit there under the sun feeling the warmth of it beating down on your skin. Also to lay on a float in the ocean and just feel the ocean as you are a part of it. Not only to feel but to hear the waves is the ultimate relaxation.

11. 5 positive effects of ofw parents, english or tagalog pooo


Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) are Filipino workers who are employed in other countries. While it can be challenging for OFW parents to be away from their families for extended periods, their work can have several positive effects on their families and the country as a whole. Here are five positive effects of OFW parents:

1. Economic benefits: OFW parents send remittances to their families, which can help improve their financial status. These remittances can be used to pay for basic needs like food, shelter, and education. As a result, OFW parents can help reduce poverty in the country and improve the standard of living for their families.

2. Exposure to different cultures: OFW parents may have the opportunity to travel and work in different countries, which can expose them to different cultures and ways of living. This exposure can broaden their perspectives and help them develop new skills and knowledge.

3. Improved education: With the financial support of their OFW parents, children can have access to better education, including private schools and universities. This can lead to improved academic performance and better job opportunities in the future.

4. Stronger family bonds: OFW parents may have to be away from their families for extended periods, but they can still maintain strong bonds through regular communication and visits. The experience of being separated can also make families appreciate each other more and strengthen their relationships.

5. National development: OFW parents contribute to the economy by sending remittances and by gaining new skills and knowledge that can be applied to the country's development. The money sent by OFW parents can be used to invest in local businesses, which can help stimulate the economy and create job opportunities for others.

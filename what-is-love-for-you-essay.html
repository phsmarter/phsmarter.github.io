21. What type of essay is the love begins when the feelings are gone ? What techniques are used in writing this essay? Do you agree with the author's ideas? Why or why not?​


Answer:

The love begins when the feelings are gone is a persuasive essay. In this essay, the author uses ethos, logos, and pathos to argue that the love between two people can still remain strong even after the strong feelings of love have faded. The author also uses personal anecdotes to illustrate their argument.

Whether or not one agrees with the author's ideas depends on their own beliefs and experiences. Some may agree with the author and believe that the love between two people can remain strong even after the initial feelings of love have faded, while others may disagree and believe that the love between two people can only exist while those strong feelings are present.

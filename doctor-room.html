16. Directions: Solve the value of X using ratio in proportion of the following. (1) The east Avenue Medical Center has a group of 9 doctors who provide assistance to 12 patients. How many doctors are required to provide assistance to 24 patients? (2) If the hospital can accomodate 25 patients in 5 rooms, how many patient can it accomodate in 20 rooms?Yong sa pic din po pasagot thank you ​


(1) We can set up the ratio of doctors to patients as 9/12 = x/24, where x is the number of doctors required to assist 24 patients. To solve for x, we can cross-multiply and simplify:

9/12 = x/24

9(24) = 12x

216 = 12x

x = 18

Therefore, 18 doctors are required to provide assistance to 24 patients.

(2) We can set up the ratio of patients to rooms as 25/5 = x/20, where x is the number of patients that can be accommodated in 20 rooms. To solve for x, we can again cross-multiply and simplify:

25/5 = x/20

5x = 500

x = 100

Therefore, the hospital can accommodate 100 patients in 20 rooms.

10. title: effect of cassava stems and leaves to growth of chili.chapter 31. methodology2.purpose of the study3.Research disign4. material and equipment5.general procedure6.data and data gathering procedurehelp me please ​


Chapter 3:

Methodology (Sorry wala ako methodology)

1. Purpose of the Study

The purpose of this study is to determine the effect of cassava stems and leaves on the growth of chili plants.

2. Research Design

The research design used for this study is a completely randomized design (CRD) with five treatments and three replicates. The treatments used were as follows: T1 (control group), T2 (5% cassava stem extract), T3 (10% cassava stem extract), T4 (5% cassava leaf extract), and T5 (10% cassava leaf extract).

3. Material and Equipment

The materials and equipment used in this study include chili seeds, cassava stems and leaves, distilled water, plastic cups, measuring cups, weighing scale, blender, filter paper, spray bottle, and ruler.

4. General Procedure

The general procedure used for this study is as follows:

a. Prepare the cassava extracts by blending the cassava stems and leaves separately with distilled water, then filter the mixture using filter paper.

b. Label the plastic cups accordingly and fill them with soil.

c. Plant one chili seed in each plastic cup and water them.

d. Spray the cassava extract on the soil and leaves of the chili plants according to their respective treatments.

e. Measure and record the growth parameters of the chili plants such as plant height, number of leaves, and stem diameter.

f. Repeat the procedure for three times to ensure the validity of the results.

5. Data and Data Gathering Procedure

Data will be gathered by measuring and recording the growth parameters of the chili plants. Plant height will be measured using a ruler, while the number of leaves and stem diameter will be recorded manually. The data will be analyzed using analysis of variance (ANOVA) to determine the significant difference between the treatments.

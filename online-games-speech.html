3. speech about online games​


Answer:

Online games have become increasingly popular in recent years, as they offer a fun and convenient way to entertain ourselves and connect with others. They come in many different forms, from first-person shooters to strategy games, and can be played on a variety of platforms, including computers, consoles, and mobile devices. One of the biggest advantages of online games is that they allow players to compete and interact with people from all over the world. This can be a great way to make friends and learn about different cultures. Additionally, online games can improve problem-solving and strategic thinking skills. However, it's important to remember to balance the time spent on gaming with other activities such as spending time with friends and family, physical exercise and other hobbies. It's also important to be aware of the risks of addiction and overspending.

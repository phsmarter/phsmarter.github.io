2.Surveys would be meaningless and incomplete without accounting for the respondents that they’re aimed at. The best survey design practices keep the target population at the core of their thought process.

It is the method used by the researchers to select a specific samplewithin the target population from which they hope to collect data2​


Both option A and B is NOT the definitions best describes the term "research".

The definitions best describes the term "research" is: It is considered as the construction of new scientific knowledge and/or the application of prevailing knowledge in a new and advanced way.

EXPLANATION:

Many problems in this world can be solved because of the innovative discoveries of researchers in various fields which then have an impact on the progress of science and technology. This will also have an impact on the progress of a nation. Therefore we need a way to solve existing problems in a complex and systematic way. One way is to do research. Research is a problem solving process using a scientific method approach in formulating or solving existing problems systematically, but basically this research is a way to find theories or add knowledge.

Research is an effort to obtain facts or principles by collecting and analyzing data (information) which is carried out in a clear, thorough, systematic and accountable manner. In another definition, research is defined as the researcher's effort to test the data he can and generate new knowledge. Therefore, research is a spearhead in the development of science. Research has two main characteristics, namely scientific and sustainable. Research is said to be scientific because it is carried out through systematic procedures and objective fact-finding. Meanwhile, in terms of sustainability, research is a continuous process. The results of a research will continue to be refined along with the development of science.

3. essay about mediocre teacher tells The good teacher explains the superior teacher demonstrates the great teacher inspires? ​


Answer:

A teacher plays a significant role in shaping the future of a student. A good teacher can make a significant difference in a student's life by inspiring them to achieve greatness. However, not all teachers are created equal. There are mediocre teachers who fail to inspire their students and create a positive impact on their lives. In contrast, some teachers go above and beyond to become great teachers who inspire their students to achieve their full potential.

A mediocre teacher is someone who is content with simply going through the motions of teaching. They may deliver their lessons in a monotone voice and fail to engage their students in the learning process. They may also lack the necessary skills and knowledge to effectively teach their subject matter. A mediocre teacher can be described as someone who is just doing their job without any passion or dedication.

On the other hand, a good teacher is someone who is passionate about teaching and genuinely cares about their students. They have the necessary skills and knowledge to effectively teach their subject matter and engage their students in the learning process. A good teacher is also someone who is approachable and willing to help their students when they are struggling. They go the extra mile to ensure that their students understand the material and are prepared for exams.

A superior teacher is someone who not only has the necessary skills and knowledge to effectively teach their subject matter but also demonstrates how to apply that knowledge in real-life situations. They use practical examples to make their lessons more relatable and engaging. A superior teacher is also someone who is constantly learning and improving their teaching methods. They are always looking for ways to improve their teaching skills and make their lessons more effective.

Finally, the great teacher is someone who inspires their students to achieve greatness. They are passionate, knowledgeable, and dedicated to their craft. They use their passion to inspire their students and create a positive impact on their lives. A great teacher is someone who goes beyond teaching the subject matter and helps their students develop life skills, such as critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication.

In conclusion, a mediocre teacher is content with simply going through the motions of teaching, while a good teacher is passionate about teaching and genuinely cares about their students. A superior teacher demonstrates how to apply knowledge in real-life situations, while the great teacher inspires their students to achieve greatness. It is essential to have great teachers who inspire and create a positive impact on their students' lives as they shape the future of our society.

Ang mga guro ay isang propesyon. Sila din  ang pangalawang mga magulang ng mga mag-aaral sa paaralan. Mahalaga ang mga guro dahil hindi pangkaraniwan ang kanilang trabaho. Mayroong iba't-ibang katangian ang guro at ito ay ang sumusunod: Sila ang nagsisilbing inspirasyon sa mga mag-aaral,  mahusay, maabilidad, may pagmamahal sa mga mag-aaral, mayroong disiplina at pananagutan.

KATAWAN NG ESSAY

Ang pagiging guro ay isang propesyon. Sila ang nagbibigay kaalaman sa mga mag-aaral. Sila ay nagtuturo sa paaralan at maari itong gumamit ng pormal at di-pormal na pagtuturo. Ang mga guro ang pangalawang magulang ng mga mag-aaral dahil nakaatang sa kanila ang mga ibang responsibilidad ng mga magulang tulad ng pagdidisiplina. Kapag nagkakamali ang mga mag-aaral ay tinatama at sinasaway ito ng mga guro. Pag nagkakasakit sa paaralan, kapag mayroong kaaway o problema ang guro palagi ang taga pamagitan.Mayroong iba't ibang katangian ay isang guro at ito ay ang sumusunod. Magaling ang isang guro sa komunikasyon dahil kailangan niyang maipaliwanag sa mga kabataan ng maayos ang mga bagay na dapat nilang matutunan. Ang guro ay mapagmahal dahil lahat ng kanyang nalalaman ay handa niyang ibigay sa kanyang mag-aaral. Sila din ang nagsisilbing inspirasyon sa mga kabataan ng tamang pag-uugali, disiplina at mabuting asal.Ang isang guro ay mahusay at maabilidad dahil hindi naman niya maibibigay ang mga bagay na wala siya. Sila ay may pananagutan sa mga mag-aaral. Kung naging matagumpay man ang mag-aaral ito ay tagumpay din ng isang guroKONKLUSYON NG ESSAY

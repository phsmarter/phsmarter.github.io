10. Should you tell a person with a mental illness such as depression to ALWAYS SMILE?​


Answer:

In my opinion you shouldn't. They might be smiling in the outside but in the inside they feel alone, broken beyond repair. It's easy to fake a smile but you can never fake emotions. Give them comfort instead and lend an ear. Just by listening to their struggles, you can make the smile genuinely. Give them advice to make their situation better. And always remember not to force them into saying their trauma, ease them in. Tell them it's okay if they won't tell you their story but remind them that you're there if they need you.

2. Reflection about its better late than never


It's better late than never. Different things take different timelines to achieve. No matter how overdue or how long it takes, what's important is the result. For example, with people experiencing "quarter-life crisis", this is highly applicable. Millennials tend to compare themselves to others' achievements, not understanding the obstacles one has to go through to reach that goal. Also, with the advent of social media, it's easy to view a person's accomplishments and not the repeated failures. Again, let's remember that a success later in life is still better than an easy and passing one.

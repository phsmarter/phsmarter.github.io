1. What is Reflexology foot massage?What is Reflexology Techniques?​


Reflexology foot massage is a type of massage therapy that involves applying pressure to specific areas on the feet to improve overall health and well-being. It is based on the belief that the feet are connected to various organs and systems in the body and that manipulating certain points on the feet can affect these areas.

Reflexology techniques involve using various hand and finger strokes on the feet to stimulate these points and promote relaxation and healing. Techniques may include thumb walking, finger walking, and kneading. Some practitioners may also use tools such as small balls or rollers to apply pressure to the feet. Reflexology may be used to address a variety of health concerns, including stress, insomnia, and digestive issues.

4. difference between acupressure and reflexology (yung maayos sana na sagot, salamat)​


Answer:

Reflexology uses reflexes that are in an orderly arrangement resembling a shape of the human body on the feet, hands, and outer ears. Acupressure uses over 800 reflex points that are found along long thin energy lines called meridians that run the length of the entire body.

Answer:

Explanation:

Dogs and cats are alike in several ways. Both dogs and cats have similar purposes in a  household. They provide friendship to their owners, and often come to be like a part of a  person’s family. Dogs and cats also both have similar sleeping patterns. Dogs and cats can both be found napping throughout the day, but also still find themselves to be sleepy at night. A third way in which dogs and cats are similar is in the way they look. Both animals are relatively small,  with four legs and a tail. Although these similarities exist, there are many ways in which dogs  and cats are different. One difference between the two is their ability to be trained. Dogs tend to be trained to do tricks much quicker and with more ease than cats. Another difference is that dogs typically like to be pet and cuddled, and cats are a more independent animal. Typically, cats do not like to be cuddled, and prefer to be left alone. Dogs, however, cuddle easily and  frequently. Finally, a major difference between dogs and cats is the sounds that each produces. A dog will growl, bark, whimper, and woof, while a cat meows, purrs, and hisses. Though there are many similarities, the differences between dogs and cats make each a unique animal.

11. similarities and differences dog and cat. 1 paragraph.​


Answer:

Dogs and cats are both domesticated animals, but they have many differences. One of the main differences is their behavior. Dogs are known for being loyal and eager to please, while cats are more independent and aloof. Dogs are often trained to do various tasks and tricks, while cats are not typically trained in the same way. Another difference is in their physical characteristics. Dogs come in a wide variety of breeds, each with their own unique physical characteristics, while domestic cats all have a similar appearance. Additionally, dogs tend to be much larger than cats. However, both dogs and cats are popular pets and can make great companions for their owners.

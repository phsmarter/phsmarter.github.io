Answer: Yes, because if you know many languages, you are able to communicate with other people who have different languages. You are also likely to have access to a wider choice of jobs. 27. It is a method of storing audio and video signals A. graphic recording B. Bit recording C. Analog recording D. audio recording ​


Answer:

C. Analog Recording

Explanation:

Analog recording is a technique used for the recording of analog signals which, among many possibilities, allows analog audio for later playback.

Analog audio recording began with mechanical systems such as the phonautograph and phonograph. Later, electronic techniques such as wire and tape recording were developed.

19. Sarah bought a pair of shoes. After 15% discount, the price of the shoes is RM 55.25. Find the original price


Answer:

RM 65

Step-by-step explanation:

To solve this problem we must be familiar with percentage, rate, and base. As we are looking for the original price, we are looking for the base.

Base = Percentage / Rate

Substitute the given. Don't forget that since a discount is involved, the rate is 100% - 15%.

Base = 55.25 / (1 - 0.15)

Base = 55.25 / 0.85

Base = 65

Therefore, the original price is RM 65.

Percentage = Rate * Base

Take note that the price of the shoes after the discount is 100% - 15%. Substitute the given,

55.25 = (1 - 0.15) * Original Price

55.25 = 0.85 * Original Price

65 = Original Price

Therefore, the original price of the shoes is RM 65.

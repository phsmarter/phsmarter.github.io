11. who is Nick Joaquin?


Answer:

                      Nick Joaquin      National Artist of the Philippines for Literature.

Who is Nick Joaquin?

Nicomedes Marquez Joaquin shortly known as Nick Joaquin, was born on the 4th of May in the year 1917. During his time, he was a known Journalist and a creative writer. He writes novels and short stories through the pen name Quijano de Manila. His wife is Salome Marquez Joaquin. He has received an awards of Ramon Magsaysay Award for Journalism, Ramon Magsaysay Award for Literature, and Ramon Magsaysay Award for Creative Communication Arts and many more!

Why is he an important figure in Philippine literature?

Nick Joaquin was considered as an important figure in the Philippine Literature for he was said to be a writer that visualizes the essence of every Filipino being as a return for past Filipino's Hispanics Activity. And as for him, he had given importance for the topic of National identity so it became evident also in his works like La Naval de Manila, After the Picnic and Summer Solstice.  He has express his words for Filipino Patriotism through his literal artistry and made it published, that had made a great contribution to the Philippine Literature.

22. 2. I find people with glasses intellectual. Joaquin is wearing glasses. Therefore, Joaquin is intellectual. Inductive or deductive?


The reasoning you provided is an example of inductive reasoning. Inductive reasoning involves making a generalization based on specific observations or examples. In this case, you have observed that people with glasses are often intellectual and have made the generalization that Joaquin, who is wearing glasses, is also likely to be intellectual.

It is important to note that inductive reasoning is based on probability and is not necessarily a guarantee. While it is possible that Joaquin is intellectual based on the fact that he is wearing glasses, it is not necessarily a certain conclusion. There could be other factors that contribute to Joaquin's intellectual abilities that are not accounted for in this reasoning.

On the other hand, deductive reasoning involves starting with a general principle or premise and then using it to reach a specific conclusion. For example, if we know that all humans need oxygen to survive, and Joaquin is a human, we can deduce that Joaquin needs oxygen to survive. Deductive reasoning is generally considered more certain and reliable than inductive reasoning because it involves reaching a conclusion based on a principle that has already been established as true.

29. ano ang mga katangian ni Saint Nicomedes? paki sagot po ng maayos at Tagalog ​


Answer:

Bago naging isa sa mga nangungunang practitioner ng panitikan ng Pilipinas sa Ingles, siya ay isang seminarista sa Hong Kong - na kalaunan ay napagtanto na mas mahusay niyang mapaglilingkuran ang Diyos at ang sangkatauhan sa pamamagitan ng pagiging isang manunulat. Ito ay makikita sa nilalaman at istilo ng kanyang mga gawa, dahil binibigyang-diin niya ang pangangailangang ibalik ang pambansang kamalayan sa pamamagitan ng mahahalagang elemento sa Catholic Spanish Heritage. Sa kanyang pag-amin sa sarili niyang misyon bilang isang manunulat, siya ay isang uri ng "kultural na apostol", na ang layunin ay muling buhayin ang interes sa pambansang buhay ng Pilipinas sa pamamagitan ng panitikan - at magbigay ng kinakailangang drive at inspirasyon para sa isang mas kumpletong pag-unawa sa kanilang kultural na background. Ang kanyang kamalayan sa kahalagahan ng nakaraan hanggang sa kasalukuyan ay bahagi ng isang sama-samang pagsisikap na mapanatili ang espirituwal na tradisyon at ang orthodox na pananampalataya ng Katolikong nakaraan - na kanyang nakikita bilang ang tanging solusyon sa ating mga modernong sakit.

1. Leadership: Sundiata was known for his exceptional leadership skills. He was able to unite different groups of people and form a powerful empire.

2. Strategic thinking: Sundiata was a strategic thinker and was able to plan his military campaigns well. He was able to defeat his enemies and expand his empire.

3. Cultural understanding: Sundiata was knowledgeable about the customs and traditions of different ethnic groups in his empire. This helped him to maintain unity and peace among his subjects.

Kahinaan (Weaknesses):

1. Physical disability: Sundiata was said to have been crippled from childhood, which could have limited his physical abilities.

2. Lack of formal education: Sundiata did not have a formal education, but he was able to learn from his experiences and the people around him.

10. Anu ang naging buhay ni maghan sundiata ng namatay ang kanyang ama?


Epiko ni Maghan Sundiata

Sa pagkamatay ng kanyang ama, si Maghan Sundiata at ang kanyang ina ay naghirap at kalaunan ay pinaalis sa kaharian. Dala ng kanyang pagiging mahina at masasakitin, si Sundiata ay naging mahina subalit ninais niya na maiahon ang kanyang ina sa kahirapan. Sinikap niyang maging matatag at malakas upang magampanan nya ang kanyang tungkulin bilang tagapag taguyod ng pamilya. Hinanap niya ang pinakamagaling na panday na kanyang ama upang magpagawa ng tungkod na bakal na sya nyang magiging kaagapay sa kanyang paglakad. Sa kanyang pagpupumilit na makatayo ay biniyayaan sya ng isang himala. Ang kanyang tungkod na bakal ay naging pana at sya nyang naging sandata at lakas. Natupad nya ang kanyang pangako sa kanyang ina na maiahon ito mula sa pagkalugmok. Nanumbalik ang paggalang ng mga tao sa kanila ng kanyang ina ng magawa nyang bunutin ang puno ng baobab at maitanim ito sa tapat ng kanilang tahanan. Naging mahusya din syang mangangaso at lider at nakipaglaban kay Soumaoro na dakilang hari ng Sosso. Sa kanyang pagkakagapi kay Haring Soumaoro, naging tanyag ang kanyang pangalan at kalaunan ay kinilala sya bilang hari ng buong Emperyong Mali.

With the Renaissance there were a lot of paintings of holy subjects and another subject called “myth”, which was basically an excuse to have paintings of half naked people in ones private quarters. There were also the ideas of the individual. No one signed their paintings before this time. So it was a humanist idea to place your mark on the painting in order to say “this is my work, I did this”.

Another point

Michelangelo was a sculptor by trade. But the Pope loved his work so much that he commissioned him to paint the Sistine Chapel.

Think about this. How much cussing and swearing was involved in learning how to paint frescos while laying on a scaffold? Then getting off of the scaffold to discover that the paintings looked tiny from the floor.

My Point is that artists were told what to create. It wasn’t until later periods that Artists had more freedom to create what they wanted.

Later artists did a lot of work with allegory up until the very tale end of the 19th century. 28. 2.WRITE AN ESSAY ABOUT TOURISM, CULTURE, ARTS AND RECREATION.​


Answer:

Tourism, culture, arts, and recreation are all interconnected aspects that contribute to the enrichment of our lives. Each element has its own unique features that attract people from all over the world, creating a global exchange of knowledge, experiences, and values.

Tourism is the act of traveling to a new place for leisure, business, or other purposes. It involves visiting different locations, experiencing new cultures, and interacting with local people. Tourism has a significant impact on the economy of many countries, as it generates employment opportunities, foreign exchange earnings, and investments in infrastructure development.

Culture, on the other hand, refers to the customs, traditions, beliefs, and values of a particular group of people. It encompasses various aspects such as language, food, clothing, music, dance, and art. Cultural tourism is a form of tourism that allows people to explore and learn about the cultural heritage of a place. It can include visiting museums, historical sites, festivals, and other cultural events.

Arts, which include music, visual arts, performing arts, and literature, are an important part of human culture. They provide a medium for self-expression and creativity, and they play a significant role in preserving the cultural identity of a community. The arts also play a crucial role in tourism, as many people travel to experience and appreciate different forms of artistic expression.

Finally, recreation refers to the leisure activities that people engage in for enjoyment and relaxation. This can include sports, outdoor activities, hobbies, and other forms of entertainment. Recreation is essential for maintaining physical and mental health, as well as social well-being.

In conclusion, tourism, culture, arts, and recreation are all interconnected and important aspects of our lives. They provide us with opportunities to learn, experience, and appreciate the diversity of the world we live in. By promoting and supporting these aspects, we can create a world that is more vibrant, inclusive, and fulfilling for everyone.

29. why creative writing is significant? and why it's an art form. essay​


Answer:

Creative writing is significant because it allows individuals to express their thoughts, feelings, and ideas in a unique and artistic way. It also allows writers to share their perspectives and experiences with a wider audience, potentially leading to greater understanding and empathy among readers.

Creative writing is an art form because it requires the writer to use their imagination and craft words in a way that engages and moves the reader. It also requires the writer to have a strong command of language and the ability to structure their ideas in a way that is aesthetically pleasing. Like other art forms, creative writing can evoke emotion, inspire creativity, and challenge societal norms and beliefs.

In addition, creative writing can be therapeutic and cathartic for the writer. It allows individuals to process and make sense of their own emotions and experiences, and can be a powerful tool for self-discovery and personal growth.

Overall, creative writing is significant and an art form because it allows individuals to express themselves in a unique and powerful way, engage with readers on an emotional level, and can be therapeutic and beneficial for both the writer and the reader.

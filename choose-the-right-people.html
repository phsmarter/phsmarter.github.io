7. For items 20-25: Choose ONE of the 5 RIGHTS that older people should have as stated in the UN Principles of Older Persons and explain comprehensively.​


Answer:

I will choose the right to independence as stated in the UN Principles of Older Persons. This right recognizes the importance of older persons' autonomy and self-determination in all aspects of their lives, including their personal, social, and economic activities.

Explanation:

Independence means that older persons have the right to make their own decisions and choices based on their individual needs, preferences, and values. This includes the right to live independently in their own homes or communities for as long as possible, with access to the necessary support and services to maintain their health and well-being.

The right to independence also means that older persons should have access to economic resources, including employment opportunities and social protection systems, that enable them to live independently and participate fully in society. This includes access to education, training, and lifelong learning opportunities that enable older persons to acquire new skills and knowledge and contribute to their communities.

Additionally, the right to independence recognizes the importance of ensuring that older persons have access to the information, communication, and technology tools necessary to participate in the digital age and connect with others. This includes access to affordable and accessible internet and digital services that enable older persons to stay connected with their families, friends, and communities and access essential services.

In summary, the right to independence affirms older persons' autonomy and self-determination in all aspects of their lives and recognizes the importance of ensuring that they have access to the necessary support, services, and resources to live independently and participate fully in society.


8. What luminaires are being used in the industries of automotive or aerospace engineering?Why do they follow a specific Philippine Electrical Code upon the installation of luminaire?How does choosing the right luminaire for a certain area affect the work/job of people in that industry?​


Answer:

1.In the industries of automotive and aerospace engineering, a variety of luminaires are used depending on the specific requirements of the application. Some of the commonly used luminaires include:

• High-intensity discharge (HID) lamps: These are used for outdoor lighting applications, such as parking lots and aircraft runways.

LED lights: These are used for both indoor and • outdoor lighting applications, such as office areas, production floors, and inspection stations.

• Fluorescent lights: These are used for general lighting in indoor areas such as offices and production floors.

• Fiber optic lighting: These are used for specific applications such as interior cabin lighting in aircraft.

2.The Philippines Electrical Code is followed upon the installation of luminaires to ensure that the installation is safe, reliable, and meets the minimum standards for electrical safety. The code covers a wide range of aspects, including the selection, installation, and maintenance of electrical equipment, to ensure that they operate safely and efficiently.

3.Choosing the right luminaire for a certain area can have a significant impact on the work/job of people in that industry. For example, selecting the right lighting for an inspection station can improve the accuracy and quality of inspections, leading to better products and higher customer satisfaction. Similarly, selecting the right lighting for a production floor can help reduce errors, improve productivity, and enhance safety. On the other hand, selecting the wrong lighting can lead to reduced productivity, lower quality output, and even safety hazards.

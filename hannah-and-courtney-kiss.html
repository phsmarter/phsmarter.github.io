12. D= light skin, d=dark skin. Hannah's mother is homozygous dark skin and Hannah's father is heterozygous light skin. What is the probability that Hannah has dark skin? ​


Answer:

50%

Explanation:

Punnett square

-----D-----d

d--Dd---dd

d--Dd----dd

There are two genotypes of Dd and two of dd. Dd is light skin, dd is dark skin. Therefore, the chance of having dark skin is 50%.

Mothers genotype: dd

Fathers genotype: Dd

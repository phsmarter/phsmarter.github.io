21. Activity 3 Write a persuasive essay about your favorite food. State why it should be available in all stores. (10 sentences)​


Answer:

Fruits are not just delicious, they are also incredibly nutritious. That's why they should be available in all stores. From apples to strawberries, bananas to grapes, fruits offer a wide variety of tastes and textures that can satisfy even the most discerning of palates.

Fruits are loaded with essential vitamins and minerals that help maintain our body's health. They are rich in dietary fiber, antioxidants, and phytochemicals that protect us against chronic diseases like heart disease, cancer, and diabetes. Moreover, fruits are low in calories and high in water content, making them ideal for those who want to maintain a healthy weight.

By making fruits available in all stores, we can make it easier for people to eat healthily. Not everyone has access to fresh fruits, and for many, the cost of buying fresh produce can be prohibitively high. By ensuring that fruits are available in all stores, we can help promote healthy eating habits, especially among low-income communities.

In addition, having a wide variety of fruits available in stores can help support local farmers and boost the economy. By purchasing locally grown fruits, we can reduce our carbon footprint by minimizing transportation costs and promoting sustainability.

Furthermore, fruits are incredibly versatile and can be used in a variety of recipes. From smoothies to salads, pies to jams, fruits can add a delicious and healthy twist to any meal. By making fruits more readily available, we can encourage people to experiment with new recipes and incorporate more fruits into their diets.

In conclusion, fruits are a vital component of a healthy diet, and they should be available in all stores. By making fruits more accessible and affordable, we can promote healthier eating habits, support local farmers, and boost the economy. So, the next time you go grocery shopping, make sure to stock up on your favorite fruits and enjoy the many health benefits they offer!

10. -Give your Reflection, Idea or Perspective about the movie a walk to remember​


Answer:

Upon watching the movie, I assumed it was just another story about a girl and a boy who fell in love despite their opposing personalities. But as the film continued, I began to enjoy it even more. The movie taught us to live in love and to seize the moment with the person we love so we don't have regrets later. I can tell that their love is so pure and authentic. Jamie's love transforms Landon into a better person, which is how love should be; you inspire the person to do good and be good. You can't force someone to change just because you want them to, if he wants to change, he'll do it the same way Landon did for the sake of his love for Jamie. Though the ending saddened me, I enjoyed the flow of the story, its uniqueness, and how the actors gave justice to Jamie and Landon's story.

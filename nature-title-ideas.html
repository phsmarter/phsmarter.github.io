The receiverdecodes the message in order to give an appropriate feedback. This processis dynamic or always changing as it occurs within a context which constantlyvaries depending on so many factors like the people involved, the place, thechannels used and the situation. Verbalcommunication is the kind of communication that uses symbols that haveuniversal meaning for all involved in the process. These symbols are knownas language used in both spoken and written communication. Nonverbalcommunication, on the other hand, is the use of symbols other than language.

7. e example of long speech admiring your mother ​


Answer:

My dear friends,

Today, I want to take a moment to talk about someone very special to me, someone who has been an unwavering source of support, love, and inspiration throughout my life - my mother.

My mother is the epitome of everything that is kind, generous, and compassionate. She is a woman who has dedicated her entire life to her family and has always put the needs of others before her own. She is a pillar of strength and has always been there to lend a helping hand whenever anyone needed it.

Growing up, my mother was my rock. She was always there to listen to me when I needed someone to talk to, to comfort me when I was sad, and to celebrate with me when I achieved something. She instilled in me the importance of hard work, perseverance, and determination, and always encouraged me to chase my dreams, no matter how big they were.

As a mother, she has always gone above and beyond to provide for our family, sacrificing her own needs to ensure that we had everything we needed. She would work tirelessly to make sure that we were fed, clothed, and happy, never once complaining or asking for anything in return.

My mother's love is unconditional, and her patience knows no bounds. She is a woman who always sees the best in people and is never quick to judge. She has taught me the value of forgiveness, the importance of empathy, and the power of a kind word.

I am so grateful to have been blessed with a mother like her. She is the embodiment of everything that is good and pure in this world, and I am constantly in awe of her strength and resilience.

Explanation

The person I admire the most is my mother. My mother is a hardworking, loving and caring woman who has always been there for me no matter what. She has been my rock and my source of inspiration since the day I was born.

In my eyes, my mother is the epitome of courage and determination. She was raised in a poor family and had to work hard to support herself and her siblings. Despite the obstacles she faced, she never gave up on her dreams and worked her way up to where she is today.

I remember when I was younger and my mother would come home exhausted from work, but she would still make time to cook dinner for us and listen to our problems. Despite her own struggles, she would always put a smile on her face and make sure that we were taken care of.

To me, my mother is the definition of strength and resilience. She has faced many challenges in her life, but she has never let them defeat her. Instead, she has used her experiences to become a better person and to inspire others to do the same.

In conclusion, my mother is the person I admire the most. She has been my guide, my mentor and my best friend. I am proud to be her daughter and I will always strive to be the best version of myself because of her.

20. what is the significance of understanding the psychological causes of crime causes? ​


Answer:

Understanding the psychological causes of crime is important for a variety of reasons. First, it can help to identify and address underlying issues that can contribute to criminal behavior. This can help to prevent crime, or at least reduce its frequency. Additionally, understanding the psychological causes of crime can help inform interventions that can be used to reduce recidivism and help those who have committed crimes to reintegrate into society. Finally, understanding the psychological causes of crime can help inform public policy decisions and help ensure that the justice system is fair and effective.

2. If it's important to you that your guests wear a certain color, you should ask politely and nicely. Don't tell your guests what to wear but rather simply ask them if they would wear a certain color.

As getting fresh fruits is not possible all the time, you can also display paintings or photo arts of fruits, or can have a variety of decor items.

4. A national flag is a flag that represents and symbolizes a given nation. It is flown by the government of that nation, but usually can also be flown by its citizens. ... The burning or destruction of a national flag is a greatly symbolic act.

5. Generally their purpose was to represent the features of the deceased, both to honour them and to establish a relationship through the mask with the spirit world. Sometimes they were used to force the spirit of the newly dead to depart for the spirit world.

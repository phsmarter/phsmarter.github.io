18. explain this quote " all this time i drank you as a cure, when maybe you were the poison‚Äã


"All this time I drank you as a cure, when maybe you were the poison."‚Äã

EXPLANATION

The cure and the poison in the quotation are both symbolism. The cure symbolizes the persona's expectation and the poison symbolizes the persona's realization.

The quotation explains the persona's experience to someone whom she or he had known. It can be inferred that the persona had an "expectation vs. reality" experience. Meaning, in the start, he had a good relationship with that person but later on, as time passed by everything went different from before, it is becoming toxic.

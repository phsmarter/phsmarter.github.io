3. Which Is A Complex Sentence?∆ I Was Late Because I Missed My Train∆ We Got Up Late So I Missed My TrainPA HELP PO BRAINLIEST PAG TAMA NONSE REPORT​


WHAT IS COMPLEX SENTENCE

A complex sentence has one independent clause and at least one dependent clause. This means that the clauses are not equal, they use a coordinating conjunction that changes the rank of one or more of the clauses to make it less equal.

Which Is A Complex Sentence?

∆ I Was Late Because I Missed My Train

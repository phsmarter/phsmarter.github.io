1. Additional Activities Directions: Asked your parents other target games they played showing different skills during their school days. Make a narrative report about the game with the procedure and rules.​


Answer:

When my parents were still young and at school, they used to play the game “dodge ball”. It is a game played with two teams, and the objective is to hit all the opponents with the ball. Here are the mechanics of the game:

1. There shall be two teams with at least two members each.

2. The players may use any rounded material, usually a ball, to hit the opponent, but make sure it is soft and sturdy enough that it would not harm the players.

3. The team who plays first shall split into two and will be at opposite ends of the play area.

4. The opponents shall stay in the middle of the play area, in between the split team members.

5. The players will then take turns to throw the ball and try to hit the members of the opposing team. Opponents who are hit shall leave the play area.

6. The round ends when all the opponents are being hit, and the two teams will switch places.

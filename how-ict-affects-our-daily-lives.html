6. what is the role of ict in our daily lives​


Answer:

Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) play a significant role in our daily lives. They are used in various ways to make our lives more convenient, efficient, and connected. Some examples of the ways ICT is used include:

Communication: ICT allows us to communicate with people all over the world through various platforms such as email, instant messaging, social media, and video conferencing.

Entertainment: ICTs provide us with access to a wide range of entertainment options, such as music, movies, and video games.

Education: ICTs are widely used in education to access online resources and materials, and also to connect with classmates and teachers remotely.

Business: ICTs are widely used in business to manage operations, track inventory, and communicate with customers, suppliers, and employees.

Healthcare: ICTs are widely used in healthcare to manage patient data, communicate with other healthcare providers, and access medical resources and information.

Transportation: ICTs are widely used in transportation to manage logistics, track packages, and communicate with drivers and other personnel.

Government: ICTs are widely used in government to manage public services and communicate with citizens, which include e-governance, e-voting, and e-taxation.

Social Services: ICTs are widely used in social services to improve the delivery of services, such as welfare, social security, and other forms of aid.

Overall, ICTs have become an integral part of our daily lives, making it easier to access information, stay connected with others, and accomplish various tasks, from work to entertainment to healthcare.

21. Name at least 4 uses of ICT in our daily lives and explain. 


Information and Communication Technology has the ability to distribute information quickly, from one place to another far away, of course provides very significant benefits in human life.

These four following are my explanation of the benefits of implementing ICT in daily life:

1. Learning Support

Many computer programs are intended to make it easier for people to learn science and foreign languages. With the support of multimedia, sciences that are often considered difficult can be presented in a form or way that makes people happy to learn them. Some material can also be studied on a computer without having to install the program because the program is installed on the provider and we can simply visit the website.

2. Cost Saving

Businesses can use ICT to run a business in the hope of making cost savings so that they can sell products more easily and increase competition with competitors. Various companies can work together to exchange information automatically, so there is no need to specifically involve other people. Automatic machines can work to replace the role of humans so that production becomes easier and uniform quality can be obtained.

3. Service Improvement

ICT can be used to improve customer service and satisfaction. ATMs, mobile banking, online shops are one form of service improvement to customers. The service improvement is expected to be able to maintain customer loyalty. Many of our governments or districts organize e-gov in order to provide convenience in serving the community or business people.

4. Elimination of Time Constraints

With the support of ICT, someone or companies can serve customers 24 hours a day. They can operate the business without time constraint. Bank customers can withdraw money at any time by simply visiting the ATM machine. Banks don't even need to pay tellers to work 24 hours non-stop.

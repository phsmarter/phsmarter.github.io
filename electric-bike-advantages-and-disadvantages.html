Using/Operating Simple Machines:

Simple machines, such as an inclined plane or a wheel and axle, make our work easier by reducing the amount of force or effort required to perform a particular task. For example, an inclined plane is used to move objects from a lower height to a higher height with less effort. A wheel and axle make it easier to lift heavy objects by reducing the amount of force needed to move them.

In order to use these simple machines, we need to have a basic understanding of their mechanics and how they work. It is also important to properly maintain and operate them to ensure their continued efficiency and effectiveness.

Using Electronic Devices:

Electronic devices, such as electric bikes, make use of advanced technology to make our lives easier and more convenient. Electric bikes, for instance, are powered by rechargeable batteries and use electric motors to assist with pedaling, reducing the effort required to cycle.

To use electronic devices properly, it is important to follow the manufacturer's instructions carefully and ensure that they are maintained and charged properly. Additionally, it is important to keep up-to-date with the latest technological advancements to ensure that we are using these devices safely and efficiently.

A physical asset is an item of economic, commercial, or exchange value that has a material existence. Physical assets are also known as tangible assets. For most businesses, physical assets usually refer to properties, equipment, and inventory.

Physical assets are the opposite of intangible assets, which include such things as brand names, patents, trademarks, leases, computer programs, customer lists, franchise agreements, domain names or trade secrets

29. Express in your own words/opinion (agree or disagree) in the statement that "Human Resources are the most important assets of an organization"​


Answer:

agree

Explanation:

Human resources are considered as the essence of an organisation, because two organisations can have same physical and financial resources but what makes the one to win over the other are human resources. Human resources are recurring in nature, i.e, men are not obligated towards the organisation for working for a life time ( unless contractual), in between they may leave and join other organisation as well. They are not purchased, they are hired by the organisation.

They are considered as a greatest asset but from accounting point of view human resources are not a part of fixed assets as they are not purchased and hence not recorded in the books of accounts but compensation paid to them are recorded as salaries and wages.

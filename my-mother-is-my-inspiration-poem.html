7. Pls help me for my report. Why does Charles Baudelaire wrote the "Song of autumn" for what reason? Who is he's inspiration to wrote that poem? What is the mesaage of the poem? Any realization about the poem?


 seasons in "Autumn Song" represent the current physical and mental state of the poet. The warmth of summer is represented as happiness and joy for the poet. While winter signals cold and dark times. "The whole of winter enters in my Being- - pain" states the pain of winter. Winter being a chilly and gloomy season, we are able to safetly make a comparison with the poet and the season. Autumn however is only mentions to describe the sensation of being in-between summer and winter. "They nail for one a coffin lid, or sound a knell-- For whom? Autumn now-- and summer yesterday!" proves that 'Autumn' represents the anxiety within the poet.

Factors and Changes that Contributed to the Decline in Toys R Us Commercial Success:

Failure to adapt to changing market trendsMaintaining a large-scale supermarket retailing modelInability to compete with online retailers like Amazon Changes in consumer needs and preferencesIncreasing competition from discount retailers like Walmart and TargetRising costs and debt burdensPoor management decisions and leadershipEconomic downturns and recessions

Categorization of Factors into Marketing Environment Components:

Micro-environment:

Maintaining a large-scale supermarket retailing modelIncreasing competition from discount retailers like Walmart and TargetPoor management decisions and leadership

Macro-environment:

Failure to adapt to changing market trendsInability to compete with online retailers like AmazonChanges in consumer needs and preferencesRising costs and debt burdensEconomic downturns and recessions

Ranking of Factors in Terms of Significance:

It is difficult to rank the factors in terms of significance as each factor contributed in its own way to the decline of Toys R Us. However, failure to adapt to changing market trends, inability to compete with online retailers like Amazon, and changes in consumer needs and preferences could be considered as the most significant factors that led to the downfall of Toys R Us.

SINGULAR and PLURAL NOUNS

A singular noun is simply a noun having only one person, thing, place, or idea. From the word “single”, singular infers a single noun. On the other hand, a plural noun is one that has multiple or many persons, things, places, or ideas. Plural nouns are usually noted with -s or -es at the end.

Some of the singular and plural nouns are:

S: girl; P: girls S: market; P: markets S: toy; P: toys S: teacher; P: teachers S: sock; P: socks

Some sentences using each plural noun are indicated.

25. The ratio of toy planes to toy cars to balls in the toy store is 2:5:8. If there are 540 toys in all, how many of each kind of toys are there?


Answer:

There are 72 toy planes.

There are 180 toy cars.

There are 288 balls.

Step-by-step explanation:

2:5:8 = 540

Let x represent the toys.

2x + 5x + 8x = 540

Combine like terms.

15x = 540

Divide both sides by 15.

15x/15 = 540/15

x = 36


Evaluate x as 36

2x

2(36)

= 72

There are 72 toy planes.

5x

5(36)

= 180

There are 180 toy cars.

8x

8(36)

= 288

There are 288 balls.


Checking:

2x + 5x + 8x = 540

2(36) + 5(36) + 8(36) = 540

72 + 180 + 288 = 540

540 = 540

21. How does woodworking knowledge help build our self confidence and strength of character.


WOODWORKING

Modeling behavior is a great way to instill self-esteem. It shows kids that something (what is being asked for or desired behavior) can be done and exactly how to do it. And it makes it relatively simple for them, all they need to do is follow your actions, and then they will have the same outcome.

Woodworking is a great medium to demonstrate the behavior. How to use tools, how to act around tools, how to take care of tools etc.

Going through experiences with someone else can help shape their self-image. It can help them see what is acceptable and help them see how other people think and operate.

Having skills gives people more choices. Not having a choice can make anyone feel powerless, stressed, and anxious.

Skills are something that your child can feel good about, and that is important. They can act as a springboard for other learning areas.

Acknowledging your uniqueness. Believe in yourself and know that you are one of a kind. Confront your fears. Nothing destroys self-confidence more than succumbing to fear. See the positive in every situation.

24. 2. How can a family be an influential factor in the building of one’s self confidence? ​


Answer

Family is where the childhood of every person began. It is proven that experiences during childhood affect an individual's self-confidence. In childhood, the Family and relatives are the first people than an individual encounter and socially interacts with. How the family treats the child would greatly affect his confidence in the future.

Examples of childhood experiences that can affect a person's confidence:A child that is always scolded in the house tends to be timid and afraid to socialize. This child grows into a person that is not sure if he is doing the right thing.Children that grow with a physically abusive parent tend to become bullies. Their confidence is defined by hurting others physically and emotionally.When a parent shows to their children a prayerful life that is dependent on God. The child learns to be patient, humble, meek, kind, and courageous to others as well.

In summary, the family plays an important role in developing a person's attitude which includes self-confidence. The experiences at home is what greatly affects a child's attitude.

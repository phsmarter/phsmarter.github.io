10. what is ascent to a grotto all about? Please help meNo joke pls​


"Ascent to a Grotto" is a painting by Jean-Baptiste-Camille Corot, an artist who was known for his landscape paintings. The painting depicts a winding path that leads up to a rocky grotto surrounded by lush greenery and trees. The painting is focused on capturing the beauty and spiritual essence of nature, which was a common theme in Corot's work. The painting invites the viewer to take a journey through the natural world, to explore the winding path that leads up to the grotto, and to experience the tranquility and serenity of the landscape. Overall, the painting conveys a sense of peace, harmony, and contemplation, which is characteristic of Corot's style.

The Municipality of Lucban or Lukban is a second- class municipality located at the foot of Mount Banahaw in the Province of Quezon. It is famous for its annual Pahiyas Festival, which is held every May 15th in honor of San Isidro Labrador the Farmer.

15. phiyas of lucban,quezon​


Answer:

Every May 15, the townsfolk of Lucban, Quezon, don their houses with fruits, vegetables and colorful kiping (a leaf-shaped wafer made of rice and dyed with food coloring) in celebration of the Pahiyas Festival. The word ‘pahiyas’ was derived from the word ‘payas’, which means decoration or to decorate. The reason behind such practice dates back to the 15th century, when farmers used to offer their harvests at the foot of Mount Banahaw.

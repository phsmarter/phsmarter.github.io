1. what is academic and non academic writing​


Academic

Academic writing is the kind of writing that is used in school and research settings. It's formal and uses fancy words, and it tries to prove arguments with evidence. It has a beginning, middle, and end, and it usually includes notes or quotes from other people's work.

Non-Academic

Non-academic writing is all the other kinds of writing like stories, news articles, personal writing, and business writing. It's written for everyday people and it sounds more like a conversation. The way it looks and sounds can be different depending on what it's about

In a quasi-experiment, 111 undergraduate students were assigned to either an observational learning or learning-by-doing condition, with or without reflection.

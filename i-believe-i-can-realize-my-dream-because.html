1. i believe i can realize my dream because?


Answer:

I believe I can realize my dream because I am equipped and I have the capacity to do so.

Explanation:

A dream should not be left as a dream only. A dream is something we want to do, we want to happen, or we aspire to become. If you dream to be a pilot, then what are you going to do to make it real?  Simple, just study and train to be a pilot. It takes effort, patience, and determination to realize a dream.

But there are dreams that are absolutely unrealistic. So you must weigh it first before you aim realizing your dreams. Check, consult, and reflect. Is this dream of mine worth realizing? If not, then it's time to dream a new one.

5. Sample script for moderator in college webinar


Here is a sample script for a moderator in a college webinar:

Good morning/afternoon everyone, and welcome to today's college webinar. My name is [Moderator Name] and I will be your moderator for this session. Before we begin, I would like to go over a few ground rules to ensure that we have a productive and respectful discussion.

Please mute your microphones when you are not speaking to minimize background noise.

Please raise your hand if you would like to ask a question or make a comment. I will call on you when it is your turn to speak.

Please be respectful of others and their opinions. We want to create a safe and inclusive space for all participants.

If you have any technical issues, please let me know and we will do our best to resolve them.

Now, let's begin today's discussion on [topic of discussion]. Our guest speaker for today is [Guest Name], who is an expert in the field of [expertise]. [Guest Name], would you like to start by introducing yourself and giving a brief overview of the topic?

11. ENGLISH: Script Writing Make a script about "AVOIDING COVID-19 TRANSMISSION AS A STUDENT"


Answer:

INT. CLASSROOM - DAY

We see a group of students seated in their desks, all wearing masks. The teacher, MR. SMITH, is at the front of the classroom, also wearing a mask.

MR. SMITH

Good morning, class. Before we begin today's lesson, I want to remind you all of the importance of avoiding COVID-19 transmission.

CUT TO:

INT. HALLWAY - DAY

We see a student, JEN, walking down the hallway. She approaches a group of students who are not wearing masks and standing close together.

JEN

(to the group of students)

Excuse me, but could you please put on your masks? It's important for all of us to do our part in preventing the spread of COVID-19.

CUT TO:

INT. CAFETERIA - DAY

We see a group of students seated at a table, all wearing masks and sitting at a safe distance from one another.

STUDENT 1

I know it's not always easy to remember to wear our masks and social distance, but it's important to keep ourselves and others safe.

STUDENT 2

I completely agree. And if we all do our part, hopefully we can get through this pandemic as quickly as possible.

CUT TO:

INT. CLASSROOM - DAY

MR. SMITH is concluding the lesson.

MR. SMITH

And remember, as students, it is our responsibility to do our part in preventing the spread of COVID-19. This means wearing masks, practicing good hand hygiene, and staying socially distant.

The students gather their belongings and begin to leave the classroom, all still wearing their masks.

THE END

Note: This script is a short and simple representation, to give you an idea about how to write a script about "AVOIDING COVID-19 TRANSMISSION AS A STUDENT". There can be more creative and elaborate ways of scripting it.

[FADE IN]

EXT. STREET - DAY

[We see a young college student walking down the street, wearing a cloth face mask and carrying a backpack.]

[SUPER: Avoiding Covid-19 Transmission as a Student]

[CUT TO]

EXT. COLLEGE CAMPUS - DAY

[We see the student walking up the steps of a college campus, taking a moment to look around.]

[CUT TO]

INT. CLASSROOM - DAY

[The student walks into the classroom, taking a seat. He looks around and notices other students wearing masks and keeping a safe distance.]

[CUT TO]

EXT. CAMPUS LIBRARY - DAY

[The student walks into the library and looks around. He notices signs posted around the room with reminders to wear masks and keep a safe distance from others.]

[CUT TO]

[The student enters the restaurant and takes a seat. He notices that all employees are wearing masks and that tables are spaced a safe distance apart.]

[CUT TO]

EXT. STREET - DAY

[The student is walking down the street. He notices people wearing masks and maintaining social distancing.]

[CUT TO]

22. sample of script about badminton ​


sample of script about badminton:

Title: "The Thrill of Badminton"

Opening Shot: Wide angle shot of a badminton court.

Voiceover: Badminton is a sport that requires agility, speed, and precision. With its roots tracing back to ancient India, this sport has evolved into one of the most popular games across the world.

Cut to a close-up shot of a badminton racket and shuttlecock.

Voiceover: The game is played with a racket and a shuttlecock. The objective is to hit the shuttlecock over the net and land it within the opponent's court.

Cut to a shot of two players warming up on the court.

Voiceover: Badminton can be played as a singles or doubles game, and the players need to possess a combination of physical fitness and strategic thinking to win.

Cut to an interview with a badminton coach.

Coach: "In badminton, it's important to have a strong grip on the racket and be able to move quickly on the court. You need to anticipate your opponent's moves and be able to react quickly."

Cut to a sequence of shots showing a game in progress.

Voiceover: As the game progresses, the players need to be able to adjust their strategies and adapt to the changing conditions on the court.

Cut to an interview with a professional badminton player.

Player: "Badminton is a physically demanding sport, but it's also mentally challenging. You need to stay focused and concentrate on your game plan."

Cut to a shot of a player hitting a powerful shot.

Voiceover: The thrill of badminton lies in the speed and power of the shots, as well as the skill and agility required to play the game.

Closing Shot: Wide angle shot of players shaking hands after a game.

Voiceover: Badminton is a sport that requires dedication, passion, and perseverance. Whether you're a professional player or just playing for fun, the thrill of badminton is something that will keep you coming back for more.

There is a deep hunger in youth and young adults—a hunger for love, for truth, for meaning, for belonging, and for purpose that the culture cannot satisfy. Answer:

The sacraments of the Church are incredibly relevant to my growing in faith, service, and communion in mission. Through the sacrament of baptism, I was initiated into the Christian community and given the grace to live out my faith in the world. The sacrament of Eucharist strengthens me as I receive the body and blood of Christ, and reminds me of my call to be a part of the body of Christ. Confession allows me to confess my sins and receive forgiveness, which is essential for growing in holiness. Confirmation, Anointing of the sick and Matrimony also provide a powerful way for me to live out my faith in different aspects of my life. They remind me of the love and grace of God, and inspire me to share that love with others.

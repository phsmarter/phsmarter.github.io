7. as. future chef cook why do you need to maintain clean and to sanitize such kitchen tools equipment after every usage​


Answer:

Cooking safe food means that you must sanitize your kitchen tools and equipment. We cannot stress enough how crucial it is to regularly sanitize your kitchen.

Explanation:

For one, cleaning removes contaminants (e.g. dust and dirt) from something (e.g kitchen tools). On the other hand, sanitizing means killing pathogens (e.g. germs) on a clean surface.

The Center for Disease Control and Prevention recommends doing both. In particular, you need to clean something first, then sanitize it.

1. Sanitizing prevents pathogens from spreading to your food.

One of the main reasons why you need to sanitize your tools is to prevent pathogens such as viruses, bacteria, and molds from spreading to your food.

When you use your tools to prepare raw food, pathogens from the raw food transfer to your tools. If you do not sanitize your tools, pathogens will spread to your finished product.

Similarly, dirty tools can transfer pathogens from one food to another. For instance, if you use baking tools and equipment to mix raw eggs and don’t sanitize them afterward, pathogens build up on your tools. As a result, the pathogens spread to the next baked treat you make.

2. Sanitizing lets you comply with health standards.

Local authorities set health standards to make sure that clean food products go to the market. Without these standards, restaurants and factories won’t be helped accountable in case their workers and customers get sick.

To ensure everyone’s health, you must follow the health standards set by the authorities. These include sanitizing your tools and keeping your kitchen as clean as possible.

12. As a future chef or as an ordinary cook, why do we have to follow proper storage of poultry and game bird products?​


As a future chef or ordinary cook, it is essential to follow proper storage of poultry and game bird products to ensure food safety and prevent the spread of foodborne illnesses. Poultry and game bird products are highly perishable and can be contaminated with harmful bacteria such as Salmonella and Campylobacter.

Proper storage of these products includes keeping them at the correct temperature to prevent the growth of bacteria. Poultry and game bird products should be stored in a refrigerator at or below 40°F (4°C) and cooked to a minimum internal temperature of 165°F (74°C) to kill any harmful bacteria that may be present.

Cross-contamination is another concern when handling poultry and game bird products. This can occur when raw poultry or game bird products come into contact with other foods, utensils, or surfaces, potentially spreading harmful bacteria. To prevent cross-contamination, it is important to keep poultry and game bird products separate from other foods, wash hands and surfaces thoroughly after handling these products, and use separate cutting boards and utensils.

Following proper storage and handling procedures for poultry and game bird products is not only important for food safety but also for maintaining the quality of the product. Proper storage can help to prevent spoilage and maintain the flavor and texture of the meat, ensuring that it is enjoyable and safe to consume. As a chef or cook, it is our responsibility to follow food safety guidelines to protect the health of our customers and ensure the success of our business.

13. As a future chef, what are the issues that you need to give consideration to become successful in this business?​


Things to do to become a successful chef

We may have seen cooking contests on television featuring famous chefs with great skills. But to be a chef is not as easy as imagined, because being a chef is not just cooking.

To become a successful chef, we must understand the characteristics of each food ingredient, and how to process them and combine them into delicious food. For that, the first and most important issue that must be resolved is knowing the basics of cooking well. With good basic skills, the future chef will increase their chances of becoming successful in the culinary field.

If a chef works in a restaurant or opens his own restaurant, then they must also be able to make recipes, and determine which foods are suitable to be combined into appetizers, main courses, and desserts. Therefore, after mastering the basics of cooking, someone who wants to become a professional chef should know different types of recipes and foods until they are proficient enough to make their own.

The chef also has the task of being a leader in the kitchen. With that, not only cooking skills, a chef must also have good leadership skills. A chef must be able to communicate clearly to manage the various tasks of other kitchen members at one time so that the dishes can be served properly and on time. Time management and team management of a chef must be good so there no time is wasted.

No less important than the skills mentioned above, a chef must have a strong mentality. Because the chef will work more than 10 hours in the kitchen with the risk of being injured by a knife or fire, he must meet customer expectations and also have to ensure that the menu is made interesting and tasty, as well as other tasks that require strong mentality.

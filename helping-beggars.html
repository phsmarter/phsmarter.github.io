18. Do you think it is justified to help beggars or street children every time? Why? Why not?​


Answer:

It is difficult to say whether it is always justified to help beggars or street children, as each situation is unique and may require different types of help. However, it is generally considered important to help those in need, especially children, whenever possible. This is because children are vulnerable and may be unable to take care of themselves. By providing help, we can improve their lives and give them a better chance at a successful future.

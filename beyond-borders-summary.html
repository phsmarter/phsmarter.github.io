6. solve the given problem A 196in² portrait ismounted with a border on a square frame, if the total area of the border is29in²,what is the length of the side of the border?​


Answer:

15 in

Solution:

196 + 29 = 225 is the total area of the frame.

Since the shape of the frame is square, the length of it is [tex]\sqrt{a}[/tex]

Thus, L = [tex]\sqrt{225}[/tex] = 15 in

Don't forget the sign!

20. give me a summary of the "beyond the return to face to face classes"​


Answer:

The "Beyond the Return to Face to Face Classes" is an article that discusses the challenges and opportunities of returning to in-person classes after the COVID-19 pandemic. The article highlights the need for schools and educators to adopt a comprehensive approach that addresses the physical, emotional, and academic needs of students.

The article discusses the importance of prioritizing the mental health of students and providing them with the necessary support to help them cope with the stress and anxiety that may arise from the transition back to in-person classes. It also emphasizes the need for schools to implement safety measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19 and ensure the well-being of students and staff.

The article also suggests that educators need to adopt innovative teaching methods that leverage technology and incorporate experiential learning opportunities to engage students and enhance their learning experience. The article concludes by emphasizing the need for collaboration among stakeholders, including educators, parents, and policymakers, to ensure a successful transition back to in-person classes

Philippines Political Party System: Then and Now 4. Filipino Caregivers in Japan 5. Travails of Senior Citizens at the LRT/MRT Stations 6. The Lone Grade VI Speed Reader of UST High School 7. Student Activism Since the Marcos Era8. Listening to Battered Wives and Raped Victims​


1. Ethnography - This type of research design involves a detailed description and interpretation of the culture and social behavior of a group or community. The Muslim wedding rites topic would be best studied using ethnography as it involves the traditions and customs of a particular group of people.

2. Case study - This research design is used to investigate a phenomenon within its real-life context and is characterized by in-depth data collection and analysis. The effectiveness of the K-12 curriculum would be best studied using a case study design as it involves examining the implementation and outcomes of the curriculum in a specific setting.

3. Historical research - This type of research design involves the study of past events and their significance in the present. The Philippines political party system: then and now topic would be best studied using historical research as it involves tracing the evolution of the political party system in the country.

4. Phenomenology - This research design is used to explore the lived experiences of individuals and how they make sense of those experiences. The Filipino caregivers in Japan topic would be best studied using phenomenology as it involves examining the experiences of Filipino caregivers working in a foreign country.

5. Grounded theory - This research design involves the development of a theory or explanation based on the data collected from the research participants. The travails of senior citizens at the LRT/MRT stations topic would be best studied using grounded theory as it involves exploring the experiences of senior citizens and developing a theory to explain their struggles.

6. Case study - This research design is used to investigate a phenomenon within its real-life context and is characterized by in-depth data collection and analysis. The lone Grade VI speed reader of UST High School topic would be best studied using a case study design as it involves examining the reading skills of a specific student.

7. Historical research - This type of research design involves the study of past events and their significance in the present. The student activism since the Marcos era topic would be best studied using historical research as it involves tracing the history of student activism in the Philippines.

8. Phenomenology - This research design is used to explore the lived experiences of individuals and how they make sense of those experiences. The listening to battered wives and raped victims topic would be best studied using phenomenology as it involves examining the experiences of victims of abuse and developing an understanding of how they make sense of their experiences.

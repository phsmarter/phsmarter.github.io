11. what is your view about divorce?are you in favour of this?​


Answer:

Yes, divorce is important to society.

Some people are not “marriage material” (they are violent, sociopaths, pedophiles, mentally ill, etc.) Other couples are simply too toxic together. It’s best to let them separate rather than kill each other and destroy other lives.

History shows us that if you don’t have divorce, you have desertion and abandonment of women and children. You also have infidelity and higher rates of domestic violence.

History of Divorce

Divorce in the Western world didn’t exist much before the 1500s. The Dutch had civil marriage (and therefore theoretically civil divorce). In America in the early 1600s, the Puritans who were influenced by the Dutch saw divorce as a necessity for keeping a peaceful society. If two people can’t live together, why not release them? At first the law prohibited remarriage, but the death rate due to disease in the New World was so high, the Puritans finally allowed divorced people to remarry (and repopulate the colonies).

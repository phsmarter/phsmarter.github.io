23. 1. How can you format a bulleted list? 2. How can you format a numbered list? 3. How can you customized a bulleted list?​


Answer:

1. To create a bulleted list:

-Select the text you want to format as a list.

-On the Home tab, click the drop-down arrow next to the Bullets command. A menu of bullet styles will appear.

-Move the mouse over the various bullet styles.

- The text will be formatted as a bulleted list.

2. Define a new number format

-Select the text or numbered list you want to change.

-On the Home tab, in the Paragraph group, click the arrow next to Numbered List.

-To change the style, click the down arrow next to Number style and choose numbers, letters, or another chronological format.

3. Define a new bullet

-Select the text or bulleted list you want to change.

-On the Home tab, in the Paragraph group, click the arrow next to Bulleted List. , and then click Define New Bullet.

-Click Symbol and then click the symbol you want to use.

-Click OK.

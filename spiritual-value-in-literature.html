12. Why is Indian literature rich in spirituality and morals?


Answer:

Just like every place has a specialty, India’s specialty lies in spirituality. This could be due to the fact that the major religion in India (call it Hinduism or Sanatana Dharma) along with other religions such as Buddhism, Jainism emphasize on spirituality and consider Liberation (Nirvana / Moksha) or Bhakti (supreme devotion to Lord) as the ultimate goal of human life. Many saints and sages have performed spiritual practices and have attained this highest goal of Liberation and Devotion in this diversified land called India. Hence the literature is rich in spirituality.

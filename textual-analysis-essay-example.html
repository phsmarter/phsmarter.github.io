17. what are the examples of textual aids


EXAMPLES OF TEXTUAL AIDS

In a lot of instances especially inside the classroom where discussion normally occurs, we tend to see textual aids. Though these textual aids are not used only inside the classroom, they can also be used in other fields. Some examples of textual aids are the following:

highlighted wordsgraphstablesmaps

Highlighted Words

Highlighted words are examples of textual aids since it serves on the purpose of helping the reader understand texts easily. These words refer to the words that are either in boldface, italicized, and or capitalized. Normally words are highlighted to be emphasized.

Graphs

Graphs are another example of textual aid. It is a visual comparison of data and information in bar or pie or line format. Its purpose is to present facts and figures to be read in a short period of time.

Maps

Another example of textual aids is the maps. Maps are drawings that show the basic shape of the land and other geographical, political, or historical features. It presents information in visual form.

Tables

Tables are also an example of textual aid. It organizes a large amount of information in a small space. A table is able to present all kinds of data.

19. example of thesis title about garbage pit​


Answer:

A Case Study about the Improper Waste Disposal in Barangay Mojon Tampoy, Philippines

One of the most common problem in the Barangay Mojon Tampoy is the improper waste disposal, which could affect the current living of the residents of the barangay. The type of research approach is a qualitative research. In this study the researchers aim to create a solution for the main problem that the researchers found out by interviewing the residents. The Theory on Waste Management (2004) by E. Pongrácz, P. S. Phillips, and R. L. Keiski is used as a reference through the progression of the study. An interview was conducted with (9) willingly residents of the chosen community, barangay Mojon Tampoy. The question asked to the respondents were their basic information, source of income and the problems that they encountered in the community.

According to the result, improper waste disposal is the major problem of the residents in Mojon Tampoy. These problems were defined as incinerating, throwing their garbage on the well and the lack of garbage collection. These problems could harm not only the residents but also the environment itself. The knowledge of the residents about their current situation is based on their own experiences. Although they were able to give advice to people that might experience the same situation by segregating their waste properly but since they don’t have enough funds to support garbage truck collection they resulted to the incinerating of their garbage.

The general objective of our study is to teach the residents of Brgy. Mojon-Tampoy on how to properly dispose their garbage, to find alternative ways in eliminating their garbage and to ensure that the community will maintain the cleanliness in Brgy. Mojon Tampoy. This study mainly focuses on how the practices of the people in the community can deeply affect, not just our nature but also their health. With our proposed project, which is the compost pit with shredder, this will help reduce the carbon footprint and smoke pollution present in the barangay. This is to guarantee that the community will bring an end to incinerating their garbage.

21. Title thesis and justification example​


Answer:

A potential thesis statement and justification for a paper could be:

Thesis: The use of solitary confinement in prisons has a detrimental effect on the mental health of inmates and should be banned.

Justification: Solitary confinement, also known as isolation, is a form of punishment used in prisons that involves isolating inmates in a small cell for extended periods of time. Studies have shown that this type of confinement can have severe psychological effects on prisoners, including depression, anxiety, and even psychosis. It can also lead to self-harm and suicide. Therefore, the use of solitary confinement in prisons should be banned to protect the mental health of inmates.

This thesis is supported by research on the psychological effects of solitary confinement and aligns with the principle of protecting the well-being of prisoners.

25. what is the best title for quantitative criminology research?​


The ‘best’ title is the one most suited to your research project. You’ve done the bulk of the research, right?

If not, let me explain how research works in criminology. You choose a topic, then read as much as you can about it to establish the gaps in research for your topic (this is called a literature review). Then comes the part where you do YOUR research; this is where you gather and analyse the data. After this, you can start forming conclusions that (if you’ve done your research properly) fill in at least one gap in existing criminological knowledge.

You’ll notice there’s one thing I didn’t mention in the above paragraph: the title. That was intentional, and there’s a good reason for it. When doing criminological research, the title is literally the last thing you do. The title of a research project has to reflect the content of that research. Therefore, you HAVE to do the research before you can decide what to call it.

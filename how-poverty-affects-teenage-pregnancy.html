23. how does teenage pregnancy affects the students?​


There are a number of ways in which teenage pregnancy can affect students. First and foremost, it can interfere with their schooling. Pregnant teenagers may have to missclass or drop out of school altogether if they cannot manage their workload. Additionally, teenage pregnancy can put a strain on the student’s financial resources as they may have to spend money on things like doctor’s appointments, child care, and diapers. Finally, teenage pregnancy can be emotionally taxing for the student involved as they navigate through both the physical and hormonal changes that come with being pregnant.

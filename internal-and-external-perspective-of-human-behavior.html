29. True or False: The consumer behavior is affected by both internal and external factors.


This statement is absolutely true. The ability of a consumer to decide is based both on internal factors and external factors. Internal factors are those that refers to the consumers need and availability of resources, if a consumer has a need to buy a certain product or to avail of a specific service because he/she believed that it fits to his/her need is an example of internal factor influenced behavior. This is based entirely of the consumers own judgement and is not affected by the surrounding or other people.

On the other hand, external factors are those factors that affect the consumers decision making because of their surrounding, the community they are in and the statusquo norm. If a person feels that he/she needs to buy a sunflower dress because it is what usually most people in his/her age wears, then his behavior is affected by the external factor which is his/her surrounding.

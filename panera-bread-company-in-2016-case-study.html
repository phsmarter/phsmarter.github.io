1. in 2017 the company's revenue 36/25 of it's 2016 revenue what is the company's revenue as a percentof its revenue in 2016


Answer:

144%

Step-by-step explanation:

Problem:

Convert 36/25 to percent.

Simply divide multiple of 100 by the denominator of the given fraction. In this problem the number is 100:

100 ÷2 5 = 4

Multiply the numerator by the result:

36 × 4 = 144

The new fraction is 144/100 or read as 144 hundredths.

Therefore:

36/25 = 144/100

144/100 = 144%

The company's revenue increased by 144% in 2017.

A tv company purchased a machinery for p 100,000.00 on july 1, 2015. it is estimated that it will have a useful life of 10 years; scrap value of p 4,000.00; production of 400,000 units and working hours of 120,000. the company uses the machinery for 14,000 hours in 2015 and 18,000 hours in 2016. the machinery produces 36,000 units in 2015 and 44,000 units in 2016. compute the depreciation for 2016 using each method given below:a. straight line method(slm)b. working hoursc. output method


Answer: 2016 Depreciation Expense

a) Straight Line Method = 9,600

b) Working Hours Method = 14,400

c) Output Method = 10,560

Explanation:

a) Depreciable Value (DV) = 100,000 - 4,000

   DV = 96,000

   Dep'n = DV divided by Useful Life

   Dep'n = 96,000 / 10 yrs

   Dep'n under Straight Line = 9,600

b) Dep'n = (96,000/120,000 hrs) * 18,000

   Dep'n = 14,400

c) Dep'n = (96,000/400,000 units) * 44,000

   Dep'n = 10,560

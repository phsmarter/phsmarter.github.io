Answer:

Reflection Paper about "Jose Rizal" Film of GMA by Marilou Diaz Abaya.

The film "Jose Rizal" directed by Marilou Diaz-Abaya is an epic biopic that portrays the life of the Philippine national hero, Jose Rizal. The film begins with Rizal's childhood in Calamba, Laguna, where he showed exceptional intelligence and artistic talent. It then follows his journey to Europe, where he continued his studies and experienced racism and discrimination. The film also depicts Rizal's romantic relationships with Leonor Rivera and Josephine Bracken.

The movie highlights Rizal's patriotism and his love for his country. It shows how he used his intelligence and creativity to fight for the Filipino people's rights and freedoms. The film portrays Rizal as a man of integrity, who stood up against the Spanish colonizers' oppression and corruption.

The main question that the film seeks to answer is, "What made Jose Rizal a hero?" The movie answers this question by showcasing Rizal's love for his country, his intellectual prowess, and his unwavering dedication to fighting for the Filipino people's rights and freedoms. It also highlights his works, such as his novels "Noli Me Tangere" and "El Filibusterismo," which exposed the injustices and corruption of the Spanish colonial government.

My own reflection and understanding based on the film is that Jose Rizal was truly a remarkable individual who used his talents and intellect to bring about change and fight for his country's freedom. The film reminded me of the importance of critical thinking, education, and standing up for what is right. It also reminded me of the sacrifices that our ancestors made for us to enjoy the freedoms and rights that we have today. Overall, the film "Jose Rizal" is a masterpiece that every Filipino should watch to gain a deeper understanding of our national hero's life and legacy.

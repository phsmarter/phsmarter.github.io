1. You would like to travel to Boracay. How can you find out where this place is located?

Answer: Atlases and maps

Answer: THESAURUS

3. Your class is doing research papers on someone who has made an impact on our country's history. Where can you find information about the person you chose for this report?

Answer: BIOGRAPHICAL REFERENCE

4. You are reading your module on English 7 and you find a word you do not know. How can you find the definition to the word?

Answer: DICTIONARY

5. This week's spelling words are hard for you to understand. You have already written the definition of the words. You believe that by finding synonyms and antonyms of the words might give you a better understanding of the definition. Where can you find synonyms and antonyms for a word?

Answer: THESAURUS

6. Tomorrow your class is going to learn about the inventors. Your teacher mentioned that Benjamin Franklin invented bifocals. How could you find more information about Benjamin Franklin?

Answer: ENCYCLOPEDIA

7. You are learning about landforms and would like to find out what an island is. Where would you look for the definition of an island?

Answer: DICTIONARY

8. You are trying to find out how far it is from your house to your favorite park. Where would you look for this information?

Answer: Atlases and maps

9. You are working on mountain language. One of the questions asked is to look for the opposite of the word. You do not know the opposite of the word. Where can you find the antonym for this word?

Answer: THESAURUS

Answer: ENCYCLOPEDIA

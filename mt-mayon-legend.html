2. According to legend how can the smoke surrounding mt mayon can be explained ​


Answer:

The legend tells of a beautiful and virtuous princess named Daragang Magayon (which means "Beautiful Lady" in English), who lived in a village near the foot of Mt. Mayon. Many suitors were attracted to her beauty, but she remained single until one day, she met a young warrior named Panganoron. The two fell in love and their love story quickly spread throughout the region.

However, a rival tribe was envious of their love and plotted to separate them. They killed Panganoron in a battle, and upon hearing the news, Daragang Magayon was heartbroken and climbed the peak of Mt. Mayon where she took her own life. It is said that the smoke surrounding Mt. Mayon represents the tears and smoke of the two lovers, still searching for each other.

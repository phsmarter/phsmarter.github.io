g) Your body posture

I'm fine with my body posture, even though I don't have the same beautiful and ideal body as others. I love myself for who and what I am.

h) How I deal with problems in life

I stay strong and positive because life must go on, and even when we face problems and difficulties, we have to accept them and move on, no matter what.

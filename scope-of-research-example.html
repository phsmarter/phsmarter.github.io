GIVE ME SCOPE AND DELIMITATIONExample: SCOPE AND DELIMITATION THE SCOPE OF A STUDY EXPLAINS THE EXTENT TO WHICH THE RESEARCH AREA WILL BE EXPLORED IN THE WORK AND SPECIFIES THE PARAMETERS WITHIN THE STUDY WILL BE OPERATING.​


Topic: New Generation Students attitude towards LGBTQIA+ and how it is Gradually embraced and accepted.

Scope: The scope of this topic will be focused on the attitudes of the new generation of students towards the LGBTQIA+ community. Specifically, the study will look into how young people perceive the LGBTQIA+ community and how this perception affects their acceptance and embrace of the community. The study will also examine the factors that contribute to the changing attitudes towards the LGBTQIA+ community among young people.

Delimitation: The study will only focus on the attitudes of new generation students, specifically those in universities and colleges, towards the LGBTQIA+ community. The research will not delve into the attitudes of older generations towards the community or that of younger students in primary and secondary schools. The study will also not examine any legal or political issues related to the LGBTQIA+ community.

Scope: The study will use both quantitative and qualitative research methods to gather data. Surveys and questionnaires will be used to collect quantitative data on the attitudes of new generation students towards the LGBTQIA+ community. Interviews and focus groups will be used to gather qualitative data on the factors that influence these attitudes.

Delimitation: The study will only focus on students from selected universities and colleges. The research will not include students from vocational schools or community colleges. The study will also not examine the attitudes of students from different cultural or religious backgrounds.

Scope: The study will analyze the data collected from the research to identify any patterns or trends in the attitudes of new generation students towards the LGBTQIA+ community. The study will also examine the factors that influence these attitudes such as exposure to LGBTQIA+ culture, education, and social norms.

Delimitation: The study will not present any opinions or conclusions about the nature of the LGBTQIA+ community. Instead, the study will focus only on the attitudes of new generation students towards the community. The research will also not examine any negative attitudes or experiences that the LGBTQIA+ community may have encountered.

Scope: The study will provide insight into how the new generation of students perceives and embraces the LGBTQIA+ community. The research will also contribute to the ongoing conversation about the acceptance and inclusion of the LGBTQIA+ community in society. Ultimately, the study aims to promote understanding and acceptance of diversity in all forms.

Answer:

cope:

1. Examining the history of LGBTQ rights and movements.

2. Investigating the current legal and social status of LGBTQ individuals in different countries.

3. Exploring the psychological and physical health disparities experienced by LGBTQ individuals.

4. Analyzing the impact of discrimination on LGBTQ individuals’ mental health and well-being.

5. Investigating the intersectionality of race, gender, sexuality, and other identities in relation to LGBTQ experiences.

6. Examining the role of media in shaping public opinion on LGBTQ issues.

7. Investigating how religious beliefs shape attitudes towards LGBTQ individuals and communities.

2. Difficulty obtaining accurate information from participants due to fear of discrimination or lack of trust;

3. Limited resources for conducting research on this topic;

4. Difficulty generalizing findings due to cultural differences between countries or regions;

5 .Difficulty obtaining funding for research on this topic due to its controversial nature;

6 .Lack of understanding from some researchers about LGBT issues which can lead to biased results;

29. scope for online seller research​


Answer:

Target Market Analysis: Understanding your target audience is key to creating effective marketing strategies. Conduct research to identify the demographics, preferences, buying habits, and pain points of your target audience.

Competitor Analysis: Analyzing your competitors' strengths and weaknesses can help you stay ahead of the competition. Research their products, pricing, marketing strategies, and customer reviews to identify gaps in the market that you can fill.

Product Research: Conducting product research can help you identify the demand for certain products in the market. You can use tools like Google Trends, Amazon Best Sellers, and social media to identify popular products and trends.

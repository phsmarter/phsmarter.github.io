1. give me an example of hyponymy​


Answer:

A "hyponym" is a word or phrase whose semantic field is more specific than its hypernym. For example: verbs such as stare, gaze, view and peer can also be considered hyponyms of the verb look, which is their hypernym. Hypernyms and hyponyms are asymmetric.

5. what is hyponymy plss answer​


Answer:

[English]

A hyponym is a word or phrase whose semantic field is more specific than its hypernym. The semantic field of a hypernym, also known as a superordinate, is broader than that of a hyponym.For example, verbs such as stare, gaze, view and peer can also be considered hyponyms of the verb look, which is their hypernym.

[Tagalog]

Ang hiponony ay isang salita o parirala na ang larangan ng semantiko ay mas tiyak kaysa sa hypernym nito. Ang larangan ng semantiko ng isang hypernym, na kilala rin bilang superordinate, ay mas malawak kaysa sa isang hipononyo. Halimbawa, ang mga pandiwa tulad ng titig, pagtingin at kapantay ay maaari ring maituring na mga hiponemya ng hitsura ng pandiwa, na kung saan ay ang kanilang hypernym.

As nouns the difference between polysemy and hyponymy

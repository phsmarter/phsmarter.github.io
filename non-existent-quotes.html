12. Task 8.2 A QUOTE ON QUOTE

14. Explain this quote"All media exist to invest our lives with artificial perceptions and arbitrary values." ~ Marshall McLuhan


Answer:

The quote by Marshall McLuhan suggests that the media we consume, including television, newspapers, and the internet, are not a reflection of reality but rather create an artificial perception of it. The media, according to McLuhan, has the power to shape our perceptions and influence our values. This is because the media selectively presents information that aligns with its own interests, agenda, or bias. Moreover, the media tends to prioritize sensational stories over more important ones, which can lead to arbitrary values being placed on certain events or issues. McLuhan argues that we should be aware of the media's influence on our perceptions and values and should actively seek out alternative sources of information to develop a more well-rounded and nuanced understanding of the world around us.
